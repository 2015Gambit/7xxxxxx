Vietcong: Fist Alpha / Vietcong: Fist Alpha
the official expansion for the game Vietcong. New actors, new weapons and aircraft. New missions for both single and multiplayer games.
'Fist Alpha' - this is your group. A squad of U.S. Special Forces, as opposed to mortal enemy - Viet Cong.
The five members of the group "Fist Alpha" from the original game adds two new Vietcong soldier - Sergeant First Class Warren Douglas and Nguyen Nam.
In the course of seven new missions group "Fist Alpha" will fight the Viet Cong units and DIA in the central plateau of Vietnam.
Go through the minefields, protect your base, locate and destroy Viet Cong weapons factories, dispose of enemy artillery positions, locate and rescue downed U.S. Air Force pilot.
Fighting in the eight new maps with eight new types of weapons, not including machetes and bayonets.
Go back to the battlefield as an American or a Viet Cong soldier in the exciting multiplayer game for 64 players over LAN or the Internet.
