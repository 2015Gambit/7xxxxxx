The BBC TV version of Sir Arthur Conan Doyle'd classic tale about an expedition to a remote part of the Amazon jungle to prove the existence of dinosaurs.

Stars:
Bob Hoskins ....  Prof. George Challenger 
James Fox ....  Prof. Leo Summerlee 
Tom Ward ....  Lord John Phillip Roxton 
Matthew Rhys ....  Edward Malone 
Elaine Cassidy ....  Agnes Clooney 
Peter Falk ....  Reverend Theo Kerr 

There are two soundtracks available:
1. The movie itself
2. Commentary by Director, Stuart Orme, and the Producer, Christopher Hall.
