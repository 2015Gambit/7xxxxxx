Guitarist Magazine July 2012

Guitarist is a monthly music making magazine published by Future Publishing in 
the UK. The magazine features reviews of newly released guitars, amplifiers and 
other equipment, plus interviews with guitar players, features on the guitar 
industry, news articles, and features on playing technique that are accompanied 
by tablature. The magazine covers both electric and acoustic guitar playing and 
each month has a comprehensive reviews section that aims to cover the definitive 
guitars and gear that have released that month.
