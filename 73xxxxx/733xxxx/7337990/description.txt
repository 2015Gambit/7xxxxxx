Shrapnel 4.1 Aka Ahzteks Weapon Overhaul by ahztek666
Aka "That mod that adds over 150 new guns to fallout new vegas"

This mod requires CASE: http://newvegas.nexusmods.com/mods/43915

How to install:
Download and install CASE: http://newvegas.nexusmods.com/mods/43915
Extract to Fallout New Vegas
Enable Ahzteks Weapon Overhaul.esp in FOMM
Make sure Archive Invalidation is enabled in FOMM

Also adds a new room to the lucky 38 with a massive armory and a new vendor to freeside.
