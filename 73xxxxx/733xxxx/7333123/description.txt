This is the complete comic set for the Serenity comics following the Firefly TV show and the movie Serenity by Joss Whedon.It includes all the comics in PDF form (with the exception of downtime which is a flash file and available online here http://www.usatoday.com/life/comics/2010-11-08-serenity08_ST_N.htm).

In the Serenity Comics rar file is:
All three Better Days Comic Books in pdf format.
All three Those Let Behind Comic books in pdf format.
The Other Half in pdf format.
Float Out in pdf format.
The Sheppard's Tale in pdf format.
And Downtime in htm format (it is actually just the usa today page downloaded)

Enjoy.
