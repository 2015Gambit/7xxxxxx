Huw Edwards presents live coverage of the afternoon's events, as the royal family travels by carriage to Buckingham Palace to mark 60 years of the Queen's reign.


1.30pm Huw Edwards chats to celebrity guests at Buckingham Palace about the weekend's events, while Fearne Cotton goes behind the scenes of last night's Diamond Jubilee Concert.


2.00pm The Household Cavalry, the Foot Guards and the King's Troop Royal Horse Artillery get into position as the Queen prepares for her carriage procession from Westminster Palace to Buckingham Palace.


2.30pm As the royal party approaches the palace, Sonali Shah and Chris Hollins chat to some of the people who have gathered along the route.



3.30pm The Queen and Prince Philip step out onto the balcony to greet the thousands of well-wishers, while the military pay their respects with a flypast by the RAF and a `feu de joie' gun salute in the forecourt of Buckingham Palace.
