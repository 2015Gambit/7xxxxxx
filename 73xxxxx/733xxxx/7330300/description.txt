Young and classy Parisian duo LOGO is back with a third EP “Jacob & Fabrice” out now on Kitsuné!

Showing a variety of influences, Logo’s new EP “Jacob & Fabrice” is a real milestone for the band. Two tracks, two curious identities, a brand new energy that comes out of electro beats and catchy loops. Logo is proving once more that the French touch still has its best days ahead!

Also featured in the package, “Jacob” remixed by the Frenchmen duo Darabi, and “Fabrice” remixed by the mysterious Crackboy and the San Francisco crazy duo 5kinAndBone5.
