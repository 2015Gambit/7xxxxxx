Second and just as grand incarnation of Peruvian garage masters Los Shains (or as they liked to spell it 'Los Shain's'). Snappy mid-sixties beat sound and wonderful organ swirls. They kept their cover selections up to date, but didn't let the heavy American songs that were starting to show up on the radio change their sound too much- all the singles collected here are still perfect for doing the swim on the dancefloor. From master tapes, even. Tracklisting: If, Purple Haze, White Room, Blues in E, Sweet Wine, Open the Door, Guau Guau a Go Go, The Sky is Fallin, Somebody to Love, Indian Love, It's All the Same, Wicked World, Never Go Back to Georgia, Sea of Joy, Good Inspirations, If.
