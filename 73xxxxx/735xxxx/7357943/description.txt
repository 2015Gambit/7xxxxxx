Video Game High School is an action/comedy internet web series by Rocket Jump Studios (of Freddie Wong YouTube fame). The series stars Josh Blaylock as Brian D, Johanna Braddy as Jenny Matrix, Jimmy Wong as Ted Wong, and Brian Firenzi as The Law. Rocket Jump Studios describes it as "a show about best friends, first loves, and finding that perfect head shot." The plot revolves around a teenager, Brian D, who defeats a gaming celebrity, The Law, on a live talk show. Brian is then invited to the premier video game high school, VGHS.
