Chyna, Olive, and Fletcher roll out the red carpet for teen movie sensation Sequoia Jones, who is playing the role of a tween musical prodigy and follows Chyna around to study for her role. But when Chyna learns Sequoia takes her acting research very seriously, chaos ensues as Sequoia tries to steal Chyna's life.
 
Special guest star: Zendaya as Sequoia Jones
