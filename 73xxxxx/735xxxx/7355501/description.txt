Grateful Dead Download Series Volume 12 - 4/17/69
Initial release : 2006

The twelfth in a series of shows made available by downloading from the official Grateful Dead site. All of the April 17, 1969 show at Washington University, St. Louis, MO. There are two additional tracks from a rehearsal at the Avalon Ballroom on January 23, 1969.

Notes

The first ten minutes of this show were not recorded on reel-to-reel tape. This portion of the show has been remastered from the cassette master of the show. The rest of the show is mastered from the master reel-to-reel tapes.
