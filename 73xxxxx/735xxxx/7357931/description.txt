Euro 2012 Highlights - 15th June 2012

Matt Smith introduces highlights of England's second match in group D against Sweden at the Olympic Stadium in Kyiv. England have been frustrated by Sweden at major championships in the past, but boss Roy Hodgson will not be worrying about history as he targets a win tonight. Plus highlights of the other game in group D - Ukraine v France. With comment and analysis from Jamie Carragher, Gareth Southgate and Gordon Strachan
