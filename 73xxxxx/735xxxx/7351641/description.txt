Psychonauts is a platform video game created by Tim Schafer, developed by Double Fine Productions and published by Majesco, starring the voice of Richard Horvitz as Raz. The game was released on April 19, 2005, for the Xbox, April 26 for Microsoft Windows and June 21 for PlayStation 2. It was released on Steam on Oct 11, 2006, as an "Xbox Original" through Xbox Live Marketplace, and on the GameTap subscription service.[1] On November 5, 2009, Psychonauts also became available through the online distribution service GOG.com through their partnership with Majesco. In September 2011, a new version with support for Steamworks features, including a Mac OS X port, was released. In May 2012, a Linux port was released through the Humble Indie Bundle V.
