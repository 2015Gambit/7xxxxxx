Zé Ramalho Canta Bob Dylan – Tá Tudo Mudando is the second tribute album by Brazilian singer-songwriter Zé Ramalho, released in 2008. This time, he pays an homage to Bob Dylan, whose "Knockin' on Heaven's Door" had already been covered by him. Most of the songs were rewritten to Portuguese language. The album cover is a clear reference to Dylan's promotional film clip for the 1965 song "Subterranean Homesick Blues". "O Vento Vai Responder", a cover of "Blowin' in the Wind", was used in the soundtrack of the Rede Globo telenovela Caminho das Índias.

The album was nominated for the 2009 Latin Grammy Award for Best Brazilian Rock Album, but lost it to Agora by NX Zero and Sacos Plásticos by Titãs.
