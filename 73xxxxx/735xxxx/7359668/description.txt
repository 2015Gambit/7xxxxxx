Wish Upon a Blackstar is the second album (third overall) from American electronic rock project, Celldweller. While the album has been a work in progress since 2004[2][3], finalized songs from the album were not released until mid-2009. The full album was released on June 12, 2012.[1] Grant Mohrman, producer of the debut Celldweller album, returned to co-produce the album with Klayton.
