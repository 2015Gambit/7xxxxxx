MasterChef România is the Romanian version of the television cooking game show franchise MasterChef.
The first episode aired on 20 March 2012 at 8:30pm on PRO TV.The show currently features judges Florin Dumitrescu, Sorin Bontea and Cătălin Scărlătescu.This season were 21 semi-finalists.
