Op disk 1 de afleveringen: (Helaas nergens in Nederlands gevonden)

1. Intervention (Part 3) - 29 september 2010 -
Everett and the crew continue their efforts to save Destiny while
Holding off the Lucian Alliance invaders.

2. Aftermath - 5 oktober 2010 -
When the crew's food and water situation become critical, Rush has
no choice but to override the ship's controls and take them to a
planet locked out of the navigation system. Meanwhile, Young and
Wray disagree on whether to trust their Lucian prisoners. 

3. Awakening - 12 oktober 2010 -
The ship find another Ancients ship that is almost identical to
Destiny, and could provide them with the means to return to Earth. 

4. Pathogen - 19 oktober 2010 -
When Eli and Rush realize Chloe is acting strangely, they investigate
and determine that a pathogen placed by her alien captors allows them
to control her... and learn the secrets of Destiny.
