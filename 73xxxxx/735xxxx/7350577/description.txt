Bluetooth ┬⌐
Description
Did it take too many steps just to turn on/off Bluetooth? You can now turn on/off just by launching this app. Just in a second, you can activate or deactivate Bluetooth.
In addition, you can chat with other users nearby.

**************************************
*** IMPORTANT READ BELOW TO ENABLE ***
**************************************
The app's setting needs to be ENABLED to turn on/off upon launch.
iPhone / iPad -> Settings ->"scroll down" -> Bluetooth -> Quick Mode "turn on switch" ->"kill the app and relaunch"
