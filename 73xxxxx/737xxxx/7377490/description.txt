Based on A Deadly Invention by Jules Verne.

The evil Count Artigas plans to use a super-explosive device to conquer the world from his headquarters inside an enormous volcano. Lice action, animation, puppets, engravings and larger-than-life performances enhance this fantastic, delightful film.

Directed by Karel Zeman. English Dubbed.

Black & White, 1958.

This is a classic every film student ought to see.

83 minutes.
