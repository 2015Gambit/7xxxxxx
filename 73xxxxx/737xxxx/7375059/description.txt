Do you want a simple and intuitive app designed to meet both professionals and amateurs users?
Your search is over: the answer to your needs is Studio Lab!

Every picture, every image can become art!

Capture images directly from the built-in camera or from the camera roll, choosing one of the many fantastic default filters.
Optionally you will be able to add one of the many photo frames, having fun creating cool "Roll" effects.

For professionals or people who are more experienced in controlling the color, there's a comfortable panel in which there sliders to adjust:
- Brightness
- Contrast
- Saturation
- Hue
- Red
- Green
- Blue

Always remember this golden rule: there are no perfect photos, pictures may be beautiful, but they can be always become better!
