"History and Lore" are the extras included in the Blu-ray discs. These extras explain important details of places, people and habits for the people that have not read the books. The narrators are the actors that perform in the TV series.

"History and Lore" son unos cortos contenidos dentro de los discos de Blu-ray que explican detalles importantes de los lugares, personas y costumbres del mundo de Westeros a personas que no han leído los libros. Los narradores son los mismos actores de la serie.
