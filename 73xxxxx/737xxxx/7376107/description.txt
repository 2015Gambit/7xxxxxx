DESCRIPTION:
"Ghosts from the past" - a game about contact with otherworldly forces. On the ancient rites to communicate with ghosts and the modern means of detecting ghosts. The game world - alive and frightening - is based on photographs Cornwall, located on the southwest coast of England. This picturesque place has inspired the creation of the mystical stories of many British writers, among them Charles Dickens, Wilkie Collins and Arthur Conan Doyle.
Along with the main character you will go to the gloomy town on the east coast of Britain. Your goal - to solve a very old mystery of the place. At your disposal - the latest technology used by professional "ghost hunters" in real life. But beware - the arrival of a stranger will be glad not all residents of the city, it does not matter - living or long dead ...
