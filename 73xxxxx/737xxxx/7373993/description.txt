The Amazing Spider-Man is an upcoming open world action/adventure video
game, based on the upcoming film of the same name starring the superhero,
Spider-Man. It is being developed by Beenox, developer of Spider-Man:
Shattered Dimensions, and Spider-Man: Edge of Time and published by
Activision. The Amazing Spider-Man is set to be released on June 26, 2012
in North America and June 29, 2012 in Europe, on Microsoft Windows, Xbox
360, PlayStation 3, Nintendo 3DS, Nintendo DS and Wii. The game will serve
as an epilogue to The Amazing Spider-Man film. The game has been scripted
and written by Seamus Kevin Fahey, of Battlestar Galactica fame.

PLOT
Spider-Man villains Alistair Smythe, Rhino, Iguana, Felicia Hardy, Vermin,
Scorpion and Lizard will be antagonists in the game. The Lizard makes a
small cameo in the game. The game is set after the film, where an Oscorp
executive with access to the research of Dr. Curt Connors (aka The Lizard)
plans to create more cross-species experiments. Meanwhile, Oscorp develops
a large group of robots to combat the cross-species individuals, but wind
up causing more harm to the city than good. It's now up to Spider-Man to
save the city from both the cross-species experiments and robots.
