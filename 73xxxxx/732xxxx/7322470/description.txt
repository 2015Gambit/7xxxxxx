QwikProducts by Mainstream Engineering - EPA 608 Self Study CD for the Universal Exam

This software is used to prepare HVAC students for the EPA 608 Universal Exam.  This software prepares sample exams (multiple choice) from actual EPA 608 exams for Core level thru Universal level.  It grades your practice exam and allows you to continue until you know all the correct answers.

You can "not answer" any of the questions, and the software will mark them all wrong, giving you the correct answers for each question during the review.

Work with these practice tests for a period of time and you will pass the Universal Exam EPA 608!
