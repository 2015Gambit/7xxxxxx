On Sunday morning Chris welcomed Thomas Mann and Norman Ornstein, authors of It's Even Worse Than It Looks: How the American Constitutional System Collided with the New Politics of Extremism, for their long-awaited first time discussing the controversial book on a national Sunday news program. Also, they talked about the upcoming recall election between Republican Governor Scott Walker and his challenger Democratic Milwaukee Mayor Tom Barrett and they discussed Florida's attempts to purge voters from registration lists.

Joining Chris were:

Michael Steele, MSNBC analyst and former Republican National Committee chairman.

Michelle Bernard, MSNBC political analyst and president of the Bernard Center for Women, Politics & Public Policy.

Ari Berman, political correspondent for The Nation.

Randi Weingarten, president of the American Federation of Teachers.

Bob Herbert, distinguished senior fellow at Demos.org.

John Nichols, Washington D.C. correspondent for The Nation.

Judith Browne-Dianis, co-director of The Advancement Project.
