Who's Missing is a compilation of rare and previously unreleased songs by The Who. Its second part Two's Missing was released on 11 April 1987.

The CD was reissued in Japan on December 24, 2011 with additional bonus tracks drawn from the Japanese only bonus disc for "Then and Now". The album was remastered by Jon Astley from the original analog master tapes.
