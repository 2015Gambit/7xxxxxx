Q9 Oldvox is analogue synth with powerful configuration. Contains an automatic mode detune. Two separate oscillator and filter and LFO. The synthesizer has a bright warm and fat sound. It's ideal for use in dance, techno, dubstep, electronica, drum n bass, big beat, dub, reggae and all other styles. LFO modulation sections was designed specialy for Dubstep genre but can be used in other styles too of course. The plug-in contains: OSC section. Filter section. LFO section. Modulator section. Master section. Delay section.
