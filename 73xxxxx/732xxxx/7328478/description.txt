The Rough Guide to the Brain is ideal for readers who want to know more about how their brain and mind works and what goes wrong when it doesn't. From how humans have evolved such an impressive organ to how it achieves the feat that is a person. The book includes numerous insights from leaders in their fields, there's no better way to stimulate your grey matter.
