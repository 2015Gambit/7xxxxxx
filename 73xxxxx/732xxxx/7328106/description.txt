The Left Hand Path is a show dedicated to the underground in Sludge/Doom/Stoner Metal from the rust belt (midwest america) and around the world. You will also get the pleasure of hearing Black Metal and some Death Metal from time to time, and depending on the mood various other genres.Kicking ass and taking names from Columbus,Ohio The Left Hand Path is all about bringing to the listener a barrage upon the ears.
