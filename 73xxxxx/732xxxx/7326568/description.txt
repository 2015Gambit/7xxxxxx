PES 2012 Tippeliga Patch by Sabatasso
--------------------------------------


- PES league transformed into the Norwegian top division
- All fake player names, team names and kits fixed
- Separate exe files for multi-patch compability


The Norwegian top division "Tippeligaen" is a fairly weak league which is ideal starting grounds for Become a Legend. It is also very fun to start out Master League in this league, where the average player is in the mid 60's in the overall rating.

Credits & installation instructions are in the ReadMe.txt file.


Have Fun!
