C&C3 Original + Expansion Set

For Mac
English Version

Mac OS X 10.4.9+ required.

Q. How to install?
A. Just copy them to your Applications folder.

Q. Minimum System?
A. Intel Core Solo. 1GB+ Memory and nVIDIA GeForce 8600M GT. ATI graphics are not tested. Intel graphics(ex. GMA950 and X3100) are tested and working but they have poor performance. Intel HD graphics are not tested.

Q. Are they Lion compatible?
A. Yes. 10.7.4 tested and working fine(Kane's Wrath has a minor graphical glitch but it's okay).

Q. Is Kane's Wrath Stand-Alone?
A. Yes, It is.

Q. Does Multiplayer work?
A. Not tested.
