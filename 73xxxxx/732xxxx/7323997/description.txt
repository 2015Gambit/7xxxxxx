"Princess Sheep": Sheep gets royally carried away playing princess. It is only after she hurts Bear's feelings that sheep learns that a true princess isn't selfish, but kind and generous instead. "The Birds": Sheep does her zany best to care for an egg she finds until the baby bird is born and reunited with its mother. "W Drought": On a hot summer day, all the water in WordWorld starts to dry up because the letter W is missing! Sheep leads the Wordfriends in the hunt to discover just who is causing the W drought. "Chef Sheep": Sheep has to take over Pig's cooking show when Pig gets a pot stuck on his face. Will Sheep summon up the confidence she needs to save the day.
