[center]Kyon: Big Damn Hero the Visual Novel
Volume I[/center]

A fan-created adaptation of the wonderful fanfic of Haruhi Suzumiya series, [url=http://soulriders.net/brian/fanfic/kbdh/]"Kyon: Big Damn Hero"[/url] by Brian Randall, to the kinetic novel format.
Quoting author, "A TVTropes website/Haruhi crossover. Kyon with a beam saber. Haruhi aware of her powers. An unsubtle harem plot. Did I mention the Higurashi crossover? Now say it with a straight face - I dare you."

Covers prologue and first 4.5 chapters.
