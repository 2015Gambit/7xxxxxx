This is the Original United States Release of Hard Truck II, not King of the Road.

Simply mount the .iso with Alcohol or anything else and install. No Key Required.

You do have to mount it every time you play however.

This game was designed to run on Windows 95 / 98 / and ME (With a Patch). The game is runable on Windows XP, but Windows Vista and 7 are not yet tested.

Offical Minimum Requirements:
Win95/98
P266Mhz
32MB RAM
4x CD-ROM
Direct3D accelerator
