MEZDEKE ( Songs For BellyDance )
Collection vol 1 to vol 11

Mezdeke is a very famous trio of ethnic Turk dancers.
They are known to never perform without their face veils 
and are internationally recognized for their CDs, 
which contain a pop mix of Arabic (not just Turkish) music. 
Their music has been described as pan-Arabic with strong Egyptian influences. 
Their dance style is reported to reflect this.
