Global Sounds 2 - Journey Into Music

Released: 2002
Genre: Folk, World, Latin
 
http://www.discogs.com/Various-Global-Sounds-2-Journey-Into-Music/release/2249217

A 30-track sampler for the price of 6,99 Euros published by the combined Dutch recordstores, -companies and rightssociety Buma/Stemra to promote WorldMusic (Global Sounds). 

It contains a booklet with 30 coupons which gave you a discount of 6,99 Euros each when buying one of the albums represented on this sampler.
