In November 2011, The Devin Townsend Project played four epic sold-out shows in London, England. 'By A Thread - Live In London 2011' contains all four sold-out shows where 'Ki,' 'Addicted,' 'Ghost,' and 'Deconstruction' were all performed in their entirety. Features guest vocals courtesy of Anneke van Giersbergen.
