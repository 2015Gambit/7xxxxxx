Clash of the Titans (2010)    Action / Adventure / Fantasy

Perseus, mortal son of Zeus, battles the minions of the underworld to stop them from conquering the Earth and the heavens.
 


Director: 
Louis Leterrier

 Writers: 
Travis Beacham (screenplay), Phil Hay (screenplay)

Stars:
 Sam Worthington, Liam Neeson and Ralph Fiennes



Wrath of the Titans (2012)    Action / Adventure / Fantasy

Perseus braves the treacherous underworld to rescue his father, Zeus, captured by his son, Ares, and brother Hades who unleash the ancient Titans upon the world.
 


Director: 
Jonathan Liebesman

 Writers: 
Dan Mazeau (screenplay), David Johnson (screenplay) 

Stars:
 Sam Worthington, Liam Neeson and Rosamund Pike
