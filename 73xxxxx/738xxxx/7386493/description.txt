Sea of Green - Vol. 2 - Closet Cultivation

Sea of Green, Vol. II: Closet Cultivation is part two of a three-volume instructional series on marijuana cultivation. The 60-minute video is a step-by-step guide that shows how to turn a closet or attic into a growing room that will produce 12 to 18 inch buds every two weeks. Topics covered in this "beginner's guide" include germination, cloning, lighting, and harvesting.
