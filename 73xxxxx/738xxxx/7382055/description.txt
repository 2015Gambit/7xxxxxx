Euro 2012 Highlights Match of the Day 23rd June 2012

Colin Murray presents highlights of France v Spain in the Euro 2012 quarter-finals, as the winners of Group C meet the Group D runners-up in the Ukrainian city of Donetsk. Home to Shakhtar, the Donbass Arena was designed by the same company responsible for Manchester City's Etihad Stadium, Bayern Munich's Allianz Arena and Beijing's Bird Nest. In the 2008 tournament, eventual winners Spain defeated Italy in a penalty shoot out in the quarter-finals
