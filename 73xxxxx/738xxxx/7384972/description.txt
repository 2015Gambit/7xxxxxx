The first three seasons of the Sci-Fi Channel gem Warehouse 13. Eddie McClintock and Joanne Kelly lay Secret Service Agents Pete Latimer and Myka Bering, who work for a top-secret Warehouse in South Dakota that retrieves, neutralizes and store powerful artifacts once owned by historical figures. With their boss, ex-NSA analyst Artie Nielsen (Saul Rubinek), the mysterious and creepy Mrs. Fredrick (CCH Pounder), and young, tech-savvy Claudia Donovan (Allison Scagliotti), they find whatever is about to ruin the world's day, snag it, bag it, and tag it. Dive into the world of endless wonder that is Warehouse 13.
