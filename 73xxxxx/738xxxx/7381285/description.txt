        Release Notes:

        Femmes de chez nous is the fifth studio album by Christine
        Fellows, released February 1, 2011 on Six Shooter Records.

        The album features songs in both English and French, Fellows'
        first album to do so. It features songs from Reliquary/Reliquaire, 
        a multidisciplinary performance work presented by a collective of 
        artists, including Fellows, Shary Boyle, Leanne Zacharias, Alex McMaster, 
        Caelum Vatnsdal and Lise Brémault, at Le Musée de Saint-Boniface Museum 
        in 2009.

        The album will also be packaged with a DVD containing a live
        performance of Reliquary/Reliquaire.
