The first three seasons of intriguing crime procedural White Collar, starring Tim DeKay as FBI Agent Peter Burke of the White Collar Unit, stage star Matt Bomer as ex-forger Neal Caffrey, who might still be in the "lie, cheat, steal" business, Willie Garson as Neal's compatriot and buddy, Mozzie, and Saved By The Bell alumni Tiffan Thiessen as Peter's wife, event planner Elizabeth Burke.
