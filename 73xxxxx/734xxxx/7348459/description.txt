Crossing  paths, The  Crossroads Festival  2012, met from  21 to 24 March with handpicked bands from  genres rock, pop, folk rock, blues-rock and indie to the expectant ears of a motley crowd. 
The location for this event  become a tradition, was the iconic concert pub Harmonie  from  Bonn-Endenich. 
Rockpalast has recorded for us, among other things, the Swedish The Soundtrack of Our Lives who played at night Wednesday 21 March.
