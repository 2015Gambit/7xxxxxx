In het belang van Jake biedt Martin een olijftak aan aan zijn schoonzuster Abigail die zij lijkt te accepteren. De steun van Abigail is waarschijnlijk niet voldoende om Jake terug te krijgen wanneer Martin de volgende set van Amalia's getallenreeks van Jake krijgt.

In Episode 10:
For Jake's sake in more ways than one, Martin extends an olive branch to his sister-in-law Abigail, which she seems to accept. Martin does provide his assistance as who the dock workers believe is "Mike" despite knowing that if they are caught and he is sent to jail, or that if the dock workers find out that he is not really who they think he is, that he will lose Jake for good.
