The Forgotten Realms comics, following the adventures of Drizzt Do'Urden, Bruenor Battlehammer, and the other Companions of the Hall.

These are from the first 6 books in the Legacy of Drizzt series, by R. A. Salvatore.
The books are split into three comics per book, including:

*Homeland

*Exile

*Sojourn

*The Crystal Shard

*Streams of Silver

*The Halfling's Gem
