SAS becomes the 222nd airline to join the World Air Routes and 10th Star Alliance airline to be featured in the series!

The 1st hour of the program focuses on the MD80 and takes you in the cockpit for 4 flights from the airline's hub at Stockholm to Copenhagen and to Lulea.

Lulea becomes the 800th destination featured in the World Air Routes!!

For the next 2 and a half hours Captain Lars Naur hosts 2 roundtips in the cockpit of the 737-600 from Arlanda to Paris and Malmo. He and his copilot will talk you through ever detail of their day and their descriptions make this another exceptional must see program!

The DVD concludes with a flight in the cabin from Stockholm to Gothenburg.
