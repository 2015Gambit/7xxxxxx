Ridiculousness is an MTV video clip show which began airing on August 29 2011. The show is rated TV-PG due to language, hosted by Rob Dyrdek and co-hosted by Sterling "Steelo" Brim and Chanel West Coast. Jeff Tremaine is the show's executive producer. Ridiculousness has been compared to shows like Tosh.0 and Web Soup. Season 2 of Ridiculousness premiered on MTV on April 30, 2012.
