Thought-inducing music from a living, breathing universe. Floating, pulsating, droning, morphing soundscapes among background noise emanating from countless stars, swimming in the warm radiant hum of a giant planetary core, dancing to the intermittent beats of a bazillion radio pulsars; all rupturing the stillness of the black void. Peter disassembles our galaxy in search of what lies beyond; space is after all, just an illusion. (http://www.discogs.com/Atomine-Elektrine-Archimetrical-Universe/master/48965)
