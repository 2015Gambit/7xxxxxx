    Game Notes:
    ~~~~~~~~~~~
    World War 3 is a fully 3D RTS with all features that characterize this
    genre. The game has been developed based on a 3D environment, so that
    the player can set the camera parallel to any plane and freely zoom in
    and out. All game objects are 3D, as well as the terrain itself. The
    units also posse unique, dynamic physical properties. For example,
    vehicles move faster on hard soil than they would in sand. There are
    three parties in the game: the USA, Russia and Iraq. Whichever is
    chosen, each allows the player to select two campaigns, consisting of
    5 missions on average. Each campaign begins and ends with a rendered
    film scene.
    World War III: Black Gold takes the player to an unknown future. The
    political crisis goes on for months, and a wave of demonstrations has
    broken out around the world. A secret conference of the Central
    Geological UN Commission reveals that worldwide oil stocks have
    plummeted to dangerously low levels, oil reserves will run out within
    8-10 years. As this proves too short for even the most highly
    industrialized countries to switch their economies over to alternative
    energy sources, protests close the world and succumbs the whole world
    economy. The leading forces have declared WW III...
