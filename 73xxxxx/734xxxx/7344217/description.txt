Entrepreneurs and professionals often get neglected by travel suppliers when it comes to favorable pricing. "Guerilla Travel Tactics" presents independent business travelers with a clear and logical, step-by-step plan for saving time and money when traveling at their own expense. Using an upbeat, can-do approach, "Guerilla Travel Tactics" provides readers with the optimism and the confidence they need to conquer the soaring costs of business travel.
