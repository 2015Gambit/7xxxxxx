Euro 2012 Highlights - 18th June 2012

Matt Smith presents highlights of Republic of Ireland v Italy and Croatia v Spain. Ireland pulled off a memorable victory against Italy at the 1994 World Cup and Giovanni Trapattoni's men will be looking to do likewise today as they take on the 
Azzurri in Poznan. Presented by Matt Smith, with guests Roy Keane, Roberto Martinez and Gareth Southgate.
