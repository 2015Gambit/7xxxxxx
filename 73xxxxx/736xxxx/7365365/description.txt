Green Day :

Is an American punk rock band formed in 1987. The band consists of 
lead vocalist and guitarist Billie Joe Armstrong, bassist and backing vocalist 
Mike Dirnt, and drummer Tre Cool. Cool replaced former drummer John Kiffmeyer 
in 1990, prior to the recording of the band's second studio album, Kerplunk, 
and has been a member of the band since.
