The Encyclopedia of Diseases and Disorders provides authoritative information on a wide variety of diseases and health disorders. Although the focus in lanning this collection of articles was on subjects of interest to young readers, the information provided here is valuable to users of any age. More than two undred articles are categorized in three major areas of interest: infections, noninfectious diseases, and mental disorders.
