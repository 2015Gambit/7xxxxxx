A simple desktop application that makes video upload on Facebook as easy as drinking a water's glass!

It supports any video format available on Facebook: 3G2, 3GP, 3GPP, ASF, AVI, FLV, M4V, MKV, MOV, MP4, MPE, MPEG, MPEG4, MPG, NSV, OGM, QT, VOB, WM

Fast Video Upload uploads your video at light speed!
