This new edition of Further Mathematics now includes extra content covering matrices and updated exam-style questions.

The Enhanced TI-N/CP Versions of Victoria's leading maths series for VCE enable teachers and students to take full advantage of the latest technologies in the maths classroom.

Integrated CAS calculator explanations, examples and problems have been updated to reflect the TI-Nspire OS3 and to include the CX CAS model. The series continues to feature the Casio Classpad. Page numbers in the printed texts reflect the previous TI-Nspire and Casio ClassPad versions allowing for continuity and compatibility.
