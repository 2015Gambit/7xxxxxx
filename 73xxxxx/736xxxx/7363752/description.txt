Bassoa Podcast #5 - Mixed by Eddie Icicle
-----------------------------------------
"Bassoa" is a new podcast from the makers EDM-blog "Random Electric Noises". Like the blog, also the podcast aims to blend and share as many different kinds of music as possible, regardless of it's age or genre. Bassoa podcasts are a bite-sized chunk of the best electronic music around.

Fifth episode features two massive tracks from Major Lazer, brand new Knife Party, a track from Eric Prydz' new album, great remix by Mustard Pimp (which is available as a free download at the time of writing) and much more. Like our Facebook page to stay tuned with upcoming podcasts:
