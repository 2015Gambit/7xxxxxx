Using a combination of bite-sized, easily accessible information, and inspiring photographs of achievable results, the Simple Steps series promotes gardening as a real pleasure rather than a back-breaking chore. No other group in the plant kingdom can match the incredible diversity found within the orchid family. Simple Steps: Orchids features the dazzling range of plant forms, and spectacular colors capture the imagination and inspire orchid enthusiasts as well as new growers.
