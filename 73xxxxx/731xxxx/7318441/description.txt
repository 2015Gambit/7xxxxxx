STINGERS is a supernatural action 5 issue mini-series that combines the tone of 28 Days Later with the absolute intensity of Aliens. 

They needed a new planet in order to survive... They just found us. Brian "Hawk" Hawkins is an Atlantic City bounty hunter and he's fed up with the world and everyone in it. But as he tracks his latest bail jumpers to a small New Jersey town he finds much more than what he bargained for. Now Hawkins must make the decision to flee town or to stay put and fight to save the one thing he can no longer stand: Humanity.
