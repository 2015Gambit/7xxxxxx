            BOLLAND & BOLLAND  -  In The Army Now  ( 1981) 
            STATUS QUO  - In the Army Now  (1986) 




       "In the Army Now" is a song by Rob and Ferdi Bolland, recorded in 1981. The song was also later recorded by the British rock band Status Quo on their 1986 album, "In the Army Now" , and their version peaked at number 2 in the UK Singles Chart.Lead singer of English rock band Slade, Noddy Holder made a vocal appearance on the Status Quo version, performing the line "Stand up and fight."
