Star Trek: The Next Generation/Doctor Who: Assimilation2 is an upcoming eight-issue
 
limited series comic book written by Scott and David Tipton, with art by J.K.

 Woodward. The series will be published by IDW Publishing beginning May 2012 and is a

 cross-over featuring the science-fiction series Doctor Who and Star Trek: The Next 
Generation.




Assimilation2 sees the Eleventh Doctor and his companions Amy Pond and Rory Williams

 encounter the crew of the USS Enterprise, joining together to stop an alliance 

between the Borg and the Cybermen.
