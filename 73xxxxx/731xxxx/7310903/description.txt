Concert held on November 7, 2010 - The first of Up and Coming Tour in South America. Uncle Macca was in full throttle! 
This is currently the best record of this show ever released on DVD (unless the MPL launch a DVD containing the official record of the concert - which unfortunately I do not think it will happen). Despite being a home production, the videos were selected from hundreds sent in by fans on YouTube. The work of video editing was the best possible within the constraints of audience videos. This is a historical record of the day when a Beatle performed in the capital of the gauchos. Mas bah, tchê!
