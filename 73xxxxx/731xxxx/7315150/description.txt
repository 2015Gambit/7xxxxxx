Vlad(Rocco Siffredi) returns from killing a rogue Vampire who left the clan. Blood Slaves wait for him. They are vessels whose only station in life is to produce blood for these Vampires, while they remain trapped between human and Vampire. They submit to the ruthless power of Vlad and Dracu to prove themselves worthy to become Vampires and spend eternal life with the perverse men of the klan. Manu contacts Father Zoltan, the priest from Szuglo, Hungry, who seems to know more about Vampires than he reveals. His search for Amira continues.
