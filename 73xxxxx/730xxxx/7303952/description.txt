This is the story of how Lord Sri Ramachandra overpowered the mighty bow of Lord Shiva. This story is called "Overpowering Lord Shiva's Bow". It is based on the original Valmiki Ramayana. The Telugu version is by Smt.C.Kusuma Kumari. This English translation is by Smt.B.Neelakar and B.P.Shasank Kalyan. This English e-book is in PDF format. Happy reading!
