-----------------------------------------------------------------------
SMORKIE NORFUL DISCOGRAPHY
-----------------------------------------------------------------------

There are 7 releases in this Smokie Norful discography...

 * 2002 - I Need You Now
 * 2004 - Nothing Without You
 * 2007 - Life Changing
 * 2009 - Live
 * 2010 - Victory Cathedral Choir
 * 2011 - How I Got Over
 * 2012 - Once In A Lifetime

Rev Smokie Norful is an American Gospel Singer and pianist, best known for his 2002 album, I Need You Now and his 2004 release, Nothing Without You, which won a Grammy at the 47th Annual Grammy Awards for Best Contemporary Soul Gospel Album in 2004.
