Spirit Walkers: Curse of the Cypress Witch


Genre: Hidden Object | Puzzle


Lift a curse and save the Cypress Witch in Spirit Walkers: Curse of the Cypress Witch! Maylynn and a group of friends go on what was supposed to be an innocent hiking trip when one of her friends gets seriously hurt. Looking for help Maylynn ends up at a beautiful and majestic cypress tree. She meets a witch that offers to help in exchange for a favor first. Now you'll have to travel to a different time 
and dimension to undo a thing that once went wrong!


* Experience the etheric dimension
* Shift between realms to complete quests
* Lift the curse and save the Witch!


System Requirements:


OS: Windows XP | Windows Vista | Windows 7
CPU: 1.6 GHz
RAM: 1024 MB
DirectX: 9.0
Hard Drive: 271 MB
