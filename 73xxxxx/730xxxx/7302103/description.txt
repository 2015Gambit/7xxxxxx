MMXII (2012 in Roman numerals) is the fifteenth studio album by Killing Joke with Jaz Coleman on vocals, Kevin 'Geordie' Walker on guitar, Martin 'Youth' Glover on bass, and Paul Ferguson on drums. The album was released on 2 April 2012 on Spinefarm Records, and is distributed worldwide via Universal Music Group. A United States release date is pending.
The album, which features ten tracks, was engineered and mixed by Clive Goddard, Tom Dalgety, Michael Rendall, and Reza Udhin, and produced by the band. It was released as a CD, a double coloured vinyl, and as a downloadable album via iTunes with 'New Uprising' as an exclusive track. (wikipedia)
