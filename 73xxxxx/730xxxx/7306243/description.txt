Arms & Armor of Medieval Knight : An Illustrated History of Weaponry in the Middle Ages

Crescent Books | 1991 | ISBN: 0517644681 | English | 190 pages | PDF | 80.2 MB

An illustrated history of the arms and armour of the medieval period and it's use. A comprehensive glossary of terms is provided, and an appendix gives details of the construciton of armor. With 170 b/w and 150 color illustrations.
