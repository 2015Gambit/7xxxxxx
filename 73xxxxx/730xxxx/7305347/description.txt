"Blackwater" is the ninth and penultimate episode of the second season of HBO's medieval fantasy television series Game of Thrones. The episode is written by George R. R. Martin, the author of the A Song of Ice and Fire novels of which the series is an adaptation, and directed for the first time by Neil Marshall. It is to air on 27 May 2012. The episode is to focus on the Battle of Blackwater Bay, Stannis Baratheon's naval assault on the realm's capital of King's Landing.
