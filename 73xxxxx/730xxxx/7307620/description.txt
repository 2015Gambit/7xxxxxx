PLOT
==========================================
Stefan and Marie are a young happy couple that only lives for each other. But the happiness they share is threatened by an old friend to Stefan, Chris, that one-day comes to visit them. Chris has served as a mercenary in Bosnia, several years but has now decided to move back home to Sweden. With Stefan's help, he wants to build a new criminal business.
