This is the complet iGOMyWay 8.4.2 for Android. It needs no hacked OS and works no the original OSes of your phones.

The package contains the main program and the various other installation apk, depending your screen resolution. 

What you to do is run the main program and after its installed, choose which map you want to download (internet access is needed). After that you can reinstall the "apk" that fits your screen resolution.

I hope you like it, i did!
