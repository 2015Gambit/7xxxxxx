Balloons, Aluminum Car Wheels, Envelopes and Whistles

Technology Documentary hosted by Zac Fine, published by Discovery Channel broadcasted as part of DC Factory Made series in 2009 - English narration


Information
------------------------------

Find out why balloons stretch when they inflate instead of pop, how can an aluminum wheel be strong enough to support a car, see how a paper airplane turns into a paper envelope, and how do whistles work?
