In this heart-pounding thriller from M.Knight Shyamalan,the writer-director of The Sixth Sense and Signs,an unstopable event threatens the world's population by attacking the most basic human instinct of all - survival.Academy Award Nominee Mark Wahlberg (2006 Best supporting Actor - The Departed) stars as Elliot Moore,an ordinary man trying to save his family from a terrifying invisble killer.As Elliot discovers the incredble possibilty of what is happening,it is clear that no one - and nowhere -is safe.
