Install:
=============================================================
Mount the ISO.
The setup is launched automatically.
Follow the instructions through to the end choosing your configuration.
If DirectX is not installed on your computer, or if the DirectX
version present is older than 8.1, you will be prompted to install this version, present on the CD.
DirectX version 8.1 is required for BATMAN to work.
