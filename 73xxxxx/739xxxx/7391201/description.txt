
AFTER ARVIND DESAI KI AJEEB DASTAN

Dilip Dhawan and Satish Shah in


Johny Fernandes left his house in Goa when he was only fifteen and rerurned ten years later to find everything changed. He had feni in a bar and following a brawl found himself in a prison. Inspector DSa gives him the keys of his house and he goes home. Soon he attracts his old friends Dolan, Joe, Kaju Khaitan and Tino. None of them works but everyone dreams of the morrow. Johny meets a destitute girl Mary whose baby needs medicines. He supplies them but the baby dies. He invites imprisonment in desperation and on release learns that Mary had ended her life. Johny dies and the group disintegrates.
