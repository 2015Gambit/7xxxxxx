Fillmore West 1969: The Complete Recordings
Initial release: Nov 2005

A 10 CD box set of "every note" played at the four Fillmore West shows from February 27th to March 2nd, 1969. 
Promoted as "the most significant audio release yet to emerge from the Grateful Dead's vault." 
Mixed from the 16 track master tapes. Mastered in HDCD. 51 tracks, nearly 10 hours of music.

Recorded live at The Fillmore West, San Francisco, February 27 - March 2, 1969

Includes 11th Bonus Disc
