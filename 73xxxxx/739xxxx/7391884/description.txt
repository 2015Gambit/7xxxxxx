3D Artist Magazine Issue 43, 2012

3D Artist Magazine offers practical inspiration for the 3D community. The 
software it covers includes 3DS Max, Modo, Zbrush and cinema 4D. The Magazine 
offers in depth interviews with leading artists in the field as well as workshop 
ideas and tips. Readers will find useful step by step guides and inspiration on 
designing and modelling animation and games. There is usually a free CD given 
away with each issue which offers tuition on new software to keep each reader 
up to date with new developments in the industry.
