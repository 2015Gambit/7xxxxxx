This "best of" anthology from the Cold Blooded Chillers series (issues 1 thru 3) features 8 great scares and 2 new extras. Winner of a Bronze medal at the 2009 Independent Publishers Book Awards. Terry Hooper at ComicBitsOnline raves: "Seriously, buy this book because it will be a collectors' item! Tell your friends you were into Cold Blooded Chillers before it became BIG." Cover art by Preston Asevedo, 144 pages, b&w
