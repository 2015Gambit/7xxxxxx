The Real World:  St. Thomas


The Real World: St. Thomas is the twenty-seventh season of MTV's reality television series The Real World, which focuses on a group of diverse strangers living together for several months in a different city each season, as cameras document their lives and interpersonal relationships. St. Thomas and Hassel Island in the U.S. Virgin Islands were first reported as the location for the twenty-seventh season in a February 2012 article on the website St. Thomas Blog.[1] Production started in March 2012, and the season premiered on June 27, 2012
