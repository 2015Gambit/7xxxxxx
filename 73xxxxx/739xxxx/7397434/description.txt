The Ed Show; June 28, 2012.

Throughout the show Ed discussed SCOTUS led by Justice Roberts ruling the Healthcare law (PPACA).

Rep. George Miller (D) California and Fmr. Gov Howard Dean (D) Vermont discussed what this decision lays out for the bills step by step implementation. (The idea of a public option was not heard).

Ed discussed the ruling with Rep. Nancy Pelosi (D) California (Minority leader).

Ed discussed the contempt vote with Rep. Keith Ellison (D) Minnesota (co-chair Progressive Caucus). Ed then cut to his exclusive interview with Pelosi, where she comments on the contempt vote. The discussion then resumed with Keith Ellison.

Karen Finney (MSNBC Political Analyst) joined Ed to discuss the reaction from the GOP. Mike Pence had compared it to 9/11 (before apologizing later).

Ed ends the show with his take on the Healthcare ruling.
