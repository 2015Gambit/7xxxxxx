Billy J. Kramer (born William Howard Ashton, 19 August 1943, Bootle, Liverpool,
England)is a British Invasion/Merseybeat singer. In the 1960s he was managed by
Brian Epstein, whoalso managed The Beatles, and he recorded several original
Lennon and McCartney compositions. 
http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Billy_J_Kramer

The Dakotas is a group of British musicians, which initially convened as a
backing band in Manchester, England. However, they are most closely associated
with the singer Billy J. Kramer, In the USA they are regarded as part of the
British invasion. 
http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Dakotas_(band)
