Olympic Massacre: One Day in September

Sociopolitical Documentary hosted by Michael Douglas, published by BBC broadcasted as part of BBC Storyville series in 2012 - English narration


Information
------------------------------

Kevin MacDonald's Oscar-winning film recounts in gripping detail the horrifying kidnapping of 12 Israeli athletes during the 1972 Munich Olympics by the Palestinian terrorist organisation Black September.

Produced in 1999 by Passion Pictures and Arthur Cohn
