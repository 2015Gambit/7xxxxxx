Ghost Adventures is an American paranormal television series that first premiered on October 17, 2008, on the Travel Channel. Produced by MY-Tupelo Entertainment (a merger of MY Entertainment and Tupelo-Honey Productions),[1] the program follows and stars ghost hunters Zak Bagans, Nick Groff and Aaron Goodwin as they investigate locations that are purported to be haunted. The show is hosted and narrated by Bagans.
