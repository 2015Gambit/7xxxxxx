Fortress (1992) Eng)(NL sub)

Nederlands:
In het jaar 2013 is er een wet uitgevaardigd, waarbij elke vrouw maar
1 Kind mag krijgen. John (Lambert) en Karen (Locklin) Brennick proberen
naar Mexico te vluchten om deze wet te omzeilen, maar worden gepakt. Ze
worden naar een zeer geavanceerde gevangenis gebracht, waarbij het nagenoeg
onmogelijk is om te ontsnappen. John is vastberaden om toch te ontsnappen,
maar moet ook nog zijn vrouw zien mee te nemen.

English:
In a futuristic USA, it's forbidden to give birth to more than one child
For each woman. As usual, you can escape to Mexico to avoid the authorities
In USA, which is exactly what John and Karen Brennick were trying to do
When Karen is pregnant with her second child (their first child was born
dead). When they think they have made it they are discovered and put to
prison (for 31 years), a modern prison called the "Fortress" where the
prisoners are controlled by lasers, neutron-cannons, cameras, mind-scanners
and electronic pain-causing devices in their stomachs. With those odds,
John still plans to escape with his wife.


Met o.a.
Christopher Lambert, Loryn Locklin en Kurtwood Smith
