------GAME INFO------

WAKFU is a global cross-media brand by Ankama Games revolving around an online tactical turn-based MMORPG. In addition to the online game, elements consist of an Xbox Live Arcade Game (Islands of WAKFU), an animated television series (WAKFU), comics/manga, a trading card game, action figures, and more.

WAKFU is set within the same fantasy universe as Dofus, but takes place approximately 1,000 years later, after a catastrophic world event known as the Great Deluge.

The MMORPG was released on February 29, 2012. It was published by Square Enix in North America while Ankama Games retains publishing rights for the rest of the world. Islands of Wakfu, a spin-off of the online game, was released on March 30, 2011, for the Xbox Live Arcade.
