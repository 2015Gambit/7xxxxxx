A new DVD narrating all secrets and techniques to paint winter vehicles. Detailed explanations for all process, like the base color, fadings, mud, dust, chipping and more. Many new techniques explained like the famous "hairspray" technique, streaking effects or mud . 3 different camo schemes: very faded winter camo over green, new winter camo over dark yellow and white snakes over dark yellow. Narrated in English with Spanish subtitles.
