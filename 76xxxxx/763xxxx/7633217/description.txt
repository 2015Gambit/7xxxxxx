Dutch

Hij heeft echter een diep geworteld geheim waardoor zijn eigen leven en familie in gevaar komen.

Englisch
A rookie cop is assigned to the 118 Precinct in the same district where he grew up.
The Precinct Captain starts receiving letters about two unsolved murders
that happened many years ago in the housing projects when the rookie cop was just a kid.
These letters bring back bad memories and old secrets that begin to threaten his career and break up his family.
