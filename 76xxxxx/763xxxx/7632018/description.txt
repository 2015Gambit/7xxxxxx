Camera ICS+ is an improved version of the official Camera app included on Ice Cream Sandwich (Android 4.0).
Our goal is to allow any Android phone or tablet can enjoy this great camera!

Features:

    No ads version
    3 modes: camera, video recorder & panorama
    Dynamic user interface (phone/tablet)
    Color effects (mono,sepia,negative,...)
    Silent mode (no shutter sound, might not work on some devices)
    Configurable volume keys
    Wide screen pictures
    Picture quality setting (low/medium/high/ultra)
    Video bitrate selection (normal/2x)
    Configurable storage source (Internal/SD card)
    Time-lapse video recording (only 3.0+)
    Video stabilization on/off (only 4.0+)
    720p face effects (only 4.0+)

What's in this version : (Updated : Aug 2, 2012)

    Added 720p video recording on Nexus 7
    Configurable camcorder audio settings
    Compatible with Nexus 7
    New fx video effects: pencil, lomo & documentary (only on Nexus with Android 4.0+)
    Fixed minor bugs

Required Android O/S : 4.0+
