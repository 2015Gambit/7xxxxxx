Look Back - Don't Stare

Arts Documentary hosted by Take That, published by ITV in 2010 - English narration


Information
------------------------------

To mark one of the most eagerly anticipated reunions in the history of British music, this programme follows the making of Take That's latest album. The band's first period of success in the 1990s was phenomenal but unsustainable. Now, 20 years after it all began, Gary Barlow, Howard Donald, Jason Orange, Mark Owen and Robbie Williams turn to face that past and share their truth collectively. With unrestricted and privileged access to the recording studio throughout the last 12 months, the programme is a raw, intimate meditation on the process of friendship and forgiveness.

The programme is filmed in black and white in cinema style 2.35 aspect ratio format.
