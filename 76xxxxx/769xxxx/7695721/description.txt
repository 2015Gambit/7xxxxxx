Lifetime-A Mother's Nightmare [2012]

Air Date: 29 Sept 2012

Summary: Vanessa moves fast to claim Chris for her own, monopolizing his time and distancing him from his friends and family. His mother, Maddie (Annabeth Gish, "Pretty Little Liars"), feels unsettled by their sudden relationship and when she discovers a dark secret of Vanessa's she uses all her resources to convince Chris to end it. (The more Maddie tries to protect Chris, the more Vanessa's hold on him is threatened) Angered and vengeful, Vanessa accuses Chris of a heinous act that turns the school against him and sparks an online attack. As things get violent, Vanessa plans her next move and Maddie must find out Vanessa's game before she loses her son forever.
