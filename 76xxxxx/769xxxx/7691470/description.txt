Genre: Romance

General Release Date: 06 Sep 2012

Running Time: 1 Hour 27 Minutes

Distributor: Metrowealth

Cast: Kamal Adli, Diana Amir

Director: Azhari Mohd Zain

Classification: P13

"Salam Cinta" tells the story of Amirul, an actor who receives the offer to play an Islamic man. He goes to a village to see for himself how an imam lives so that he can better embody his role. In the village, he meets Laila and begins to fall for her after seeing her graceful, soft spoken and kind manner with her family. A playboy at heart, Amirul's attitude changes little by little in order to win Laila's heart.

Salam Cinta mengisahkan tentang Amirul, pelakon yang menerima tawaran memegang watak lelaki yang kuat pegangan agama. Oleh kerana dibesarkan dalam suasana moden, dia ke sebuah kampung untuk bertemu seorang imam untuk mengetahui cara hidupnya.

Di kampung itu Amirul bertemu Laila dan jatuh cinta dengannya selepas melihat kelembutan, sikap rendah diri dan berbudi pekerti kepada keluarganya. Walaupun 'playboy', Amirul mula berubah sikap untuk memenangi hati Laila.
