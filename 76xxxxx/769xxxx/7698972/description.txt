The Beatles' Nowhere Man EP was released 8 July 1966. It included four songs from the (British) album Rubber Soul. The EP was only released in mono, with the catalogue number Parlophone GEP 8952.

(In the United States, "Nowhere Man" and "Drive My Car" were on "Yesterday and Today", not on "Rubber Soul".)
