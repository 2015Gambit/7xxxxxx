A young, idealistic poet, turns his back on civilization and goes to small,
backwood village, rents a bed in the house of an old woman, and decides
to make his living as a lumberjack. Soon he realizes that the world
around him is far from perfect. He does not make any real contact with
the people in the village. An then another outcast like him
rents a bed in the same house.
