One-man project 0TRUJA started in the end of 2011, and it's music is all about resampling and glitching, about soft landscapes and sharp objects.

First album "Allghoi khorkhoi" was about feelings of person, that faced some supernatural, weird things, about fear, disturbence etc. More power, more aggro motives, more harsh sounds, more noise in this thing.

Second album "I've Lost My Invisible Friend" about unexplainable relationship of a teenager and his invisible friend in some not so ordinary world of miracles and dreams. It's much more softer, that "Allghoi khorkhoi", and there is more ambient stuff instead of noise and glitch.
