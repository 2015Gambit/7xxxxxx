In Dream Tale: The Golden Keys you must help Julia find her lost dog, Fifo. Join her as she searches through 4 magical worlds in this classic platform game. Use amazing powers given by stars to avoid different poisonous creatures located in strategic and challenging places. Find the 19 golden keys and unlock the final mystery on this hard and memorable journey. Will you join Julia on her quest?
