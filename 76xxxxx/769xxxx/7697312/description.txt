The Gallery Girls are back in New York minus Chantal, who has skipped off to Paris with her boyfriend. This only adds to the tension, since Claudia is left to hold down the fort at EOC while unpaid bills continue to pile up. Maggie is over-confident that she has a new job, while Amy searches frantically before her two-week deadline runs out. In the meantime, Kerri struggles with deciding between her job and internship.
