Australian Geographic - 101 Australian Birds 
Australia is very much a land of birds - some 750 species all told. Each of Australia's varied landscapes has its own particular mix of species - deserts, woodlands, rainforests, rivers and seashores. Covering everything from enormous Emus to tiny Emuwrens, laughingto carolling Magpies, exotic Parrots to birds of prey, the collection bursts with colour, movement and beautiful scenery.

=== Extra: Australia's Most Beautiful Birds ===


Australia has over 750 species of birds. Here we look at some of the most fascinating and beautiful. We travel across the country to discover a vast array of birdlife, from the small Parrot to the large Emu and Cassowary. See the spectacular colours of the Rainbow Lorikeet. Experience the majestic Wedge Tailed Eagle, go deep into the bush for the laughingand fascinating Lyre Bird.
