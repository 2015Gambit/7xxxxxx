Land of Crossing, Maghreb

History Documentary with no narration published by NHK broadcasted as part of NHK Treasures from the Past series in 2008 - English language


Information
------------------------------

Explore the shifting sands of North Africa with Treasures from the Past. We're heading to the Maghreb region to see rock paintings in Algeria and the markets and mosques of old town Tunis.

List of World Heritage sites in this episode:
1. Medina of Tunis (Tunisia)
2. Site of Carthage (Tunisia)
3. Amphitheatre of El Jem (Tunisia)
4. Dougga / Thugga (Tunisia)
5. Kairouan (Tunisia)
6. Tassili n'Ajjer (Algeria)
7. Ksar of Ait-Ben-Haddou (Morocco)
8. Medina of Marrakesh (Morocco)
