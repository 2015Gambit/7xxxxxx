Marine SharpShooter 4: Locked and Loaded brings players back into the hotzone as the legendary team of sniper and spotter in this high octane thrill ride. The nuclear threat has evolved and the world is once again in peril. Your mission will span the globe as you hunt down a covert Private Military Corporation and bring them to justice. Use all the weapons equipment and tactics at your disposal to accomplish the mission at any cost.



Minimum System Requirements:

Operating System: Windows 2000/XP/Vista
Processor: Pentium 4 or AMD Athlon 2.0 GHz processor or faster.
Memory: 256 Mb Ram
Hard Disk Space: 1.5 GB Free 
Video: DirectX 9.0 compatible video card (ATI Radeon 9600 or /NVIDIA. GeForce 4 Ti 4600 with 128 MB RAM) or higher.
Sound: DirectX and Windows - compatible sound card. /DirectX:  DirectX version 10 or higher 
Cd Rom: 4x CD-ROM or faster.
Input: Microsoft-compatible mouse and keyboard.
