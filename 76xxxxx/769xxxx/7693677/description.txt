The Terminal Experiment is a science fiction novel by Canadian novelist Robert J Sawyer. The book won the 1995 Nebula Award for Best Novel and was nominated for the Hugo Award for Best Novel in 1996.

An experiment has gone terribly wrong. Dr. Peter Hobson has created three electronic simulations of his own personality. One will test life after death; another, immortality. The third one is the control unit. But now all three have escaped from Hobson's computer into the worldwide electronic matrix. And one of them is a killer.Robert Sawyer has won many awards for his science fiction, which is praised for its blend of high-tech mystery and suspenseful pacing.
