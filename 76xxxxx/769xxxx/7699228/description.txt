Are you searching for a replacement for Notepad? GetDiz is an ultrafast text editor with unique features.

Additional features include an option to auto-copy selections, convert Unix files to the Windows text format, and highlight web links in text files, so that they can be sent to the browser with a simple click.

You can also install GetDiz on a USB stick for mobile usage.

ASCII art is a graphic design technique that uses pieces of printable text defined by the ASCII standard character set alongside with proprietary extended characters. 
The term is also used to refer to text-based art in general. 
ASCII art is often found in DIZ and NFO text files, which in turn are included within ZIP and RAR archives. 

Among with correct ASCII art display, GetDiz offers you the possibility to save any .txt, .diz or .nfo files as GIF images.
