New to This Town is the second studio album by American country music artist 
Kix Brooks. It was released on September 11, 2012 via Arista Nashville. It is 
Brooks' first album after his split as one half of Brooks & Dunn. Brooks 
produced the album and co-wrote nine of its twelve tracks. Its first single, 
the title track, features Joe Walsh on guitar and was co-produced by Jay 
DeMarcus, one third of Rascal Flatts.
