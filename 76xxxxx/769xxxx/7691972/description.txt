Simple DNS Plus is the one and only GUI-based DNS server for Windows client systems. Great for speeding up your Internet surfing by using your own computer as your DNS server instead of relying on your ISP:s external DNS servers. Of course, it's also great for use with your web server or mail server. With Simple DNS Plus you don't need to park your domain names on external hosts or services. Simply condigure Simple DNS Plus to be your own domsin parking spot. A very versatile and handy software for those who know how to use it.
