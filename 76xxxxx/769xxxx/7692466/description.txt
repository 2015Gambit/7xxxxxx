The Hounds were a Swedish pop group formed in 1965 in Stockholm. The group
consisted of Jan Ahlen, Henrik Salander, Lars Wallander, Jan Bråthe and Janne
Önnerud.
The Hounds got their first hit, Exodus, 1966. 1967 they released their cover
versions of Sealed with a Kiss and The Lion Sleeps Tonight, which became long-
running on the Swedish Top Ten chart. The group had one last big hit in spring
1968 with a cover song, The Gypsy Cried, before later that year they dissolved.
Their characteristic sound merged from their ability to sing in harmony. The
group has reunited briefly several times.
