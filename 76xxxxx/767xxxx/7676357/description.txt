Season 1 - Episode 23
The House of the Seven Gargoyles
Air Date: Feb. 18, 1965

Dr. Ericson has constructed a device that can reverse gravity. The key component is known as the "Ericon Bar." Ericson lives in a remote part of Norway in a castle with stone gargoyles. On the eve of a visit by Dr. Benton Quest, one of the gargoyles seems to come to life.
