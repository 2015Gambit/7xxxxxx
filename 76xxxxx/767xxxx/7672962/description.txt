Instant Notes in Molecular Biology 
P. Turner

Taylor & Francis, Dec 22, 2005 - 360 pages PDF


BIOS Instant Notes titles focus on core information and are designed to help undergraduate students come to grips with a subject quickly and easily.

The new edition of Instant Notes in Molecular Biology is an updated and revised version of a successful and well-received text. Updates include information on micro RNAs, RNAi, genome sequences, and RNA splicing. Two important sections will be added, covering the exciting new fields of functional genomics and bioinformatics in the same accessible style.
