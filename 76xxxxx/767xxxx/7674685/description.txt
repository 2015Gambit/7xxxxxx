Super Snake

Nature Documentary hosted by Tom Cotcher, published by National Geographic broadcasted as part of NG Dangerous Encounters series in 2009 - English narration


Information
------------------------------

Reptile expert Dr. Brady Barr has spent his entire career studying some of the world's most amazing and deadliest animals. In the hit series, Dangerous Encounters, Barr's goal is to study these dangerous creatures in their natural environment before their habitat is destroyed so that he can better understand what they need to survive in the wild. Best of Dangerous Encounters features Brady's most dangerous encounters with some of the planet's most unapproachable animals In Super Snake Brady's research pays off in a face-to-face encounter. As he wrestles anacondas, seeks out deadly cobras and he's after the biggest snakes the world has to offer. He travel's to remote regions to track down creatures that have long eluded others. He's never met a snake he didn't like, even one four metres long isn't enough to scare Brady away from tracking the Super snake.
