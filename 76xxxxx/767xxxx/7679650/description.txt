Sorg (aka. Grief)  

Produced in 2007  

Robert Ronning's first short feature. Shown in cinemas around Norway. A young mother struggle with the loss of her newborn child and an excruciating grief.  

Cast: 
Heidi L. Kristoffersen 
Carina Hornstuen 
Alfred Schulstad

Written and directed by Robert Ronning
