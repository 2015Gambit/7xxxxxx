Season 1 - Episode 10
Shadow of the Condor
Air Date: Nov. 20, 1964

A mechanical failure on the Quest's jet forces them to land on an hidden strip in the heart of the Andes. They are received by a strange German Baron who hunts condors for a hobby, and his Bolivian servant. Little they know their host used to be a war pilot in World War I and he's is looking for one last dogfight...
