
Simpsons Comics Issue 194 - September 19, 2012

Title - Canine Decoder

24 Pages | HQ PDF | English | 18 MB 



Homer embarks on a new career as a "dog listener", where he solves dogs' problems. When word gets out about his skill, his services become a big hit among Springfield's dogs and dog owners alike, but he quickly develops burnout and quits. 

Meanwhile, Lisa sets out to prove that gym class is unfair, but ends up using her intellect to analyze gym and become a success at it. 

When Homer's quitting as dog listener triggers a doggy revolt in Springfield, Lisa's new gym skills save everyone at school from being attacked by the angry dogs. 

The canine rebellion is quelled when Santa's Little Helper comes to Homer's rescue and persuades the other dogs to call it off.
