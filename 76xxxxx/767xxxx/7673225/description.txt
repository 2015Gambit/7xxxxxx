Synopsis
Naruto is captured and interrogated over the
assassination attempt of Kumogakure leader
Raikage and the killings of Jonin and
Iwagakura.
Naruto is imprisoned at Hozukijo prison
known as the "Blood Prison." Mui, the master
of Hozukijo prison,
uses special techniques to steal powers from
the prisoners. Naruto's life is now targetted.
To prove Naruto's innocence and uncover the
truth behind the killings ... a battle begins.
