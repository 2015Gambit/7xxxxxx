Learn Korean is an easy to use mobile Korean phrasebook that will give visitors to Korea and those who are interested in learning Korean a good start in the language. Learn Korean is recorded using a native speaker and we have tried our best to be authentic in the pronunciation whilst ensuring it is easy to understand. Recommended app for tourists and business people visiting Korea.
Features
- Over 800 common words and phrases
- High quality audio recorded by native speaker
- No internet connection needed
- English, Korean, and Korean romanization
- Store frequently used phrases in Favourites
- Search by keywords
- Include useful tips for travellers
- Ad-free!
