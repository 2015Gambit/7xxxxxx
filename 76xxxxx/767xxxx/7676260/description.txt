Season 1 - Episode 4
Pursuit of the Po-Ho
Air Date: Oct. 9, 1964

A friend of Dr. Quest is captured by a deadly group of natives known as the Po-Ho, so the Quest group must travel to the jungle to save him. When Dr. Quest himself is then captures, and the two scientists have a date with the Po-Ho fire god, some tricky maneuvers from Race are in order? Can he save the scientists, or is this the last episode for the Dr.?
