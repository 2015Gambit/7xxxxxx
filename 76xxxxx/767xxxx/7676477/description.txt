MARCO MINNEMANN

2001 release. Marco's second video filmed live in L.A. at the Musician's Institute in 2001, which displays the four limb independence and incredible drum technique that have made him a sensation throughout Europe and the US. Performing his own material, as well as a tribute to Buddy Rich, and music by Ralph Towner and Ryuichi Sakamoto. Running time: 1 hour 22 minutes.
