Vikings (BBC)

History Documentary hosted by Neil Oliver, published by BBC in 2012 - English narration


Information
------------------------------

Neil Oliver reveals the truth behind the legend of the Vikings.

Part 1.
Neil begins by discovering the mysterious world of the Vikings' prehistoric ancestors. The remains of weapon-filled war boats, long-haired Bronze Age farmers, and the Swedish site of a royal palace and gruesome pagan ritual conjure up an ancient past from which the Viking Age was to suddenly erupt.
