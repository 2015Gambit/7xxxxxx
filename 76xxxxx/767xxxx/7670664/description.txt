Our mission at Johnston Karate is to provide well rounded martial arts and fitness instruction that will help students meet their personal goals, while learning practical self defense skills. We do our best to keep up with the latest in martial arts research, training methods, and news. Our core program is our karate classes, which primarily focus on traditional training, as well as realistic self defense, confidence building, general fitness, and personal development.
