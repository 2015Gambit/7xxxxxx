Goatfell Race

Travel Documentary hosted by Dougie Vipond and Deziree Wilson and Cameron McNeish, published by BBC broadcasted as part of BBC The Adventure Show series in 2012 - English narration


Information
------------------------------

The Adventure Show travels to Arran for the Goatfell Race. This is a closely fought hill run, 15 kilometres up and down the island's highest mountain. It's a challenging course leading from road to rough hill track and finally a scramble up huge granite boulders to reach the dramatic summit. Then there's a tricky descent and the final sprint to Brodick.

Also, find out how light you can go on a backpacking trip and discover what's motivated one of Scotland's best climbers to complete an epic new route in the Himalayas.
