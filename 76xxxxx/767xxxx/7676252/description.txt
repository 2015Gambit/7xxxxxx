Season 1 - Episode 3
The Curse of Anubis
Air Date: Oct. 2, 1964

Dr. Kareem intends to unify Arab nations by stealing an artifact and making it appear as if Westerners had done so. In the course of the theft, he inadvertently awakens a mummy. Kareem wants to frame Dr. Benton Quest as the thief. Jonny and Hadji try to rescue Dr. Quest and Race Bannon. Meanwhile, unknown to all concerned, the mummy is getting closer.
