Mark Greaney has a degree in International Relations and Political Science and is pursuing his Masters in Intelligence Studies with a concentration in Criminal Intelligence. The Gray Man became a national bestseller, and was nominated for a Barry Award in the Best Thriller category. A feature film adaptation of The Gray Man is in development by New Regency Pictures. Mark has also collaborated with New York Times best selling author Tom Clancy on his Jack Ryan Jr. series with Locked On and the upcoming 4th addition Threat Vector due for release this winter.
