Episode 16: What Happens in St. Barths Doesn't Stay in St. Barths
The 'Wives wonder what LuAnn will tell Jacques, while Aviva and Sonja try to make-up

Back home after the crazy trip, Ramona invites Sonja and LuAnn to their mutual friend's home for a dinner party. LuAnn brings Jacques, causing Ramona and Sonja to speculate over whether or not she's told him the truth about what happened with Tomas in St. Barths. Meanwhile, Carole is in for a surprise when Sonja takes her to her shirt tailor, only to find he is one of Carole's former suitors. In an attempt to put St. Barth's behind them, Sonja and Aviva for dinner, but all does not go as planned.
