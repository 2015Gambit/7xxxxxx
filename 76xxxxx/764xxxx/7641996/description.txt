Shakespeare Unlocked Julius Caesar

Arts Documentary hosted by Paterson Joseph and Ray Fearon, published by BBC in 2012 - English narration


Information
------------------------------

Shakespeare's most popular and widely studied plays are unlocked by actors and directors at the Royal Shakespeare Company.

Three key scenes from Julius Caesar are explored through performance, to unlock the meaning of the language and development of character. Act 1, Scene 2 - Cassius and Brutus discuss Caesar's growing power. Act 3, Scene 1 - Caesar's assassination. Act 3, Scene 2 - The Orations.
