Free film adaptation of the popular Hasbro board game known as "Battleship" or "Battleship". In this case the battle will be between the U.S. fleet and an alien fleet. Epic adventure that takes place by sea, sky and land, in which our planet fights for survival against a superior force. Lieutenant Hopper (Taylor Kitsch) is a naval officer John Paul Jones intended. Sam Shane (Brooklyn Decker) is a physical therapist in the Navy and Hopper's girlfriend. Stone (Skarsgard) is Hopper's older brother and commander of Samson. Petty Officer Raikes (Rihanna) is the weapons specialist John Paul Jones. Hopper and Stone are under the command of Admiral Shane (Liam Neeson), who is the father of Sam.
