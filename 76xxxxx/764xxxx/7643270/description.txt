Abraham-Hicks - The Secret Lives of Pets


In this fascinating program compiled from workshops held during the first half of 2005, 
Abraham offers insights into the multifaceted role that "the magnificent beasts" play in 
our lives and our consciousness.

Subjects included:

    * Feeling Second-class To Dogs
    * Your Cat's Health and You
    * Humans and Beasts
    * Animal Consciousness
    * Tuning In To Dog Talk
    * Why Cats Quarrel
    * When Good Pets Go Bad
    * Beasts As Spiritual Teachers
    * Pets and Reincarnation
