Mellisa Harris-Perry; Sunday, September 16, 2012.

Peter Goodman (Executive editor at Huffington Post, Author of "Past Due"), Allison Kilkenney (Co-Host of "Citizen Radio", reporter for The Nation), Wade Henderson (Civil rights advocate, The Leadership Conference), Jim Frederick (International editor of Time magazine)
