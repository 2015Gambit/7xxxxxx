Abachoorina Post Office is a collection of Kannada stories by Poornachandra Tejaswi. All the stories are set in small towns or villages. The master storyteller sets up the ambience superbly and brilliantly weaves hearsay, traditions and customs of that region. These stories grow on you and you feel like reading them again.
