'The Quitter'

Vertigo/DC Comics, 2005, 106 pages

Written by Harvey Pekar

Art by Dean Haspiel with Gray Tones by Lee Loughridge 

The Quitter tells 'the story' of Pekar's youth. Kind of like an 'American Splendor' issue zero, or a prequel.
