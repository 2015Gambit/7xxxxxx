Automatic time of day, custom players and uniform, animations and more!
We present you a series of live 3D wallpapers called "Good Point". Now Animated!
As a true football fans we've wanted to take a look inside the game's most exiting moment - sliding tackle.

Features:

    You can choose players.
    You can select each player's uniform. We've used the most common colors. It will also affect the color of the flags and particles.
    You can change the time of day on the stadium!
    You can change the ball.
    You can turn on/off animated fog and particles (can affect performance on old devices).
    Good Point wallpaper fully supports horizontal orientation and looks amazing on tablet devices as well as on phones.

More Good Point Wallpapers are on the way!
