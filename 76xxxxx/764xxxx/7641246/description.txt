Every spring, paddlers from around the world migrate to eastern North America in pursuit of the massive early season flows. b.EAST follows some of the most committed and accomplished on this journey.
With challenging, awe inspiring whitewater, b.EAST takes you on a thougth provoking ride that offers a window into the soul of the sport.
