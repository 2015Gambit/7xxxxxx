Matrix 3D Cubes 3 Live Wallpaper is renderer with Open GL ES2.0 and are enhanced with more options, touch enabled, more cubes (125 cubes). Report any problems to me by email thanks! No issues reported! Tested on Samsung Galaxy S3 but work on other devices as well.

Crash issue resolved when setting wallpaper.

In settings you can change,

    Framerate Control Enabled (try this if your device has trouble to render at full framerate)
    Framerate (1-60 Hz)
    Dome image
    Dome brightness
    Dome On/Off
    Color shifting of cubes color and specular color
    Use static cube color that you can choose
    Use static specular color that you can choose
    Touch enabled or not.
    Touch sensivity
    Speed
