Men in Black{1997}

Barry Sonnenfeld
Writers:
Lowell Cunningham (comic), Ed Solomon (screen story), and 1 more credit ┬╗

Barry Sonnenfeld

Director:
Barry Sonnenfeld
Writers:
Etan Cohen, Lowell Cunningham (Malibu comic)
Stars:
Will Smith, Tommy Lee Jones and Josh Brolin
