Jump game : Feed Arthur the little monkey by throwing fruits through 30 baskets!

Arthur is a circus monkey. He never learned how to live in the hostile environment of the jungle. Since he's gone, you must help to feed by providing fruit and comfort to try to make him come back home.
Alone on his branch, he expects you to bring up his favorite meal like oranges, bananas and lemons, try to gain his trust and get him back on track.

But Arthur does not like to wait at all, you will have to use all your skill to blast the fruits from baskets to baskets in time avoiding traps.
The number of fruit and the time are limited, as the patience of Arthur is not his strong point ....
3 scenes await you in this crazy adventure of 30 levels.
Bonuses will help you earn extra fruit, and beautiful scenery will immerse you in the heart of the jungle and the ocean.
