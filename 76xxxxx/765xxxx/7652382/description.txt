Pinjra | 1972 | V.Shantaram's Immortal Marathi Classic | Phantom

Pinjra aka "The Cage" is one of greatest masterpiece by master of Cinema, V Shantaram. Made in 70s it explores a complex web love, sex and relationships. Why and how people lose the respect in their own eyes, of choices or lack of choices people make in their life.

Its a must watch movie for anyone who wants to explore complex web love , life and life with respect and love with meaning and how all of this can be turned upside down by a very basic human urge of sex. A masterpiece & classical Greek tragedy.
