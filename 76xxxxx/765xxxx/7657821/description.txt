Issues 1-3 of 'California Comics'

California Comics, 1974-1977

California Comics was a comic shop in San Jose, CA.

Contributors: Ed Watson, John A. Williams, Scott Shaw, Will A. Meugniot, Lucie Severino, Robert Kline, Han Hale, Tom Bird, Richard V. Corben, Barney Steel, Bill Loudin, Bob Sidebottom, Larry Sidebottom, Al Davoran, Melvin Wong, John A. Williams, Dave Ross, Ray Weiland

Issues 2 and 3 contain an alphabetical 'Comixography' listing known underground comics at the time.

For the most part these are some real nice looking books. I've been looking forward to doing the 'Restoration' on these and I'm happy with the way they turned out.
