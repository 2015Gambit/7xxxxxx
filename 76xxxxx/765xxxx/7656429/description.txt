Bike Mania - Racing Game
Race your way motorbike across 60+ challenging levels over grass, scrapyards and ice. Battle against your ghost to try and beat your times or compete with players online by posting your score up on leaderboards. If your a fan of quad or dirt bikes then you will love the animation and physics this game provides.
GAME FEATURES
- Addicting gameplay
- Simple and effective controls
- Real-time biking engine
- Over 60 challenging levels
- Play against your ghost to better your time
- Global leader boards via OpenFeint
