In Atlantis Rising, seismic disturbances at sea force world militaries to investigate the deepest part of the oceans of Earth, where an underground civilization emerges to wage war with the planet.

This story arc is a Fantastic Four/Namor crossover, featuring (among others) the Inhumans, and Thor as the slave of Morgan Le Fay.

Dreamworks have optioned the five-part comic book mini-series from Platinum Studios, and have set away Transformers and Star Trek writers, Alex Kurtzman and Roberto Orci, to produce and possibly scribe the movie adaptation.
