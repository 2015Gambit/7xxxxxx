Influential Jazz bassist Doug Watkins released only two albums as leader before his untimely death at the age of 27 in a car accident.


1956: Watkins at Large (with Donald Byrd, Hank Mobley, Duke Jordan, Kenny Burrell, Art Taylor)
1960: Soulnik (New Jazz Records, with Yusef Lateef, Hugh Lawson, Herman Wright, Lex Humphries)
