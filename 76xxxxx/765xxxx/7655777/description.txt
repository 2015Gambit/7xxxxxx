A compilation of language activities useful for English teachers teaching to EFL and ESL students. Lessons include:

- Business English (Beginner to Advanced)
- Business Idioms and Terms
- Phonics
- Narration Using Comic Strips (Beginner- Low)
- New Introductory
- Side by Side
- Skytalk Library
- TOEIC Business (Listening Exercises)
- TOEIC Daily Life (Listening Exercises)
- TOEIC Reading & Speaking
- TOEIC Vocabulary & Grammar
