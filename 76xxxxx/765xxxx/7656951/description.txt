MANIC DRIVE DISCOGRAPHY
---------------------------------------------------------

This Manic Drive discography may or may not be complete.  It contains 4 releases...

 * 2005 - Reason For Motion
 * 2007 - Reset And Rewind
 * 2009 - Blue
 * 2011 - Epic

Manic Drive is a Canadian Christian rock band formed in 2004 by founding members Michael A. Cavallo and Shawn Cavallo in Toronto. They are internationally known in North America and Europe and have established themselves through the Christian/Gospel music industry. They still continue to build momentum touring and collaborating with such artists as Skillet, Switchfoot, Thousand Foot Krutch, Superchick, Pillar, Kevin Max of dc Talk, Fireflight, Stellar Kart, Group 1 Crew, and Stryper.
