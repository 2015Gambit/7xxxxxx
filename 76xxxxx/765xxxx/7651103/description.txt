With Call Reminder Notes you can create and assign a Reminder Note to one or more of your Contacts.
Reminder Notes are shown on screen during call (incall and outcall), allowing you to access relevant information at the right moment, when you actually speak over the phone with a person.

After Call features pack gives you advanced options to effectively organize your after call activity: add Reminders, Google Tasks and Calendar Events.

App is available in the following languages:
English, French, Spanish, Italian, German, Korean, Japanese and Traditional Chinese

Features :

    Create a Reminder Note and assign it to one or more Address Book Contacts, Reminder will be shown on the screen during call 
    Reminder Note is automatically Deactivated after talking to the assigned Contact(s) 
    Reminder Note can be set to remain Active even if you cancel/reject a call 
    Assign a Callback Alarm to a Reminder Note, to make sure the reminder is shown by a certain date 
    After Call Features 
    Turn After Call features ON/OFF from Settings Screen 
    Assign Reminder, even if the number is not in your Phone Book 
    Create Calendar Events on your default phone Calendar 
    Create Google Tasks directly on your Google Account 

What's in this version: (Updated : Sep 17, 2012)

    in "ManageReminders" reminders are now sorted by contact name and by content
    fixed some minor bugs.

Required Android O/S : 2.1+
