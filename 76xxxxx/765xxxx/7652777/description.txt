--------------------------------------------------------------
MARK HEARD DISCOGRAPHY
--------------------------------------------------------------

This Mark Heard discography may or may not be complete.  It includes 14 releases...

 * 1975 - On Turning To Dust
 * 1979 - Appalachian Melody
 * 1980 - Fingerprint
 * 1981 - Stop The Dominoes
 * 1982 - Victims Of The Age
 * 1984 - Ashes And Light
 * 1985 - Mosiacs
 * 1990 - Dry Bones Dance
 * 1991 - Second Hand
 * 1992 - Satellite Sky
 * 1993 - Reflections Of A Former Life
 * 2000 - Mystery Mind
 * 2000 - The Millennium Archives
 * 2007 - The Last Artifacts Of An American Poet

Mark Heard was a record producer, folk-rock singer, and songwriter originally from Macon, Georgia, USA. Mark Heard released 16 records in his lifetime, and produced and performed with many other artists as well, such as Sam Phillips (aka Leslie Phillips), Pierce Pettis, Phil Keaggy, Vigilantes of Love, Peter Buck of R.E.M. (who co-produced VOL's album Killing Floor with Heard), John Austin, The Choir, Randy Stonehill and Michael Been of The Call. Heard produced part of Olivia Newton-John's The Rumor, which also included a cover of Heard's own "Big and Strong" (originally called "How to Grow Up Big and Strong").
