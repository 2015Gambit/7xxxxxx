Engrenages (French for "gears" or "cogs") is a French television police drama series created by the TV production company Son et Lumière. It is marketed in the English-speaking world as Spiral. The first eight episodes series started on Canal+ in France on 13 December 2005.

Creators: Guy-Patrick Sainderichin, Alexandra Clert

Stars: Fred Bianconi, Caroline Proust and Grégory Fitoussi
