Requirements: 2.2 and up
Overview: Warp Runner is an innovative Augmented Reality Puzzle/Platform game.

Your character is trapped in the real world, help him to escape. Warp Up/Down the real world with your fingers in order to solve the puzzles and create a path through the exit. Find your way out through all the levels and regain his freedom.
Game Features:
- Tabletop Augmented Reality game: The game level appears to be in the real world on the game card.
- Motion and touch controls: Interact by moving the phone around the card and by touching the screen to warp the real world.
- 16 fun and challenging puzzles.
- Built with Unity3D and Qualcomm's Vuforia Augmented Reality SDK.
