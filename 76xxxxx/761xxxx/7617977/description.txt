
TOEIC Mastery CD-ROM
is designed to prepare you to take the TOEIC test and to evaluate your mastery of English. It provides a very realistic simulation of the actual paper-based test while taking advantage of the flexibility and interactivity of computer technology. This CD-rom comprises numerous listening and reading exercises (over 1000 test items) aswell as advice about how to approach taking the test itself. An excellent tool that will help you achieve the highest scores possible when taking the TOEIC test.
How to install:
1. Unpack files from archive,
2. Run setup.exe and when 'user/serial' window appear press 'Cancel',
3. Overwrite 'TCM.lic' key to your installed directory (C:/Program Files/ALA/TCM).
