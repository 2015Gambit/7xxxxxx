Title: Kohan Warchest 
Genre: RTS / isometric / fantasy
Released: 25th May 2010
Developer: TimeGate Studios
Publisher: TimeGate Studios
Description:
Includes Kohan: Immortal Sovereigns, Kohan: Ahriman's Gift and Kohan II: Kings of War.

In the land of Khaldun, a race of immortal beings known as the Kohan are caught in a desperate struggle for their existence. For the first time, fans and newcomers alike can experience the entire saga in one conveniently packed bundle. Ready your companies and prepare for battle - the fate of this civilization is now in your hands.

How to play:
1) Burn disc images or mount them on a virtual drive;
2) Run setup.exe and install the games;
3) Play the games without the discs.

! Notice
When launching each game for the first time, you will be asked to enter a serial number. Check Serial Numbers.txt file for the codes.

! Notice
Games tested and working fine on Windows 7 x64.
