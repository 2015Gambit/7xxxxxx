--------------------------------------------------------
ISRAEL HOUGHTON DISCOGRAPHY
--------------------------------------------------------

This Israel Houghton discography may or may not be complete.  It contains the following 12 releases...

 * 1996 - Whisper It Loud
 * 2001 - New Season
 * 2001 - Nueva Generacion
 * 2002 - Real
 * 2004 - Live From Another Level
 * 2005 - Alive In South Africa
 * 2006 - A Timeless Christmas
 * 2007 - A Deeper Level
 * 2009 - The Power Of One
 * 2010 - Love God Love People - The London Sessions
 * 2012 - Decade
 * 2012 - Jesus At The Center

Israel Houghton is an American Christian singer and worship leader, mostly known for his cross-cultural style of Christian music that fuses elements from gospel, jazz and rock. Houghton is usually credited as Israel & New Breed. Houghton is a worship leader at Joel Osteen's Lakewood Church in Houston, Texas. "All Around" is on the Digital Praise PC game Guitar Praise.
