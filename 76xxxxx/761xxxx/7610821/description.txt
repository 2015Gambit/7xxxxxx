Title	Modern Air Combat: The Aircraft, Tactics and Weapons Employed in Aerial Warfare Today
Authors	Bill Gunston, Mike Spick
Edition	illustrated
Publisher	Salamander Books, 1983
ISBN	0861011627, 9780861011629
Length	224 pages

This book is "modern" in title only but still one of the best books on air combat as it is detailed enough to please a seasoned enthusiast but still easy enough and well illustrated to interest a beginner.
