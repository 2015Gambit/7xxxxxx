-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
                                 Release Notes
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Tendor's debut album LOGCHOPA (English: Splittist/Reactionary) has nine tracks. 
All tracks are original Tibetan songs composed and performed by Tendor, and 
recorded by Students for a Free Tibet's long-time friend Alex Vittum. The songs 
are mostly political and philosophical, while some are casual and funny. 
They're all in plain Tibetan.
