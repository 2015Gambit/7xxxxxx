Talking Tom & Ben have become famous TV anchors. Talk to them and they will take turns to repeat. Create and record funny conversations between them.

PLEASE NOTE: When running the app for the first time, you will be prompted to download additional 4-15 MB to get the best graphics quality for your device.

You can also create your own videos of Tom & Ben News and will comment on them. Just press the TV button in the app to record a video with the camera or choose one of your videos in the Photos application. The videos will then run the app and you can comment on them as Tom & Ben.
