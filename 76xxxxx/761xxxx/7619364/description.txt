Ben and Kate is an upcoming American comedy series that is scheduled to premiere on Fox on September 25, 2012, as part of the 2012ΓÇô13 television season. In May 2012, Fox placed an order for the series.

The series focuses on the lives of a brother and sister who are exact opposites: Ben is an over-the-top dreamer, while younger sister Kate is a single mother trying to make ends meet as a bar manager. After Ben realizes that his sister needs help with both her life and taking care of her daughter Maddie, he decides to move in so Kate can start rediscovering what she missed out, while at the same time Kate hopes to return the favor by seeing how she can bring her brother back to reality.
