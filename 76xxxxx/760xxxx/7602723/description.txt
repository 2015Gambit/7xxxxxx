Requirements: Android 2.2+
Overview: This add-on adds additional functionality to the Droid Notify application.


**NOTE: This is an add-on for the Droid Notify application.**

This add-on adds additional functionality to the Droid Notify application. It allows you to tap into the Status Bar Notifications and display a popup window on your screen for any application that is installed on your phone that use Status Bar Notifications.

Within moments you can be notified about all applications installed on your phone.
