Requirements: Android OS 2.1+
Overview: Soar through the clouds with this beautiful 3D live wallpaper! Colorful balloons drift through the scene, randomly or based on your unread messages! Lots of settings for cloud density, speeds, etc!


Not a movie! Full support for landscape viewing and home screen switching!

TO USE: Home->Press Menu->Wallpapers->LiveWallpapers

Not all handsets support the live wallpaper feature. If you're unsure, please check for a 'live wallpapers' category as the directions above state.
