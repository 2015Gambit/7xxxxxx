Atlas of World Interiors, with five hundred projects included, is a comprehensive portfolio of up-to-date interior projects from all over the world. In countries such as Iran, United Arab Emirates, South Africa, Morocco, and Tunisia, the history and culture of particular locations were subtly integrated into interiors using innovative approaches.
