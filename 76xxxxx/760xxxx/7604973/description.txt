Love And Zombies - Not Looking For Love (2012):

Official Release - Not Looking For Love is the 2nd Love And Zombies Single of 2012. Originally conceived in 2008, "Not Looking For Love" is one of the first tracks ever written for the Love and Zombies project. It's a bit slower and heavier than previous tracks, both lyrically and musically.
