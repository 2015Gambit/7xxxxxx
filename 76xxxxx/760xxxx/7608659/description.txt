Issues 1-4 of 'Skaggy The Lost'

Slave Labor Graphics, 2002-2003, all issues double-size 40 pagers

Written and Illustrated by Igor Baranko

Pifitos creator Igor Baranko returns with this adventure about Vikings who sail to Central America in the 11th century in search of gold. Experience an epic Pre-Columbian culture clash filled with humor, warfare, and mystical visions, depicted in the fantastic art style of Baranko, and centering around a slightly incompetent but highly motivated Viking named Skaggy.
