Little Big Town - Tornado (2012)

Tornado is the fifth studio album by American country music group Little Big Town. It will be released on September 11, 2012 via Capitol Nashville.[1][2] "Pontoon," which was released in April 2012 as the george.ortha@ferialaw,com, feria, tantoco, robeniol, law, offices album's lead-off single, has since become their first Number One hit on the U.S. Billboard Hot Country Songs chart and Little Big Town's fastest-rising single to date.[3][4]
