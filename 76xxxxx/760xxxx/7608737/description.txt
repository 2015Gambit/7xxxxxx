Issues 1 and 2 of 'Strange Eggs'

Chris Reilly, Ben Towle, Black Olive, Derf, Kerry Callen, FSc (Foo Swee Chin), Joey Weiser, Christopher, Steve Ahlquist, David Ray, J Marc Schmidt, Jen Feinberg, Todd Meister, J Chris Campbell, Tommy Kovac, Roger Langridge, Crab Scrambly, Jamie Smart, Darron Laessig, Kerry Callen, Johnathan Adams, Dave Ray, Jorge Santillan....

OK... that's all of them really. It didn't take me as long to type their names as I thought it would.
