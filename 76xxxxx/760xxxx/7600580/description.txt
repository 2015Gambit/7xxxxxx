The Missing Parts are an acoustic quartet from Tucson, Arizona comprised of violin, lap-steel guitar, cello, and guitar.

Oliver Baylock - violin
Douglas Francisco - lap steel guitar, banjo, miscellaneous screaming
Brian Hullfish - cello, chromatic harmonica
Paul Wright - acoustic guitar

Also featured in this album:
José Alfredo Soto de Mesa - piano
Kat Peña - vocals
Levi Hudman - vocals

Please do what you can to support these artists!
