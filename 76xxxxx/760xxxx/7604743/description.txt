The Bode Broads - A Portfolio by Vaughn E. Bode

Bagginer Press, 1977

Another one where I wasn't perfectly happy with the 'Restoration', so I included the original in the torrent as well. In the end, I think some of the images look better in the 'Restoration' and some look better in the original.

Remember that part of the reason for 'Restoring' these books is so the images can be enlarged. After opening the 'Restorations' in a comic book reader be sure to shrink them down a bit to improve the image quality.
