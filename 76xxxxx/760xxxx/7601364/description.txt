Lama Gyurme & Jean-Phillipe Rykiel - Rain of Blessings

One of several collaborative works between Lama Gyurme and Jean-Philippe Rykiel.

"The meeting of a Tibetan Buddhist monk of the Kagyupa School, messenger of one of the most ancient philosophies, and a French musician known for his mastery of the keyboards. Powerful chants with lush textures that capture the serenity of the Tibetan monasteries."

All tracks in FLAC.
