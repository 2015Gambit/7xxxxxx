The official E-book of Entropy. 

The true and brutally honest story of a young boy who grows into a man while embarking on his journey to escape the chaos of our system. 

Jordan Gardiner learns the science of attraction, using it for minipulation and seeking love. Through the multitude of women he seduces, he learns that he won't find genuine love without a genuine interaction. The women he becomes involved with land him in legal, life-threatening, and chaotic problems that spiral out of control. 

Drugs, world-class parties, wreckless choices, and their consequences.
