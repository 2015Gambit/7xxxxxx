All at Sea

Travel Documentary hosted by Timothy Spall, published by BBC in 2012 - English narration


Information
------------------------------

Timothy Spall and his wife Shane are back on board their beloved barge the Princess Matilda as they conclude their trip around the British coast.


1)  Message in a Bottle 
In Scotland, Timothy and Shane take on Rattray Head in the face of a huge storm.

2)  Stags by the Sea 
The Spalls visit Northumberland, Newcastle and Hartlepool as their journey continues.

3)  Gods Own Coast 
Tim and Shane find the Whitby hotel where Dracula novelist Bram Stoker once stayed.

4)  The Last Splash 
After six years Tim and Shane conclude their trip, but not before a frightening twist.
