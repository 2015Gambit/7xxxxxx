Well, I started reading Empowered once again, to prepare for book 7, and enjoy it as always, so I figured you should all get a chance to enjoy the best manga style sexy superhero comic.
This pack include Empowered 1-7, both specials, AND my scan of the sketch book.


Not only is costumed crimefighter Empowered saddled with a less-than-ideal superhero name, but she wears a skintight and cruelly revealing supersuit that only magnifies her body-image insecurities. Worse yet, the suits unreliable powers are prone to failure, repeatedly leaving her in appallingly distressing situations. . . and giving her a shameful reputation as the lamest cape in the masks-and-tights business. Nonetheless, she pluckily braves the ordeals of her bottom-rung superheroic life with the help of her thugalicious boyfriend (and former Witless Minion) and her hard-drinking ninja girlfriend, not to mention the supervillainous advice from the caged alien demonlord watching DVDs from atop her coffee table . . .
