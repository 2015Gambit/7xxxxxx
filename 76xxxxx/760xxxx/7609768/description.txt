Angry Words is a multi-user word game that you can play against your friends.
It's available in many languages including English and Spanish and has a clean, sophisticated and intuitive interface.
Game animations make it even better by letting you see your opponent's recent moves.

The game supports the following languages: English, British English, Spanish, French, Italian, German, Dutch, Portuguese (Brasil), Portuguese (Portugal), Swedish, Catalan and Basque.

This version does not contain ads.
