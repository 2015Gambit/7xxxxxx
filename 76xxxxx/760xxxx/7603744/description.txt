Back at Sea

Travel Documentary hosted by Timothy Spall, published by BBC in 2011 - English narration


Information
------------------------------

Untrained mariner Timothy Spall has spent a fortune on technology for his new challenge - the unpredictable Irish Sea - as he and his wife continue their mini-odyssey around Britain.


1)  The Luck of the Irish Sea 
Timothy Spall visits Aberystwyth and finds a lovely cove at Porthdinllaen.

2)  Mad about the Buoys 
Timothy and Shane encounter problems with buoys when trying to dock at Liverpool.

3)  The Bit in the Middle 
Timothy and Shane visit the Isle of Man, Northern Ireland and Scotland on their trip.

4)  Scotch Mist 
Timothy and Shane encounter several Scottish ports and islands in the misty drizzle.
