The movie revolves around Shanaya (Bipasha Basu), who enjoys a successful Bollywood career and passionate affair with a film director Aditya (Emraan Hashmi). Success is a very short-lived word in Bollywood. Soon, a newcomer Sanjana (Esha Gupta) takes her place in the industry as well as Aditya's life.
