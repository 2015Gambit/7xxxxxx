No sweat Cantonese is a fun easy way of learning to speak what is truly a difficult language to learn.

However, it is not a textbook for just learning the Cantonese language, but it is also a way of learning the HK Cantonese culture. For instance it provides the reader with useful places to go for shopping, sightseeing, the beaches in Hong Kong etc. It is a book that provides language learning, culture, idioms, slang expressions and is therefore a very comprehensive book.
