Guitarist is Europe's longest-established guitar magazine. Its aims are clear: to improve our readers' knowledge of the instrument, help them make the right buying choices and assist them in becoming a better player. Our in-depth reviews inform, our interviews entertain and our techniques section offers lessons from the UK's finest tutors. Our exclusive cover CD, Guitarist Interactive, provides an extra dimension. With this, you can listen to new products, see them demo'd, practice techniques over our backing tracks and listen to great artists in action. As 'the guitar buyer's bible', Guitarist enthuses, informs and entertains. Want to know more about guitars or the guitar market? Choose Guitarist!
