The Scarlet Letter (2004)

Release date : 28 October 2004 (South Korea)
Directed by : Hyuk Byun          
Genre : Drama / Mystery / Thriller
Plot Outline : Complex emotional relationships and human nature's
darker sides are explored in this captivating suspense drama about
a cop whose lust for love threatens to get in the way of his professional life.
Starring : Suk-kyu Han, Eun-ju Lee, Hyeon-a Seong,
Ji-won Uhm, Kim Jin-geun I, Do Yong-goo.
