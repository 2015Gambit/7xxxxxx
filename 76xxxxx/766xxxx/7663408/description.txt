Match of the Day 22.09.2012

Duration: 1 Hour, 20 Min

Language : English 

Gary Lineker presents highlights from the Premier League. Chelsea host Stoke at Stamford Bridge, as the Potters go in search of their first-ever Premier League victory over the Champions League winners.

Two of the game's biggest managerial personalities go head to head at Upton Park, as Sam Allardyce's West Ham welcome Martin O'Neill's Sunderland.

Elsewhere, Southampton face Aston Villa, Swansea host Everton, Reading travel to West Brom, and Wigan take on Fulham.
