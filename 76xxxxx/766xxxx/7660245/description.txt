============================================
Description:                               =
============================================

Small Tanks is a 3D game based on the popular tank theme. A simple mission begins and ends with a meat grinder. If it becomes very difficult, you can call a second player to join the fight. Successful missions require sweat and blood, and you will earn points and raise your rank along the way. Good luck!
