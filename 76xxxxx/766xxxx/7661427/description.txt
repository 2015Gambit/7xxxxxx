Godfather is a 2012 Indian Kannada-language drama film directed by cinematographer-turned- director Sethu Sriram starring Upendra and Soundarya Jayamala in the lead roles. Catherine Teresa also plays a prominent role and Bhumika Chawla makes a special appearance.
The film is remake of the 2006 Tamil blockbuster Varalaru that starred Ajith Kumar, Asin Thottumkal and Kanika Subramaniam in the lead roles. Internationally acclaimed composer, A. R. Rahman, who composed music for the original Tamil version, is the composer of the soundtracks and Rajesh Ramanath is the background score composer.
The film was released on 27th July 2012 and opened to a large number of highly positive reviews from the critics. Upendra's performance of a Bharatha Natyam dancer and an old man pegged to his wheel chair was highly acclaimed by the critics and is considered as one of his best performances in his career.[3] Godfather did moderately well at the box office and was declared an Above Average Grosser.
