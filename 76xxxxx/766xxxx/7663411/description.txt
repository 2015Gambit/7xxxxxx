This limited edition set came with a DVD of Atari Teenage Riot music videos, and a Bonus DVD of other Digital Hardcore Recordings artists such as Alec Empire.

There is also a CD called 'Rare Tracks '91-'94' which has previously unreleased early Atari Teenage Riot material.

DVDs are disc images.
DVD (92 mins.) + bonus DVD (73 mins.)
CD is encoded in FLAC format.
High resolution art included.
