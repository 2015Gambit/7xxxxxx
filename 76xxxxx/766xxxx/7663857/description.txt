Melissa Manchester

Pioneer Artists Presents
The Music Of Melissa Manchester

Recorded in 1980 at the Wilshire Theatre in Los Angeles for an HBO special.

As each of her highly personal albums illustrate, Melissa Manchester is the most compelling female singer and songwriter in contemporary music. With a voice that goes straight to your heart and emotionally charged lyrics, Melissa possesses the ability to create and sustain moods and emotions until the very last listener is thoroughly entralled. Now for the first time and in a manner never before possible, you can see Melissa Manchester as she appears in concert, with all the excitement, emotion and energy that is Melissa. Here are the award winning and best selling songs Melissa has made famous around the world. These songs were composed by Melissa and her friends, who are among the greatest songwriters of our time - Bernie Taupin, Carol Bayer Sager, Kenny Loggins and Peter Allen, among others. Melissa's songs celebrate life and the importance of self-affirmation. And she performs them with a ferver and vitality that will leave you cheering and applauding for more. (From the liner notes)
