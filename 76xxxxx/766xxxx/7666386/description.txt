Anagram Bounce is a classic Anagram word game with a twist... The letters never stop moving!

Anagram Bounce offers simple time killing game play as well as a number of optional challenge modes to test your word scrambling mettle. Below are some of the features:
* The word list contains over 100,000 all-age appropriate words. You will never play the same game twice!
*Various difficulties ranging from eay 3 letter words up to large 6 letter words. Believe me, 6 letter Anagrams are DIFFICULT!
* The game is simple and intuitive so there is no learning curve. Jump right into the fun stuff!
* The game keeps track of high scores so you compete against your friends, family or even yourself.
* With both challenge modes and endless modes the game is ideal for a quick 30 second fix or a sit down word finding marathon.
