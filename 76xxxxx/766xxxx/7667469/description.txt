The "Glacier Express" train between Zermatt and St.Moritz in the Swiss Alps is 
the probably nicest railroad line in Europe.

This is DVD 01 of 02 of a cab ride (train driver's view) concerning the 
villages Zermatt - Brig - Andermatt - Disentis.

The journey was recorded in June 1999.
