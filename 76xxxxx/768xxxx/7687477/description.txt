Actie / Thriller

geregisseerd door Paul Greengrass

Wanneer Irak Israël dreigt met Scudraket-aanvallen, moet de anti-Irak coalitie Saddam en getrouwen zien te stoppen. Het Britse SAS-team Bravo Two Zero, onder commando van sergeant Andy McNab krijgt de opdracht om raketinstallaties in Irak op te sporen en te vernietigen. Deze missie mislukt echter faliekant: drie SAS-commando’s worden gedood, en vier worden er gevangen genomen; slechts één weet te ontsnappen - kapitein Chris Ryan.
English

The existence of the anti-Iraq coalition was threatened when Saddam targetted Israel. In order to keep Israel out of the war, and the coalition together the Scuds had to be stopped. SAS patrol Bravo Two Zero, under the command of Sgt. Andy McNab was tasked to destroy these weapons. Three of the SAS troopers were killed and four captured: Only one escaped - Cpl. Chris Ryan. This is his version of what happenned.
