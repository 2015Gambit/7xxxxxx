The First King of Shannara:

Dark forces are on the move from the Northlands, 

and Bremen, an outcast Druid, learns of the huge Troll 

armies on the march and the Skull Bearers who act as 

their spies. To save the Druids, Bremen must convince 

the people of the Four Lands that their only hope lies 

in uniting -- and in using the magic they fear above all 

else.
