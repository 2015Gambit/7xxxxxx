This is a collection of the writings of Julius Evola.  It includes the following texts:

Revolt Against the Modern World
Men Among the Ruins
Ride the Tiger
The Doctrine of Awakening
The Metaphysics of Sex
American "Civilization"
Hitler and Secret Societies
Introduction to Magic
Meditation on the Peaks
The Hermetic Tradition
The Mystery of the Grail
The Yoga of Power

Also included are some texts on Evola - by both admirers and critics - and two interviews discussing Evola's thought.
