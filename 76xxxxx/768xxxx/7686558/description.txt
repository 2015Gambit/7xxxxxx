Krantiveera Sangolli Rayanna (English: Legendary Warrior Sangolli Rayanna) is a 2012 historical Kannada film directed by Naganna and produced by Anand Appugol.[2] Challenging Star Darshan Thoogudeep, Jayaprada and Nikita Thukral play lead roles. The film is about Sangolli Rayanna, the prominent freedom fighter from Karnataka, India. He fought the British till his death. He was the army chief of the Kingdom of Kittur ruled at the time by Kittur Rani Chennamma.[3] Initially, Arjun Sarja was selected for the lead role which is being portrayed by Darshan.[4][5] This is one of the highest budget films in the Kannada film industry,
