      RITA ORA  - How We Do (Party) (PO Quick Edit) (2012)



             "How We Do (Party)" is a song recorded by British recording artist Rita Ora, from her debut studio album ORA (2012). It was written by Victor Alexander, Kelly Sheehan and Bonnie McKee, while production was handled by The Runners and The Monarch. The song borrows lyrics from Biggie Smalls's 1993 single "Party and Bullshit".              "How We Do (Party)" was released as the lead single from the album in the United States, Canada, Australia and New Zealand on 20 March 2012. Upon its release, "How We Do (Party)" peaked inside the top-ten in Australia and New Zealand. It was released as the album's second single in Ireland on 10 August 2012 and in the United Kingdom on 12 August 2012 and reached number-one in both regions.
