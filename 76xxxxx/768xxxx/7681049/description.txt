The Lost Child
Faith,the recently adopted baby daughter of Stephen and Frances Groves, is abducted in broad daylight,leading Gently and Bacchus to the home for unmarried mothers from where the couple got Faith. Mrs Dunwoody,the owner,tells them that Faith had a twin brother and the mother Susan Faulkner disappeared with him after Faith's adoption but,when traced,Susan is unable to help the enquiry.
