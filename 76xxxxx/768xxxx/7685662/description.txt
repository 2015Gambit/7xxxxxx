A compelling story about a girl in the eighteenth century, who was brought up by her absent father who gambled and was forced to sell his daughter to a mysterious rich gentleman. Vivian's life came crumbling down when she caught her best friend betraying her, and she decides to commit suicide after hearing her father had sold her to clear his debts. Little does she know that soon enough, her life will change when she becomes the property of a coven leader of witches and wizards, who saves her broken body, when he magically binds then as one. Vivian meets Naomi, who hates her for taking her place beside the coven leader. She also meets Bell, who is taken with her from the moment they've met. Soon enough, Vivian's life is torn between good and evil, and she faces who aren't real.
