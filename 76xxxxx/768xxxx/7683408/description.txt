If you think Perfect Smiles has anything to do with above the waist, then you just don't know Jim Holiday. He approaches beauty contests the same way he does everything else - focus on the fun and fluff, add tasteful raunch, then satirize and ridicule the hell out of the rest of the deal...There's no Miss Congeniality in the Miss Whole Package Contest. There is a Miss Thimbles, Miss Perfect Slit, and a Miss Perfect Pucker winner. Devin Wolf Bangs Jill and Shayla backdoor in separate scenes, while Evan Stone does likewise to Sydnee Steele and Barett Moore. Just four of the eight rearenders in this twelve scene sex-travaganza. Perfect puckers and perfect vertical smiles galore...
