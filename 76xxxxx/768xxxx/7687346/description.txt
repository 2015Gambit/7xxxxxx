Ritchie is a British-born musician, singer & songwriter who emigrated in 1972 to Brazil, where he has enjoyed a successful recording career. In 1983 he released his 1st multiplatinum LP, "Vôo de Coração", which spawned 5 chart-topping singles, including the smash hit, "Menina Veneno". He has released 9 solo albums and recorded with Caetano Veloso, Gilberto Gil, Wagner Tiso, Jim Capaldi, Steve Hackett, Dominic Miller, Lulu Santos, Lobão, Ultraje a Rigor, Flávio Venturini, Kid Abelha, Vinicius Cantuária & Patrick Moraz, among other artists.
