Le Boreal

Technology Documentary hosted by Barbara Budd, published by Discovery Channel broadcasted as part of DC Mighty Ships series in 2011 - English narration


Information
------------------------------

The Le Boreal is on her maiden voyage as an expedition cruise ship. For 15 days the passengers are treated to a combination of fine dining and exploring the amazing environment of Antarctica.
