Shirdi Sai is a Telugu drama film, directed by K. Raghavendra Rao starring Akkineni Nagarjuna, Srikanth, Srihari. The music director is Keeravani. It is the cinematic of land mark life episodes of Shirdi Sai Baba.[4] The film was released worldwide on September 6, 2012,[3] and has received moderately positive reviews.
