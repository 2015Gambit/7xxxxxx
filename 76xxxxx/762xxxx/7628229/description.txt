BulletFlight 3.1.0

Bulletflight ballistics calculator, which predicts the trajectory of bullets fired from high-powered sniper rifles by taking into account half a dozen variables including wind speed, distance, outside temperature and altitude.

After digesting all the information, Bulletflight tells you where exactly on the sniper scope you should position your target in order to hit it.

Works perfectly on Jailbroken IOS 5.1

You must have installed App Sync from the hackulous repository to use apps that were obtained by sources other than the apple store.
