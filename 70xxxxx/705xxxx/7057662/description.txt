Storyline

When everyone except Ross pools together to buy tickets for the lottery, Phoebe gets a prediction from 
her psychic that they will win. Ross, despite his skepticism, gets in on the action. Meanwhile, Chandler 
is anxious to find out if he gets a coveted assistant job at his advertising firm, and Emma is due to say her first word.
