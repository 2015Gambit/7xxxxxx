who first found fame with the rock band Traffic. In his long career, Mason has played and recorded with 
many of the era's most notable rock musicians, including Jimi Hendrix, Delaney Bramlett, Michael Jackson
The Rolling Stones, Eric Clapton, George Harrison, Fleetwood Mac and Cass Elliot. Mason's best known song 
is "Feelin' Alright" recorded by Traffic in 1968 and later by many other performers, including Joe Cocker 
who had a major hit with the song in 1969. For Traffic, he also wrote "Hole in My Shoe" a psychedelic pop
song that became a hit in its own right. "We Just Disagree" Mason's 1977 solo hit written by Jim Krueger
has become a staple of Classic Hits and Adult Contemporary radio playlists..
