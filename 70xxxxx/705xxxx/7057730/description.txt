For english-only speaking:
This book is in german language. English title would be: The legal mafia - Secret societies in control of Austria (1990). Karl Steinhauser exposes the masonic networks that control Austria's government, political parties, economy, churches, arts and media. A fascinating book that names names and describes this masonic despotism that has got a deadly strangehold on all Austrians. 310 pages.
