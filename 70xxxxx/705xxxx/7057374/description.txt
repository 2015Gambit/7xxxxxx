Poison Farm: A Murderer Unmasked After 60 Years

From BBC Radio 4 Extra. Written by David Williams; Read by Robert Lindsay. Abridged.

The true story of a philandering farmer who was poisoned in 1938, leaving police baffled as to the killer's identity.

Part 1: Who killed wealthy womaniser William Murfitt in 1938?

Part 2: Evidence mounts against his wife.

Part 3: The spotlight falls on James Walker and his housekeeper.

Part 4: Secrets are revealed at the inquest.

Part 5: Under cover of darkness, the police make one last trip to the murder scene.
