Call Me Maybe is the lead single from Canadian artist, Carley Rae Jepsen's second studio album. The song hit #1 on the Canadian Hot 100 and was featured in a video starring Justin Bieber, selena Gomez, Ashley Tisdale and Big Time Rush.

The song charted on #1 on the Itunes Canadian charts for several days before charting on Billboard. The song is one of the first done by an Canadian Idol star to chart #1.
