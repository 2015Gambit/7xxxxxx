Stratovarius is a Finnish power metal band that formed in 1984. Since their formation they have released 13 studio albums and one live album. Along with Helloween, Blind Guardian, Rhapsody of Fire and Gamma Ray, Stratovarius are considered one of the leading groups of the power metal and symphonic metal genre.

Destiny is the seventh studio album by Finnish power metal band Stratovarius, released in 1998 through Noise Records.
