Highlander: Way of the Sword is a four-issue, monthly comic book limited series published by Dynamite Entertainment from 2007 to 2008.

It is inspired from Highlander, the franchise about Immortals battling each other throughout history. The comic book centers around Connor MacLeod's search for the sword of his mentor Juan Sanchez Villa-Lobos Ramirez, after losing it in the ocean to The Kurgan by accident. He eventually retrieves the sword just before the events of the first Highlander film.
