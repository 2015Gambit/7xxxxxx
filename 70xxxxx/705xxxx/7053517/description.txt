The Hollow Man's 2006 Album 'Mabuse' features a collection of dark melodic electronic and mostly instrumental pieces. Guests including Strokes Producer Gordon Raphael (Voice on 'Hello'), Poetry Slam Master Etrit Hasler (Voice on 'Kaltekriegkinder') and classical musician Karl Lassauer (Trombone on 'Kellerfenster').
