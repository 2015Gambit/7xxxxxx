For english-only speaking:
This book is in german language. English title would be: The climate catastrophe lie - The most gigantic web of deception of our time - Manipulated fear as means to power (2008). Hartmut Bachmann exposes the environmental panics that are created to put humanity in fear and ultimately in chains. He systematically describes a web of interlocking institutions that have invented the climate catastrophe problem and are using it to further policies that prevent developement from taking place as instead poverty results. Their network extends all over the globe, taking many naive and uneducated people with them to create environmental squads to terrorize the world. Is humanity really the biggest produced or CO2 or is it something else? Find out how extremely selfish ambitions of pure power guide the environmental movement from the very beginning. 270 pages.
