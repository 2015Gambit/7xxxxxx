Scorpions were a rock band from Hannover, Germany, formed in 1965 by guitarist Rudolf Schenker, .[9][10][11] They are known for their 1980s rock anthem "Rock You Like a Hurricane" and many singles, such as "No One Like You", "Send Me an Angel", "Still Loving You", and "Wind of Change".
