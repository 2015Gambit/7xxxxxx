Excellent album from Argentina's progressive rock scene. Instrumentals like "Pollo Frito" and "Abismo Terrenal" show an amazing music capability. A CD that every progressive rock fan must have.

"Cronologia" aka "Kronologia" contains the only two studio albums released from Crucis: "Crucis\" and "Los Delirios del Mariscal", now in one CD. Plus, there's a live recording from a recital in the theatre Luna Park (bad quality :C)
