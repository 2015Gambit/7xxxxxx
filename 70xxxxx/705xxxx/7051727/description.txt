Six gorgeous step-by-step projects illustrate how to create vivid pictorial scenes and artistic renditions on fabric with dyes, paints, resists, leaves, salts, airbrushes, and stencils.

About the Author
Well-known fiber artist Ginny Eckley is a previously published author with That Patchwork Place. She has exhibited her work in countless shows and exhibitions throughout the country. Along with travelling around the country to teach her techniques to quilt guilds and art groups, Ginny has designed large commission pieces for both retail and residential clients.
