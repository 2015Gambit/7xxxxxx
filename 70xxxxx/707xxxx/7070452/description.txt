Danko Jones is a Canadian rock band from Toronto, Ontario. The band consists of Danko Jones (vocals/guitar) and John Calabrese (bass guitar). John Calabrese is also known as JC. Atom Willard has replaced drummer Dan Cornelius in the band, though his status is unclear as of February 2012. Their fifth album Below the Belt was released on May 11, 2010 and produced by Matt DeMatteo. (wikipedia)
