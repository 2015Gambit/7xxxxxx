Synopsis
Neil Gibson, the Gold King and former Senator approaches Sherlock Holmes to investigate the murder of his wife Maria, in order to clear his children's governess, Grace Dunbar, of the crime.
Maria Gibson was found lying in a pool of blood on Thor Bridge with a bullet through her head and a note from the governess in het hand agreeing to a meeting on Thor Bridge.
The revolver with one shot fired was found in Miss Dunbar's warrobe. Holmes agrees to look into it in spite of the damning eveidence against Miss Durban.
Holmes uses his power of deduction to solve the crime.
