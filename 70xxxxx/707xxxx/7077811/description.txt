Reflection for Mac lets you use AirPlay Mirroring to see your iPhone 4S or iPad 2 screen on your Mac! Just put both on the same Wifi network, turn it on, and you're good to go!

From the website, reflectionapp.com:
Demo With Ease
Easily demo iOS apps on any Mac running OS X Lion using AirPlay Mirroring on iOS 5.


Features Galore
Enable an iPhone or iPad wrapper and more. Features? You got em.


Game On
Kick back and play any iOS game on your Mac's screen. It's time for some GTA.
