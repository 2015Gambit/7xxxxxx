
HELLFIGHTERS


Director: 
Andrew V. McLaglen

 Writer: 
Clair Huffaker (screenplay)
 
Stars:
 John Wayne, Katharine Ross and Jim Hutton 


The story of macho oil well firefighters and their wives.The adventures of oil well fire specialist Chance Buckman (based on real-life Red Adair), who extinguishes massive fires in oil fields around the world.
