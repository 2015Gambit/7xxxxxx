ABOUT THE ALBUM
This is the album that Love made with Meredith Brooks. It was a bit funny when 
Love was on The View promoting this release. I was a bit hesitant to touch any
CD that came from Zomba / Jive due to their mainly having manufactured pro-tools
heavy groups but eventually i picked this up for my little birthday present to 
myself in 2006 and i was not disappointed. 
 
http://www.discogs.com/release/2557480  This is from the CA release but Discogs
link is for the EU release.
