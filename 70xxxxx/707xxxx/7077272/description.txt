» The Dust of Time is the acclaimed Greek director's second installment of the trilogy that began with 2004's 'The Weeping Meadow'.

An American film director of Greek ancestry is making a film that tells his story and that of his parents. It is an epic tale that unfolds in Italy, Germany, Russia, Kazakhstan, Canada and the US and journeys into the events of the latter half of the 20th Century.
