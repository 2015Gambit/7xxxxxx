Samantha Stewart, Julia Garvey, Aimee Sweet, Alexis Chao

Penthouse Video brings viewers a detailed look into the lives of three Penthouse Pets. 2000 Pet of the Year runner-up Alexus Winston, 1999 Pet of the Year Nikie St. Gilles, and Pet of the Month Aimee Sweet are featured in this thorough examination of a Penthouse photo shoot. The women unveil their personal fantasies and intimate desires to each other and to the camera. Penthouse takes viewers behind the scenes of the three-girl shoot, showing what really happens on the set. Photographer Earl Miller is the inspiration behind this feature, which focuses on the combination of fashion and beauty that is required to make a Penthouse photo shoot successful.
