actor, uses sex to get his landlady, Lydia, to reduce his rent, and then

even more than slightly important at the height of her career. Sally tries 
to help Joey, until he realizes that she just isn't well-connected enough
to be of any service to him. The affair is complicated by Sally's psychotic,
maybe-lesbian-or-maybe-not daughter Jessica, who tries to muscle in on her 
mother's relationship with Joey.
