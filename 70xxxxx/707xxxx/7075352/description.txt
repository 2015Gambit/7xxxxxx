Nederlands:

Musical over het leven van musical-regisseur, acteur, danser en zanger George M. Cohan. 
De film volgt hem van zijn dagen als kindsterretje tot zijn comeback,toen hij een medaille kreeg van de president.


Engels:


A musical portrait of composer/singer/dancer George M. Cohan. 
From his early days as a child-star in his family's vaudeville show up to the time of his comeback at which he received a medal 
from the president for his special contributions to the US, this is the life- story of George M. Cohan, 
who produced, directed, wrote and starred in his own musical shows for which he composed his famous songs.
