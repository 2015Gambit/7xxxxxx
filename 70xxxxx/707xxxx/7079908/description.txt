How do I Install it? 
+++++++++++++++++++++++

1) Open the Zip-File (DONE!)
2) Locate Folder in Zip-File named openBVE
3) Open up your root drive, this is usually C:
4) Go to your Program Files Folder. 
5) Drag the folder in the Zip-File called openBVE, and drop it into
	your program files.
6) Open up your folder that is now in Program Files.
7) Right Click on "OpenBve.exe" and click on COPY
8) Navigate to your desktop, and right click, then "Paste Shortcut"

openBVE 1.4.0.10 is now installed on your system, and can now be run.
