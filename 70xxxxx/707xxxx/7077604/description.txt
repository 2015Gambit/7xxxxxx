Mark of the Devil - La tortura delle vergini [XviD Ita Eng][TNTVillage]

Original title: Hexen bis aufs Blut gequält
AKA Mark of the Devil (USA)
Language: Italian, English
Subtitles: Italian

Stars: Herbert Lom, Udo Kier, Olivera Vuco, Reggie Nalder, Herbert Fux,
Johannes Buzalski

Release Date: 1970 
Genres: Drama History Horror
Director: Michael Armstrong

IMDB:
----- 
http://www.imdb.com/title/tt0065491/

Plot:
-----
Udo Kier is a witch hunter apprentice to Herbert Lom. He believes strongly
in his mentor and the ways of the church but loses faith when he catches 
Lom strangling Reggie Nalder to death for calling him impotent. Kier begins
to see for himself that the witch trials are nothing but a scam of the church
to rob people of their land, money, and other personal belongings of value
and seduce beautiful big breasted women. Eventually, the townspeople revolt,
Herbert Lom escapes but poor Udo is captured by the towns people. 
This film contains very strong graphic torture including a women's tongue 
being ripped out of her head,nuns being raped(in the opening credits), 
and lots of beatings.

Trama:
------
Nell’Austria del ’700, la banda di Albino (Reggie Nalder) compie sanguinosi
misfatti dietro il paravento della caccia alle streghe. Il conte Cumberland
(Herbert Lom) e il suo assistente, il giovane conte Christian von Meruh
(Udo Kier), vengono inviati nella zona per controllarne l’operato. Christian
si innamora di Vanessa (Olivera Vuco), una ragazza accusata falsamente 
di stregoneria, e si rende conto che le cose non vengono gestite 
correttamente: sconvolto, perde la cieca fiducia che ha sempre riposto 
in Cumberland quando vede che anche lui non è esente da colpe.
