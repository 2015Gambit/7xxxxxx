PLAYBACK:
I have encoded & tagged the files for playback in iTunes and on Apple devices (iPad, iPhone 4, etc) that can handle HD video, including *both* versions of the AppleTV set top box. I've included two English subtitle tracks - the first with Hearing Impaired info stripped out, the second with the HI information left in - within each M4V file. Both tracks are included externally as .srt files, as well.
