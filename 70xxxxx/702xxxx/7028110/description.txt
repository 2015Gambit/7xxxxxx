[Enigmatic Music Collection] Aba Structure - 4 releases

Japanese producer and musician with a background in both classical violin playing and Detroit house and techno. Has only a handful of releases, but gained considerable exposure in 1999 when "Illusion" was included on John Digweed's "Bedrock" double-mix-CD compilation. Has since taken up worldwide touring with a show that includes several laptops as well as live violin playing and singing.
