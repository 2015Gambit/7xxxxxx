No End In Sight
A documentary film by Charles Ferguson (English Version, no subtitles)

This great film shows the failures of the US in Iraq between spring 2003 and 2006. It includes interviews with many true insiders and won the jury price at Sundance 2007.

A must see! And a must spread!

Charles Ferguson also made \"Inside Job\", winning the 2011 Academy Awards.
