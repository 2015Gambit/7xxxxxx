Adapted from his autobiography, the film recounts the story of Hiroito, The King of Boca do Lixo (a region in downtown SÃ£o Paulo of the fifties where various nightclubs, strip joints, prostitution, bars, and drugs can be found). Hiroito was a well born bohemian and at the age of 21 was accused for the murder of his father, who was violently stabbed over 40 times with a razor.
