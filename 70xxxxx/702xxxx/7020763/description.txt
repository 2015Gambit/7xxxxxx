a reputation as one of the better giallo films of the early 1970s among 
fans of the genre.

Italian teacher Enrico Rosseni is having an affair with one of his lovely
young students. While on an afternoon outing the two lovers learn that a fellow
student is brutally murdered mere yards from them. Now other female students

like a suspect.
