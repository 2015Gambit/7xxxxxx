The Rembrandts are an American pop/rock duo formed by Phil Solem and Danny Wilde in 1989. They had previously worked together as members of Great Buildings in 1981. The Rembrandts are best known for their song 'I'll Be There for You', which was used as the theme song for the television sitcom Friends  (wikipedia)
