This wonderful rural smalltown romantic comedy is from the early part
of Dino Risi's long career, and it feels like a film of the generation 
before him. It is obviously very different from the comedies of the 
'60s and '70s, in which Risi excelled in holding up a mirror to the times 
with a satiric smile. The film was based on a novel by Pasquale Festa 
Campanile, and Risi had a star-studded cast at his disposal: Peppino 
De Filioppo, Sylva Koscina, Renato Salvatori, Paolo Stoppa, Tina Pica 
and Renato Rascel.
