Chinese Martial Arts: From Antiquity to the Twenty-First Century /by Peter Lorge. In the global world of the twenty-first century, martial arts are practiced for self-defense and sporting purposes only. However, for thousands of years, they were a central feature of military practice in China and essential for the smooth functioning of society.
