Full Game Collections: Nintendo 64
==================================

This torrent contains the full collection of N64 roms, according to the
No-Intro database, dated 2012-01-13.

Contents:

  roms/                  All N64 roms, torrentzip'ed
  data/                  Files used to build this collection and other info
    N64_have.txt         List of games in the collection
    *.dat                Corresponding No-Intro datfile

My apologies to those who expected a goodmerge'd torrent. Although using
GoodMerge produces significantly smaller files, it also makes it harder to
keep your collections up-to-date, and sharing them. For now, I'm giving the
torrentzip approach a try.

  About torrentzip
  ----------------
  General guide: http://mametitles.co.uk/tz/torrentzip.pdf
  
  Using this tool, it's easier to update your ROM collections, and share them
  electronically. A zip file processed with TorrentZip is always the same,
  byte-for-byte, independently of the host machine used. That means you can
  easily consume torrents with torrentzip'ed files to update your local
  collections, and even start sharing immediately using your local files. Please
  use it to improve the  health of the torrent.
