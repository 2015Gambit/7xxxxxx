For english-only speaking:
This book is in german language. English title would be: With swastika to the Moon - The suppressed truth about the Third reich's secret space program and its successors (2008). Friedrich Georg documents how the Third reich had its own secret space program and how the Germans launched satellites years before Sputnik ever circled the Earth. Was the first man in space in reality a German? Where did all the know-how for project Apollo come from and why has there been no such effort to send a man to space after it? Find out how all today's superpowers are using technological breakthroughs that really stem from the massive R&D projects during World war 2 done secretly all around Germany. Has the secret space program behind the official one continued to this very day? 290 pages.
