Window Adjustment is a simple window management for OS X.

Features:

* Shrink to Left

* Shrink to Right

* Shrink to Top

* Shrink to Bottom

* Maximize, Full Screen

* Shrink to Center

* Shrink to Top Left

* Shrink to Top Right

* Shrink to Bottom Left

* Shrink to Bottom Right

* Open at Login.
