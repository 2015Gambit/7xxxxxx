- Release Notes ------------------------------------------------------------- -

  I never thought I'd hear Silverstein covering The Swarm. Or Orchid. Especially 
  right after making a toast for the douchebags, assholes, scumbags and running
  away... though that cover did sort of rule.

  Here's to Silverstein's 22 entries in 1999's "Short Songs For Short People"
  comp.. none of which made it on. 

  Anyways, here's Silverstein introducing the kids to what 16 year olds used to
  listen to back in our day. Like the 7" in 2011, this is a great choice of
  bands to cover, and the new songs are pretty great too. Overall, it's good
  nostalgic fun. A lot of the kids will hate it.. which makes it worth picking
  up.
