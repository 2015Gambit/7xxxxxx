Skins S06e08: Liv 

Liv has been partying non-stop since her new best friend Alex came to Bristol, but after he goes away during the weekend when she needs him the most, Liv seeks comfort in all her other friends, only to find out they have excluded her from their lives. Without anyone to rely on, Liv is forced to face school alone, and discovers she might not be as strong as she thinks she is. Nick and Alo are absent from this episode.
