Grand Master Chess - chess simulator is designed for beginners, a wide range of chess fans and players of high level.

The game is designed not only to these grandmasters, but the most inexperienced players, only started to learn the wonderful world of chess. A lot of game settings allow everyone to create the unique atmosphere of intellectual combat. Different versions of chess - the standard chess, chess Fischer, playing blind - could challenge even for experienced chess players.

An excellent workout for the brain in the form of the game has a lot of settings, sets of plates and figures, the ability to save games. Also, it has tips for beginners and multi level undo moves. So imagine yourself sitting in front of the famous chess player and make your first move!
