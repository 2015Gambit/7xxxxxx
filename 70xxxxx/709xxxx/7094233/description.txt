Porsche Panamera

Technology Documentary hosted by Tony Hirst, published by National Geographic broadcasted as part of NG Megafactories series in 2012 - English narration


Information
------------------------------

Megafactories
Lifts the lid on how millions of your everyday products and iconic designs begin life on the assembly line. From bottles of beer to the latest video games to souped-up supercars, follow every cog and conveyor belt of these fascinating production processes as you enter some of the planet's most impressive factories.
Megafactories: Porsche Panamera
Explore the state-of-the-art $200million factory where the controversial four-door Panamera that divided sports car fans worldwide is made.
