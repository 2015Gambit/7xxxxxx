Georges Brassens, 22 October 1921 - 29 October 1981), was a French singer-songwriter and poet. Brassens composed about 250 songs, of which 200 were recorded, the other 50 remaining unfinished. His songs have a major influence on many French singers across several generations, including Maxime Le Forestier, Renaud, Benabar and others. (wikipedia)
