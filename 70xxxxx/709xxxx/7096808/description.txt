The Default Location for Photoshop Brushes in Win 7 is :  "C:Program FilesAdobe

Adobe Photoshop CS5.1PresetsBrushes"


So, whenever, you have to install Brushes in Photoshop, Copy / Paste them in 

"C:Program FilesAdobeAdobe Photoshop CS5.1PresetsBrushes"


Within Photoshop you can assess Brushes from    Windows - Brush ( or by pressing 

F5) , which will open the Brush Palette.
