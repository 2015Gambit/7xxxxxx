Fans and critics agree, this 1991 concert, "Rolling Stones at the Max" is the ultimate opportunity to experience the Rolling Stones at their best. The concert was filmed as the "Steel Wheels" tour made stops in London, Berlin and Turin, and it boasts fifteen songs and an extraordinary stage set. This special presentation was originally shot in the IMAX format and will be broadcast in true HD on HDNet.  

Fans will experience all of their favorite Stones songs including, "Start Me Up," "Sad Sad Sad," "Tumbling Dice," "Ruby Tuesday," "Rock and a Hard Place," "Honky Tonk Woman," "You Can't Always Get What You Want," "Happy," "Paint It Black," "2000 Light Years from Home," "Sympathy for the Devil," "Street Fighting Man," "It's Only Rock 'n' Roll," "Brown Sugar," and "(I Can't Get No) Satisfaction."
