2003 - Acadia / 2057 / CD

The Sons Of Champlin - Loosen Up Naturally

This album is the Holy Grail for fans of this band.
They are one of the best rock bands with horn sections that
surfaced from the San Francisco Area music scene
of the late 1960's along with other bands like
Cold Blood, Tower of Power and The Electric Flag.

01 - 1982-A

04 - Rooftop
05 - Everywhere

07 - Get High
08 - Black And Blue Rainbow
09 - Hello Sunlight
10 - Things Are Gettin' Better
11 - Freedom
