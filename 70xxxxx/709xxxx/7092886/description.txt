Summer of Hate

Sociopolitical, Technology Documentary hosted by John Robinson, published by Discovery Channel broadcasted as part of DC Forensic Factor series in 2005 - English narration


Information
------------------------------

The murder of a gay couple in rural California has police stymied - who would want to hurt the well-like pair? As investigators and forensic scientists dig deeper, they suspect the killings are linked to a series of arsons that have destroyed several synagogues and an abortion clinic. The trail leads them to the sinister world of hate crimes - and a chilling plan that must be stopped.
