Savage Garden was an Australian pop rock performance and songwriting duo. Darren Hayes (vocals) and Daniel Jones (keyboards, sequencing, guitar) formed the group in Brisbane, Queensland in 1994. Their international success occurred between 1997 and 2001, including number one hit singles with 'Truly Madly Deeply', 'I Want You', 'To the Moon and Back', 'The Animal Song' and 'I Knew I Loved You'. Their first two albums, Savage Garden and Affirmation reached number one in Australia and peaked in the top ten in both United Kingdom and United States. (wikipedia)
