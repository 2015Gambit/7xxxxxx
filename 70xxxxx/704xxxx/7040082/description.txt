The Incredible Hulk (2008)

Dr. Bruce Banner, thanks to a gamma ray experiment gone wrong, 
transforms into a giant green-skinned hulk whenever his pulse rate 
gets too high. Meanwhile, a soldier uses the same technology to 
become an evil version of the original.

Director: Louis Leterrier
Writers: Zak Penn (screenplay), Zak Penn (screen story)
Stars: Edward Norton, Liv Tyler and Tim Roth
