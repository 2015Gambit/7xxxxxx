Saxon Hoard: A Golden Discovery

History Documentary hosted by Dan Snow, published by BBC in 2012 - English narration


Information
------------------------------

Documentary in which Dan Snow travels across the old Kingdom of Mercia to unravel the secrets of one of Britian's most significant discoveries - the Staffordshire Hoard. The Hoard offers new insight into the Dark Ages, and Dan pieces together the lives of the people living in these long-forgotten kingdoms.
