King of Hearts is the first single by Cassie for her second studio album. The song followed unsuccessful attempts such as Official Girl. The song is a departure from her previous Urban theme and focuses more on the dance beat of 2012.

The song has a video on Vevo as well as gainer over 600 thousand listeners in a week. The song is produced from Bad Boy Records.
