For english-only speaking:
This book is in german language. English title would be: Behind the doors of power - An intelligence officer's story (2002). Armin Herold brings forward the little known story of Bundesnachrichtendienst (BND) - the German CIA. Starting from Gehlen's intelligence agency the author, himself a BND officer, offers first-hand knowledge of the inside workings and evolution of one of the biggest intelligence agencies in the world and its relations with other similar agencies around the globe. 310 pages.
