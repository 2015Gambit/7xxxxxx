English | 116 pages | PDF | 88 Mb


Outdoor Photographer Us Magazine is a publication which is devoted to bringing the reader all the latest news and product reviews on photography equipment, which is particularly good for use in the great outdoors. There will be helpful articles on the best new software, lenses and travel destinations with the best opportunities for photography. There will also be stunning images with additional information on the camera and equipment used, so readers can try to recreate the striking images themselves.
