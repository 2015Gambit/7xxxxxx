Go on a truly magical adventure to save a beautiful island from tragic devastation! The island has been haunted by an evil spirit who has left a trail of disaster in the form of horrible spiny plants. Now you need to try and restore the now repulsive island back to its former glory!

Pick up trash and remove the polluting plants, while planting wonderful vegetation in its place. Watch out for other magical fairies that may need your help in Mother Nature! A really miraculous adventure!
