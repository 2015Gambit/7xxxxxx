Dustforce (2011) PC [ENG]

Description:
Clean up the world with style! As an acrobatic janitor, you are an adept force against dust and disorder. Leap and dash off walls and ceilings,
and deftly traverse precarious environments. Cleanse each level swiftly and thoroughly to achieve mastery in this 2D sweep-'em-up platformer.

Features:
- Over 50 levels varying in difficulty from elegant exploration levels to intense challenges for hardcore players.
- Compete on the online leaderboard and instantly watch replays of every players' best attempts.
- Play against your friends with local multiplayer matches.
- Roam through a myriad of beautiful environments accompanied by an original soundtrack.

System requirements:
- Windows XP/Vista/7
- DirectX 9c
- 2 GHz
- 1 GB RAM
