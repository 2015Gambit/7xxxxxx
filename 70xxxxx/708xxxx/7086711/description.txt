Title: Sinking Island
Genre: adventure / puzzle-solving / 3rd-person
Released: 4th October 2007
Developer: White Birds Productions
Publisher: Big Fish Games
Description:
Walter Jones, an eccentric billionaire, is found dead on the Maldivian island of Sagorah. There, he had built an enormous art deco tower that he hoped to turn into an exclusive holiday resort. His death seems like a nightly accident at first, but turns out to be a mysterious murder. Furthermore, a heavy storm is raging, almost cutting off the island from the rest of the world. At the time of Walter Jones' death, there were eight people staying in his tower. As inspector Jack Norm, you have to investigate which of the suspects might have a reason to want Walter Jones dead, talk to everybody, collect evidence and compare fingerprints. This adventure game will require you to use all your wits to solve the mystery and find the murderer!

Bonus content: artworks, interview with Benoit Sokal (French audio), soundtrack, strategy guide, wallpapers.

How to play:
1) Download the game;
2) Mount or burn disc image;
3) Run setup.exe to install;
4) Play the game without the disc using main executable or shortcuts.
