1902 high quality color photos by Sergey Prokudin-Gorsky.
Taken between 1905-1915 in Russia.
These are all in the highest resolution available at http://www.loc.gov/pictures/collection/prok/
Meaning all the TIFF-files from there have been converted to high quality JPEG with no resolution scaling.

Aside from this, 122 digital rendered photos of these are included, in the same quality.

To browse:
A nice selection: http://www.boston.com/bigpicture/2010/08/russia_in_color_a_century_ago.html
