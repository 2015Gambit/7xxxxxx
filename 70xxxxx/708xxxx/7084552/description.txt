Serious Sam musics.

Musics from the great Croteam games:

Serious Sam First Encounter,
Serious Sam Second Encounter,
Serious Sam II,
Serious Sam BFE.

The musics has simply been extracted from the game data,
sorted and renamed.

The new names sort the musics by level and intensity.

I tried to make an order that allows the greatest
enjoyment; just as a wave musics start slow-placed and
become intense and more intense to return slow-paced
and different.

Enjoy your dose of Serious Sam even while working.
