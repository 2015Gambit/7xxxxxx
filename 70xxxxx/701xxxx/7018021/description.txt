Lonely Are the Brave is the debut studio album by Irish singer Maverick Sabre. It will be released on 27 January 2012 in Ireland and 6 February 2012 in the United Kingdom. The album includes the singles "Let Me Go" and "I Need". The album entered the Irish Albums Chart at number 3.
