Your name is Hermann Ronalds, you live in your mansion in Prussia, and things have gone terribly, terribly wrong. The Turn is a full conversion mod for Amnesia: The Dark Descent, following the adventures of Hermann Ronalds. It will be connected to an alternate universe from Amnesia: The Dark Descent, with connections to the original story, and also connects to the story of the Penumbra series(Kind of).
