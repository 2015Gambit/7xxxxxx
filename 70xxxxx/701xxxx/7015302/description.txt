ALLinONE for 1.3PB6 was built to allow the easy installation of most all Battlezone II 1.3PB6 community content, which includes mods, maps and updates compatible with Battlezone II 1.3PB6.2.
ALLinONE for 1.3PB6.2 includes:
1.3PB6.2 Beta Patch | Custom Shortcuts to mods (created on desktop & c:\games\1.3\battlezone II\Shortcuts - ALLinONE\) | Newkeys | 1.3 Strategy Map Pack | Epic Mod | Fleshstorm 2 Mod | Forgotten Enemies Mod | G66 v3 Mod | GGsE Mod | QF Mod | RaceProject Mod | Uler Mod | ZE Mod | ZTV v3
