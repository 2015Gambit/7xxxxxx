For english-only speaking:
This book is in german language. English title would be: The Secret Knowledge of Gold Investors (2007). Bruno Bandulet, an insider of gold markets, writes the story of money and power. Written for gold investors that either already have or would like to have gold and would like to know all the important things about it. At the same time the author connects the dots between currencies and financial systems with plenty of behind-the-scenes stories about money and gold. For many gold is a mystery but for Bandulet it's his home. 280 pages.
