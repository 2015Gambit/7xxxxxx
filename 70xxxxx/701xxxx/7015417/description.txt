But Simoncelli has left a void in the fans that follow the World Championship but also in those who, although not motorcycle enthusiasts, have appreciated the joviality and desire to live this italian boy.  
The cameras have followed live in Coriano the funeral of ace of MotoGP opened its microphones to all the fans, the rivals, close friends and family of "Sic" bringing a last greeting all the fans and the fans who have always followed the career of the young champion.
