Windows Registry is the central database of a computer. The database stores all the information related to your hardware, software, files etc. If the registry is damaged, the operating system files can become corrupt and many things may stop functioning. Even the operating system may stop working entirely, rendering it unusable. As you continue to use your computer, the registry keeps on growing. Installing software and hardware keeps on adding to the registry. Even renaming a document or creating a shortcut creates entries in the registry, thereby making it more and more complicated and bulkier. Adding to the worse uninstalling programs or hardware removes the program from the machine but does not remove all the entries created earlier in the registry. This makes registry cluttered and fragmented. The growing size of registry slows down PC performance and can eventually result in computer freezing and even system crashes. Therefore, it is of utmost importance to have a clean and trimmed registry for achieving optimum performance from your computer.

RegClean Pro is a simple to use utility which scans, detects and fixes invalid registry entries in Windows. It scans the odd areas of the registry to identify the obsolete entries which are left behind during regular usage of a computer. RegClean Pro also finds fragments scattered all over the registry. Fragments are nothing but registry blocks that store no data but still occupy space as it maintains reference to the next block. Removal and repair of such entries results in a compact and cleaner registry, enabling your computer to run faster, smoother and in a more stable manner.
