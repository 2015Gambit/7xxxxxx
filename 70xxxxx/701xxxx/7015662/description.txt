"Masterpiece" is a song recorded by American singer-songwriter Madonna. 

It was composed by Madonna, Julie Frost and Jimmy Harry and produced by Madonna and William Orbit. The song was created for the film, W.E. (2012) and included on the soundtrack album released by Interscope Records. 

The song won the Golden Globe Award for Best Original Song at the 69th Golden Globe Awards.

"Masterpiece" is played over the end credits of the film and will be included on her twelfth studio album, MDNA, whose release was confirmed to be in March 2012 by Madonna's manager Guy Oseary.
