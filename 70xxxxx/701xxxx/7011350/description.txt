IMPORTANT:
This season of Justice League was produced open matte 4:3 by the creative team, with the intention of cropping the top & bottom for widescreen presentation. (It was presented this way on select "widescreen presentations" on Cartoon Network. The creators discuss this in their commentary for episode 7, starting at about 2:25.) So, this encode preserves the creator's intended ratio by presenting these episodes in 16:9 (1280x720). 

PLAYBACK:
I have encoded & tagged the files for playback in iTunes and on Apple devices (iPad, iPhone 4, etc) that can handle HD video, including *both* versions of the AppleTV set top box. I've tested playback within iTunes on Macs running Lion, Snow Leopard and Leopard, as well as PCs running Windows 7 and Vista.

I've included two English subtitle tracks - the first with Hearing Impaired info stripped out, the second with the HI information left in - within each M4V file. Both tracks are included externally as .srt files, as well.
