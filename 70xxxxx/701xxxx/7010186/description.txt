

Manos Hadjidakis (October 23, 1925 â€“ June 15, 1994) was an Academy Award-winning Greek composer. He was born in Xanthi.
In 1960 he received an Academy Award for Best Original Song for his Song 'Never on Sunday' from the film of the same name.
He is widely popular among Greeks and can be credited with the introduction of bouzouki music into mainstream culture.
He died on June 15, 1994, aged 68, from heart disease and diabetes. (wikipedia)
