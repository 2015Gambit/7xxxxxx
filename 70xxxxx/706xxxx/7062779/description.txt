Until today, Brynn only attended private school with all her rich little girlfriends. That's why she's such a good well behaved girl. Unfortunately, she now has to go to a regular school. On her first day, she found herself in the class of Miss Alexander. Turned on by the arrival of this new bird in her class, Miss Alexander, with the help of her student Xander, decided to welcome her with the kind of private lesson that only public schools can provide. Brynn will not regret her new school.
