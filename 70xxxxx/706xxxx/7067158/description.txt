A FEW WORDS ABOUT THIS ENCODE:
While the video bit rate on this file is pretty high (at least by my encoding standards), DO NOT expect a pristine, reference-quality image-- this film was not restored prior to Blu-ray release. If you click on the screenshots, you'll see that the film betrays its age (25+ years) and low-budget origin with dust, hairs, and rough line work. This is the best it's EVER looked, but don't expect Lion King-like video quality.

There are 2 commentaries tracks: one with director Nelson Shin, writer Flint Dille and voice actor Susan Blu; another with a group of fans assembled by Hasbro.
