Description:
A young couple spends their first honeymoon. They are enjoying each other, making love, where possible. In one of the towns they attracted the attention of a mansion, and they decide to stay in it. The inhabitants of the villas make them, quite literally, with open arms. In this house, even the walls exude a pungent aroma of lust.
In this somptuous mansion with huge rooms richly decorated, Babeth is soon going to discover women and men with strange behaviors: high society women in nice lingerie or dressed in leather offer themselves to their masters in total obedience.
