PegasusMir is a MMORPG Based in an Oriental Midieval world. Choose to be The Strong Warrior facing your enemies toe to toe with a variety of up close melee weapons, or perhaps the Wise wizard casting spells from afar wreaking havock on your enemies. Perhaps A Taoist is more your style, a priest class that can cast healing ind protection spells on their party, while cursing and doing spell damage to opposition, while being a fair warrior in their own right. Whatever you choose you are sure to have fun in this fun and free to play game. Join us @ PegasusMir.com
