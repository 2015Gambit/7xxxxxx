Orbitron Revolution RIP-Unleashed

Launch into a relentless battle against a robotic horde bent on the destruction of an Orbital Power 
Station. Blast your way through three intense game modes with one of two selectable space fighters.
Addicting, classic shoot em up gameplay, built upon a uniquely modern structure, featuring beautiful
3d graphics, and a soundtrack by Audio Antics await you in Orbitron: Revolution!

Publisher: Firebase Industries
Developer: Firebase
Genre: Shooter

Install Notes:
         
1. Install by running unl-orre.exe 
2. Start game with Orbitron Revolution.exe
3. Enjoy!
