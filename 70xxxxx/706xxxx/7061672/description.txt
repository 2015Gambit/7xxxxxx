

Artist: Lolek

Year: 2009

Quality : flac (lossless)

Tracks:
1. Have You Noticed?
2. I Went to the Beach Alone
3. Since I Am a Soldier
4. These Guns
5. River of Diseases
6. A Valse of a True Romance
7. Now Cry!
8. Alone With Lucy
9. Oh Mama

Lolek is the solo project of Giannis Anagnostatos from Athens, Greece. Alone is his debut album released from the great Greek label Inner Ear records. 
It is an album full of melancholic melodies and nostalgic atmosphere. A fantastic debut! Highly recommended!
