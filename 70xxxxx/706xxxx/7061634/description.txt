Description
The #1 mobile snowboard game franchise in the US with more than 7 million players now comes to your desktop!

Get ready for some wicked-cool 3D snowboarding action on your Mac! Crazy Snowboard has something for everyone:

- 3D graphics
- 30 missions
- 13 riders to unlock
- 13 boards to unlock
- 16 in-air tricks to unlock
- In-air trick system: spin, flip and grab!
- Grinding: rails, boxes, logs, candycanes!
- Smashable obstacles: zombies, snowmen, elves!
- Intuitive controls - really feels like you are boarding!
- Quick pick-up-and-play
- 4 Endless free-ride modes
- Hours of challenging game-play

Waiting for a bus? Stuck in line? No matter where you are, now you can hit the slopes! Ready for some massive airs? Just press spacebar and you'll crouch ready to spring. Release on top of a ramp and you'll get a huge boost. Then link your tricks together to increase your multiplier and score big.

How about some grinding? We've got rails, boxes, logs, candycanes (yes, candycanes!) all ready for your grinding pleasure. See how many you can link together to ramp up your bonus multiplier and boost your score.
