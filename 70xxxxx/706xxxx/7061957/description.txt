DJ Shadow (born Joshua Paul Davis, June 29, 1972, Hayward, California) is an American music producer, DJ and songwriter. He is considered a prominent figure in the development of instrumental hip hop and first gained notice with the release of his highly acclaimed debut album Endtroducing....., which was constructed almost entirely from samples.
