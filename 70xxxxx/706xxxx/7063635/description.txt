The Bourne Trilogy is a series of novels by Robert Ludlum, which have been adapted to film in a series starring Matt Damon. 

The original three Jason Bourne novels are:

The Bourne Identity (1980)
The Bourne Supremacy (1986)
The Bourne Ultimatum (1990)
