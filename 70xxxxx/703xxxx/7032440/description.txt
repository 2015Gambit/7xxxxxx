- ARTIST
George Harvey Strait (born May 18, 1952) is an American country music singer, actor, and music producer who is referred to as the "King Of Country,". He is known for his unique style of western swing music, bar-room ballads, honky-tonk ballads style, and fresh yet traditional Country music. George Strait holds the world record for more number-one hit singles than any other artist in the history of music on any chart or in any genre, having recorded 58 number-one hit singles as of 2011.
