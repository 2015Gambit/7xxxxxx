Overview: Photoshop User Magazine is the official publication from the National Association of Photoshop Professionals (NAPP). It is created for members of the organisation by the other members themselves. Photoshop User is entirely devoted to helping readers improve their skills when using Photoshop and all the programs which are associated with it. There will be features on in-depth tutorials, news, reviews on the latest programs and how to illustrate and use designs more effectively.
