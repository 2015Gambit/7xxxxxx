


Easily create standard and slim covers for CD, DVD, VHS, Blue Ray, Playstation, PS1, PS2, PS3, PSP, xBox, Nintendo Wii, etc.



DVD Slim Free is a small and easy to use application that allows you to quickly create and print covers.

It allows you to print traditional covers for CDs, DVD, VHS, Blue Ray, Playstation Portable, PS1, PS2, PS3, PSP, Xbox, Nintendo Wii, Standard Disc Labels, Mini-discs, and something completely new, can transform a standard cover in a 14 mm SLIM 7 or 9 mm and 5 mm even SLIM SOFT, scaling proportionally individual items! 


The software is free.
