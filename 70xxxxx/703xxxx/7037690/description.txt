Dream Theater - "A Walk Beside the Band"
International Fan Clubs DVD 2005 
NTSC Format

This is a 2 hour long documentary produced as a joint effort by the international Dream Theater fan clubs, and made available only to fan club members.  It was recorded in September and October of 2005 in various countries on the European leg of the Octavarium tour.  

It features exclusive backstage footage and interviews with band members, fans, and selected live performances of full songs.  This is the full original DVD in NTSC format (no re-encoding of files).  

To watch on a Windows PC, open the file VIDEO_TS.IFO in Windows Media Player.  On a Mac, in DVD Player go to File - Open DVD Media and select the VIDEO_TS folder.  Or of course, burn as a video DVD and watch in any DVD player.
