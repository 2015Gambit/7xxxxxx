Realtime Skype translator

Clownfish is an online translator for all your outgoing messages in Skype. Now you could write on your native language and the recipient will receive translated message. There are different translations services you could choose from.

Build-in spell check support ( OpenOffice compatible). Text-to-speech support for all incoming messages.

The application also comes with support for sending predefined ASCII or emoticon drawings.
