- ARTIST
Troyal Garth Brooks (born February 7, 1962 in Tulsa, Oklahoma), is an American artist who helped make country music a worldwide phenomenon. Brooks has enjoyed one of the most successful careers in popular music history, breaking records for both sales and concert attendance throughout the 1990s. His world-wide sales now exceed 166 million units.
Brooks officially retired from recording and performing from 2001 until 2009.
He announced the end of his retirement in 2009, when he signed a 5 year concert deal with the Encore Hotel and Casino on the Las Vegas Strip.  [from Wikipedia]
