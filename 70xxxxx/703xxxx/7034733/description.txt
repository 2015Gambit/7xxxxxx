Feature, Spoofs & Parodies
---------------------------

Starring: Lexi Belle, Chanel Preston, Breanne Benson, Ash Hollywood, Nicole Aniston, Rocco Reed, Anthony Rosano, Xander Corvus, Tommy Pistol


Experience the Pop-Culture of the 80's like never before. The music, the dancing and the leg warmers are all back. And so is the welding, the water dancing, the conflicts and inspiration. But this time the leg warmers are coming off and everyone gets soaking wet. Oh, what a feeling!
