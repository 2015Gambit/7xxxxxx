True Ingredients are cooking up a musical revolution. With some of the freshest sounds around they are also the first band to release their debut album on sunglasses. The album \"Prepare and Assemble\" is available in ten magnificent colours from www.trueingredients.com and selected retailers.

The album features the vocal talents of Mr Milk, Aina and BluRum13, three amazingly talented lyricists and performers who take us on a journey through true music laced with hip hop, introducing various vocalists and musicians on the way. The single \"Who\'s Next?\" also features Natty, Mr Basista and special guest musicians...
