                            Nicki Minaj - Starships [MP3 2012]



Title : Starships
Artist : Nicki Minaj
Album : Starships (Single)
Year : 2012
Coverart : Included


Starships is the official lead single from rapper Nicki Minaj's second studio album, Roman Reloaded. The single follows several promotional songs including Stupid Hoe and Roman In Moscow. The song is a pop upbeat electronica track, similar to Super Bass and affirms Nicki's stay in the pop industry.

The single was released 2012, following a performance at the Grammy Awards. The song has a simple cover and will be accompanied by a music video.
