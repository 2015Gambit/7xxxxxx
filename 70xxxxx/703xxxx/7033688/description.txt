Yppah, a reversal of the word happy, pronounced yippah, started out as Texas native Joe Corrales solo electronic project before evolving into a full-fledged rock outfit. Roping in Chemical Brothers beats, 60s psychedelic, post-rock, and shoegaze, the result is an atmospheric, soothing soundscape that smoothly jumps from style to style.
