Ellie Pop - Ellie Pop (1968/2005)

Genre: US, Psychedelic Pop Rock

A never before reissued album from 1968 by this little known Anglophile US pop/psike/folk band. Fresh breezy folky psych pop originally released on the highly collectable Mainstream label, also the home to Superfine Dandelion, Jelly Bean Bandits, Tangerine Zoo, Growing Concern and Amboy Dukes.
