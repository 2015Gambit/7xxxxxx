Five Years of "Only An Excuse" from BBC Scotland

2008,2009,2010,2011,2012 episodes inclusive:

2012 Episode Guide:

Football's premier comedy team returns with its traditional New Year helping of satire, looking back on a year that saw Rangers enter administration and re-emerge in the Third Division, and Scotland manager Craig Levein being relieved of his duties in November after a terrible start to World Cup qualifying. Starring Jonathan Watson, Gerry McLaughlin, Julie Austin and Chris Forbes
