Plot - 
'Satya Savitri Aani Satyavan' reminds all of us about the famous mythological tale of Savitri, who did her best to save her husband- Satyavan from the clutches of death. But, today , can we find a Savitri ? That's what the film poses a question to the audience? The greater the power, the more dangerous the abuse. That's what the film has tried to convey. The director of the film- Sarvesh Parab, has claimed that it's an original story , inspired by the Marathi one act play 'Smrutipuja'.
