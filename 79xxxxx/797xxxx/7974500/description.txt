Northlanders Book 2: The Cross and the Hammer

Genre - Action/Adventure

English | CBR | 6 Issues

Written by Brian Wood
Art by Ryan Kelly
Cover by Massimo Carnevale

The second NORTHLANDERS collection, takes place during the tail end of Viking rule in Ireland. A series of mysterious murders and arsons against wealthy citizens leaves the Viking occupiers worried that a potential uprising might ignite. When surprising details involving the crimes are revealed, though, their jobs become much harder!
