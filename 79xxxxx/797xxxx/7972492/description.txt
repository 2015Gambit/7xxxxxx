This album features a number of big names, whether session musicians or stars of the French music scene. For arrangements, Aznavour called Eumir Deodato (500 albums recorded, including some for Frank Sinatra, Aretha Franklin, Astrud Gilberto, Björk or Christophe) and Yvan Cassar (Mylene Farmer, Johnny Hallyday, Claude Nougaro or Nusrat Fateh Ali Khan); and disc travels from Paris to Brazil (as in "Ce printemps-là", or the last two tracks), to Broadway, and even by Spain "Flamenco, flamenco". Aznavour's voice and her unmatched ability of interpretation remain intact, and luckily we still offers gems like this record.
