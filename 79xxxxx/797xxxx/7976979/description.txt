Writer: Pradeep Tungare. 

Director: Nikhil Mehendale

Synopsis: This is a powerful play presenting the meaning of life and death through the eyes of people working in a Crematorium. It is the last day of work for Kardile who is retiring from his duty of incinerating corpse's at the Crematorium. Joshi and More want to celebrate the occasion and the symmetry's Guruji is on the way to bid farewell to Kardile. In his entire career, Kardile has burned 12,994 bodies and this being his last day, Joshi and More want him to make a record of 13,000. As the day passes the four men face the tragedies of the past and question their existence while a group of protesters revolt to shut down the Crematorium. How does the day end in the crematorium? Can Kardile make the record?
