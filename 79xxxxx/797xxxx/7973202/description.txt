 
How is the internet changing the way you think? That is one of the dominant questions 

of our time, one which affects almost every aspect of our life and future. And it's 

exactly what John Brockman, publisher of Edge.org, posed to more than 150 of the 

world's most influential minds. Brilliant, farsighted, and fascinating, Is the 

Internet Changing the Way You Think? is an essential guide to the Net-based world.
