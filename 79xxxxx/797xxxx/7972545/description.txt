50 of the most obscure songs and sought-after from the original era of Rockabilly!
Two Discs packed with rockin' rarities released on small American labels of the 1950s, many of which have never before been released on CD.
Featuring Glenn Honeycutt, Jimmy Patton, Don Woody, Carl Mann, Jimmy Lloyd, Joey Castle, Marvin Rainwater, Don Willis, Nat Couty, Benny Joy, Benny Ingram, Ric Cartey, Luke McDaniel, Bobby Roberts, Bill Allen and many others.
