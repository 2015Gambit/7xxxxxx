Story:
PS Officer Abhiraj Roy a.k.a Abhi (Dev) is a daring super-cop in Mumbai. He is on a mission to apprehend a mafia don Naik (Ashish Vidyarthi), who is involved in illegal drug trade, extortion and arms trafficking. His father (Tapas Paul), who was a politician driven by idealistic values and a sense of social service, met with a fatal accident. It is later revealed that his father actually survived the accident but ended up in a state of coma. However, this truth is hidden by their family from the people. Abhi maintains a low profile to catch that mafia don. In an undercover operation, Abhi gets hold of the mafia don's brother (Surajit Sen). Later, it is revealed that one corrupt opposition leader was the mastermind behind his father's accident.
