Simple Projects You Can Make at Home
By Electronics for You magazine
EFY Enterprises | May 2011 | English | ISBN: ISBN 978-81-88152-24-7 | PDF | 302 pages | 24 MB

This book, a collection of hardware-based projects which appeared in Electronics For You from year 1979 to 2004, is brought out for the benefit of our new readers. It is a compilation of 60 construction projects tested at EFY Lab. Some projects also cover testing steps, equivalent part names of some hard to find components and lead/pin identification details of semiconductor devices and integrated circuits (ICs).
