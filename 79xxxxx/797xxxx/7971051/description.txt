Match Of The Day ΓÇô Saturday 29th December 2012

 Gary Lineker presents highlights of the day s eight Premier League games.

   Manchester City travel to Norwich, a side they fired 11 goals past last term,
including six at Carrow Road during their title-clinching, six-game winning
run at the end of the season. Manchester United take on West Brom,
looking to continue their record of never having lost to the Baggies 
during the Premier League era.

  Arsenal play host to Newcastle, a side they have beaten only once in
four attempts since the Magpies returned to the Premier League, 
while Reading welcome West Ham to the Madejski Stadium in a 
battle of two of last season s promoted clubs.
