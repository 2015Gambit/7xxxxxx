Condemned for a crime he didn't commit, Scott Landus is sentenced to life without parole on Colony, a galactic devil's island where the fascist Galactic Confederacy disposes of its human and alien refuse.

Over ten years in the making! Colony is based on the popular web comic created by legendary Iron Man co-author/artist Bob Layton and drawn by the late, great Batman illustrator, Dick Giordano.
