Swedish subtitle track is marked as disabled so compliant videoplayers shouldn't show it by default.

Still, if subtitles do bother you, you can simply get rid of them by removing the text stream from the MPEG-4 file.


Use mp4box from command prompt:


> mp4box -rem 3 "Mysteriet på Greveholm - Grevens återkomst - 17 - Ägg i fara.mp4"


If you need separate subtitle files you can simply extract them with:


> mp4box -srt 3 "Mysteriet på Greveholm - Grevens återkomst - 17 - Ägg i fara.mp4"
