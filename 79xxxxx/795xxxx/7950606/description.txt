Match of the Day 22.12.2012

Gabby Logan introduces the best of the action from Saturday's Premier League matches, with studio guest Alan Hansen.

There is a clash of the champions at the Etihad as Roberto Mancini's Premier League winners, Manchester City, take on Brian McDermott's Reading, who won last season's Championship.

Arsenal travel to Wigan, where they enjoyed a convincing 4-0 win last year, while Martin Jol's Fulham visit Anfield looking for a repeat of May's victory against Liverpool.

Elsewhere, Newcastle host QPR, Southampton face Sunderland, Tottenham take on Stoke, Norwich travel to West Brom and West Ham play Everton.
