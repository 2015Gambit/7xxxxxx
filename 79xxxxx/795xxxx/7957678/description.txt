The guitar that Eric Clapton used during the sessions was a sunburst 1960 Gibson Les Paul Standard with two PAF pickups. Claptons innovative style of playing on this LP was nothing short of brilliant and influenced many young guitarists for years.

Vinyl: Decca SLK 16 547-P
Turtable: Bang and Olufsen Beogram 2000
Cartridge: Bang and Olufsen MMC4
