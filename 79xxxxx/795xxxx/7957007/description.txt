HiDownload compact and affordable software for managing your downloads. This software allows you to upload a file or list of files to download at the highest speeds.
At the same time to save time. 
- Supported protocols HTTP, FTP, MMS / Mmst / Mmsu, RTSP, PNM. 
- Supports file sharing and uploading sites rapidshare, megauplad, hotfile, fileserve and ... 
- the ability to record video and audio online. 
- view the contents of zip files before downloading. 
- Download and extract individual files from the archive zip.

Support Meta-files ram, Smil, asx, wax, Wvx, m3u, pls, ... 
- Resume and restart it after the download stops for any reason. 
- Check to prevent users from downloading malicious files virus. 
- Full compatibility with all browsers and monitor them. 
- low volume and high-speed applications. 
- Support cookies.
