Buying a home can be stressful and overwhelming, especially if you do not know the ins and outs of real estate. This book provides easy-to-understand answers to some of the most common questions that home buyers have.

Topics discussed include:

--Finding property
--Figuring out how much a house in worth
--Preparing an offer
--Financing 
--Laws that apply to real estate
--Tax issues

The Home Buyer's Answer Book is a quick, authoritative reference for consumers who want immediate answers to their most pressing home-buying questions.
