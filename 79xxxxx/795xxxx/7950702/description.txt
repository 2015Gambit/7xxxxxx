Escrito e dirigido pelos criadores de SHANK. Father Jack Gillie (Daniel Brocklebank) é condenado por um crime. Seus companheiros acreditam que ele foi condenado por pedofilia e começam a plantar essa ideia na mente do adolescente e companheiro de cela, Torre (Wayne Virgem). Após salvar Torre de uma briga, Jack se torna a presa dos presos. A proteção vem na forma improvável de um funcionário da prisão, Martin (Garry Summers), com quem Jack se apaixona e juntos vão embarcam num romance perigoso e ilegal. Jack confessa ao amante a verdade por trás do crime pelo qual ele foi preso.

Written and Directed by the creators of SHANK. Father Jack Gillie (Daniel Brocklebank) enters prison a guilty man, convicted for a crime that sees the Church abandon him, his congregation desert him and his faith challenged. His fellow inmates believe he's been convicted of paedophilia and begin to plant the seed of doubt into the mind of his teenage cellmate, Rook (Wayne Virgo). After rescuing Rook from a beating Jack now becomes the inmates prey. Protection comes in the unlikely form of a prison officer, Martin (Garry Summers) with whom Jack falls in love and together they embark on a dangerous and illicit affair behind cell doors. As trust forms between the two men so Jack feels enabled to confess the truth behind the crime for which he has been imprisoned. Emboldened by Jack's honesty the two men plan their lives together post Jack's release.
