Das Boot Documentary

Arts, History Documentary hosted by Wolfgang Petersen, published by Empire in 2011 - English narration


Information
------------------------------

Contains six documentaries that accompany the Wolfgang Petersen film Das Boot.
The film itself isn't included.


1)  Maria's Take (00:09:13) 
Assistant director Maria Petersen describes her role in the making of the film.

CRF=18

2)  The Perfect Boat - The Director's Cut (00:12:58) 
Writer and director Wolfgang Petersen describes how the Director's Cut was created.

CRF=18

3)  Captain's Tour - Inside the Boat (00:08:19) 
Jürgen Prochnow takes you on a tour inside U96.
Contains 7 parts appended together with chapters.

CRF=18

4)  Behind the Scenes (01:00:16) 
Jürgen Prochnow describes the filming of the special effects.

High@L3.1 640x480 4:3 29.976fps CRF=21

5)  The Battle of the Atlantic (00:40:13) 
Contains early B&W footage of the Kriegsmarine and various interviews including one with the actual captain of U96.

Mainly in German with some English.
960x720 4:3 CRF=24

6)  Back to the Boat (00:44:44) 
Wolfgang Petersen describes the difficulties with filming.

CRF=18.5
