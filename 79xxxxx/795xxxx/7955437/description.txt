Bloons Tower Defense 5 Deluxe is a package for Bloons Tower Defense 5 that was announced on May 30, 2012. It is offline and includes more tracks, better graphics, more towers and Premium Upgrades unlocked by using Monkey Money. It is available for purchase, currently on sale for $14.99 or 150 NK Coins. In total there are 31 tracks. As of December 18, 2012, Bloons TD 5 Deluxe is updated to version 1.0.5.
