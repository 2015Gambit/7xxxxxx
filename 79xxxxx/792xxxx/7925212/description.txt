BEST OF: THE VIDEOS is a one-disc DVD collection that contains a total of 33 commercial single videos that helped jump DEF LEPPARD to success after the release of their breakthrough 1983 album, PYROMANIA. The "Bringin' On the Heartbreak" video featured on the compilation, is the 1984 version.
This DVD collection was released on October 25, 2004 alongside its own compilation CD album.
