Russia's Radical Chic

Sociopolitical Documentary hosted by Marcel Theroux, published by Channel 4 broadcasted as part of CH4 Unreported World series in 2012 - English narration


Information
------------------------------

Glamorous young Russian socialite Ksenia Sobchak has swapped high-profile TV stardom for a life leading political protests against President Putin, who also happens to be a close family friend. Unreported World reveals how far Sobchak is risking her livelihood and privileged lifestyle to confront the strongman of the Kremlin, who has dealt ruthlessly with other political opponents.
