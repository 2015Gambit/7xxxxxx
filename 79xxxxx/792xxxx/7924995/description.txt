I Elena kai i Maria einai dyo ploysiokoritsa, einai poly kales files kai zoyn sti Glyfada. I Elena einai parthena kai pianei tin kolliti tis na pidietai me to agori tis ton Dimitri. Gia na toys ekdikithei gia to farmaki poy tis dosane, dinei tin parthenia tis se dyo perastikoys tsigganoys kai stelnei dyo mpraboys kai biazoyn tin kolliti tis Maria. I moira toys fernei na xanabriskontai sta mpoyzoykia opoy to giortazoyn oloi mazi me enan poly idiaitero tropo.  -
