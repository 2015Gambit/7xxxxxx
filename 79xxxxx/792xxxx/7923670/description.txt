Shooting Iwo Jima

War Documentary hosted by Salvatore Vecchio, published by Smithsonian Channel broadcasted as part of History in HD series in 2008 - English narration


Information
------------------------------

Witness history's most compelling and life-altering events as they've never been seen before. From battlefields to deep space and beyond, History in HD unearths a treasure trove of rare, archival footage, restored in stunning high definition and presented with a modern day perspective.
This touching film introduces Bill Genaust, who captured on film the flag-raising on Iwo Jima. Meet this forgotten hero through his Photo Unit commander, through the last living relative to remember him, and in his forceful, visceral footage of two of the fiercest battles in World War II.
