

I ekdikisi tis nyfis

I Ilektra opos kai kathe timia Ellinida kanei epiteloys to oneiro tis pragmatikotita, pantreyetai ton Dimitri me koymparo ton giatro Ntino. I zoi omos den mas ta fernei panta opos ta programmatizoyme. Mathainei oti dyo meres prin ton gamo tis, o Dimitris sto bachelor parti toy, tin keratose me mia koini... O monadikos tropos gia na pnixei ton thymo tis einai i EKDIKISI...
