The exciting film tells the story of a plastic surgeon Tessa van Asselt. She is the owner of a private clinic and the process of building a successful career. When the renovation of her house, led by her husband Roger, threatening to end in a disaster, her marriage begins to falter and there are business problems, beginning her so perfectly constructed world slowly began to crumble. Then she gets some help from an unexpected source, a former schoolmate, the charming Johan. He promises that he will solve all her problems ...
