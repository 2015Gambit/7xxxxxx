Perhaps I've been watching anime for too long, or maybe my collection of Japanese games has grown too large, but when I heard that the next Armored Core game was being subtitled "for Answer," I honestly didn't flinch. That makes me -- quite possibly -- the only U.S. gamer in existence that didn't laugh at the incredibly Japanese title of From Software's latest mech action game for the PS3 and 360. Yes, I'm entirely aware that adding a single "s" to the whole affair would have broken fewer grammatical rules, but I think Armored Core has a perfectly fine name as is. But to be fair, surreal English/Japanese phrases don't faze me anymore, so...
