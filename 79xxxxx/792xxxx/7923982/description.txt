Issues is a metalcore band from Atlanta, Georgia, formed in the summer of 2012 and were signed to Velocity Records shortly after their formation. The majority of the band's members originated from the ex-members of Woe, Is Me, being Tyler Carter, Michael Bohn, Cory Ferris and Ben Ferris. Their debut EP, Black Diamonds, was released on November 13, 2012.
