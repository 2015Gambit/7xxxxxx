The first book on anxiety written specifically for adults with AS, Asperger Syndrome and Anxiety provides real solutions for the many people who suffer from alienation and anxiety when confronting a world that seems totally foreign to them. Author Nick Dubin, who has been diagnosed with AS and learned to overcome his struggles with anxiety, shares his tried-and-tested solutions to managing anxiety more effectively, along with up-to-date research on stress management. The result is essential reading for anyone with AS, or knows someone who does, who has trouble managing stress.
