It's Halloween in 1965 Hollywood. A group of High School students who call themselves the "Hollywood Knights" are celebrating a final evening at their favorite hangout, a drive-in called Tubby's, before it is torn down. There is no end to how far they will go to make sure the night goes out with a bang.


The Hollywood Knights is an American Graffiti-like comedy starring Tony Danza, Michelle Pfeiffer, Fran Dresher and Robert Wuhl.
