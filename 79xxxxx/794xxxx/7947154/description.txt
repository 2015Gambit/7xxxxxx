Vampires Suck
Vampires Suck is a 2010 vampire spoof film based on the Twilight film series and directed by Jason Friedberg and Aaron Seltzer. It stars Jenn Proske, Matt Lanter, Christopher N. Riggi, Ken Jeong, Anneliese van der Pol, and Arielle Kebbel. Wikipedia
Release date: August 18, 2010 (USA)
Directors: Aaron Seltzer, Jason Friedberg
MPAA rating: PG-13
DVD release date: November 30, 2010
Running time: 82 minutes
