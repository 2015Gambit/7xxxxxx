Orgies Romaines - Roman Orgies (1991)

In the prisons of ancient Rome, behold wild orgies in which valiant warriors battle felines and vicious slaves! Discover in the Middle Ages the subtle sexual tortures inflicted on beautiful and voracious prisoners lusting for pleasure! And this is only the beginning of this trip back tot the time where perversity reigned.
