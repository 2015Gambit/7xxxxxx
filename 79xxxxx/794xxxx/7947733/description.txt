Title: THE DELAY / LA DEMORA

2012/ Uruguay, Mexico, France/ color/ 84 min/ Drama

Language: Spanish

No Subtitle

Plot: María is a forty-something single mother of three who is never alone, takes care of everyone, barely sleeps, and works too much. Augustín is her forgetful father who is senile and aware of it. María’s trip to the social security office reveals that she is too poor to put her father in a retirement home, yet too wealthy to qualify for benefits. Her cruel solution is to abandon her father at a square, leaving Augustín to wait for her delayed return.
