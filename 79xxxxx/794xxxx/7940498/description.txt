One roommate, a man-magnet, teaches her more shy girlfriend how to make her sexual fantasies into realities. Meet roommates Kelly and Jay, two beautiful, sensuous young ladies with a passion for men. Kelly (Robin Canes) has no problem bedding her male prey, while shy Jay (Heather Wayne) only fantasizes on the carnal pleasures of sex.
"Naked Night" is a passionate sensual film that gives us the steamy, torrid pleasures of raw animal passion, as well as the soft, loving rapture of new found romance. We learn that courage is the only thing that can bring these experiences together.
