"Ebbie" or "Miracle at Christmas: Ebbie's Story" is a 1995 TV movie directed by George Kaczender, written by Ed Redlich, and starring Susan Lucci in the title role. It is a gender-reversed retelling of A Christmas Carol by Charles Dickens, with a hard-hearted female character in place of the miserly Ebenezer Scrooge.

---------------------------------------------------------------

In this updated retelling of Dickens' "A Christmas Carol," ruthless business-woman and shopping store owner Elizabeth "Ebbie" Scrooge is taught the true spirit of Christmas by three Spirits who visit her.
 
http://www.imdb.com/title/tt0112929/

---------------------------------------------------------------

The classic story of "A Christmas Carol" is given a major makeover when Ebbie, a contemporary female Scrooge, must face the demons of her past, confront the reality of the present, and fight the phantoms of her future, in order to reconnect with the goodness within herself.
