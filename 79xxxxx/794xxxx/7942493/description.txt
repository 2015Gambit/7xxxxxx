This is a map pack for GTAIV/EFLC (add-on for Liberty City), and has been made in order to play on multiplayer (free mode is recommended), but anyways > 8 race/drift tracks are useless on singleplayer.



My specs:

Phenom II 955 3,2GHz clocked to 3,6GHz
Sapphire HD6950 2GB (Shaders unlocked and no OC)
Asus M4A785TD-M EVO
4GB Ram 1333
Seagate 7200.12 HDD 500GB (highly defragmented)

and runs great, you can test with your specs and make feedback, but a quad-core CPU, and at least 4GB Ram and 1GB Vram is recommended.







HOW TO INSTALL:

1. Copy/paste "COMMON" and "PC" folders to your root GTAIV/EFLC folder.

2. Share with your friends who want to play it online and enjoy.

+info in readme file.
