Live 2008 performance from Germany's most successful musical export. In cooperation with Greenpeace, The Scorpions ventured deep into the Brazilian jungle for a sold out show in front of 40,000 enthusiastic fans. Includes 13 songs from their concert in Recife, Brazil, recorded on September 7th, 2008 plus five songs from their concert in Manaus, Brazil, August 9th, 2007, recorded for Greenpeace to support the campaign to save the destruction of the Amazonian Rainforest. Features scorching versions of classics like 'Big City Nights', 'Bad Boys Running Wild', 'Rock You Like A Hurricane' and 'Still Loving You'
