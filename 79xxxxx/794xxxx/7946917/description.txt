Ana Criado Collected We are proud to deliver the third edition of our Collected compilation series that profiles the work of some of our favorite artists in the Electronic Music community. Previous editions featured the music and work of Estonian phenomena - Beat Service and the Irish diva Neev Kennedy. On this third Episode we are discovering the unique and remarkable body of work of Ana Criado. Collaborating with Armin van Buuren, Markus Schulz, Alex Morph, Max Graham, Robbie Rivera, W&W and many more have placed her at the front of the Trance community and Ana Criado's "Collected" will show you why. The combination of angelic vocals and amazing songs that Ana delivers to perfection.
