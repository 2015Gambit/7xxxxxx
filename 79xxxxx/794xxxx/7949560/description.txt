Saxon Heavy Metal Thunder The Movie


This documentary about the veteran British Heavy Metal band. Includes interviews with members of Saxon, past and present as well as fellow musician Lars Ulrich, Lemmy, Fast Eddie, Harvey Goldsmith, Steve Strange, Dave Poxon, Geoff Barton and many others. Apart from the documentary, this release also includes a plethora of bonus live performances from various stages of the band's career.
