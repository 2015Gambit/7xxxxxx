Synopsis:
       This OVA shows the profession Sakaki Shio. Shio gets a job to be bodyguard of an underworld smuggler Gauche and protect him from a Yami's French assassin Christopher Eclair, who uses a Savate. While doing his job, Kenichi gets kidnapped by Christopher, and calls Sakaki for an exchange.. And Skaki calls Miu for help. During exchange Sakaki and Christopher fight resulting in defeat of Christopher.
