Brave (Disney)

Disney's New Classics

Merida is a young Scottish princess, who has a great passion: she's a very skillful archer! When her passion puts too much strain on the demands of her life as a princess, Merida runs away from home and turns to a witch to help her. But the terrible witch casts a spell that may doom Merida and her family, and now the young princess has to use all her courage to break the curse and save her family ... and the whole kingdom!

Published by Disney
Written by Alessandro Ferrari
Art by Manuela Razzi
Genres: Anthropomorphic, Children's, Comedy, Fantasy, Graphic Novels, Movies & TV

49 Pages
Digital Release Date: 12/20/2012
Age Rating: All Ages
