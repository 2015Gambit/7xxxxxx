Katarina is 20 years old. With a troubled past in a dreary suburb, her lifeseems to be already set in stone - until she discovers music. Everythingchanges when she hears a performance of Mozart’s Requiem at the GothenburgConcert Hall that sends her reeling and opens up a beautiful new world. Shefeels that she has to change her life and get as far away from her uglyreality as possible. But the path she has to follow proves a treacherousone, filled with lies, betrayal and a dangerous liaison with the marriedconductor Adam. Yet Katarina is ready to do anything to gain her newidentity. TILL DET SOM ÄR VACKERT (PURE) is dramatist Lisa Langseth’slong-awaited feature film debut. An uncompromising depiction of power,passion, identity, and the profound potency of music in the salons of highculture.Alicia Vikander gives a magnificent debut performance inthe film as Katarina, carrying the whole film on her shoulders. SamuelFröler (The Best Intentions) makes a powerful comeback as the conductor Adam.
