Cloverton is a Christian Contemporary-Progressive-Rock band from Manhattan, Kansas.[1] The band is composed of Kirby LeMoine and Lance Stafford, Layne Stafford and Josh Svorinic.[1][3] The band released their debut single "Take Me into the Beautiful" on the record label Fair Trade Services.[2] The song was released on February 11, 2011,[4][5] and it has charted on a couple of Christian song charts. Furthermore, the song has been put into rotation by major Christian music station K-LOVE.
