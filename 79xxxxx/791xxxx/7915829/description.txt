Ep 5: Explosive Reveals
 Martyn redoes Stacey Dash's home, while Jeffrey's custom rugs are nowhere to be found.
 PREMIERE: Tuesday December 11, 10 pm


Jeffrey's installation of a multi-million dollar beach house in La Jolla, California, grinds to a halt when he discovers his custom designed rugs are nowhere to be found. Mary's best friend Nathan Turner moves in and helps her overcome her indecisiveness about how to redecorate her home. Martyn helps longtime friend and actress Stacey Dash redesign her kitchen, dining room, and master bedroom to give each the glamour and sex appeal they deserve.
