Show Info:

Tune in tonight at 6/5c as X-Play presents its hour-long Best of 2012 Award Show special! They're handing out hardware in over twenty categories, including the coveted Game of the Year. See the full list of nominees here, or get a preview below!

Game of the Year
The nominees are: Journey, Halo 4, Assassin's Creed 3, Call of Duty: Black Ops 2, Borderlands 2, XCOM: Enemy Unknown, The Witcher 2 Enhanced Edition and Hitman: Absolution.

Best Multiplayer Game
The nominees are: Halo 4, Guild Wars 2, Borderlands 2, Mass Effect 3, and Journey.

Best Shooter
The nominees are: Halo 4, Call of Duty: Black Ops 2, Max Payne 3, Far Cry 3 and Borderlands 2.

Best Role-Playing Game
The nominees are: Mass Effect 3, Guild Wars 2, Xenoblade Chronicles, The Witcher 2 Enhanced Edition and Dragon's Dogma.

Best Action/Adventure Game
The nominees are: Assassin's Creed 3, The Walking Dead, Mark of the Ninja, Sleeping Dogs and Hitman: Absolution.

Best Writing
The nominees are: Borderlands 2, Spec Ops: The Line, Far Cry 3, Assassin's Creed 3 and The Walking Dead.
