

INSTALL NOTES

1. Unpack release.
2. Put your Sleeping Dogs game files into TINcft.for.HA.v1.0.433.1steamappscommonSleepingDogs
3. Run TINcft.exe and click "OK"
4. Select SleepingDogs (english) - appmanifest_202170.acf
5. Select "Apply update" from the menu
6. Place SleepingDogs.2012-12-08-02.update in the field (as text) and click load update
7. On the next screen click "Aplly update" and wait (it will take about 20 min)
8. After update you can return your game where it was before.[/center]
