THE SUM OF US
Australia, 100 min.
__________________________________________________________________________________

Director: Kevin Dowling, Geoff Burton
Screenplay: David Stevens

Original Music: David Faulkner
Cast: Jack Thompson, Russell Crowe, John Polson, Deborah Kennedy, Joss Moroney, Mitch Mathews, Julie Herbert, Rebekah Elmaloglou, Jan Adele.
__________________________________________________________________________________

Awards: Australian Film Institute: Best Screenplay, Adapted; Cleveland International Film Festival: Best Film; Film Critics Circle of Australia Awards: Best Actor – Male, Best Screenplay – Adapted, Best Supporting Actor – Male; Montréal World Film Festival: Best Screenplay.
__________________________________________________________________________________

Set in Sydney, Australia. A (heterosexual) father and his gay son are trying to find Ms/Mr Right respectively. The film shows their relationships with one another and the objects of their affection as tradgedy strikes. There is no overt 'message' in the film, just a very natural, entertaining story-telling.
