                     PART  440


 
           DANA INTERNACIONAL   - Free (1999) (VOB)




     "Free"  is a  song written and performed by Stevie Wonder, released as a single in May 1987 and included in the album "Characters".
     In 1999, Dana International recorded a version  which appears on her fifth studio album "Free". The "Free" album, in fact Dana International's international debut, was released in three different editions: European, Japanese and Israeli. The album mainly contains songs in English; however, one or two Hebrew tracks appear as well and even more so on the Israeli edition.
