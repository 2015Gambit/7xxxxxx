Transformers Spotlight - Orion Pax

IDW

W: James Roberts
A: Steve Kurth
C: Kurth, Andrew Griffith

Full Color, 32 pages              


Before Optimus Prime -- there was Orion Pax!

Four million years ago, the future leader of the Autobots disappeared into the Cybertronian wilderness to save a friend.

What happened next would take him to the very brink -- and have startling repercussions on the current comics!
