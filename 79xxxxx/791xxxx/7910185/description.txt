These are the complete collection of opening, ending, insert, character songs, drama CDs and original soundtracks for the anime and the game of Steins;Gate together with the contents of the disks as listed at http://www.anime-mp3.com/2011/04/stein-gate-ongoing.html.  Thanks to the original uploader, but the whole collection is a pain in the ass to download in individual rar files.  Plus, here the tracks are named in romaji (Latin script) rather than kana (Chinese script) and without semicolons, percents and other symbols that might be troublesome for some programs on English language operating systems.  (But, the track listings with kana are still available in the web pages contents.)  So, here is everything in one easy to download package.

There are 32 disks included, many of which are drama disks and other spoken word performances.  Bonus disks 1-9, Character songs 1-8, Drama disks 1-3; two game OSTs; OP and ED singles for the anime and the game.  Here are some highlights:
