Crossover characters struggle with the Cartoon Network. Developed by Papaya Studio 18 fighters of the factory, plus 19 other characters can be called into battle and serve as assistants. The game features a total of 21 scenarios in which to fight. The animated series that are present are: Ben 10 Alien Ultimate, Chowder, Code KND, Dexter's Laboratory, Foster's Home for Imaginary Friends, Samurai Jack, The Grim Adventures of Billy and Mandy, Flapjack and The Powerpuff Girls.
