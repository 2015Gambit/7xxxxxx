Nu tändas tusen juleljus (Now a thousand Christmas candles are lit) is a
Christmas album in Swedish, recorded in 1980 by Swedish pop singer and ABBA
member Agnetha Fältskog and her at the time seven-year-old daughter Linda
Ulvaeus. 
http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Nu_t%C3%A4ndas_tusen_juleljus_(album)
