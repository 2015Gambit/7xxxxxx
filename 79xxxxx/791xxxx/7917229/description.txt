Gothenburg Cathedral's Boys' Choir was founded in 1962 by music director
Birgitta Persson. Under her inspiring leadership the choir soon developed
in to one of Europe's leading boys'choirs, with concerts throughout the
country and tours worldwide.

A personal note: Even if you do not speak Swedish, I think you can hear
and appreciate the intimate christmas atmosphere this album provides.
