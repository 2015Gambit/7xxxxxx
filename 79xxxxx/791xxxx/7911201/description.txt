Egypt: Sex, Mobs and Revolution

Sociopolitical Documentary hosted by Ramita Navai, published by Channel 4 broadcasted as part of CH4 Unreported World series in 2012 - English narration


Information
------------------------------

Unreported World examines the increase in sexual assaults and harassment in Egypt. The programme reveals claims that young men are being paid to carry out horrendous mob attacks on women. It is claimed that this started under the Mubarak regime and it is suspected by some to still continue. Women have been at the forefront of the Egyptian revolution but are now often fearful of taking part in the regular public demonstrations.
