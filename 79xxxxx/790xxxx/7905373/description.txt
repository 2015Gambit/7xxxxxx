Pokémon the Movie: Kyurem vs. the Sword of Justice , originally released in Japan as Pocket Monsters Best Wishes! Movie: Kyurem vs. The Sacred Swordsman: Keldeo ( 劇場版ポケットモンスターベストウイッシュキュレムVS聖剣士ケルディオ gekijôban Poketto Monsutā Besuto Uisshu Kyuremu tai Seikenshi: Kerudio ? ) is the fifteenth film based on the anime Pokémon , and the second film in the series Pokémon Black & White . The film was released on July 14 of 2012 in Japan . Kyurem vs. the Sacred Swordsmen was first revealed during the teaser trailer after the end of the previous movies Victini and the Black Hero: Zekrom and Victini and the White Hero: Reshiram (later released internationally as Pokémon The Movie: White-Victini and Reshiram and Black Victini and Zekrom .) Japanese show Oha Suta revealed a trailer 15 minutes of the film, while revealing his provisional. [1] [2] The January issue of CoroCoro Comic also reveals the release date of the film, which was July 14 of 2012 . [3] The film will be screened in the U.S. on December 8 of 2012 on Cartoon Network in U.S. television.
