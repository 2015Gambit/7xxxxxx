This is the complete discography of DIR EN GREY from 1997 to 2012. 

It includes all singles, mini-albums, albums and unofficial audio-rip of their concert DVDs/Blu-ray. All Limited Edition's content also included.

Everything is properly renamed with track numbers, release date and cover image. Japanese songs have romaji filenames and japanese tags.

Pretty much every official release is in 320kbps. 

There are some miscs files such as rare and unreleased tracks and a lot of live bootlegs.
