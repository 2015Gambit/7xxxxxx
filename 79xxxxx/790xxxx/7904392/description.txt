Match of the Day 08.12.2012

Gary Lineker introduces the best of the action from the day's six Premier League matches, with Alan Hansen and Alan Shearer providing expert analysis.

Early season pacesetters Chelsea make the trip to Wearside looking for an eighth successive win at Sunderland.

Arsenal host a West Brom side that have impressed this season, while there is a skirmish between two of the promoted sides as Reading visit Southampton.
