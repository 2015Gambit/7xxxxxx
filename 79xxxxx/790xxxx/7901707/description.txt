Language: English

The sequel to Umbrella Chronicles, Darkside Chronicles takes place in Resident Evil 2's Racoon City. It's September 29, 1998, and the city has turned to hell due to the effects of the T-virus. Players take control of Claire Redfield and Leon S. Kennedy to deal with the undead. Only this time, the enemy is not the Umbrella Corporation ... instead, players venture into the unexplored areas of the Resident Evil story, along with the darker passages of the characters' histories.
