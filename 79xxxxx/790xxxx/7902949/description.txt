Alan Carr: Chatty Man
Series 9. Episode 12/14
========================

The host is joined by stand-up comedian Michael McIntyre, who tells him all about his latest stand-up DVD Showtime, and Celebrity Juice presenter Keith Lemon, who discusses his new autobiography, Being Keith - and no doubt more besides. Black Eyed Peas star Will.i.am is also in for a chat, and the music is provided by Daley featuring Jessie J.
