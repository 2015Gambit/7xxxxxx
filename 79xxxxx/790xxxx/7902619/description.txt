Working with Hair and Makeup
You asked for it: Sue Bryce's hair and makeup artist Simona Janek is coming back to creativeLIVE to teach photographers all about hair and makeup! She'll talk about how to start offering hair and makeup in your photography studio, how best to work with hair and makeup artists, and more. In two full days of practical instruction she'll teach technique and style. She'll teach communication and how to get the look you want. And as a bonus she'll also teach women the fundamentals of how to apply their own makeup!

Instructor: Simona Janek, Sue Bryce

School: Photography

Date: 2 Day Workshop

More info: http://www.creativelive.com/courses/hair-and-makeup-sue-bryce-and-simona-janek

Enjoy!
