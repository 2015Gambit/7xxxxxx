              DEREK AND THE DOMINOS  -  It's Too Late  (1971)



     "It's Too Late"  is the single taken from  Derek and the Dominos album "Layla and Other Assorted Love Songs". (1970)  The album is often regarded as Eric Clapton's greatest musical achievement, in ensemble with a talented supporting cast of Bobby Whitlock on keyboards and vocals, Jim Gordon on drums, Carl Radle on bass, and special guest performer Duane Allman on lead and slide guitar on 11 of the 14 songs.In 2000, the album was inducted into the Grammy Hall of Fame and Rolling Stone ranked it number 117 on its list of "The 500 Greatest Albums of All Time".
