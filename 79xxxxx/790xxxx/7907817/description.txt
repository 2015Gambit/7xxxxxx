
Caedmon recorded Sybil Thorndike and Lewis Casson in the evening of their careers. Sir Lewis was a prominent actor-manager and his wife Dame Sybil a mainstay at the Old Vic.(Shaw wrote St Joan expressly for her)



The major poems read here are The Lady of Shallott, Ulysses, Morte d"Arthur and In Memoriam.(Prologue, VI, VII, XI, LIV, LXX, L, CXXIX, CVIII) For comparison, I have appended a much earlier recording of an excerpt from Macbeth.
