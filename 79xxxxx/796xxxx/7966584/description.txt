Amerzone - The Explorer's Legacy is a first person adventure game similar to the Myst series in how it plays. In this game by famed Syberia creator, Benoit Sokal, you play as a young reporter who sought out an old and dieing man. This explorer is the only known man to ever be able to explore a jungle region known as Amerzone.

The natives of Amerzone trusted this explorer but he betrayed their trust by taking the egg of a rare and endangered white bird from that land to prove his story, however now near death he regrets that decision.

Alexandre Valembois the explorer gets this young reporter to promise to take the egg.. sixty years after he stole it, back to Amerzone. Will you be able to make it into this area closed off by a dictator and return the egg to its rightful home?
