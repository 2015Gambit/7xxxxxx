The five-issue Madrox (stylized "MadroX") miniseries was published by MARVEL KNIGHTS in 2004. (It was later collected as "Madrox: Multiple Choice.")

The scans in this torrent are from the digital release in 2010.

Written by Peter David, who had previously written the character in X-Factor, this story focuses on Madrox as head of a noir-style detective agency, first called "XXX Investigations." He and the other members of his detective agency now star in the revamped X-FACTOR monthly series that is again written by Peter David and that has lasted over 8 years.
