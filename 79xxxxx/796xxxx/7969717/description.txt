This product DOSCH 3D: Animals contains 48 very detailed and fully textured 3D-models of various animals. The product contains 12 dog breeds, 7 different large cats, 6 domestic cat breeds, 7 "exotic" animals, incl. bear, elephant, dromedary, zebra, crocodile, as well as various smaller animals like goat, hare, hedgehog, turtle, frog and more. 
The completely textured 3D-models are provided in multiple file formats: 3DS, Lightwave (version 6 and above), OBJ, FBX and Cinema 4D (version 7 and above).

As with most Dosch and other companies products, some models are good, others are not. Try the good models, before you pay together for the crap ones.
