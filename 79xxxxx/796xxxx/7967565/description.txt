Polish Airports 2 is a package of highly detailed sceneries of EPSC Szczecin Goleniów, EPLL Lódz Wladyslaw Reymont and EPZG Zielona Góra Babimost airports in Poland. Polish Airports 2 is a payware scenery however there is a fully functional demo scenery provided that you can use for any online and multiplayer flying.
