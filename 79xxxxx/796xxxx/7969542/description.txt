ABOUT THE MOVIE:
Gaalipata is a Kannada-language film directed by Yogaraj Bhat of Mungaru Male fame.[1] The film has a star cast of three heroes and three heroines. Ganesh is in the 
lead role along with Diganth and singer Rajesh Krishnan. The three heroines are Daisy Bopanna, Neetu and Bhavana Rao. Southern film industry cinematographer Ratnavelu
was the cameraman; V. Harikrishna was the music director.

Plot:
Three friends, Ganesh (Ganesh), Diganth (Diganth) and Kitty (Rajesh Krishnan) come to Mugilupete. Ganesh falls in love with Sowmya (Daisy Bopanna), a widow, staying
with her in-laws Kodandaram (Anant Nag) and Padma (Padmaja Rao) while Diganth and Kitty fall for Kodandaram's daughters Radha (Neethoo) and Pavani (Bhavana Rao) 
respectively.While Diganth-Radha and Kitty-Pavani get the green signal for marriage, Sowmya is hesitant to accept Ganesh as her life partner. The director shows how 
Ganesh wins her heart in a unique way
