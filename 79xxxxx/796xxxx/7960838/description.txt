Alan Carr: Chatty Man 2012 Christmas Special (S09E15)

The host presents a festive edition of his talk show, with music by his house band for the night, McFly, who perform a medley of their greatest hits. Chart toppers JLS join Alan on the sofa along with Dragons' Den panellist Hilary Devey, who talks about her two new programmes for Channel 4. Actor David Walliams also drops by to discuss new BBC family comedy Mr Stink, based on his children's book of the same name.
