Classification: U 

Genre: Romance

General Release Date: 06 Dec 2012

Running Time: 1 Hour 30 Minutes

Distributor: Metrowealth

Cast: Yana Samsudin, Pierre Andre, Diana Amir

Director: Ahmad Rusli Mohamed Khadri

"Strawberi Cinta" chronicles the journey of a Japanese man named Ichigo who travels to Malaysia for a vacation along with a tour group from his country through a travel package by Strawberry Vanilla Travel & Tours. As the secrets of Malaysia's beauty are revealed to them, Ichigo is also shown to harbour secrets of his own. His true intention of travelling to Malaysia is to search for something that has long eluded his life...
