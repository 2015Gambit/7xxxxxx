The Cat People wait for their prophecy to be fulfilled: the coming of the Queen of the Cats. Selena Steele rules the tribe as Supreme Cat--until her wicked little sister (Raven) comes along. As the tensions mount, they fight for control of their men, and soon of the entire nocturnal, sexual tribe!
