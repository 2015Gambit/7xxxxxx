The Monolith

Genre: Action/Adventure/Mythology/Supernatural/Occult

English | CBR | 99 Pages

Written by Justin Gray and Jimmy Palmiotti
Art by Phil Winslade

Alice Cohen, a down and out ex-junkie who inherits a house in Brooklyn from her deceased grandmother. Alice discovers her diary and begins to read the tale of a lost love and revenge that begins in the factories of New York during the depression and shows the creation of a monster bent on revenge for the slaying of a good honest man. Originally published by DC comics.
