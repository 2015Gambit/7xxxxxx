About the Author
John Allen and Dr. Jochen Gartz are two of the World's leading experts on ethneongenic mushrooms. Mr. Allen has also picked more mushrooms than any living human and has done so on 4 continents. He brings a practical knowledge and down-home charm to his books. Jochen Gartz from a scientist form Germany is the author of several papers and books on mushrooms.
