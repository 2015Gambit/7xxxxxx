'Eating Raoul'

Filmmakers Newsletter Co., 1982, 36 pages

Adapted by Kim Deitch from a screenplay by Paul Bartlet and Dick Blackburn

Pencils and Inks by Rich Chidlaw, Kim Deitch, Warren Greenwood, Shawn Kerri, Carol Lay

Yes, this is an official underground comic book adaptation of the movie, because shit like that could happen in the early 80s.
