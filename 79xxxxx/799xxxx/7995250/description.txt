Movie that follows the lives of three different people, Frank McLean, Will and Caryn.

With the information in the diaries, Frank delves into his lover's past in an attempt to get to know more about the man that he never really knew. 
Will is a party boy who is a friend of Frank and Frank's late lover John. Will, who is in an open relationship with Ben, just can't see why Frank is so devastated by his lover's death. 
Caryn has lost her love to Jen, who is Franks best friend, after refusing to come out to her parents. Still in love with Sandra Caryn finds that she has made a mistake by letting love get away.
