========================
 Chess Informants 1-115 
========================

in Chessbase .cbh format complete with keys

The Chess Informant series of books (volume numbers 1-115) have been a leading source of chess information for the past 46 years (1966-2012). They contain 111,336 annotated chess games and theory. Contributors include many top grandmasters and all world chess champions from Euwe to Anand.
