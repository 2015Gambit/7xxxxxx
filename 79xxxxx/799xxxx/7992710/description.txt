2)  Pippa 
Friday April 29th -- The whole world turned up and tuned in to see the wedding of commoner Kate Middleton to the world's most eligible bachelor, Prince William of Wales, but what they walked away talking about was Kate's gorgeous maid-of-honor, her sister Phillipa "Pippa" Middleton. And, while Pippa was seemingly introduced to the world that famous day in April, she'd actually been cultivating her image and socialite status for years. And now, Pippa has reached a new level of fame, one that is even trickier to navigate, but this ambitious young women has been waiting her whole life for this day, and is ready to take center stage.
