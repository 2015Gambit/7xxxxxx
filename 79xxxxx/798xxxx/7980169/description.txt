On 8 December 1995, Jean-Dominique Bauby, a journalist with the French Elle and 
only 43 years old, had a stroke. He fell into a coma and when he regained 
consciousness he found that his body was left paralyzed except for one eye. The 
film tells the true events and how Bauby was locked in his dysfunctional body. 
His one working eye was his only link with the outside world, by simply 1 time 
blink for yes and twice for "no."
