Synopsis: Four bachelors; Vaman, Bajrang, Vasant and Tukaram share a room in a chawl owned by the brut landlord, Mr. Vaag. Vaag wants Vaman to marry his daughter so that the other three men can be driven out of the chawl. Faced with a possible eviction, Bajrang, Vasant and Tukaram device a scheme to foil Mr. Vaag's plan. The house maid Saku Bai, Vasant's girlfriend Kamal and Vaman's Nana all get sucked into their crazy scheme which spins out of control leading to hilarious situations. Wacky events follow suit when a thug wants to kill Tukaram, Kamal thinks Vasant is cheating on her, and Vaman is blamed of having an affair with Bajrang's sister. What is leading to all the chaos? Is someone executing a counter plan? Can the three men save themselves from being dislodged?
