All Stars and Stripes
 ReleasedJan 03, 2013

 The Project Runway designers pay their tribute the armed forces! The four remaining designers are paired with four female veterans from an array of different military branches. The designers will have to battle it out to see who can create a design that best suits each veteran for each of the special events they are attending. Special guest judges for this episode include actress Katie Holmes and designer Carmen Marc Valvo.
