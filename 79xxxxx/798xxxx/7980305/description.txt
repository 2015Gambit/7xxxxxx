From the tales of Cinderella, Sleeping Beauty, The Little Mermaid and Snow White comes Damsels!

As the twin armies of Perrault and Caumont thunder across the plains, Rapa and Heinrich search for clues to the true identity of Queen Talia in Perrault castle but soon find themselves fighting for their lives.

Meanwhile in Villeneuve, Queen Belle tries to reason with her husband the King, as her subjects take matters into their own hands.

Writer: Leah Moore, John Reppion
Artist: Aneke
Cover Artist: Joseph Michael Linsner
