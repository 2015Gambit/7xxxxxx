PUT UP YOUR DUX


Directed by Jesse Barrett-Mills, Daniel Gallai


Frank Dux is the martial arts expert whose life was the basis for the popular movie "Bloodsport", starring Jean-Claude Van Damme This documentary looks at Dux's life, hearing from the man himself, controversy not far behind - from the ring, to court room drama with Van Damme, and rumours of involvement in undercover agencies...


Frank Dux is the martial artist expert whose life experience was the basis for the jean-Claude Van Damme starring classic Bloodsport.


This documentary gets the full lowdown from the man himself, with controversy never far behind; from under cover government agency rumors, court room drama with Van Damme and disputes about live martial arts exhibition feats, this fascinating character makes for an even more fascinating real life story.
