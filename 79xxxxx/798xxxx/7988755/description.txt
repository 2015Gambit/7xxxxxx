Rotten Vol. 2 Revival Of The Fittest

Genre: Historical/Horror/Zombie/Western

English | CBR | 6 Issues

Written by Robert Horton and Mark Rahner
Art by Dan Dougherty

The acclaimed zombie Western epic with the topical bite returns! Agent Wade is fully recovered, lethal and armed with new tricks as he travels to an undead outbreak in the Pacific Northwest. But who could be prepared for the species he encounters? Agent Flynn looks for answers as a hate-monger is whipping up a crowd against a new concept called 'Evolution'.
