Travel back to Hollywood in the 1950s, where a starlet could be made overnight - if she had the right assets! Brie Livingston plays a young waitress that has everything it takes, and uses her sexual charms to make Hollywood do her bidding. We follow Brie as she makes her climb through agents and big-time stars, all the way to the top. Her incredible rise all comes together in a stunning awards ceremony that puts her witty and erotic life into focus.
