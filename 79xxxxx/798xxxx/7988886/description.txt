TLC's new series "Sisterhood" takes us inside the lives of some of Atlanta's most powerful First Ladies.We'll meet Domonoque, a bold, brash preacher's wife with a past; Ivy, a former member of the girl group Xscape; Christina, a sassy mother of two; Tara, a recent Los Angeles transplant struggling to find her way; and DeLana, a first ladies who's equal parts doting mother and outrageous rock star.

Follow these women as they give us an inside look at what it's like to be a first lady, and the challenges of balancing faith, friendship, family and church. As Domonoque explains, "The only thing we agree on is that we all love God. Everything else is up to chance. (Source: TLC)
