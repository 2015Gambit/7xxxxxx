Written by Alan Bleasdale who also wrote the Liverpool (UK)-based 'Boys from the blackstuff' this wonderful black comedy takes place one New Year's Eve in the squalor of a slum nightclub and its catchment. It seems the local Orange Order pensioners have been booked in for the evening, as well as the local Belfast Irish Catholic pensioners group. Why? What happens?  Watch and you shall see ...
