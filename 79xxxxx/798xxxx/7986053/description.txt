Tales of Army of Darkness

A monster sized annual featuring 48 pages of all-out horror and action from the Army of Darkness! Inside, you'll find 5 fantastic and terrible tomes by the following creative teams: Shawn Spurlock and Sanford Greene, Jim Kuhoric and Nick Bradshaw, Jim Kuhoric and Filip Sablik, Robert Napton and Michael O'Hare. Special guests include The Walking Dead writer & Invincible art team Robert Kirkman and Ryan Ottley!
