The "Torn Apart" webisodes that were released online after the first season of The Walking Dead have been edited here into a single 17.5-minute clip for convenience. Torn Apart tells the story of the immediate events that preceded the transformation of one of the more memorable walkers from the first episode, titled "A New Day".
