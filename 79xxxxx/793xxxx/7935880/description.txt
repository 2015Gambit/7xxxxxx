ABOUT THE MOVIE:
Govindaya Namaha is a 2012 Kannada Comedy-drama movie directed by debutante Pawan Wadeyar who has worked as assistant director for popular filmmaker Yogaraj Bhat.The film was released on 30 March 2012 across cinema halls. It stars Komal Kumar, Anna Georgia, Madhulika, Rekha Vedavyas and Parul Yadav in the lead roles. The music is composed by Gurukiran
