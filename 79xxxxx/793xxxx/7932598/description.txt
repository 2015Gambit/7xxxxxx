Planet Minigolf delivers a single-player campaign that transports players to several courses around the world and comes loaded with cartoon-style visuals immersing players into a fun-filled world of putters, balls and crazy obstacles. Players can choose from six customizable characters and complete up to 144 unique holes packed with a variety of unusual hazards and interactive objects, all while trying to navigate the detailed terrain and sink that tricky hole-in-one.

Planet Minigolf also feature a full suite of multiplayer options including a local hotseat mode, regular weekly tournaments and worldwide competitions where players can battle it out to see whose nation reigns supreme.
