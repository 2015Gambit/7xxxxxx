'Encyclopedia of the CIA' is a directory that contains information about the purpose and structure of the various secret services of the world, the intelligence terminology, operations carried out by the Central Intelligence Agency, biographies of famous spies (including Soviet Kim Philby, Rudolf Abel, etc.). Reference illustrated diagrams and photographs. It is of interest for those interested in the history of intelligence.
