Bradley Wiggins: Tour de Force

Sociopolitical Documentary hosted by James Richardson, published by Eurosport in 2012 - English narration


Information
------------------------------

A look back at the 2012 Tour de France which saw Bradley Wiggins become the first ever British rider to win the prestigious yellow jersey.
