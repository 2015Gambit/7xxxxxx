Ep 6: Mansion Madness 
The decorators work on rooms for historic Greystone Mansion, but Mary and Kathryn can't see eye-to-eye.
PREMIERE: Tuesday December 18, 10 pm

Martyn, Mary, and Kathryn each design rooms in the historic Greystone Mansion in Beverly Hills as part of an exclusive Luxe Magazine show-house event. Competition ensues as they battle to see who can design the most beautiful space, but an all-out war erupts between Mary and Kathryn when they discover their rooms are adjacent to one another and can't agree on how to connect the spaces.
