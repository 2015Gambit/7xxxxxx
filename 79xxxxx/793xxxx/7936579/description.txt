Na twee nationale sterren was het in 2008 de beurt aan wereldster Lionel Richie.

Englisch
The double-disc live album Symphonica in Rosso documents Lionel Richie's triumphant live performance
at the Gelredome in Arnhem, Netherlands, on September 6, 2008.
The show was a triumph for Richie because he'd scored a Top Five smash hit earlier in the year in the Netherlands
with "Face in the Crowd," a duet with Dutch pop singer Trijntje Oosterhuis from his recent album Just Go (2008).
It was also an honor, for he was taking the stage at one of the Netherlands' premier concert stadiums
and performing in part of a series of Symphonica in Rosso shows that had spawned hit albums
by Dutch superstars Marco Borsato and Paul de Leeuw in past years.
