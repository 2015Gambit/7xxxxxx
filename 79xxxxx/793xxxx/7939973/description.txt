By winning last week's surprise challenge against Jordon, Christina gets a second shot at winning Project Runway and earns the right to join Leah, Tristan, and Jamie in the finale. After months of hard work, the big day finally arrives, and when the four finalists see the venue for the first time, their excitement is palpable. With four incredible collections to choose from, the judges have to make their toughest decision yet. Who will be the winner?
