Long. Live. ASAP (stylized as Long. Live. A$AP) is the upcoming major label debut studio album by American hip hop recording artist A$AP Rocky, set to be released on January 15, 2013 under A$AP Worldwide, Polo Grounds Music and RCA Records (Sony Music Entertainment). The album received multiple push backs, with prior release dates being September 11 and October 31, 2012, the latter being the first anniversary of his first full-length project, a mixtape titled Live. Love. ASAP (2011).
