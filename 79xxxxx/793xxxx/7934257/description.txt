ENJOY.


This CD is a collection of 47 tracks spanning 1970-1977 featuring the original
line-up, led by Ronnie Van Zant. 

15 of the tracks are previously unreleased.

Hardcore fans will be interested in such previously unreleased early demos as "Junkie," "He's Alive," and a truncated version of "Freebird," as well as such standards as "Sweet Home Alabama," "On the Hunt," "You Got That Right," and of course the official version of "Freebird."
