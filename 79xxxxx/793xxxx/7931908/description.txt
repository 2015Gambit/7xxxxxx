Ep 7: I Do...But, I Won't
 Porsha pushes one of the ladies to her boiling point, which results in a full-on feud.
 PREMIERE: Sunday December 16, 9 pm

Romance is in the air and a Housewife decides to say "I do" during this couples trip to Anguilla. While in paradise, NeNe begins to question Kenya's behavior and her relationship with her boyfriend, Walter. This infuriates Kenya and puts her on the defense. Ultimately, it's Porsha who pushes one of the ladies to her boiling point, which results in a full-on feud.
