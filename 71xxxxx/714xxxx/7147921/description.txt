Beautiful is a 2011 Malayalam musical drama film written by Anoop Menon and directed by V. K. Prakash. The film stars Jayasurya, Anoop Menon and Meghana Raj in the lead roles. It tells the story about the intense bonding of two friends, one paraplegic and other a musician. The film released on 2 December 2011 to predominantly positive reviews.
Stephen Louis (Jayasurya) is a paraplegic by birth who is immensely rich, but he believes in celebrating each moment of life. He always loves to see beautiful things and speaks about celebrating life even though he can't move any part of his body other than his neck. He is always assisted by his Manager Kamalu (Nandu) and his caretaker cum driver Karunan (Jayan) in a palatial house in Fort Kochi. As most of his relatives looks to take advantage of his position, Louis has kept all of them at bay, barring his cousin brother Alex (Tini Tom) with whom he shares good relationship. John (Anoop menon), a musician at a hotel enters his life. John is completely opposite from Stephen's nature. He is always worried about the future. Though poles apart in their attitudes, both enjoy a good friendship and try to make each other happy. But with the arrival of a beautiful home nurse named Anjali (Meghana Raj), the attitudes of both friends towards their life turns around. The movie take unexpected twists and turns from here, with a thrilling suspense.
