1. Install Microsoft Office 2010 with setup.exe in the Setup folder

2. Run Office 2010 Toolkit.exe

3. Click the "Activation" tab

4. Click "Activate" and wait for it to process

5. Click "Check" and look at "REMAINING GRACE:" at the bottom, it should say "180 days"

NOTE: You will need to repeat steps 2-5 every 180 days
