Xavier Price escapes from prison in the midst of a candlelight vigil outside the penitentiary. The Breakout Kings are immediately brought on the case to try to track him down. It turns out this violent criminal is a "collector," and has a history of kidnapping women, torturing them, and holding them captive in basements. Once the Breakout Kings learn he currently has a women hidden away somewhere still alive, the race is on for them to find this woman and save her.
