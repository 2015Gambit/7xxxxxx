Nina Simone - Forbidden Fruit
Original 1961
Remaster 2005

* * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * *

Nina Simone needs no introduction. This is one my favourite albums showcasing her early 60s period when she was at the top of her game. This remaster includes 10 bonus tracks covering jazz, blues and standards. Essential, if you ask me. Enjoy!
