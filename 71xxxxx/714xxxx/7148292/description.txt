Jon Anderson toured after his first departure from Yes with his own band The New Life Band in 1980. Among others in this band was Chris Rainbow, well known for his harmony singing voice on various albums of The Allan Parsons Project.
These 5 songs were captured from the rehearsal floor, probably somewhere in England.

1. For You For Me
2. Some Are Born
3. Don't Forget
4. Everybody Loves You
5. Yes Medley
