-------[Release Notes]--------------------------------------------------------

Recovery is the upcoming seventh studio album by American rapper Eminem. Recovery was confirmed during a press release by Eminem's label on March 5, 2009 and was originally titled Relapse 2. It was slated to be the second Eminem album released in 2009, after Relapse, but was pushed back to 2010. The confirmed release date is June 21 in the United Kingdom, while in the rest of Europe it is June 18 and in the United States June 22.
