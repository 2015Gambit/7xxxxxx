In Frontline Commando, you are the one man in the one war to complete all the deadly missions with an arsenal of deadly weapons.

You are stranded on the frontline and hell-bent on payback as the sole surviving Commando of a renegade attack against a ruthless dictator, . You must survive the onslaught of the enemy forces and avenge your fallen soldiers with all of your specialized skills.

Now jump in the heat of battle and push your Android Phone to the final limit! The ultimate 3rd person shooter with precise controls, stunning console quality visuals, advanced physics and destructibles.

Emerge from destructible cover points to take down helicopters, jeeps and heavily defended enemy bases. Fight off increasingly difficult waves of enemies over a variety of combat mission types.

Take to the frontline with an artillery of sniper rifles, med kits, shotguns, assault rifles, rocket launchers, armor and more!
