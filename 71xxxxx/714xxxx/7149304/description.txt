Also known as Fireworks Symphony

Stereoscopic 3D movie of the annual Suwa Lake Fireworks Festival in Nagano, Japan.

Direction/Camera: Takashi Sekitani (STEREOeYe)


Note: This video is meant to be played on the 3DS

Resolution: 400x240 (the movie has the original aspect ratio)


Bonus 3D Photo's:

I've included 3D photos (10) from STEREOeYe's 3D Photo Gallery for Nintendo 3DS


Install:

Copy the movie and photos (mpo's) to the DCIM/100NIN03 directory of your SD card
Open the Nintendo 3DS Camera app and press View Photos/Videos
Wait for the 3DS to update the management file and enjoy the trip!
