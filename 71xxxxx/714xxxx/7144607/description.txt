10 Years of Hits is the first compilation album released by Boyzone frontman, Ronan Keating. The album was released on October 11, 2004, and included all of Keating's singles to date, The album peaked at #1 on the UK Albums Chart, becoming the third out of four of Keating's albums to do so.
