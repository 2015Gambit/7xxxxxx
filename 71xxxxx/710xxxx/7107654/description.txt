Guinness

Technology Documentary hosted by Tony Hirst, published by National Geographic broadcasted as part of NG Megafactories series in 2012 - English narration


Information
------------------------------

Megafactories
Megafactories: lifts the lid on how millions of your everyday products and iconic designs begin life on the assembly line. From bottles of beer to the latest video games to souped-up supercars, follow every cog and conveyor belt of these fascinating production processes as you enter some of the planet's most impressive factories.
.................................... Megafactories Guinness....................................
A behind-the-scenes look at Guinness's Dublin factory, learning about a process that involves harvesting, roasting and brewing to produce one of Ireland's favourite exports., also we explore how Guinness's Dublin factory prepares for the busiest months in Irish beer.
