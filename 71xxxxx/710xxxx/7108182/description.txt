ROBERT PLANT - Ship of Fools (1988)


"Ship of Fools" is a rock song performed by Robert Plant. It was the second single released from his 1988 album "Now and Zen", following "Heaven Knows". It reached number 76 on the UK singles chart, number 84 on the Billboard Hot 100 and number 3 on the Billboard Mainstream Rock chart. It was Plant's tenth top-ten solo hit on the Mainstream Rock chart.
