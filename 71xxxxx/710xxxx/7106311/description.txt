Kick-Ass 2 is the sequel to Mark Millar and John Romita Jr.'s original series Kick-Ass. The story follows the titular character Kick-Ass who is trying to form a team of real life superheroes, as well as preparing for the arrival of The Red Mist, who has been gathering a team of "Supervillains" to take both Kick-Ass and Hit-Girl down.
