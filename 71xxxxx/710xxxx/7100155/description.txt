Living Space by M.J. Elliott

Dying for a new apartment? A young couple learn too late to trust their suspicions, when they find themselves caught in a trap devised by a demented genius. With less than an hour until they meet a particularly horrible death, can they outwit their captor, or are they in a cell with only one way out?
