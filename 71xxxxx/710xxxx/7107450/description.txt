Word by word presents over 3000 words through lively illustrations. This innovative Picture Dictionary offers students the essential vocabulary they need to communicate effectively in a wide range of relevant situationas and contexts. It organizes vocabulary into 100 thematic units.

Este diccionario ilustrado innovador ofrece a los estudiantes el vocabulario esencial que necesitan para comunicarse eficazmente en una amplia gama de situationas. El vocabulario está organizado en 100 unidades temáticas.
