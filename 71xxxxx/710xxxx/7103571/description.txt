[][ ..:: RELEASE DESCRiPTiON ::.. ][]

iResizer is a handy way to resizing images non uniformly while
preserving the key features of the picture. iResizer avoids
distortion of the important parts of the image.

In addition, this application provides users with the ability
to erase large areas from their images.

For example, you can turn a landscape picture into a square
picture and close up the space between people in a image if
they are a long way apart. It also works in reverse and you
can use it to make a photo larger.
