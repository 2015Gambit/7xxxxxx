Synopsis : Kyaa Super Kool Hain Hum is an upcoming Bollywood sex comedy film directed by Sachin Yardi, and produced by Ekta Kapoor and Shobha Kapoor. The film is a sequel to the 2005 hit, Kyaa Kool Hai Hum and features Tusshar Kapoor and Riteish Deshmukh reprising their roles from the previous film.
