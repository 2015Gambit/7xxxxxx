Synopsis : Jannat 2 is a Bollywood crime thriller film and a sequel to the 2008 hit film, Jannat. The film is directed by Kunal Deshmukh, and produced by Mahesh Bhatt and Mukesh Bhatt. It features Emraan Hashmi, Randeep Hooda, Imran Zahid and Esha Gupta in lead roles.The storyline is based on the issue of illegal arms in Delhi.The first look unveiled on 15th March 2012, whilst the film is to hit theatres on 4 May, 2012
