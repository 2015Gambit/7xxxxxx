Discover the third largest airport of Norway in extreme detail! Approaching this high resolution airport scenery will give you an unforgettable experience.

Details:
 

The original and highly detailed realization of the airport, buildings and installations creates a perfect and lively atmosphere of Trondheim-Vaernes airport. Lighting, night effects and apron markings as well as taxiway signs make this scenery truly realistic. Photorealistic ground textures of all four seasons round off the perfect feel for this Norwegian airport.

In its beginnings this airport was used for military purposes and was only later turned into a civil airport by the Norwegian government. Due to the great demand in flights and destinations this airport has seen some big developments over the past years and is now the most important airport of western Norway.
