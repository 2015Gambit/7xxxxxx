Bix Siegurdson is an Icelandic DJ who, although virtually unknown outside his home country, is one of the best living electronic artists north of the 46th parallel. 

His debut album Animalog, was released in September 2011. His work has already been used in numerous commercials, and has continued producing new material through to today.
