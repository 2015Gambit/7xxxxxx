WHAT NOT TO WEAR

Two fashion stylists and a team of hair and makeup advisors help revamp the look of individuals who have been nominated by their friends for makeovers due to their lamentable appearance, using a $5,000 budget.

SEASON 8 EPISODE 26 - Becca

Becca is a 27 year old medical student living in New Orleans with a totally sick collection of tattered low slung jeans and an endless heap of itty-bitty tank tops. Lifelong insecurities and a wicked rebellious streak caused her to opt-out of a style. Now this case of fashion malpractice is putting her at risk of not being taken seriously by patients, or other doctors. With med school coming to an end Clinton and Stacy will use their style scalpels to convince Becca to grow up and start dressing like a medical professional. Or will her tank-top toting, flesh-belt wearing, condition be incurable.
