Red Dwarf Series 1 - Episode 1 (1988)

A radiation leak wipes out the crew of the mining ship Red Dwarf leaving one survivor - chicken soup machine repair man Dave Lister. After three million years in suspended animation, Lister emerges to find he is the last human being in the universe. But he is not alone. Starring Chris Barrie, Craig Charles, Danny John-Jules and Norman Lovett. Produced and Directed by Ed Bye. Executive Producer Paul Jackson.
