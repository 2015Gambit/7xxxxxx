WHAT NOT TO WEAR

Two fashion stylists and a team of hair and makeup advisors help revamp the look of individuals who have been nominated by their friends for makeovers due to their lamentable appearance, using a $5,000 budget.

SEASON 8 EPISODE 21 - Deana

Deana is a mother from Brooklyn, New York whose maternity wear has lived on long after it should have been retired. After a lifetime of hand-me-down clothes and the post-baby-weight blues Deana has a serious case of arrested style development. With some upcoming life changes including a move and the opening of a new business, her husband hopes she can freshen up her wardrobe.
