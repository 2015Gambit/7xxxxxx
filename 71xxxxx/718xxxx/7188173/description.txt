When it comes to painting Citadel Miniatures (Warhammer Fantasy, Warhammer 40K and Lord of the Rings), this DVD is an absolute must-have. This mighty tutorial is crammed full of information about painting; from undercoating your miniatures and applying your first layer of paint, right through to your final highlight.

90 minutes in duration it shows you how to put these techniques into practice and get the most out of the extensive Citadel Paint Range.
