The Call of the Wild

From BBC Radio 4 Extra. Written by Jack London; Full-cast dramatisation starring Robert Jack, Finn den Hertog, Robin Laing, Nick Underwood, Grant O'Rourke and Melody Grove; Dramatised by Robert Forrest.

Buck is a domestic dog, living in a wealthy household in California. When he's kidnapped and sold he finds himself in the frozen landscape of the Yukon, pulling a sledge.

Through Buck's eyes we witness his battle for survival in this harsh, new environment.
