X305 Concept Aircraft - 1/100th scale model
===========================================

Designed as part of the AVDASI Airbus Design Project 2011/2010 (University of Bristol), the X305 aircraft is a
dual role business (30 PAX) and economy (100 PAX) commercial jet aeroplane designed for STOL out of London City
Airport (LCY). Engines are Un-Ducted Contra Fan engines (blades not modelled).
