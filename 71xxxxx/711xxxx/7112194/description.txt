Monsieur Pamplemousse Investigates

From BBC Radio 4 Extra. Written by Michael Bond; Full-cast dramatisation starring Gordon Kaye and Trevor Martin; Dramatised by Alex Row.

The French detective and his talking dog, Pomme Frites, investigate crimes.

Episode 1: Monsieur Pamplemousse investigates dirty tricks at restaurant directory Le Guide.

Episode 2: The detective tries to discover whose thumbprint is on the chocolate and the whereabouts of Madame Grant.

Episode 3: The trail leads the detective and his trusty hound to Pere Lachaise Cemetery, before a final watery showdown.
