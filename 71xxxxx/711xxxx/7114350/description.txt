Audacity - free, easy-to-use audio editor for Windows, Mac OS X, GNU / Linux and other operating systems. You can use Audacity to record audio, digitize analog recordings (cassettes, LPs), edit the files in Ogg Vorbis, MP3 and WAV, the physical editing of multiple files (cutting, gluing, mixing), change the speed or pitch of a recording, and much more .
