Austin, TX native Mark McKinney brings you his sophomore album, Middle America. This comes two years after the release of his debut album, Get It On (Comfortable In This Skin, Party Foul, Bonfire, Stompin' Ground). The first single and title track, Middle America, was a success at radio and the music video received airplay on both GAC and CMT Pure. In addition, this album got the attention of NASCAR fans when songs from it were featured on ESPN broadcasts.
