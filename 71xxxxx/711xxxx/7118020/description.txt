================================
:: Tweak Pack for PowerDVD 12 ::
================================

Blu-Ray Region Reset.bat:     Resets the Blu-Ray region settings
Disable BD Mouse Pop-up.bat:  Disables the Blu-Ray disk Mouse support Pop-up
En/Disable   Enables/DisablesRemix and Comments
En/Disable CLFLVSplitter.bat: Enables/Disables the FLV Splitter *
Disable PowerDVD12Agent:      Disables PowerDVD12Agent and PowerDVD12DMREngine **

Note: After you reset the Blu-Ray region, it is a must to reinstall PowerDVD!!


*   CLFLVSplitter.ax can cause a crash when you play flv videos in Windows Media Player. Disable_CLFLVSplitter.bat will unregister the FLV Splitter, which fixes the problem

**  Disables PowerDVD12Agent and DMREngine from running automatically at Windows startup. They both launch when you run PowerDVD 12
