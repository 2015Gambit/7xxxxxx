Jaguar XJ

Technology Documentary hosted by Tony Hirst, published by National Geographic broadcasted as part of NG Megafactories series in 2011 - English narration


Information
------------------------------

Megafactories lifts the lid on how millions of your everyday products and iconic designs begin life on the assembly line. From bottles of beer to the latest video games to souped-up supercars, follow every cog and conveyor belt of these fascinating production processes as you enter some of the planet's most impressive factories.
Megafactories: Jaguar XJ
Enter the Jaguar mega factory in Castle Bromwich, England, and discover the first car in the company's history to use aerospace influenced aluminium space frame technology. Designed to fly, see how the iconic Jaguar XJ gets its new makeover into the 21st century.
