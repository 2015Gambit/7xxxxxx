PipeRoll ... Connect the plug-holes to each other as fast as possible to rotate the pipes into the correct position. There are 100 levels with 1, 2 , 3 or 4 different types of fluid and a lot of trick. Test your brain in this amusing puzzle game!

Features:

    100 custom-designed levels,
    4 different types of fluid
    country and city environments


Requirements
Intel, Mac OS X 10.6.6 or later
