The Great Wild Nature

Nature Documentary published by Discovery Channel broadcasted as part of NHK Treasures from the Past series in 2008 - English narration


Information
------------------------------

List of World Heritage sites in this episode:
 1) Galápagos Islands (Ecuador)
 2) Península Valdés (Argentina)
 3) Manú National Park (Peru)
 4) Cerrado Protected Areas: Chapada dos Veadeiros and Emas National Parks (Brazil)
 5) Los Glaciares (Argentina)
 6) Canaima National Park (Bolivarian Republic of Venezuela)
