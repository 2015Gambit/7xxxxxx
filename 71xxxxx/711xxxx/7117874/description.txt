Skylar Grey, released on January 17, 2012, through KIDinaKORNER Records and 

Interscope Records. The release contains full and original studio versions of songs

that Grey had previously only written and featured on with other artists, or
 
performed live herself ("Love the Way You Lie" by Eminem and Rihanna, "Coming Home"

by Diddy ΓÇô Dirty Money, and "Words I Never Said" by Lupe Fiasco).
