Celebrating the world's greatest Supercars, from Ferrari supercars and sleek Alfas, to classic Jaguars and Aston Martins, Evo Supercars MagBook presents a exhilarating tour of the world's most desirable supercars in all their glory. Featuring head-to-head drive trials, under the bonnet specs as well as stunning photography, this is the definitive publication for any self-respecting petrol-head!
