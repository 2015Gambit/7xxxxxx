New Life is the seventh studio album by American R&B recording artist Monica. It is scheduled to be released on April 10, 2012 in the US and will serve as the singer's debut release with RCA, following the disbandment of her former label, J Records in October 2011. Production on the album was handeled by Bryan-Michael Cox, Jermaine Dupri, Missy Elliott, Lamb, Rico Love, Polow da Don, and Salaam Remi, among others.

Preceded by the Missy Elliott-crafted buzz singles "Anything (To Find You)" and "Until It's Gone", released on August 2 and September 27, 2011 as digital downloads respectively, the album's first single "It All Belongs to Me", a duet with singer Brandy, was released on February 14, 2012.
