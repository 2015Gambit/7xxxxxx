Eviscerate is a 5 piece original Metal act looking to explode!
Formed in June 2011 with a hard hitting and unique sound, this band shows passion for writing and playing modern metal music.
Shooting for the Exosphere, the group is hungry for live shows and limited only by resources.
Influences ranging from Korn to Chimaira, Slipknot and Parkway drive, all members are showing copious amounts of energy and creativity.
