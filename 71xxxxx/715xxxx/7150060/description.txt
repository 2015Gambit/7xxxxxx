[b]Future Wars
Publisher: Meridian4 
PC 
340Mb 
Genre: Strategy

Description:

 Similar to classic titles such as Advance Wars and Battle Isle, the game takes turn-based strategy gaming away from the micro-management overkill that it has become and using simple, proven rock-paper-scissors principles, reopens the genre to a wider audience. With a modern 3D engine providing the graphics, a proven, easy to learn, but difficult to master set of rules surrounded by an engaging plot and challenging gameplay, the game will appeal to newcomers and hard-core gamers alike. Also available is unlimited access and infinite possibilities to mod the game through open data structure, open scripts and an easy-to-use editor. 

Features:

* Modern 3D graphics and proven gameplay embedded in an engaging and sometimes, humorous plot
* Infinite modding possibilities through open data structure, open scripts and editor
* Novel and unique game modes such as AI vs. AI or play-by-email
* Rock-paper-scissors principle makes Future Wars easy to learn but difficult to master
* In-game achievements allows for hours of replay to acquire top honors

System Requirements:

Windows XP/Vista/W7
Pentium 3.0 GHz or better
1 GB (2 GB for Multiplayer)
DirectX 9.0c
ATI Radeon X1600XT or capable graphics adapter supporting PixelShader 2.0 or nVidia Geforce 8800GTS or better
DirectX 9.0c compatible sound card
500MB free disk space [for full version][/b]
