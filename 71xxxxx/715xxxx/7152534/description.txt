Anxiety is the second studio album by New Zealand recording artist Ladyhawke, set to be released on 25 May 2012. Recorded in early 2011 with co-writer and producer Pascal Gabriel, the album will be Ladyhawke's return to music in two years.
 
Ladyhawke premiered several of the tracks from the album during performances at the 100 Club in London, the Phoenix Bar in Sydney and The Tote Hotel in Melbourne, including tracks such as "Vaccine", "Blue Eyes" and "Sunday Drive".

"Black White & Blue" was released as the album's lead single on 24 January 2012
