"The Prisoner" is a television show from 1967-1968, and stars Patrick McGoohan as a former British secret agent. Upon suddenly resigning, he is kidnapped and taken to a place known as 'The Village'. Here no-one has a name--only a number. As 'Number Six' he tries to escape, cause revolts, and in general create chaos among/for whatever organisation is behind all this. 

Everything increasingly gets more into the depths of science-fiction & madness.
