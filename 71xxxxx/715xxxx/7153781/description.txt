Palm Heroes 2 Deluxe is an old-school strategy for Windows, Mac. Beautiful graphics and well-thought gameplay allow you to spend hours playing this game! You control heroes, that gather resources, conquer castles and new lands, and fight your opponents.

Features:
7 factions: Knight, Barbarian, Necromancer, Sorceress, Wizard, Warlock, Varangian.
Hot-seat mode that allows several people to play using one computer.
180 creature types
114 artifacts
Over 70 maps to play
