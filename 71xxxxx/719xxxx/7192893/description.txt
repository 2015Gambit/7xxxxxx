Promised Land

Technology Documentary hosted by John Robinson, published by Discovery Channel broadcasted as part of DC Forensic Factor series in 2005 - English narration


Information
------------------------------

When a Russian prostitute is found murdered, Los Angeles Homicide detectives quickly find the case to be anything but routine. Following a tangled string of clues, investigators are drawn into the murky world of high-end prostitution, illegal alien smuggling and Russian Organized Crime. Police must navigate their way through the dark underworld of Los Angeles - and hope that forensic science can nail the killer.
