Kokoyakyu: High School Baseball is a 2006 documentary film about high school baseball in Japan, the pastime that has turned into an obsession. The film follows two schools as they compete and head towards the 2003 (86th annual) tournament.[1]
Kokoyakyu: High School Baseball was directed by Kenneth Eng and written/produced by Alex Shear. It was aired on PBS in 2006 as part of its Point of View series.
