European Railway Journeys 
Filmed over twenty years ago and now available on DVD for the first time, the series of European Railway Journeys films explores the many varied cultures and countries across Europe, travelling by train which gives a different perspective to the cities and towns visited on the way. Stunning scenery, breathtaking architecture and beautiful landscapes combine to give the viewer a unique travel experience. This box set consists of: Rhine Express, Belgian Byways, Austrian Explorer, Riviera Bound, Sicilian Connection and Andalusian Express.



2)  Riviera Bound 
Few destinations conjure the romance and beauty of glorious France. Starting our journey in Paris, you'll view all landmarks which have made Paris famous. The journey visits the cities of Avignon and Marseilles, where sunlit cafes do old fashioned squares. Arriving on the Riviera, you'll be spellbound by the enticing beauty of the glamorous cities of Cannes, Nice and Monaco.
