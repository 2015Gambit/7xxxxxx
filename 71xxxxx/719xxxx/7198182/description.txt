Bad Teacher (2011) 
****************************************************************************
 A comedy centered around a foul-mouthed, junior high teacher who, 
after being dumped by her sugar daddy, begins to woo a colleague -- a 
move that pits her against a well-loved teacher. 

 Director: Jake Kasdan
 Writers: Gene Stupnitsky, Lee Eisenberg
Stars: Cameron Diaz, Jason Segel and Justin Timberlake
