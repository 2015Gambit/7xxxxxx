----------------------------------------------------------------
GWEN STACY DISCOGRAPHY
----------------------------------------------------------------

This Gwen Stacy discography contains 4 releases, including...

 * 2005 - I Believe In Humanity EP
 * 2006 - Demo
 * 2008 - The Life I Know
 * 2009 - A Dialogue

Gwen Stacy was an American metalcore band that was signed with Solid State Records. Their name came from the comic book character Gwen Stacy, who appeared in the comic book The Amazing Spider-Man as the girlfriend of Peter Parker. They were from Indianapolis, Indiana, and were active between 2004 and 2010.
