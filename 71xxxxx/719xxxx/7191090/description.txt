The Falklands: Healing the Wounds

War Documentary hosted by Matthew Gravelle, published by BBC in 2012 - English narration


Information
------------------------------

Matthew Gravelle narrates this documentary following three Welsh veterans of the Falklands conflict as they return to the islands to make peace with their pasts. With contributions from Dilwyn Rogers, David Jones and Stephen Dawkins.
