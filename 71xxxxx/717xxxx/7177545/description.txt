Björn J:son Lindh  is a Swedish flautist, keyboardist and composer. Although Lindh is a flautist, he has made use of various synthesizers on many of his recordings. Some of his songs are relatively experimental, using up-to-date technology and instruments of that time, such as the Synclavier.

Staffan Scheja is a succesful swedish consert pianist. He mostly plays classical music.

Together, Björn and Staffan has created music in a mix between New Age and classical.
