Encode:
well how many dvd do you need for making a dvdrip - yes normally 1, in this case i had to use 3.
what a complicated release, took me couple hours to do.

there is a russian dvd,a german dvd and a french dvd already out for a uk movie.
all pal, but every dvd itself didnt have what i needed to make a perfect version.
the russian dvd has:
english audio but no eng subs which are needed for the french speaking parts only russian subs

the german dvd has:
english subs for foreign parts only but no eng audio just german and french

the french dvd has:
english audio but only french subs no english one

also videoquality was different on all discs.

so i had to use all dvds to make one.
took the best video from the french dvd, the audio from the russian, and the subs from the german dvd.
had to manually adjust the subs from the german to the french video since the intro was different and hardcoded them onto the french video.

all together now, will be identical to the retail disc thats coming out in 2 month in UK and US perhaps too.

Added brightness,sharpness,rgb and denoise
got very nice
