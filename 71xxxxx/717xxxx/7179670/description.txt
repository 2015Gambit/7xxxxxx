WHAT NOT TO WEAR

Two fashion stylists and a team of hair and makeup advisors help revamp the look of individuals who have been nominated by their friends for makeovers due to their lamentable appearance, using a $5,000 budget.

SEASON 8 EPISODE 1 - Mindy Cohn

Mindy Cohn was an adorable child star from the popular '80s sitcom, The Facts of Life. Today she's a vibrant 44-year-old actress who's confident and spunky and very happy with herself and with her life. The problem is that she's struggling to balance her private and public persona and hiding in a frumptastic wardrobe filled with oversized shapeless frocks. Stacy and Clinton show Mindy how her outside image doesn't match up with the confident, sunny woman on the inside. So how did one of television's most beloved kid actors fare?
