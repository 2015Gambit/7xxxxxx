Pickin' on Nashville is the debut album of American country rock band The 
Kentucky Headhunters. It features the singles "Walk Softly on This Heart of 
Mine", "Oh Lonesome Me", "Dumas Walker", and "Rock & Roll Angel", all of 
which charted in the Top 40 on the Hot Country Songs charts. "Oh Lonesome 
Me" was also the highest charting, at #8. The album won a Grammy Award 
for the band in 1991.

"Walk Softly on This Heart of Mine" is a cover of a Bill Monroe song, "Skip a 
Rope" a cover of a Henson Cargill song, and "Oh Lonesome Me" a cover of a 
Don Gibson song.
