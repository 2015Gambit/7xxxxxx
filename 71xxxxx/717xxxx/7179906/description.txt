WHAT NOT TO WEAR

Two fashion stylists and a team of hair and makeup advisors help revamp the look of individuals who have been nominated by their friends for makeovers due to their lamentable appearance, using a $5,000 budget.

SEASON 8 EPISODE 11 - Maggie

Maggie is a smart, vibrant 35-year-old mom of two with an adoring husband and a corporate administrator job she wants to take to the next level. She used to dress to impress in the latest country western style, but a lifetime of crippling low self-esteem and poor body image is having serious consequences: Her "I give-up" style is keeping her career at a standstill. Can Stacy and Clinton convince this mom that by making herself a priority, the whole family prospers? Or will she continue to suffer from her lifelong confidence issues?
