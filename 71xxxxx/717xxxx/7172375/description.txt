This documentary by Kevin Brownlow, one of the most respectable film historians, highlights European silent cinema. Its 6 episodes, consisting of archival footage, film snippets and new interviews, cover the most important developments of the first decades of cinematic language in Europe. The narration by Kenneth Branagh and the music by Carl Davis (and others) make this lengthy introduction in film history a very pleasant learning experience.
