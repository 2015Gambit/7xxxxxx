Haiyoru! Nyaruko-san - Episode 01



The story of Haiyore! Nyaruko-san centers around Nyaruko, a formless Cthulhu deity of chaos (Nyarlathotep) who can take on the shape of a seemingly ordinary silver-haired girl. Mahiro Yasaka is a normal high school boy who is chased by aliens one night, until Nyaruko saves him.



Release date       : April 09, 2012


Audio language     : Japanese


SubTitle language  : English


Genres             : Comedy, Parody, Sci-Fi


Producers          : Xebec
