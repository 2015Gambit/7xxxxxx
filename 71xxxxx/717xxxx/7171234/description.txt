From BBC Radio 4 Extra. Written by D.H. Lawrence.

Rawdon's Roof (read by Andy Hockley)

A man swears that no woman shall sleep under his roof again, but what does his lover feel about it?

Her Turn (read by Peter Meakin)

When her husband's colliery goes on strike, a wife attempts to gain a share of his strike pay.

The Christening (read by Peter Meakin)

A grown-up family are haunted by their brutal father's influence. Will a new baby bring about a change?

Goose Fair (read by Peter Meakin)

An unexpected fire at Selby's factory proves to be a turning point in the relationship between Will and Lois.

The Shades of Spring (read by Peter Meakin)

Addy Syson returns to the unchanging 'country of his past'. But what of the girl he left behind?
