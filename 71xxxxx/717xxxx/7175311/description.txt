 Dagger Of Betrayal was formed in June 2009 in Thessaloniki, Greece by Antonis Kakaris and Angelos Tzamtzis. After some rehearsals and lives the final line up is Antonis Dimitriou (vocals), Angelos Tzamtzis (guitar), Antonis Kakaris (guitar), Odysseas Galatianos (bass) and Kostantinos Noulas (drums). Drawing influences from melodic death metal and metalcore bands they started writing songs and in March 2011 they released their first demo called "Retribution".
