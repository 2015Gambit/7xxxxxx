Filmed at the Birmingham NEC in July 1986 on the tour prior to the release of his "August" album, this sees Eric Clapton ably supported by Phil Collins on drums (and vocals on his No.1 hit "In The Air Tonight"), Nathan East on bass and Greg Phillinganes on keyboards going through many of his best known tracks including the full length version of "Layla". 

Tracklist:
01: Crossroads
02: White Room
03: Run
04: Miss You
05: Tearing Us Apart
06: Holy Mother
07: In The Air Tonight
08: Layla
09: Sunshine Of Your Love
