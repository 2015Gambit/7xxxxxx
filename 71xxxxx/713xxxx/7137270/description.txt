Wrath Of The Titans v1.1.1
Requirements: 2.2+
Overview: The official mobile game of the highly anticipated sequel to " clash of the titans " 2010.


5 enviroments to explore: The prison of tartar, argo city, labyrinth, village and the sky. 

Rich and challenging gameplay featuring platforming, fighting, chasing, and puzzle-solving.

A wide range of weapons and magical spells to use, including swords, hammers and spells.


Dozens of enemies from harpies & makhai to mythological beasts like the minotour and kronos.
