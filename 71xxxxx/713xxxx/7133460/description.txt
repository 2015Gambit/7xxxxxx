Carl's Classics is a game collection with 14 classic games such as chess, backgammon, checkers, reversi, parchisi, crosswords, puzzles, and more. Select the title you want to play from the intuitive main menu and enjoy the bright, full-color graphics. Each title includes numerous settings to make the game easier or more difficult to play, and in many cases there are undo options and hints. Detailed help instructions teach you how to master unfamiliar games. The game also allows you to play against a person over a network or across the Internet, and solo gamers will enjoy competing with an animated computer character that will challenge your every move.
