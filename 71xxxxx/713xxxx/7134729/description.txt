
The one and only magazine designed for aquatic gardeners and dedicated to Nature Aquarium, an aquarium style that recreates a natural landscape and ecosystem in an aquarium. Since its initiation in the 1980's by a world-renowned aquascaper, Takashi Amano who is also the editorial supervisor of this magazine, Nature Aquarium has gained its fans and followers around the world. As its layout techniques and artistry have continued to progress, AQUA JOURNAL features the latest technical know-how, beautiful aquascape galleries, seasonal topics, popular plants and fish, and global Nature Aquarium trend, providing tips to realize your dream aquascape.
