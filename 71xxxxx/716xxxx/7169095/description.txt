Word Wonders: The Tower of Babel is is an innovative mix of word game and roleplaying game! Fight in thrilling duels against mighty creatures and unleash enormous spells and attacks by forming magic words. Unravel mysteries, create powerful artifacts in the ancestral forge, battle daunting foes, and build up your hero's powers - all through the power of words!
