Chelsea Wolfe is an American singer-songwriter and eponymous band from California, USA, currently operating out of Los Angeles. Her style has been described as "doom-folk", and her music has been described as a "specific brand of drone-metal-art-folk".

She has released two full length albums: The Grime and the Glow, released December 28, 2010, and Apokalypsis in October 2011. Both albums were released on Pendu Sound Recordings.
