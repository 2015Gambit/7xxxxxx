Enter the Labyrinths - a DJ mix by the Tapestry Weavers

A DJ mix of sliced-up techno, pop, electronic, leftfield rock and classical music, inter-laced with spoken word and TV themes. The mix takes influence from the DJ mixes of Erol Alkan and Squarepusher, taking in the weird and the wonderful - ranging over Gothic Beauty to Zany Craziness and back again.
