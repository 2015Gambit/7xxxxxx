The Secret of Margrave Manor: Remastered brings Edwina's first escapade back more beatutifully than ever before! This classic hidden object adventure game is set in a spooky old manor house in the English countryside - you and razz assume the role of Edwina Margrave, trying to find your grandfather who went missing in mysterious circumstances 10 years ago.


Your only clues to his whereabouts are scattered around the Manor. It is your job to collect and decipher the clues by searching the manor room by room to uncover its secrets and discover your family's forgotten past.
