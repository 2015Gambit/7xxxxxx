Grateful Dead Download Series Volume 4 - 6/18/1976
Initial release : August 2005

The fourth in a series of shows made available by downloading from the official Grateful Dead site. Music from the June 18, 1976 show at the Capitol Theater, Passaic, NJ. Also includes bonus tracks from other shows in late June 1976.
