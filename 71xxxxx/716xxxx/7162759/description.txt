ABOUT THIS GAME
************************
Hitman Absolution, the latest game in the works at IO Interactive and scheduled for 2012, on PC, Xbox 360 and PS3. Hitman Absolution follows Agent 47, a cold-blooded assassin, who takes on his most dangerous contract to date. Betrayed by those he once trusted - and now hunted by the police - he suddenly finds himself at the center of a dark conspiracy and must embark on a personal journey through a corrupt and twisted world.
