The Chocolate Lovers' Club

From BBC Radio 4 Extra. Written by Moya O'Shea; Full-cast dramatisations starring Eleanor Bron, Paula Wilcox and Sylvia Syms.

At a village club, five women take turns to create a chocolate delight and tell a chocolate tale.

Episode 1: The villagers enjoy Sarah's inaugural creation as she recalls life selling chocolate.
Episode 2: Rosie harks back to her 21st birthday to reveal a shameful chocolate memory.
Episode 3: Charlotte recalls how chocolate led to a French adventure with a stranger.
Episode 4: Clementine remembers her stint as the owner of the Muchly Bottom village chocolate shop.
Episode 5: Mysterious Mrs Drexl is last to share a chocolate tale - and the members soon discover why.
