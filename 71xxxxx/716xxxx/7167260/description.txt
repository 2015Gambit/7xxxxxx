Diely Moussa Kouyaté - Le Temps
2008

This is Kouyaté's second solo album, after having played with Rail Band de Bamako and every other important name in Mandingo music, it's no surprise many of his friends came for a little help, among them Salif Keita, Kanté Manfila, Abdoulaye Diabaté, Check Tidiane Seck, Mamani Keita, Amadou Sodia and many others. It's an impressive list of friends and it's a very enjoyable album.
