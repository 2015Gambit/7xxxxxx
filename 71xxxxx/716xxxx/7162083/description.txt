NDrive 10.1.15

Key Features:

TTS - Text-to-Speech - NDrive is now able to "communicate" with the names of the streets. With this feature, you hear the voice instruction, pointing to a particular street, you are going to lead. 
 
Signposts - Road signs are displayed on top of the map in order to better understand the complicated intersections and highways. 
 
Lane Assistance, - choose the right lane and the safest route. 

Online POIs - POIs retrieved in real time. 

2010 Maps - latest maps. The maps are updated to the latest version, equipped with the latest changes on the road and POI updates 

NDrive Store - Now you can easily download new maps, voices and radars.
