The A1 WMA Audio CD 1.0 is software that allows you to burn WMA (Windows Media Audio) and many other audio files (such as the popular MP3/WAV audio formats) to a standard audio CD and to WMA/MP3 CDs. fast and easy!Also, A1 WMA Audio CD 1.0 contains the ability to edit the WMA tracks on a graph so you can cut and edit your files, easily.
