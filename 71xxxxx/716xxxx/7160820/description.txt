"Dance Again" is a song by American recording artist and actress Jennifer Lopez, featuring American 
rapper Pitbull. It was released on April 2, 2012 by Epic Records. "Dance Again" is an uptempo dance-pop song, 
written by AJ Junior, The Chef, Enrique Iglesias, Pitbull and RedOne, who also produced the song. It marks the 
third time that Lopez and Pitbull collaborated on a song; the previous songs being the promotional single
"Fresh Out the Oven" (2009) and the single "On the Floor" (2011).
