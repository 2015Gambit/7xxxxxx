Cast:
Jedara Bale is a Kannada film starring Dr Rajkumar, Jayanti, K S Ashwath, Narasimharaju and Udaykumar. It was the first of series of movies created along the lines of the James Bond films. The film was directed by DoraiRaj-S K Bhagavan.

Summary:
Dr Raj plays a CID Police Agent, who is code-named as "999". The story revolves around the attempt to stop a formula which can covert any metal into gold reaching the hands of hooligans. Uday Kumar plays the honcho of the Villain Gang.

Trivia:
The movie had many firsts in the Kannada Industry, like the hero not using wigs, the heroine donning swim suits, cabaret dances.
The success of the movie inspired 3 more Bond movies were made with Dr Rajkumar
Goa dalli CID 999
Operation Jackpot nalli CID 999(debut film of Hindi actress Rekha)
Operation Diamond Racket
More movies followed like ratnagiri rahasya and Dwarakish's kulla series.
