In Dragon Age 2, players find themselves in the role of a poor refugee Hawke, who comes to power to become the most important figure in the fantastic world of Dragon Age. The Legend of Hawke's rise is shrouded in a cloud of myths, stemming from his reputation of a man who could survive infection and become a champion Kirkwall. The new story takes place in a span of 10 years and the players influence its development through their own difficult decisions, collecting party resourceful allies in battle, gaining fame and fortune and Leaving tracks in their own history. The way you play the game, his signature on the fate of the world.
