iNFORMATiON
-----------

'Now That's What I Call Music 81' is the jaw dropping eighty-first instalment in the music franchise that compiles the crème de la crème of the most current and up-to-date chart toppers each and every year. '81' is no exception, sealing all the biggest and best tracks of this past year into one splendid release that is sure to delight the ears of all modern mainstream music lovers.

This release boasts some of the biggest names in the music industry and their number one chart smashers. 'Now That's What I Call Music 81''s tracklist ranges from Gotye's 'Somebody That I Used To Know', Coldplay's 'Paradise', and Jessie J's 'Domino', to LMFAO's 'Sexy And I Know It', and Lady Gaga's 'Marry The Night', to name but a few. Spanning across the genres of hip-hop and R&B (Drake's 'Take Care' featuring Rihanna), to the more indie influenced (Florence + The Machine's 'Shake It Out'), and of course classic pop (Lana del Rey's 'Born To Die' and Pixie Lott's 'Kiss The Stars'), this release has it all.


Genre: Pop
Year: 2012
