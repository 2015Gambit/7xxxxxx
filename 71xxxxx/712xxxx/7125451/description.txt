Direction Reaction Creation is an anthology released by the British punk rock band, The Jam, in 1997. 
It includes 117 tracks over 5 Discs, including all of the songs from their singles (although the A 
sides are present in the studio versions only) and six studio albums. Songs marked with indicate 
a Paul Weller solo recording.

The box set reached #8 in the UK charts. A year later, another anthology box set, this time by 
Weller's second band The Style Council was released, titled The Complete Adventures Of The Style 
Council.
