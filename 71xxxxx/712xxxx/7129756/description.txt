A path beyond is a W3d based online fps based in Command & conquer red alert.
Repair with engineers,Drop c4 with tanya, cruise  in light tanks,break through enemy defenses with mammoth tank.
Longbow,hind helicopters. Stealth phase tanks and shocking tesla tanks.
Dont forget the nuclear silo!.
Play Red alert through the eyes of Soviet or Allies.

Minimum requirements:
Pentium III 500 Mhz or equivalent
RAM: 128 MB
Video Memory: 32 MB
Hard Drive Space: 900 MB
