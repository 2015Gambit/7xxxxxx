Foreign vibes of all kinds have captured the British imagination over the years, as this 
sunny clipathon proves. Thrill again to Vanessa Paradis’s Joe le Taxi, Doina de Jale from 
Romania’s Gheorghe Zamfir and the similarly panpipe-tastic Cacharpaya by part-Chilean
combo Incantation.

To get you into holiday mood there’s Y Viva España (hard to hear without striking a castanet
clutching pose) and the Gypsy Kings’ irresistible version of Volare. The pub-piano knees-up 
of Winifred Atwell sounds amusingly incongruous here, about as exotic as a cone of jellied eels.

About this programme

Compilation of archive performances by international artists who found success in the UK charts, 
including Greek singer Demis Roussos, French star Vanessa Paradis, Swedish vocalist Sylvia and 
Romanian pan-pipe player Gheorghe Zamfir.
