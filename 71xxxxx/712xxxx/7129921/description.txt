In an unknown remote part of Africa attend Aquatigans, a coastal nation that worships Balu their living god. The area is rich in pearls which are sacrificed to the god. The only one who could give life to Balu is the high priest Palanth. He forces the young Mara to marry the god Balu, but she is already in love with another and manages to escape. She ends up with Tarzan and Jane, but her people have not forgotten her and warriors sent to fetch her back.
