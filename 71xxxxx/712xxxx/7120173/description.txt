The Tales of Dunk and Egg is a series of novellas written by George R. R. Martin, set in the world of his Song of Ice and Fire cycle. Three novellas have appeared so far: 

1. The Hedge Knight (1998)
2. The Sworn Sword (2003)
3. The Mystery Knight (2010)

The stories relate the adventures of Dunk and Egg, beginning with their meeting eighty-nine years before the events of A Game of Thrones.

All stories are in PDF format, properly formatted and error free (typos from the source texts have also been corrected).
