Eindhoven, located in the Dutch province of Noord-Brabant, is an essential airport for the region. OryxSim has recreated the airport as well as the adjacent industrial area for FS2004, FSX and Prepar3D. The airport is a base for Ryanair.

Features:

    Highly detailed rendition of all areas of Eindhoven Airport
    Custom ground textures (incl. seasonal textures)
    Corrected roads.
    Custom night lighting
    Custom Vehicles
    (FSX/Prepar3D Only) Volumetric Grass
