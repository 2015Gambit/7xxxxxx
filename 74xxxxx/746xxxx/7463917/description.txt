Catch a Fire (2006)

Dutch
Drama over het terrorisme in Zuid-Afrika tijdens de Apartheid.
Gebaseerd op het waargebeurde verhaal van Patrick Chamusso (Derek Luke),
die in z'n eentje aanvallen tegen het regime uitvoert.
Een politieman (Tim Robbins) onderzoekt de zaak.

Englisch
A political thriller: the real-life story of a South African hero's journey to freedom.
In the country's turbulent and divided times in the 1980s,
Patrick Chamusso is an oil refinery foreman and soccer coach who is apolitical - until he and his wife Precious are jailed.
Patrick is stunned into action against the country's oppressive reigning system, 
even as police Colonel Nic Vos further insinuates himself into the Chamussos' lives.
