This Amazingly Symmetrical World
by Lev Tarasov


This book explores the concept of symmetry starting from everyday concepts and going all the way upto symmetry in Quantum Mechanics. This is one of the few Mir books which were technical and was printed completely in color.

The book was translated from the Russian by Alexander Repyev and was first published by Mir in 1986.
