Today I Caught the Plague is a Canadian progressive metal that takes use of using clean vocals entirely. Today I Caught the Plague is an up and coming band that is very talented, touring with acts such as Protest the Hero.

Today I Caught the Plague is:
David Journeaux - Vocals
Matt Young - Keyboards
Ben Davis - Guitar
Steve Rennie - Guitar
Eric Stone - Bass
Michael Ieradi - Drums

Track listing:
1) The Consequence of Fratricide
2) From Bulwark to Bane
3) Cimmerian Dusk
4) Irradiant Dawn
5) Of Fang and Flesh
6) Tempting Haiwa
7) A Cluster of Rogues
8) Sorrowbred
9) All Gallant's Oath
10) Origins (Of Measurable Being)
