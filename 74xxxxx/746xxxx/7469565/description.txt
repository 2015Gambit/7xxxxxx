Adler's Appetite, previously Suki Jones, is an American rock band from Los 
Angeles, California, formed in 2003. As of 2011, the lineup consists of Patrick

band perform covers of Adler's former band Guns N' Roses, primarily songs 
from the album Appetite for Destruction.
