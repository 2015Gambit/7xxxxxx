Ice Nine Kills is a hardcore/metalcore band that hails from Boston, MA. Their name comes from the fictional substance Ice Nine from the science fiction novel Cat's Cradle by Kurt Vonnegut. 

Ice Nine Kills is:
Spencer Charnas - lead vocals
Justin "JD" DeBlieck - lead guitar, vocals
Justin Morrow - rhythm guitar
Conor Sullivan - drums
Steve Koch - bass, vocals
