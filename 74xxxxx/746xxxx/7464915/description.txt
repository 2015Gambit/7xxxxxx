Plot :
Indian Rupee is a 2011 Indian satirical film written and directed by Ranjith, and produced by August cinema. The film stars Prithviraj, Thilakan, Rima Kallingal, Tini Tom and Jagathy Sreekumar in the lead roles. The film released on October 6, 2011, to highly positive response from critics and fans alike. Indian Rupee received numerous accolades including Kerala State Film Award for Best Film and National Film Awards for Best Feature Film in Malayalam.
