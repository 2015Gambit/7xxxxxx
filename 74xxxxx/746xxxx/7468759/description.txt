The Contortionist is a progressive deathcore band from the United States. They make use of varying time signatures and employ clean vocals on some very spacey melodies, all around a very good album. 

The Contortionist is: 
Robby Baca - Guitar
Jonathan Carpenter - Vocals / Keyboards
Joey Baca - Drmus
Cameron Maynard - Guitar
Christopher Tilley - Bass
