Season 1: Episode 5 (Ms. Palin Goes to Washington): Original air date: 7/3/12. Bristol and Willow join their mom, Sarah Palin, on her trip to Washington, DC for the CPAC convention. While in DC, Bristol encounters hecklers, hosts a book signing and comes face to face with a Sarah Palin impersonator/imposter. Sarah, Bristol and Willow provide a behind-the-scenes look as they experience a whirlwind weekend packed with political punch
