Laser Age in Optics by Lev Tarasov is a popular science book concerned with new trends in optics arising from development of the laser. The book has clear and concise description of many of the concepts in lasers and holograms and many things which are usually ignored are also talked about and is presented for a school level student.

Translated from the Russian by V. Kisin and published by Mir in 1981.
