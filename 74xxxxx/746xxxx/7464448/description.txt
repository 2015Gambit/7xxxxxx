Questions and Answers in School Physics 
by 
Aldina Tarasova and Lev Tarasov

The book is written in style of dialogue between a teacher and students, with misconceptions of the students explored in all branches of basic physics at the school level.

To put it in the words of the authors themselves:

    (Reader) will find this text to be perhaps more like a freely told story or, rather a freely conducted discussion.

This is a book on physics education. 

The English edition of this book was published in 1973 and was translated from the Russian by Nicholas Weinstein.
