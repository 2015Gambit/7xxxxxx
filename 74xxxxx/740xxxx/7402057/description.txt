Recently Defunct Indiana based Rockers formed from Farty Carbunkle and the New Twisted Riddlers and The Roach Brothers.  Their music was fresh but carried that Midwest Balls to the Wall drive you hear from a lot of Indie bands.  They were starting to make a name considering they come from SMALL TOWNS such as Logansport, Royal Center and Lucerne, In. Soon Becoming  local Festival Favorites in the land of the Corn.
