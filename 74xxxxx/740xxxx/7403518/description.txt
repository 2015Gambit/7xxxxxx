Two erotic short stories by Deidre Blue

The shaming of Jess (2012)

Jess is a romance writer who is just as uninspired in her personal life as she is in her professional life. When her rough -around-the-edges brother in law Leo crashes at her house for the weekend, she accidentally catches him pleasuring himself, and is suddenly forced to face her most primal desires. 




Blue Balls: A tale of Lust (2012)

Left in an uncomfortable state by his virginal girlfriend, Edward tries to relieve the pressure in his balls with a quick jerk off session. His private moment is interrupted, however, when an acquaintance of his mother, Mrs. Feldman shows up on his doorstep. Edward soon finds out that she's desperately in need of something, and he offers to give it to her. If she'll just do one thing...
