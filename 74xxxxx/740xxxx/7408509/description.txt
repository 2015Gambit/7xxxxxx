


           
              Jay-Z interrupts Kanye West speech -BET Awards 2012




On the BET Awards last night, Jay-Z gave Kanye West a taste of his own medicine.But what happened while Kanye was giving his acceptance speech left everyone in stitches.
While accepting his award, Kanye began, "I want to thank BET for.." when he was suddenly cut off by Jay-Z. The crowd erupted in laughter as they were taken down memory, when Kanye interrupted Taylor Swift at the 2009 VMA's. Nicki Minaj,Beyonce & Kim Kardashian also had a good laugh. The pair received award for "Video of the year"
