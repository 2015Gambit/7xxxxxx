Chic was an American disco and R&B band that was organized during 1976 by guitarist Nile Rodgers and bassist Bernard Edwards. It is known best for its commercially successful disco songs, including 'Dance, Dance, Dance (Yowsah, Yowsah, Yowsah)' (1977), 'Everybody Dance' (1977), 'Le Freak' (1978), 'I Want Your Love' (1978), 'Good Times' (1979), and 'My Forbidden Lover' (1979). The group regarded themselves as a rock band for the disco movement 'that made good on hippie peace, love and freedom'. (wikipedia)
