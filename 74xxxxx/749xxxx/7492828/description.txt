Three Kingdoms is a Chinese television series based on events in the late Han Dynasty and Three Kingdoms period of Chinese history. The plot is based on Luo Guanzhong's classical novel Romance of the Three Kingdoms, the historical text Records of the Three Kingdoms, and other related stories. Directed by Gao Xixi, the series had a budget of over 180 million yuan (US$30 million)[citation needed] and was released in May 2010.[1] The series was well received both domestically and internationally, earning an estimated 800 million yuan (US$133.3 million) in total as of May 2012
