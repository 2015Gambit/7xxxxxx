A promotion CD distributed with copies of the 3 CD After Midnight: Kean College, 2/28/80 release that were re-ordered from www.jerrygarcia.com. The CD comprises songs from the Jerry Garcia Band shows on 2/29/1980 and 3/1/1980.

Tracks

    * Dear Prudence (Lennon / McCartney)
    * When I Paint My Masterpiece (Dylan)
    * Russian Lullaby (Berlin)
    * That's All Right, Mama (Crudup)
    * Deal (Garcia / Hunter)
