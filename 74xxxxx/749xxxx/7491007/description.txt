Ovnimoon Records proudly presents the long awaited full length remix album release from Lupin the Spanish EDM master.

Lupin gathers the worlds top progressive psytrance producers for a special remix album that has had major impact on the dance floors across the world. 'Espacio de Mezclas' contains tracks from other artists like Zyce and Flegma, Inner State, Suntree, Ovnimoon and more, reworked with Lupin's sound imprint.

Born 24th of August in 1985 in Lleida, Spain, Miguel has always been amazed by electronic music. When he was 13 he began to experiment with techno sounds, following the preferences of his older brother. Two years later his brother acquires dj mixing equipment, moving Miguel's interest rapidly to the art of music mixing. After years studying music theory, playing piano, and getting a lot of new experiences producing new music in the studio, at 22 years old, his progressive psytrance project is born as Lupin.

Already well know for his debut album 'Siempre Secuaces' (Parabola Music), remix album 'Siempre Secuaces Remixes' (Synergetic), and compilation 'Progressiveland' (Synergetic) plus releases on labels like Fatali Music, Yellow Sunshine Explosion, Spiral Trax, Ovnimoon Records, Synergetic, Plusquam Recs, Zero Gravity Records, Dropland Records, his live act is asked for in countries like Greece, Croatia, Belgium, Hungary, Slovenia or Spain.

Lupin is open for bookings worldwide just get in touch for a blasting live act, a dj set, or all together!
