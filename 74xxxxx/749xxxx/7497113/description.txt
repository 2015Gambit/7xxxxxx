STORY: Corbeyran
ART: Alice Picard

SUMMARY:
On the day she was born, it was prophesied that Weena would cause misfortune on her family and people. With her gray hair and brown skin, she stood out among her people. One day a cloaked figure tells her that she is a descendant of a powerful magician and that she must keep this secret from others.

In another land Prince Morckoor fights against his father and the curse upon his bloodline. To defeat the curse and gain power, he must marry and have a child of a heiress of a "invisible branch".
