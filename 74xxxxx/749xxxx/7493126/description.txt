Gisingin Natin ang Gabi_1986_Pinoy Bomba Era(Lampel Cojuangco)


TAGALOG MOVIE (No Subtitles)Starring Daniel Fernando , Lampel Cojuangco Angelo is a painter currently searching for the path of his career and life. He therefore goes to Baguio wherein he meets a mysterious lady named Monica (Lampel Cojuangco) Eventually, he gets the recognition as an artist and he finally rises to fame after he makes a beautiful naked painting of her. Things go out of control as behind this seductive paintin les secrets, love, sex and crimes that seek justice.
