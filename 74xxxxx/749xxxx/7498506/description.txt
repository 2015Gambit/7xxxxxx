Glasgow: The Grit and the Glamour

Arts Documentary hosted by Alan Yentob, published by BBC broadcasted as part of BBC Imagine series in 2012 - English narration


Information
------------------------------

The story of the group of artists and curators who stormed the international art world and turned Glasgow into a global capital for contemporary art. Alan Yentob meets 2011 Turner Prize-winner Martin Boyce, along with previous winners Douglas Gordon, Simon Starling and Richard Wright, to recount how Glasgow became a city as famed for its contemporary art as it once was for its shipbuilding.
