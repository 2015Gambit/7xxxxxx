I am Super Ape - Primal Scream EP (2012)

1. Monki

2. Bipopular

3. Untergrund

4. Hit the Plane


First EP released by the Swedish band I am Super Ape.

Recorded at Studio Möllan with Jon Rinneby and Emil Isaksson and in the living room of Jonas Hagberth in Malmö, Sweden.
Mixed by Jonas Hagberth.
Mastered by Peter North at Frequency mastering, CA.

Produced by I am Super Ape.

Cover art by AKI ZUM.

Released in May 2012.
