I am Super Ape - Psychonaut EP (2012)

1. Semi Dog

2. Growin' Old with Jesus

3. Ape It


Second EP released by the Swedish band I am Super Ape.

Recorded at Tambourine Studios with Erik Sunding and in a commune in Bergsgatan 12, Malmö, Sweden. 
Mixed by Viktor Rinneby.
Mastered by DMS, UK.
Produced by I am Super Ape.

Cover art by Hampus Sundén.

Released in August 2012.
