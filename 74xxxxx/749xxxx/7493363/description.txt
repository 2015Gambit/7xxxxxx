Description

You are a very Hungry Dragon who has been trapped by an evil power. You must find food and eat as much as you can so you can grow and break free of your cage.

Fans of the original snake will love Hungry Dragon. The exciting graphics, new controls and innovative camera perspective gives this classic a modern and exciting twist!

FEATURES
+ Hours of endless gameplay
+ 4 gorgeous locations
+ Simple to play yet challenging to master
+ Original music tracks
