One-off documentary revealing the technological advances that have
enabled North Branch Correctional Institute in Maryland, USA, to
become one of the most secure prisons in the world, holding more
than 1,400 of America's most dangerous convicts. Surprisingly, the
building was made possible thanks to historic engineering
breakthroughs at four earlier prisons - including the Tower of
London and Alcatraz Island - each of which featured an innovation
that allowed engineers to build even more secure facilities. Now,
with its impenetrable cells able to withstand bomb blasts and
technology ensuring dangerous inmates are kept safely away from each
other, North Branch may be the toughest yet
