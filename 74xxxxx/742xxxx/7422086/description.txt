================================

These files are from the DVD "The Prisoner: The Complete Series (40th Anniversary Collector's Edition) (1968)":
 
http://www.amazon.com/Prisoner-Complete-40th-Anniversary-Collectors/dp/B000FOQ03C/).

The order of episodes on that DVD is not the order in which they were first broadcast, either in the U.K. or the U.S. The order is the order sanctioned by "The Prisoner Appreciation Society":
 
http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/List_of_The_Prisoner_episodes

These files are MKV-x264-AAC files. Windows Media Player cannot play them without an additional codec pack such as "DivX Plus Codec Pack":
 
http://www.divx.com/en/software/divx-plus/codec-pack

Use VLC instead:
 
http://www.videolan.org/vlc/
