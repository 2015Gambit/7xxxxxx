WebCam Deluxe - software for web-cameras, that allows you to remotely monitor just outside the shesnadtsatyu (in Deluxe version) cameras. It uses H264 video converter that allows you to get a good picture quality with a small stream. Shoots video can be recorded in the formats MP4, MOV, FLV, SWF or AVI. Implemented the ability to schedule recording and tracking of moving objects in the frame. The program allows you to automatically send email notification to work with FTP and has the ability to secure network connections. The program also enables you to capture video from any USB and analog cameras with up to 30 frames per second. Supports a huge list of IP cameras. There is a built-WEB-server and the ability to broadcast video.
