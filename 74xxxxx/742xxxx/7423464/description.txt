Cue Club
 
Cue Club is a pool game in which you can customize the table, the cues and your set of balls. It features a wide variety of games, such as: 8 Ball, 9 Ball, Snooker, speed ball, killer, practice, slam, two tournament modes, and more. 
 
In order to use Cue Club you have to meet the following system requirements:
 
* Operative System: Xp / 2000 / Vista / 9x
 
Minimum System Requirements:
 
* Processor: 800 mhz / memory RAM: 256 MB
