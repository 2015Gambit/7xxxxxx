This is an extended version of Grindhouse.

I've taken both of the separate versions of Planet Terror and Death Proof as well as the 2-in-one version with the fake trailers in the middle, recoded them and cut them both together.

No quality loss whatsoever, looks as good as usual, now it's just the 2-in-one version but with both of the extended cuts of Death Proof and Planet Terror. Subtitles are in English and fitting for all.
