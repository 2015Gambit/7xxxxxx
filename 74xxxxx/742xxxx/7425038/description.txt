Description:
K-Lite Codec Pack - a free pack of codecs, DirectShow filters and tools associated with them. This set differs from other similar packages, the absence of conflicts between codecs, easy to install and use that deservedly made him the most popular. The components of this kit allow you to do to play all the popular and a large number of rare video and audio formats. The most complete versions are versions of K-Lite Codec Pack Full and K-Lite Mega Codec Pack. The latter includes the K-Lite Codec Pack Full, and also contains an additional component of Real Alternative, focused on playing formats Real. More recently become available, another variation called Corporate. It is intended to apply to organizations that need to comply with certain conditions on the commercial software. In all of the assembly included a powerful alternative player Media Player Classic.
