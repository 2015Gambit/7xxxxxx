THE INVINCIBLE IRON MAN V5 #1 - 520 [MARVEL]

This Eisner Award winning series, written by Matt Fraction and with art by Salvador Larroca and Frank D Armata, is the flagship Iron Man title. This is the fifth volume of Invincible Iron Man which began in 2008, 40 years after the first volume of Invincible Iron Man which began in 1968. In January 2011, it was renumbered back to its original numbering and celebrated its 500th issue.

The series has been collected in the following Storylines:

Invincible Iron Man: The Five Nightmares - (#1-7)
Invincible Iron Man: Worlds Most Wanted 1 - (#8-13)
Invincible Iron Man: Worlds Most Wanted 2 - (#14-19)
Invincible Iron Man: Stark Disassembled - (#20-24)
Invincible Iron Man: Stark Resilient 1 - (#25-28)
Invincible Iron Man: Stark Resilient 2 - (#29-33)
Invincible Iron Man: My Monsters - (#500, #500.1)
Invincible Iron Man: Unfixable - (#501-502)
Invincible Iron Man: Fear Itself - (#504-509)
Invincible Iron Man: Demon - (#510-515)
Invincible Iron Man: Long Way Down - (#516-520)
