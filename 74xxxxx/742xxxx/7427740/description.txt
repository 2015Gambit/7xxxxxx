Original Source: MSPaint Adventures
Episodes: 12
Language: Japanese
Subtitles: English

Plot:

On his 13th birthday, John Egbert receives the latest computer game, an immersive simulation game called Sburb Beta. Working with his friends Rose Lalonde, Dave Strider and Jade Harley, they discover that the game allows the players to manipulate their reality. After starting their session, the kids realize it is much more than meets the eye, and John needs to take up the role as leader to ensure his friends survive.
