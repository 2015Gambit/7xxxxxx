Cousin Bette, 720x480

IMDB tt0068062

BBC 1971 mini-series

based on the novel by Honoré de Balzac

starring Margaret Tyzack, Colin Baker and Helen 
Mirren

Set in mid-19th century Paris, it tells the story 

of an unmarried middle-aged woman who plots the 

destruction of her extended family. Bette works 

with Valérie Marneffe, an unhappily married young 

lady, to seduce and torment a series of men.
