   The Laughter of God, and More Laughter are two books of philosophy by John Roland Stahl, including many essays on a wide variety of subjects: social, economic, and political philosophy, in addition to the Metaphysics drawn from Pythagoras, Hermetic Alchemy, the Kabbalah, and the I Ching. 
 
   This release includes the updated third edition of More Laughter, and also Tetragrammaton, the most abstract summary of the Keys of the Mysteries.
