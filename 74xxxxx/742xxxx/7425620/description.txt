Superbikes - When Britain Ruled the World

History Documentary hosted by Dave Moore, published by ITV in 2012 - English narration


Information
------------------------------

Documentary about World Superbike racing in the 1990s with contributions from Frankie Chili, Carl Fogarty, Neil Hodgson, Keith Huewen, Julian Ryder and James Whitham.

Produced in 2012 by Greenlight TV
