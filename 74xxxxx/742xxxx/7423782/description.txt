A misguided asteroid crashes with the moon in a tremendous explosion of rock and gravel. Within a few days begin to catastrophic and abnormal events occur on earth. The world's leading scientists, Alex Kittner (David James Elliot, from the television series JAG) and Maddie Rhodes (Natasha Henstridge, Species) has 39 days to stop the moon, otherwise the Earth - and all of humanity - to go under.
