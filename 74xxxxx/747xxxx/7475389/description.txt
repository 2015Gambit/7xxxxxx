DAMITA HADDON DISCOGRAPHY
----------------------------------------------------------------------------------

This Damita Haddon discography is made up of the following 3 titles...

 * 2001 - Damita
 * 2008 - No Looking Back
 * 2012 - Anticipation

Damita Haddon is an American gospel singer. She is married to Stellar Award winner Deitrick Haddon. Haddon released her first album, entitled Damita, in 2001. on Atlantic Records. Her second album, No Looking Back, was released in 2008 on Tyscot Records. The album's first single was the title track, "No Looking Back." Damita just recently released her third in 2012 entitled "Anticipation."
