Star-studded cast on a ship sailing from Veracruz to Bremerhaven in the 1930s. If you want to see "Col. Klink" in a serious role, this is your chance!.

A lot of strange interactions take place Grand Hotel-style, and much of the conversation deals with how the characters will relate to the darkening scene in Europe - mostly by ignoring it.

The dwarf has a great line at the end - it's worth wating for.
