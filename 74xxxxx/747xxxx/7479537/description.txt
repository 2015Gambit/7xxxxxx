Strap on your helmets or fasten your seat belts coz you can choose between motorcycle or car this time!
Dare to drive & tackle oncoming traffic for the first time!
Reckless is an engaging action arcade racing game where players have to be at their senses all the time! A little distraction can cause an accident!

How to play:

    Tilt to steer
    Tap to accelerate
    Action button for fire

Features:

    Choose between motorcycle or car
    Addictive gameplay!
    Adjust Sensitivity control

Required Android O/S : 2.2+
