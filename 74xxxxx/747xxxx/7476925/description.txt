Men in Black director Barry Sonnenfeld and comics genius Grant Morrison's project, in which dinosaurs fight aliens in a massive multimedia endeavor is set to span a film trilogy and several tie-in comics. It's described as "a philosophical treatise on manifest destiny, genocide and indigenous revolt" and "instead of anotherblockbuster thrown onto Hollywood's disposable entertainment pile, it's a pointed critique of overreaching civilization at the edge of oblivion."
