~Just Dance Wii 2~


Just Dance Wii 2 is the name of the Japanese edition of the Just Dance video game series for the Wii and is a music video game developed by Ubisoft. It was announced by Satoru Iwata in a Nintendo Direct conference and released on the Wii on July 26, 2012.
