Adi (Tushar Kapoor) and Sid (Riteish Deshmukh) are two friends. Adi who is the aspiring hero and falls in love with Simran(Neha Sharma), a call-centre agent. Sid is an inspiring DJ who is waiting for a big break meets his match at a Fashion Show(Sarah Jane Dias). Things start to get confusing when the two girls go to Goa to meet Sarah's father. Marlo(Anupam Kher) is the one who thinks his mother is reincarneted as a dog when baba 3g (Chunky Pandey) tells him. Then things take hilarious turn when Sid's breeding dog takes things in his own hands.
