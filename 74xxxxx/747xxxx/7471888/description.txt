Peter Parker is an outcast high school student who was abandoned by his parents as a child and raised by his Uncle Ben and Aunt May. Like most teenagers, Peter tries to find out who he is and how he became who he is today.

Peter is also in love for the first time in his schoolmate Gwen Stacy, and together they fight with love, commitment and confidentiality. When Peter discovers a mysterious suitcase that belonged to his father, so begins a quest to understand his father's disappearance and it leads him to a lab at Oscorp and Dr. Curt Connor, his father's former partner. When Spider-Man collides with Connor's alter ego, The Lizard, Peter must make life-changing choices and use their powers to create their own destiny and become a hero.
