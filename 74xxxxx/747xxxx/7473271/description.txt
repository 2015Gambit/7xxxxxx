Scooby-Doo! Camp Scare is the fifteenth direct-to-DVD movie based upon the Scooby-Doo Saturday morning cartoons, released on September 14, 2010,[1] seven months after the release of the Scooby-Doo! Abracadabra-Doo. The movie sold 53,389 units in its first week and as of March 2012, it has sold approximately 190,000 units.[2]
