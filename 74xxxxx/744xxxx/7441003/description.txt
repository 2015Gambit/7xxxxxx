21st Century Sex Slaves
Undercover investigators hunt down a ruthless multi-million-dollar trafficking ring that forces thousands of Asian women to be sex slaves.
This lucrative business is run by ruthless gangs who target vulnerable women and force them to work on the streets
In Bangkok, where sex is one of the city's biggest commodities, a crack team headed by investigator Steve Galster, attempts to bring down the leaders of a brutal trafficking ring who will stop at nothing to avoid capture.
