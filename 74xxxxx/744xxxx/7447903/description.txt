Description

Destroy your desktop!

Are you stressed out from work? Or once again mad at your mac? With this app, you can now cool down.

Destroy your desktop as you wish.

With the keys 1 to 6 you may select the following weapons:

1: Use a hammer to smash the screen
2: Cut the desktop with a chainsaw
3: Shoot with a machine gun
4: Make it sizzle with your flamethrower
5: Put stamps on the desktop
6: Let the termites work for you
