Laurent, poster advertising, organizes a party at his house to celebrate the beginning of the rehearsals for the first show he produces (a musical). But its star, Juliette, is the victim of a traffic accident and Laurent must urgently find a replacement. To his girlfriend Valerie, student at the Conservatoire, it will be "tonight or never" opportunity to demonstrate the extent of her talents by playing all her charms to get the role that Laurent does not think to offer her.
