Now That's What I Call Music! 82 is the stunning eight-second instalment of the UK NOW music franchise. This edition includes 12 number songs on the UK charts and comprises some of the biggest artist in the industry with the likes of Justin Bieber's Boyfriend; Fun's We Are Young, Maroon 5's Payphone as well as Chris Brown's Turn Up The Music. Featured artist includes Roc Nation's Rita Ora; Taylor Gang Or Die Wiz Khalifa; Eva Simons, Lazy Jay and many more.

NOW! 82 is the perfect compilation and collection of music in time for the biggest sporting event in the world - London Summer 2012 Olympics (Games Of The XXX Olympiad)
