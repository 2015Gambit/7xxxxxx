Unsupervised is an American animated comedy series created by David Hornsby, Scott Marder, and Rob Rosell. The series premiered January 19, 2012 on FX.[1] On March 30, 2012, Unsupervised was renewed for a second season.The series follows two 15-year-old best friends, Gary (Justin Long) and Joel (David Hornsby), as they try to navigate through teenage life, while also trying to do the right thing without the help of any parental supervision.
