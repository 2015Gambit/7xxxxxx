Periphery II: This Time It's Personal is the second album by Periphery, and was released on the 29th of June through Roadrunner Records Australia and on July 3, 2012 through Sumerian in America. Periphery II features guest solos from Guthrie Govan (The Aristocrats), Wes Hauch (The Faceless) and John Petrucci (Dream Theater).

On September 18, 2011, a live version of Facepalm Mute surfaced the web and was eventually confirmed to appear on their second album. On May 30, 2012, Periphery posted an album teaser featuring the intro track "Muramasa." The album's first single, "Make Total Destroy," was released on iTunes Tuesday, June 5th. The song "Scarlet" (originally an instrumental by Haunted Shores) was debuted on SiriusXM's Liquid Metal station on June 14th. On June 27, 2012, the full album was streamed on The Music's website.Additionally, the album was streamed in Sumerian Record's Youtube Channel on June 28, 2012.
