Uganda: Experiments in Aid

Sociopolitical Documentary hosted by Humphrey Hawksley, published by BBC broadcasted as part of BBC Our World series in 2011 - English narration


Information
------------------------------

Humphrey Hawksley reports on a UN project in northern Uganda which aims to end the region's long-term dependency on aid.
