Notes▼
Recorded in 2005 at Melodiya studio, mixed and mastered in 2007-2008 at Magihead studio. 
Vinyl cut at Dubplates & Mastering, Berlin May 2011.
The ritual Tibetan instruments as used by the ensemble include the following: Dunchen, Gyaling, Silnyen, Bub, Damaru, Kanling, Nga, Shang. The ensemble adheres to the rgyud-skad tradition of Tantric overtone chanting.
Originally released on CD by Sketis Music, Russia, 2008.
