Bluegrass Album, Vol. 6 ΓÇô Bluegrass Instrumentals is the sixth and final album by bluegrass supergroup, Bluegrass Album Band, released in 1996. Violinist Bobby Hicks and bassist Todd Philips reunites with the group, while Vassar Clements remains in the lineup for this ultimate recording to produce a fantastic double-fiddle sound together with Hicks.
 
Most of the songs are classics by Bill Monroe, two are delivered by band's mandolinist, Doyle Lawson.
