Gabriel Munro is a North American Folk Rock songwriter and artist. He has been involved with awareness campaigns and donates much of his proceeds to charitable causes.

This includes On The Beach, in his latest epic album, The Uninvited Gabriel Munro, and Amarok Mastered, a fundraising song for the Amarok Society of Canada.
