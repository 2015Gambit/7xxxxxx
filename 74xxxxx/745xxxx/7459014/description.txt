"Ready / We're Fire" is kevn's 2nd single, and first double a-side single.
"Ready" is an electropop song with a heavy beat, and powerful lyrics that show a strong side of kevn.
"We're Fire" is a pop song that takes inspiration from Spanish music, with lyrics that talk about not giving up no matter what happens.
The single also includes the instrumental version of "stardust", without the main vocals.
