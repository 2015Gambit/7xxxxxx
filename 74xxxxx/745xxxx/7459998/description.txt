I chockvågorna efter andra världskriget, under den kalla vintern 1946, skickas av misstag en grupp tyska krigsfångar till ett ryskt kvinnofångläger som styrs av kvinnliga fångvaktare. Fångvaktarna, som förlorat många närstående på grund av Nazisternas brutalitet, mottar de nyanlända fångarna med stor fientlighet och ser en möjlighet till hämnd...

Regissör: Tom Roberts
Manus: Natalia Portnova, Simon van der Borgh

Skådespelare: Vera Farmiga, Thomas Kretschmann, John Malkovich, Daniel Brühl, Natalie Press, Patrick Kennedy, John Lynch, Guy Flanagan



In the winter of 1946, in Leningrad, a group of German prisoners of war are sent to a female transit camp by the cruel Russian Commander Pavlov. When they arrive, the Russian female soldiers show the hostility to the enemies that have killed their husbands, families and friends; only Dr. Natalia and the cook treat the prisoners with dignity. Natalia has an agreement with Commander Pavlov to keep her former lover, who was wounded on the head during the war and is slow, in the camp instead of sending him to an institution in Siberia. Pavlov assigns Natalia to disclose members of the SS infiltrated in the group of prisoners. Natalia and the prisoner Max feel a great attraction for each other while the prisoner Klaus tries to convince Max to denounce a couple of prisoners to satisfy the Russian. Natalia convinces the businessman Yakov to organize an orchestra with the prisoners; they are invited to play in a ball...
