The Ed Show; July 10, 2012.

Ed started with Gov. Martin O'Malley (D) Maryland on Romney's tax evasion.

Ed talked with Johnathan Alter (MSNBC Political Analyst, Bloomberg View Columnist) about Obama's ideas being similar to Clinton.

Ed discussed 30 failed attempts by the House to repeal the Affordable Care Act with Rep. Steve Israel (D) NY (also chairman of DCCC).

The panel talked at Pennsylvania's blatant attempt to disenfranchise voters to let Romney win with Donna Lieberman (Executive Director of the New York Civil Liberties Union), John Bramnick (R) NJ (NJ assembly leader), Catherine Crier (Attorney, Author).

Ed discussed how Gov. Rick Scott (R) Florida closed the last Florida TB clinic in the middle of a TB outbreak.

Ed spoke with John Judge (Scranton, PA Firefighter, President of IAFF Local 60) about the cuts to emergency worker and public employees in the city to minimum wage.
