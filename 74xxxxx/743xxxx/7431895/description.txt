The Ed Show; July 9, 2012.

Ed starts with Howard Fineman (The Huffington Post, MSNBC Political Analyst) Obama's tax plan and Romney's support for the Bush tax cut.

Sen. Dick Durbin, Majority Whip (D) Illinois joined Ed on Mitt Romney's tax havens.

Richard Wolffe (MSNBC Political Analyst) discussed the GOP delusion that food stamps are slavery. 

The panel looked at Romney supporters and their problem with the middle class.
In the panel were Ron Christie (Republican Strategist), Keith Boykin (Democratic Strategist), John Fugelsang (Political Comedian).

Ed discussed how emergency service workers and public officials are taking massive pay cuts (some to minimum wage).

Chris Wojcik (Marine Biologist, Shark Expert) discussed sharks and their interactions with humans.
