Description
The game consists in knocking down the stars, using bombs.

To drop the bomb just tap where you want to throw it.

You have 2 types of bombs, black bombs that explode two seconds after released, and the red bombs that explode only when you tap the screen again.


- Real time 3D physics simulation

- Destructible environments, break walls, sculptures, etc..

- Use your headphones to experience 3D game sounds!

- You have many levels and 4 different worlds to play.


The game was made using the UDK engine (Unreal Development Kit) from Epic Games.

Enjoy it.
