The Polynesian languages constitute a branch of the Oceanic languages within the Austronesian language family, which includes major languages such as Filipino-Tagalog, Malay-Indonesian and Javanese.
This pack contains learning resources for four Polynesian languages:
1.Maori, an official language of New Zealand.
2.Hawaiian, an official language of the state of Hawaii.
3.Tahitian, which is spoken in French Polynesia.
4.Samoan, an official language of the Samoan Islands.
