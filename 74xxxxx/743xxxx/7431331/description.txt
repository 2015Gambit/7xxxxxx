Up with Chris Hayes; Sunday, July 8, 2012.

Climate Change was discussed, the panel included: Esther Armah (WBAI.org), Thomas Mann (Brookings Institution, co-author of "It's Even Worse Than It Looks:") Joan Walsh (MSNBC Political Analyst, author of "What's the Matter with White People", editor at Salon.com), and Eric Klinenberg (NYU Sociology Professor, author of "Heat Wave").

Bill McKibben (author of "eaarth", founder of 350.org) joined via satellite.

After the break Norman Ornstein (American Enterprise Institute) joined the panel (replacing Eric Klinenberg) and they discussed the healthcare decision and the ACA (healthcare law).

Then the panel discussed Romney's offshore investments as Stephen Moore (Former President of Club For Growth, online.WSJ.com) joined via satellite.

"You Should Know" appears on Sundays.
