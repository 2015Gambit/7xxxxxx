Description

Crazy Cubes: A simple and Brain teasing puzzle game to test your skills. The objective of the game is to remove all green blocks in a level by rolling the cube over it. You'll come across many crazy blocks to handle as you progress with different levels.

96 levels and more level coming soon in next update.

Controls: Swipe the screen in the direction you want to move the cube.
