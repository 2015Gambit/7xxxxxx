
Connect M is an addictive tile matching game. Simply click two tiles of same color and shape to remove them. They will go away if they can be connected with at most three lines.

Features:

37 levels in 9 worlds. Each world has its extra feature
High quality graphics
High quality sound effects and soundtrack

What's New in Version 1.2

Level selection:
You can now choose to start the game in every world you completed at least once.

Requirements
Intel, Mac OS X 10.6 or later
