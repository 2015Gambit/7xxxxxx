
Description

Jetlane is a sci-fi racing game where you get to race on the most extreme tracks in the galaxy!

Race through loopings, tunnels, huge jumps and defy gravity!

There are 24 unique tracks with stunning space atmospherics.

You start with your grandpa's old spaceship but soon enough you will unlock new and faster spaceships!

Jetlane has a selection of awesome music to enhance your racing experience!

Requirements: OS X 10.6.6 or later
*Note: not recommended for Macs with integrated Intel GMA950 chips. (macbook before november 2007)
