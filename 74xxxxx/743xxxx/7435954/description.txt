Description

Attention! The game is very addictive!
Game rules are very simple. Move the colored shapes on the field at the center of the screen. Do not let the "wells" filled. Areas of the figures of the same color and break free space. The game has different bonuses: slowing the game, bomb and many others. You can go play through the levels sequentially or in a "survival".

Nice game!

Requirements: OS X 10.6 or later
