English
St. Jude Square is a neighborhood living in fear and despair.
The dueling gangs of local kingpins Dash and Antuan terrorize the streets and the citizens live without a shred of hope...
until mysterious stranger Ryan Hong arrives in town.
He begins to play one gang against the other using his unparalleled martial arts skills,
and by calling on the teachings of his brilliant mentor Tiano to find the strength to battle back.
However, just as he begins to bring the community under control, Hong is confronted by Mr. V, the town's ruthless and corrupt police chief.
At first Mr. V is impressed by Hong's skill, but soon sees Hong as a threat to his regime,
and the two warriors are locked in a head-to-head battle,
pitting the fear and corruption of Mr. V's regime versus the new beginning Hong represents for the people of St. Jude Square.
