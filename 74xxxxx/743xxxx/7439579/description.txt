3 Bachelors is a 2012 Hindi comedy/romance movie directed by Ajai Sinha. This movie was released on 06 July 2012 in cinemas. Movie was originally made almost 10 years ago and was never released for unknown reasons. It was finally released on 06 July 2012 amidst lot of controversy and litigations between producers and lead actor Sharman Joshi.[1] The film stars Sharman Joshi, Raima Sen, Riya Sen, Manoj Pahwa, Himani Shivpuri, Manish Nagpal, Master Salin, Master Kashish and Nigar Khan amongst others. Movie was released after the recent release and success of much talked about Ferrari Ki Sawaari which also had Sharman Joshi as lead actor.
