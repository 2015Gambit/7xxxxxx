Description
~~~~~~~~~~~
Twixtor intelligently slows down, speeds up or changes the frame rate of your image sequences.

ATTENTION!!
~~~~~~~~~~~~~
Delete previous version of Twixtor and delete Library-Preferences-REVisoneffects before installing plugin.

Compatibility
~~~~~~~~~~~~~
Twixtor and Twxtor Pro supported in Final Cut Pro 6.0.3 and up,and FCP 7.
Twixtor supported in Final Cut Pro X 10.0.3 and up.
