Sin City 2005 Re-Recut, Extended, Unrated


What's always bugged me about the Sin City Recut is the credits after every stupid part. This might be fun if you watch them seperatly, but seriously, who the hell does that anyway? 

Well, this version fixes that, no credits after each story, only the credits of the theatrical version at the end. The sound is just a tad bit cut, but I'm sure you won't mind.

Unfortunately no AC3 surround audio, just plain mp3 2.0 ... and I do not want to invest all that time again downloading the source files since I've deleted them since.

Comment on whether you like this version, or not. The quality of the video should be superb.
