iDocument makes documents managing as easy as creating them. It helps you to organize and manage documents on Mac; it puts all of your documents in order in its library. With Tag, search, smart folder and flag features, iDocument not only gives you full range of the managing function for you to manage documents on Mac, on top of that, it is the first semi-automatic document management program in the Mac world, it can record your operational habits, and batch process it automatically.
