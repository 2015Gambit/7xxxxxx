The early 1980's were a step backward for the gay movement as Aids, the Gay Disease, created a new atmosphere of intolerance.

While struggling to find his own identity, Johnny Thorne is kicked out of his home when his parents learn his secret, and he begins his journey which ultimately leads him to the top of Hollywood's elite, only to have his past return to bring it all to a tragic end.

A cast of colorful characters make Johnny Thorne a "can't put down" read.
