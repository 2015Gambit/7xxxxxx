Description:

 The main hero of the game will be the General Loh, who is going to avenge the murder of his father. As the game progresses you will need to overcome a lot of challenge-rooms, fighting kung-fu masters and earning new abilities. In this common strategy button mashing will not help much here, you need to really fight with the mind, to build a combination, put the blocks away from enemy attacks.
