Lemonade Tale v1.0.0

Requirements: Android 2.1+ 
Overview: Androids first real Lemonade Stand simulator has arrived! 

Can you keep all your customers satisfied while making a profit?

Some features include:
Buying Ads to increase business
Achievements for lasting replayability
Upgrades to your cart to increase appeal and stock limits
Adjustable ingredients
Statistics to measure progress
Colorful graphics
Catchy tune

Will you water down your lemonade to cut costs?
Or have a healthy mix to keep customers happy?
