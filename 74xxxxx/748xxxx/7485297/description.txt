This nifty little patch/tool from Jenkey1002 allows you to unlock various teams and settings in the (1st) PES 2013 demo. Released on July 29th 2012.



*** Unlock tool features list ***
- Unlock all setting
- LOD mixer
- Replay disable (switchable)
- Blur disable (switchable)



*** Patch's contents ***
- Added team list : 7 classic teams, Brazil, Spain, Real, Barca, Inter, Chelsea, Man U, Bayern, Galatasaray, PSG
- Added Over 30 fantasy teams
- Real face & hair for all players
- Bonus : 32 balls unlocked pack
