From Wikipedia:



Under the Volcano is the 1987 Capitol/EMI album that Bob Rock and Paul Hyde of the Payolas released under the alternative band name Rock and Hyde. It was released on LP, cassette and Compact Disc, making it the only 1980s Payolas album available on CD, apart from compilations.
