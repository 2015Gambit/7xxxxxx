Ranger's Apprentice is a series of fantasy novels written by Australian author John Flanagan.
The series follows the adventures of Will, an orphan who is taken as an apprentice Ranger, as he strives to keep the Kingdom of Araluen safe from 

invaders, traitors, and threats. He is joined on his adventures by his mentor Halt and his best friend Horace.
