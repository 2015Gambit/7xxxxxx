Starring Matt Berry

Writen by Matt Berry and Arthur Matthews

Sitcom following Steven Toast (Matt Berry), a successful actor who has made a recent career faux pas by electing to star in a new controversial West End stage play


Pilot episode:
Toast is asked to audition for long-running television series 'Summer Time Murders'. However, the producer has been imprisoned for racial chanting on the set of his own production, so Toast has to audition for the part of a gay corrupt detective during prisoners' visiting time.
