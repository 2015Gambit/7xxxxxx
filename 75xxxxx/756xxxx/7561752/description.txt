Issues 1-4 of 'Sandman Presents - Thessaly - Witch for Hire'
Vertigo/DC Comics, 2004
Writer - Bill Willingham

Sandman Presents: Thessaly - Witch for Hire (four issue mini-series, April-July 2004), in which Thessaly (from the Sandman storyline A Game of You), the most deadly of the Thessalian witches is pitched against Cupid and a host of supporting monsters.
