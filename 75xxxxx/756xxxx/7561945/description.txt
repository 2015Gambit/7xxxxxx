Megafactories
Take a look at the most amazing factories in the world. Lift the lid on how millions of your everyday products and iconic designs begin life on the assembly line in a brand new series of Megafactories
Michelin
A visit to a famous tyre manufacturer's factory in Clermont-Ferrand, France, where engineers are working to create a new design to help cars become more fuel efficient. The cutting edge research and development by one of the largest tire companies in the world that manufactures everything from car and motorcycle to jumbo jet tires and now focuses on a new tire design that will increase mileage for cars.
