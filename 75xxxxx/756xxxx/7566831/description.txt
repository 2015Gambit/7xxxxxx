Sa ilalim ng cogon_2005(PINOY INDIE FILM)(VCDRip)

Sa Ilalim Ng Cogon 
Category Suspense
Horror

Tagalog Movie

Sa Ilalim Ng Cogon is a mystery-action-horror-thriller that tells the story of Sam (Yul Servo), a former soldier who unwittingly gets embroiled in a heist gone wrong.

Escaping with the bag of stolen loot, he hides inside an abandoned mansion where he meets the mysterious young woman Latia (Julia Clarete), bathing nude inside the homes filthy leaf-choked pool.

They begin a passionate and torrid affair even as the crime kingpin Johnny B (Dido dela Paz) and his armed goons launch a desperate search for the money.

Directed by Rico Maria Ilarde
