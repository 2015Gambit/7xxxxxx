Hypnotic supermodel Paola Rey portrays a sex addict. Despite her trust in those around her, they are eager to exploit her insatiable weakness. Emotionally troubled by the spiraling replay of her compulsive sexual obsessions, Paola attempts to escape her Dark Odyssey.

Starring Paola Rey & Charlotte Stokely with Sophia Santi - Kelly Summer - Otto Bauer - Kira Kane - Eric Swiss - Natasha Nice - Essi-X - Aaron Wilcox

Produced, Directed, Photographed & Edited by Andrew Blake
