Issues 1-3 of 'Sandman Presents - The Corinthian, Death In Venice'
Vertigo/DC Comics, 2001-2002
Writer - Darko Makan
Artist - Danijel Zezelj

Sandman Presents: The Corinthian - Death in Venice (three issue mini-series, December 2001 - Feb 2002), the story follows the Corinthian as he arrives in Venice shortly after leaving The Dreaming during Lord Morpheus' long absence.
