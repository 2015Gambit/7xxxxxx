Requirements: Android 2.1+
Overview: The rough play, the dust, the nostalgia of cricket in your own gully; that is what Street Cricket is all about! In this version the rules are modified to suit the ground situation while the usual excitement of the game remains intact.


Overs:
You can play 5, 10, 15, 20, 25, or 30 over match.
Toss: By winning the toss you have the option to bat or bowl first.
Your Team:
There are five players to a team. Batsmen are predefined but you can select your bowlers from the players available. After finishing one game you can start another.

How to Score:
You can score single runs by running between the wicket
If you hit over the building you score 2 runs
If you hit the wall you score 1 run
