Episode 2: What Goes On

 Claudia deals with the fallout of the EOC Opening, while Maggie questions her future at Eli Klein's.The morning after the End of Century Opening, Claudia arrives to find everything a complete mess and to make matters worse, Chantal shows up two hours late. Maggie returns to Eli's gallery to complete her internship, and he doesn't waste any time with assigning her tedious tasks -- forcing Liz to question why she puts up with him. All of the ladies run into each other at an art auction, will they be able to mix their Brooklyn vs. Manhattan lifestyles?
