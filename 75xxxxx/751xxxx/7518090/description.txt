Proletaryat - Prawda (2010) [WavPack]

Genre: Hard Rock/Heavy Metal
Origin: Poland (Pabianice)


Proletaryat is a polish band founded in november 1987. The are famous in Poland thanks to their first two post-punk albums “Proletariat” & “Proletaryat” and for their performance at Jarocin Festival in 1989, where they managed to get 3 awards.
