The Esteban (Costas Stefanakis) comes to Athens after losing his job as a cop. He lives with his handicapped uncle (Babis Kourtis) and looking for work. It broke and disappointed.
In approaching the security manager (Christos Natsios) and assigns to clean 2 of the most dangerous criminals in the city: The Manthos (Vangelis Mourikis) and Bachramis (Vangelis Alexander) are the ones that nobody can be catch-official protection, drugs, illegal sex shops, they all play.
Esteban is the target not only for the underworld but also for his "bosses" .
