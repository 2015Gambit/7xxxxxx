Purlie! was a 1970's musical which won several Tony Awards for Broadway, including Best Musical.

The musical was based upon the play "Purlie Victorious" written and starring the great actor, Ossie Davis. 

Sherman Hemsley, who recently passed away, had a supporting role in Purlie! and was discovered by TV Producer Norman Lear in Purlie! and cast as Mr. Jefferson in "The Jeffersons". 

Melba Moore, who won the Best Supporting Actress Tony Award for Purlie! gives one of the great vocal Broadway performances in this live revival production filmed in 1981.
