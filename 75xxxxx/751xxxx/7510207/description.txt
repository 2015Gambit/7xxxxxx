An in-depth examination of the culture and fashion of the sneaker, from its creation as athletic wear to its place as a cornerstone in hip-hop fashion.

The documentary looks back at hip-hop culture and how it shaped the world of fashion as it is today. From the early days of breakdancing to early rap groups like Run-DMC, to big-time sports celebrity sponsorships, Just For Kicks covers seems to cover all important aspects of the culture then and now.
