P2P released the latest Downloadable Content (DLC) of the popular game Mass Effect 3 for PC. This addon adds seven weapons for use in the single-player campaign: the Indra and Krysae sniper rifles, Reegar Carbine, Harrier battle rifle, Geth SMG, plus an all-new Blood Pack Punisher SMG and Adas Anti-Synthetic rifle!
