Bram is very good friends with his cousin Haas.
Faris, Haas' uncle, manages the pizzeria of Brams father.
After Brams father and Faris get into conflict with each other,
Faris decides to leave and start his own pizzeria on the other side of the street.
A true pizza war erupts between the two pizzeria,
putting Bram and Haas friendship under a lot of pressure.
