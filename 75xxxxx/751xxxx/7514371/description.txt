medieval - The Ensemble Organum (Marcel Peres) Discography

  
http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ensemble_Organum


They are a french ensemble performing since the 80s founded by the french musicologist Marcel Pérès; 
 
http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Marcel_P%C3%A9r%C3%A8s

Their repertoire is based on the ancient medieval music... starting from the pregregorian era (the protochristian period), passing through the Ecole of Notre Dame, digging the ancient manuscripts of the tradition and covering also early ethnic corsican drifts.
I discovered them only recently, scavenging Youtube for ancient music and I remained thunderstruck by the subterranean vibration of their voices which triggered electric sensations up and down my spine...
It does not need any lore to be caught by their magic... If you have a basic ear for music you wont be displeased... believe me :)

There are 34 albums.
All the albums have a sampling of 320kbps. Enjoy.
