Description:

In Rigonauts you play the mechanic that plans and engineers his own machines and battles his way 

though 30 levels fighting enemy constructs. Throughout the game you will be able to upgrade your 

vessel with armor, new weapons and balloons. The goal of the game is to survive with your construct 

and ultimately defeat the boss at the end of the game, thus proving your construct is the best.
