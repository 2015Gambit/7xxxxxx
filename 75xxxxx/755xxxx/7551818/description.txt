Extreme Civil War Reenactors

History Documentary hosted by Tom White, published by National Geographic broadcasted as part of Explorer series in 2011 - English narration


Information
------------------------------

Outfitted with period authentic Civil War uniforms, we embedded with a handful of America's most hard-core Civil War reenactors, who eat, sleep and breathe the gun smoke of history. From the firing on Fort Sumter to the iconic battlefields of Manassas/Bull Run and Gettysburg, the mecca of reenacting, we set out to discover out how far these folks are willing to go to relive history.
