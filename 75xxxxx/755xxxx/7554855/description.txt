Issues 1-3 of Charles Vess' 'Book of Night'

Dark Horse Classic Comics, 1987

Most of these stories were originally published in issues of Epic Illustrated.

Issue One: The Children of the Stars by Charles Vess, Bug Tales by Charles Vess, Morrigan Tales - Zealots Lake by Elaine Lee & Charles Vess

Issue Two: The Children of the Stars by Charles Vess, Jack Tales by Charles Vess, Legend by L. Sutton & Charles Vess, Priest by Charles Vess

Issue Three: The Children of the Stars by Charles Vess, Spirit by L. Sutton & Charles Vess, A Jack Tale - Two Sisters by Charles Vess, Visions from the Book of Night by Charles Vess
