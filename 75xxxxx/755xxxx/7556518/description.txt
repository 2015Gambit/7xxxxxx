Little Stars is a space real-time strategy (RTS) game, where the player has a simple objective: move through the neutral star systems in order to capture all enemy systems. Once you start playing, you will lose yourself in fun levels, ranging from amusing to mind-challenging!

Commander - The galaxy has plunged into chaos and little wars have broken out along the border - fleet-ships are standing by for you to take control of the battlefield. Form your strategy, conquer enemy systems, and claim victory for your forces!

Features :

    Multitouch: pinch to zoom, tap to control.
    54 levels. And more new levels in the future!
    Auto-paths: auto-paths system for quick and easy control over the battlefield. (touch and drag)
    Upgrades: specialize your star systems to fortify their defensive capabilities, or, advance their ship production.
    Color Customization: don't like the default colors? Tune to your liking!


What's in this version: (Updated : Aug 21, 2012)

    Added Local Multiplayer. Play with friends up to 3 people on one device!
    Procedural level generation has been improved;
    Statistics fixed;
    Other minor changes;
