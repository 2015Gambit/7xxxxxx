H Psixi Sto Stoma 2006
Direction: Yannis Economides
Scenario: Yannis Economides, Lenia Spyropoulos, Henry lychees
Starring: Henry lychees, Vangelis Mourikis, Maria Nafpliotou, etc.

Plot:

Takis passively watch those who ripped of his life. The boss humiliates him using even as masseurs, colleagues at work who did not leave in silence, his wife cuckold him, his sister who is mentally ill, usurers who persecute ... The whole society has in put under fire and life becomes unbearable weight. He accepts everything articulating occasionally two or three words to defend himself ...
