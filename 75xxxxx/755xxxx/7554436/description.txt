Hi!
We're a polish band called The Abstinents and we present you our first LP - Punk not Drunk! 
We think that our music should be totally non profit.
We give you our music all for free and the only thing we want to achive is to share with you our POSITIVE VIBRATION JAH RASTAFAR! (we don't play reggae on this LP :( ) and to show you that you can do everything without counting on payback.
If you like our music, and you want to help us someway, you can help our friend's daughter and "buy" this LP on www.allegro.pl - type in the abstinents in searchbar . (all money that you'll pay in will be donated for her)
