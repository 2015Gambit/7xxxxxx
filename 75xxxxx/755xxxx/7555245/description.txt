Here comes the new theme, iLike. Hope you like the fresh and clean style. There are some notices I'd like to point out:
1. iLike theme requires 480*800 resolution or above to achieve the best effect. Please do consider if your phone resolution is lower than 480*800.
2. Add any 7 application shortcuts to the locker screen. Add one by one to set the apps order.

Features:

    Add any 7 application shortcuts to the locker screen and access to the application you want to open while unlocking the screen. 
    Fresh and clean as the style is, it just looks like your containing box of your phone.

Installation instructions:

    Make sure GO Launcher EX V2.58 or above and GO Locker v1.05 has been installed;
    Click Menu>Plugins>GO Locker, choose a theme you like and apply it to your phone

Required Android O/S : 2.0+
