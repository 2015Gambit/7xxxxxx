Both versions of Neil Gamain's 'Sandman - The Dream Hunters'

Vertigo/DC Comics, 1999 & 2009


1999 - 122 page prose graphic novel with numerous, beautiful illustrations by Yoshitaka Amano.


2009 - 4 issue (144 page total) comic book adaptation of Neil Gaiman's prose from the original illustrated graphic novel. Adaptation and art by P. Craig Russell.
