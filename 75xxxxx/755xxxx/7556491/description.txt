Cocktail is a 2012 Indian romantic comedy-drama film directed by Homi Adajaniaunder the banner of Illuminati Films. The film stars Saif Ali Khan, Deepika Padukone and Diana Penty in lead roles, with Dimple Kapadia, Boman Irani and Randeep Hooda appearing in supporting roles.Cocktail released worldwide on 13 July 2012 and received postive to mixed comments from critics, with major praise directed to Padukone's performance. The film also emerged as a major commercial success in both India and abroad.After three weeks run, Boxofficeindia declared the movie a hit in India and abroad.
