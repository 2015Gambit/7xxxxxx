Forty-seven years after man landed on the moon, the team of the spaceship SAGAN is on the way to renew with historical event. Their mission : To land on Mars. More of 13 billion viewers follow in live this tremendous adventure. Soon inexplicable incidents happen, threatening not only the success of the mission, but also the life of the crew. The commander of the vessel has to act rapidly against this threat coming either from a sabotage, an alien presence or an onboard crew mutiny. Little by little, the SAGAN directs to an inextricable abyss at the limits of the galaxy....
