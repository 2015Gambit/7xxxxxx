Pulleys, blocks, hamster wheel, hamster ball, ladders and tunnels. All of the ingredients for a great puzzle game. Move and lift blocks by strategically connecting a system of pulleys to the hamster wheel. Run on the hamster wheel to pull your blocks! Then roll the hamster ball to the finish! Collect pulleys by climbing ladders, crawling through tunnels, and positioning the blocks to reach higher ledges. 72 increasingly difficult levels!
What's in this version : (Updated : Aug 19, 2012)

    Made the first few levels easier to learn the ropes. Made Hero run faster. Now saves mute and tablet/phone options from previous session. Many thanks to fans for the feedback!

Required Android O/S : 2.2+
