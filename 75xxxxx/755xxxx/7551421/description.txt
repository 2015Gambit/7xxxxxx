Niyaz is an Iranian musical trio. Niyaz's music, described as "mystical music with a modern edge", is primarily a blend of Sufi mysticism and trance electronica. Niyaz adapts Persian, Indian and Mediterranean folk sounds, poetry and songs including the poetry of Sufi mystic Rumi, with Western electronic instrumentation and programming.

Sumud is the third album by Niyaz. The album contains songs in Persian, Urdu and Turkish and features the band's first lyrics in Arabic and Kurdish.
