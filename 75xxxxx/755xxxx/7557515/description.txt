


Kadhalar Dhinam is a 1999 Tamil romantic film directed by Kathir. This film is based on a relationship which begins through an internet chatroom and takes a turn after the protagonist has second thoughts about the relationship.





Release date: April 14, 1999 (initial release)

Directors: Kadhir, Kadhir

Running time: 151 minutes

Music: A. R. Rahman

Cinematography: P. C. Sriram
