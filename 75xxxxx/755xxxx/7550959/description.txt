Requirements: Android 2.2+
Overview: One of the best-looking mobile games ever created - ign.com

What's new in version 2.1.0? 
The new version of an expanded storyline that consists of 4 new levels, which are events in the prehistory of the events of the game SHADOWGUN, in which you have participated. In the new version, you'll fight a new enemy creature, using new weapons, and all this will be accompanied by new soundtrack! The game has developed a new system of game interaction, improved and updated combat system, new effects such as shaking the camera or tracking the trajectory of the bullet. Also added the ability to do somersaults. In the game you can customize the difficulty level, choose the weapons and enemies
