This program is a powerful automated PC Shutdown utility.

Here are some key features of Shutdown Manager:
Shutting down the computer at a certain time.
Shutting down the computer after a certain amount of time has elapsed.
Shutting down the computer in the case of low CPU usage.
Scheduling a shutdown for a whole week, for each day, for workdays or holidays.
Shutting down the computer if it has been idle for a certain amount of time.
Clearing the computer of unnecessary files before shutting it down.
Quick shutdown and other options with the help of hotkeys.
Shutting down other LAN computers.
