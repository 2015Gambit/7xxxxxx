This torrent includes the complete nine issue run of the wonderful Vertigo series Spaceman, brought to you by the creative team responsible for 100 Bullets, Brian Azzarello and Eduardo Risso.  All scans are done at 1440px width, and both c2c and noAds versions are provided.
========================================================


Brian Azzarello and Eduardo Risso--the Eisner Award-winning creators of 100 BULLETS--return to Vertigo! Set in a post-apocalyptic near future, SPACEMAN tells the story of Orson, a hulking, lonely loser who spends his days collecting scrap metal and dreaming of the startrekking life he was promised.
