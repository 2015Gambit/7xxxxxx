Build Your Own Kitchen Cabinets
Language: English
ISBN10:	1558704612
Date: 1997-09-15
Format: pdf

Practical information on kitchen design which makes kitchen cabinetmaking accessible to woodworkers of all skill levels, this volume walks the reader through the stages of planning, design, construction and installation and features a range of cabinetry and a full-colour gallery of kitchen design.
