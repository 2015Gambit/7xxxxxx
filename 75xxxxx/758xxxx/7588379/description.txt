"Ash" features one of Herbert's best-loved characters, David Ash, the sceptical paranormal detective, first encountered in "The Ghosts of Sleath" and "Haunted" both number 1 bestsellers. Ash is investigating a mysterious and secluded stately home, deep in the countryside. There have been reports from locals about strange goings on, they think it might be haunted...What Ash eventually discovers is truly shocking. Prepare to be chilled to the marrow....
