A surrealist art film by Circus Devils featuring music by Robert Pollard of Guided By Voices (GBV).

The plot is this:  Dr. Gregory Fleer, a once brilliant scientist, has created a drug called I Razor - a drug that splits minds into 7 distinct personalities. But when a portion of the male population takes too much, they become sub-superhuman freaks. Follow Sgt. Disco (Steve Five) and Professor Salmon (Cory Race) on a dark journey as phantasm Mother Skinny (Cory Race), the face of pure evil, along with her right hand subhuman The Villain (introducing Brad Visker) and her band of demons seeks to destroy them.
