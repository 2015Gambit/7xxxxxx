The AndroZip File Manager helps you to copy, delete, move, search and organize your files, music, photos and folders just as you would on your PC.

With over 5 million downloads and its support of ZIP, encrypted ZIP, RAR, TAR, GZIP and BZIP2, the AndroZip File Manager is definitive leader among other applications on the Google Play
Besides the functionality of compression / decompression, it also lets you send files, eg via email.

Optimized especially for displays of phones and tablets. Due to several new features like functionality "drag and drop" the AndroZip File Manager is preferred among tablet users.

Use it also to back up your applications and install new application APK on your phone, and manage phone memory and resources via task manager.

Can be installed on the SD card.
