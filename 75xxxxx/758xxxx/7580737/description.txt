This was Bob Marley's first appearance in the Netherlands. It's written about here:  
http://www.bob-marley.es/audio/Babylon-By-Bus-EN.pdf (Chapter Three). 

The source had numerous operations & repairs performed: re-blended to mono, adjusted 
pitch, attenuated some crowd noise & clapping, fixed drop-outs & levels, etc. There 
are areas of audible tape damage here and there. Also, there's a cut in Get Up Stand 
Up at 3:06 that's been cross-faded together. The crowd is very loud and chanting at 
times. Note that small FLAC file-size is due to mono WAVs (nothing lossy) ...Enjoy!
