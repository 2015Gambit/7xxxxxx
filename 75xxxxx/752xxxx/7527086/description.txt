Usborne released some great books in the 80's about
electronics and computers of that age. Some of them go
deep into programming and to the insides of the CPU while
still explaining the details in a simple manner and with
many drawings fun especially for kids. This release has
four of those books in finnish. They are hard to find
scanned in Finnish hence this release. I hope the books
still warm your hearts or bring some fun to new readers
no matter about the age.
