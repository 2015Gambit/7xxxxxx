London Olympics Opening Ceremony

Sociopolitical Documentary hosted by Paul Dickenson and Colin Jackson, published by BBC in 2012 - English narration


Information
------------------------------

Coverage of the opening ceremony in 3D. Film director Danny Boyle produces a stunning cultural show ahead of the athletes' parade, during which over 200 countries are expected to be represented followed by the official opening, the arrival of the torch and the lighting of the cauldron.
