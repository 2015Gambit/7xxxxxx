Boston Beer, Cast Aluminum Pots, Bricks and Baseball Gloves

Technology Documentary hosted by Zac Fine, published by Discovery Channel broadcasted as part of DC Factory Made series in 2009 - English narration


Information
------------------------------

How does a brewer create the flavors of a beer, what do you need to add to an aluminum pot to handle the heat, why do bricks have holes in them, and why does a baseball glove get pounded before it makes it to the game?
