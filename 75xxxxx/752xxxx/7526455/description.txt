Hep Stars, Sveriges populäraste band på 60-talet.
The Hep Stars är deras andra studioalbum och innehåller flera låtar skrivna av
Benny Andersson. Låten "Isn't it easy to say" är resultatet av första samarbetet
mellan Björn Ulveaus och Benny Andersson. 
http://sv.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hep_Stars

The album The Hep Stars is the probably best ABBA related of the pre-ABBA
period. It is a must have for students of the songwriting of Björn and Benny. It
features several songs written by Benny and "Isn't It Easy To Say", the first
joint composition by Benny and his new friend Björn Ulvaeus, then a member of
the acoustic group Hootenanny Singers, who mainly sang in the Swedish language. 
http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hep_Stars
