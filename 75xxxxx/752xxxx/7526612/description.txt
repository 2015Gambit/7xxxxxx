Melo's Magic is a new 3D game from indie developer in style like Tower Defense. But here you play as a mage named Melo. Your purpose is to defend the castle and not let the monsters break down gate. Melo has a unique set of spells that need to be combined to achieve the best effect.

Collect crystals and find the booty. Combine the magical abilities and act fast! Monsters at any time can take you by surprise. And remember: Your main weapon is a tactic and agility!

Features:

    Old-school control system like in fantasy MMORPG
    16 unique spells and talents. And also a variety of useful items and achievement
    Minimum indicators during the game. Test your skill!
    A special survival mode. The crowds of monsters!

Required Android O/S : 2.1+
