Sujinho is a latin jazz collaboration album between American hip hop producer and MC Madlib and drummer for the Brazilian funk band Azymuth, Ivan Conti. This album features Brazilian samba music heavily and features one of Madlib's interests in Latin Jazz. So far this album has been released twice, once in the Netherlands on Kindred Spirits in 2x vinyl and in The US on Mochilla Records in CD format.
