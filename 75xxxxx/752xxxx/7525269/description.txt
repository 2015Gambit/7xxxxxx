It contains 51 compositions and constitutes the eighth volume of the Tirumurai, the sacred anthology of Tamil Shaiva Siddhanta.

The main message of the hymns is LOVE with Almighty. We should have love to reach him. These hymns guides us to develop the LOVE. No one is needed to define the meaning. Manikkavacakar himself told, the meaning of Tiruvacagam is ALMIGHTY. The meaning of ALMIGHTY is love.
