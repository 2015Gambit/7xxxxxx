AxeWound is a British-Canadian supergroup formed in 2012 as a side project for Bullet for My Valentine lead vocalist Matt Tuck. The band is composed of Matt Tuck on guitar and backing vocals, Liam Cormier of Cancer Bats on lead vocals, Mike Kingswood of Glamour of the Kill on guitar, Joe Copcutt formerly of Rise to Remain playing bass and Jason Bowld of Pitchshifter on drums. "Cold" is the second single from their debut album, Vultures, which will be in store october 1st.
