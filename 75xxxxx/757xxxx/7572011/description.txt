Space Files

Science Documentary hosted by Roger Liddle, published by BBC in 2004 - English narration


Information
------------------------------

SPACE FILES is a visual compendium of space and astronomy presenting the Solar System, our Galaxy, and the Universe beyond. SPACE FILES is a comprehensive tour de force comprising an enthralling overview of our cosmic habitat. A flow of superb graphic animation and the latest images of the planets, their moons, the Sun and deep space deliver a stimulating visual exposition.
SPACE FILES is the latest in a highly-successful format begun in 1993 with Encyclopedia Galactica, followed in 1998 by The Complete Cosmos. SPACE FILES carries the genre brilliantly into the 21st Century.
