Planet Dinosaur Ultimate Killers

Science Documentary hosted by John Hurt, published by BBC in 2012 - English narration


Information
------------------------------

Adapted from the multi-award winning BBC1 series, Planet Dinosaur 3D recreates the lost world of the dinosaurs in a groundbreaking stereoscopic production. One of the most ambitious animated programmes ever attempted for broadcast TV, and recreating every detail of these extraordinary animals in an entirely digital production that stretches the boundaries of broadcast 3D with a scale and ambition normally reserved for Hollywood feature films. Planet Dinosaur 3D is a thrilling and immersive journey into a lost world. Pulling together cutting edge research from around the world and using the latest fossil evidence to chart the rise and fall of the 'Ultimate Killers'; from the Spinosaurus, the largest predator ever to walk the Earth, to Microraptor and the feathered, flying dinosaurs from China. Thanks to the advances in technology, and for the first time ever, these monsters can be experienced in all their full, magnificent wonder.
