Anna Moffo (June 27, 1932 ΓÇô March 9, 2006) was an Italian-American opera singer and one of the leading lyric-coloratura sopranos of her generation. She possessed a warm and radiant voice of considerable range and agility, and was an affecting singer-actress of great physical beauty.  This gorgeous compilation is a must-have for opera geeks and noobs alike.
