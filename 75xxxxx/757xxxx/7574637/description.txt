Do you think the photo of contact is too small when you get or make a phone call. You could try to use this app, this app will display full screen and high quality photo of contact when you get phone call, make a phone call, miss a phone call or get SMS message. 

It will help you to quick know who is calling, who is sending to you a SMS message or who is you missing a phone call.

Features:

    Full screen for incoming call.
    Full screen for outgoing call.
    Full screen for missed call.
    Full screen for SMS message.
    You can choose a high quality photo as a full screen photo of contact.
    Offer seven different themes.
    Choose picture from facebook

What's in this version : (Updated : Jul 9, 2012)

    Fix some bugs. Support payment for Taiwan.

Required Android O/S : 2.1+
