Play Asphalt 7 offline for non-rooted phones
1.Install APK 
2.Copy the folder: com.gameloft.android.ANMP.GloftA7HM  to  sdcard/Android /obb
3.Launch the game & connect to Wifi for the first time (if the screen becomes dark after checking for license, do not worry about that, this issue can be solved when you run the game again)
4.If you have a problem with Chinese language after running the game, just go to settings / select the 4th line downward / then select US flag.


                      THIS WAY WAS TESTED BY ME AND IT WORKS ON MY 
                                 SAMSUNG GALAXY S I9000
