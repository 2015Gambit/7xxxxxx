Original title: Shikeidai no erebêtâ 
Also Known As: Elevator to the Gallows

The film is a Japanese remake of Louis Malle’s 1958 French film 
“Ascenseur pour l’échafaud” and was made with the blessing of the 
late director’s son, Manuel Cuotemoc Malle.

Director: Akira Ogata
Stars: Michiko Kichise, Hiroshi Abe and Keiko Kitagawa 

Michiko Yoshise plays Meiko, the wife of a wealthy medical company president (Masato Ibu). 
She also happens to be in the midst of an affair with a charming young doctor named 
Tokito (Hiroshi Abe). Meiko meticulously plans out the perfect murder, 
giving Tokito specific instructions which will leave her husband dead and all 
the evidence pointing to an obvious suicide.
Confident her plan would go off without a hitch, Meiko waits for Tokito’s car 
at a nearby cafe where they’ve arranged to meet, However, 
a confluence of unlikely events leaves Tokito trapped in an elevator 
as a young couple (Keiko Kitagawa and Tetsuji Tamayama) drive off in his car 
following their own unrelated crime.
