----------------------------------------------------------------
FIRST CALL DISCOGRAPHY
----------------------------------------------------------------

This First Call discography may or may not be complete.  It includes the following 12 releases...

 * 1985 - An Evening In December, Vol 1
 * 1986 - Undivided
 * 1987 - An Evening In December, Vol 2
 * 1987 - Something Takes Over
 * 1989 - God Is Good
 * 1992 - Human Song
 * 1993 - Sacred Journey
 * 1994 - La Razon De Cantar
 * 1995 - Beyond December
 * 1996 - First Call
 * 2006 - Rejoice
 * 2007 - The Definitive Collection

First Call is a Contemporary Christian music group, originally consisting of Mel Tunney, Marty McCall, and Bonnie Keen. Mel's husband, Dick Tunney, acted as the arranger and producer for many of the group's early projects. First Call was a recipient of GMA Dove Awards in the "Group of the Year" category for both 1988 and 1989. They also have multiple Grammy nominations.
