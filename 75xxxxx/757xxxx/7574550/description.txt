St. Ives is a 1976 American action film directed by J. Lee Thompson and starring Charles Bronson.
 
Abner Procane hires Raymond St. Ives, a fromer crime-reporter and ex-policeman, to return of five 
ledgers stolen from his safe. St. Ives becomes embroiled in the task and the deaths of those 
involved in the theft. The ledgers are eventually returned minus four pages, and St Ives becomes 
drawn into robbery to try and right the situation.
Based upon the novel The Procane Chronicle by Oliver Bleeck(Ross Thomas).
