Overview: Want to strengthen and tone your legs in 5 minutes a day?
Daily Leg Workout contains two 5 to 10 minute daily leg routines that step you through twenty of the best lower body exercises. This workout is developed and demonstrated by a certified personal trainer, and spending just minutes a day can strengthen and tone your legs.


The routine's simple interface, complete with video and timer, allows you to easily follow along and understand each exercise.
