FLV Video Player

Play your flash videos (flv files) from your SD-card with this simple player.
You don't need to install Flashr Player Plugin,Adobe AIRr or any other plugin to use this app to play flv videos.

Some of the supported video formats are:
avi, mp4, mpeg, 3gp, gif, flv, mpg, mpeg, ogg, windows media (wmv), swf, ogg, quicktime (mov), matroska (mkv)
High definition (HD) video is supported. Subtitles are also supported
Some of the supported audio formats are:
mp3, wav flac real audio
