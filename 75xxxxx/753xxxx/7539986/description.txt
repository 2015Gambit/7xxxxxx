A famous rock-group gets in creative and financial crisis after the death of their leader, and only a new top-ten record can bring them back. Limited by time, with no creative energy, they turn against each other. Through conflicts, accusations and self-examination, they manage to get a new identity. A new leader imposes himself, and succeeds in getting them out. During some big charity concert, the group experiences major success, and reborn, they start a new life...
