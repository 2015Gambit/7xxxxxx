JIMMY PALMIOTTI & JUSTIN GRAY and NORBERTO FERNANDEZ  ask the question: Who is the Tattered Man? A spirit of vengeance spawned from the concentration camps of Germany lies dormant 'til it is resurrected in modern day New York and finds although times have changed, its work is far from done. Horror and heroics mix, and the results are deadly.
