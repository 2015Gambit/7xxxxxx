Fältskog, Benny Andersson, Björn Ulvaeus and Anni-Frid Lyngstad. ABBA is an
acronym of the first letters of the band members' first names and is sometimes
stylized as the registered trademark ᗅᗺᗷᗅ®. They became one of the most
commercially successful acts in the history of pop music, topping the charts
worldwide from 1972 to 1982.
