Walk Away Renee - Despite their differences, all the women finally gather for Leah's mother breast cancer walk. When Leah requests the group to stay together, Pia and Renee create more tension by distancing themselves from the group. This does not sit well with Leah. Meanwhile, the ladies are moving up and on with their lives. Pia tries to resolve her issues with both Christina and Nora while also looking to kick-start her new modeling career. Nora gets closer to finding closure with her father's death. Christina doggedly pursues a career in fashion, and Renee and Dave make one last attempt to save their relationship-- but is it too late?
