Description in English 
---------------------------------------
 A decade after his heroic defeat of the monstrous 

Kraken, Perseus is attempting to live a quieter life 

as a village fisherman and the sole parent to his 10-

year-old son, Helius. Meanwhile, a struggle for 

supremacy rages between the gods and the Titans. 

Dangerously weakened by humanity's lack of devotion, 

the gods are losing control of the imprisoned Titans 

and their ferocious leader, Kronos, father of the 

long-ruling brothers Zeus, Hades and Poseidon. The 

triumvirate had overthrown their powerful father long 

ago, leaving him to rot in the gloomy abyss of 

Tartarus, a dungeon that lies deep within the 

cavernous underworld.

Perseus cannot ignore his true calling when Hades, 

along with Zeus's godly son, Ares, switch loyalty and 

make a deal with Kronos to capture Zeus. The Titans' 

strength grows stronger as Zeus's remaining godly 

powers are siphoned, and hell is unleashed on earth. 

Enlisting the help of the warrior Queen Andromeda, 

Poseidon's demigod son, Agenor, and fallen god 

Hephaestus, Perseus bravely embarks on a treacherous 

quest into the underworld to rescue Zeus, overthrow 

the Titans and save mankind.
