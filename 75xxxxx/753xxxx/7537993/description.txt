Let us introduce you the best modern arcade game! Fling yourself into a thrilling adventure! Embark on a dangerous journey with the Golden Ninja and rescue his girlfriend from the Dark Ninja. Check your agility, reaction, and acumen in the vibrant and colorful worlds.

Intuitive and unique control system will make you feel like a real ninja, while amazing soundtrack along with the brilliant exclusive design will help you to immerse yourself in an unforgettable atmosphere of the game!
You will be climbing vines, swim, fly, jump, teleport, dodge the saws and monsters! You will go through the fire and ice, collect stars, and gain medals and achievements. 

Compete with players in your world on the speed of walkthrough. You will never be bored with this game! The walkthrough of levels will make puzzle over even experienced gamers! Be the best! The time of the Golden Ninja has come!

Features:

    Unique setting 
    3 unique different worlds 
    125 levels 
    Bonus level: Star Game 
    Amazing music 
    Unique gameplay 
    Simple and intuitive control system 
    Exciting story 
    Global ratings
