RELEASE NOTES

Join The Mobile Trench Brigade and protect humanity from the evil
Monovision menace in this Tower Defense Shooter from Tim Schafer's
Double Fine Productions. Use a combination of mobile firepower and
stationary defenses to turn the tide of war in our favor. Customize
your trench with thousands of possible loadouts. March side-by-side
with allies from around the world as you fight your way through Europe,
Africa, and the Pacific.

After defeating the monovision threat across Europe, Africa, and The
Pacific, continue your service in the mobile trenches with Iron
Brigade: Rise of the Martian Bear. Face Vlad's most horrific new
Monovision Constructs in a brand new Martian Campaign. Unlock over 80
new pieces of loot to battle the Tubes in 4 defense missions, 2
survival missions, and an all new Boss mission!
