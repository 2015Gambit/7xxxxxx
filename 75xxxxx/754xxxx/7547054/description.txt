The Jimi Hendrix Experience box set is not for the faint of heart. This
four CD box set of unreleased tracks, alternate versions of well known
songs, and rare concert footage is a cornerstone of any Hendrix
collection. This box set is no place to start if you are not familiar with
Hendrix. I would recommend his first three studio albums for any type of
Hendrix indoctrination. This set, however, is a good place to complete
your collection or at least to explore his legacy a little deeper.
