Pope's Arkansas Mountaineers were formed in 1928, as a way to promote business for J.D. Pope's piano store in Searcy AR. They were one of the early recorded Ozark string bands, but were only together for the recording session, and then dissolved. Recorded Monday, February 6, 1928 in Memphis TN.
