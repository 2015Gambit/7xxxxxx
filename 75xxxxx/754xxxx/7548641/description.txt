Some of you might know Jordi Bernet, one of the great european artists.
His main works are Torpedo (see my The Complete Torpedo 1-4 torrent), Kraken, Custer, Dark Tales, and Clara, but he has also done work on american comics, such as Jonah Hex, Batman, and an issue of 100 Bullets.

Most of his books are oldschool noir or gangster books, from back when men were men, women were gorgeous, and a pistol could solve a lot of the worlds problems.

A few of the european books have been officially released, but all in this pack are scanlations.

Bernets books are NOT child friendly, and feature both nudity, sex, and violence, although nothing worse than you can see in the average HBO series. Just dont hand these books to your kid without having read and OKed them first :D

The pack include the following comics:
Dark Tales
Custer
Kraken
Snake - A Fistful of Dollars
The Nature of the Beast
The Cry of the Vampire
Beauty and the Beast
Sarvane 1-2
Betty by Night 1-2 + 1 more album
Bang Bang 1-3
