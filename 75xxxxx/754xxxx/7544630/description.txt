Adguard 5.3 - new release of the popular program to block online ads and pop-ups. Behind the scenes working on the computer, Adguard protects users from annoying advertisements, saves bandwidth and speeds up page loading. In addition, the program protects users from switching to questionable sites that may pose a danger to users' confidential data. The program supports all popular browsers, 32 - and 64-bit version of Windows. In version 5.3 Adguard for convenience has been introduced a mechanism to automatically determine the language filter. The developers have paid great attention to optimizing the program and significantly reduced during cold start.
