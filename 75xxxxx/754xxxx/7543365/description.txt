Slackintosh from the original developers web site. This is the latest current version of 12.1.

Slackintosh is a port of the popular Slackware Linux Distribution that has been has been around for a long time!

Linux is an Open Source GNU project. There is no License keys needed and this can be freely distributed.

Just burn the 4 CDROM ISO images and boot disk 1 on your Mac and you should be ready to roll. Works on G3s and up. Very space efficent and you will enjoy the results even on old crufty hardware.
