This is a CD rip of the original full Warzone 2100 PC retail CDs.

This rip preserves the original audio tracks that are present
on the retail CDs inside the CUE/BIN files.

REQUIREMENTS:

* You will need Nero 10 or higher.
* CD-R media (not DVD media) because CUE/BIN contain audio-track information.

TO BURN:

* Extract CD1 and CD2 cue/bins from .7z archive.
* Start Nero.
* Go to Recorder > Burn Image... and select CD1 .cue file.
* Repeat for CD2.

And don't forget to install Warzone 2100 1.10 patch!

Happy Gaming!
