Beef IV is the fourth installment of the Beef series. It is a documentary about Hip hop rivalries and beefs (arguments). It was released on DVD on September 18, 2007. It was directed by Peter Alton and lasts approximately 108 minutes. It was narrated by Charlie Murphy. However; this fourth addition to the series doesn't give any new material, it's simply the "Best Of" uncensored version from the Television series of the same name that displayed on BET. 

The beef's included

    Southside Houston vs Northside Houston
    Paul Wall vs Chamillionaire
    Daz Dillinger vs Kurupt
    Khia vs Trina vs Jacki-O
    The break-up of Rocafella
    LL Cool J vs Jamie Foxx
    Terry Kennedy vs Lupe Fiasco
    Nickz vs ENJ
    Gucci Mane vs Young Jeezy
    The Game vs Ras Kass
    Remembering Proof
