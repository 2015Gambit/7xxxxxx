An ultimate collection of "Dora the Explorer" (in English).

This collection includes 114 episodes from 5 seasons, and 4 DVDRips.

All episode numbers are named after: http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/List_of_Dora_the_Explorer_episodes

7 episodes (out of 121) are marked "missing", though you can find 3 of them in "Dora DVDs" folder. There are many Dora's DVDs out there but I didn't include them here because each DVD is just a collection of 3-4 normal episodes (for more detail here: http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/List_of_Dora_the_Explorer_videos#DVD_Compilations).
