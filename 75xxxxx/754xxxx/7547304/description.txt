<b>Name:</b> IRON HANDS
<b>Author:</b> Johnatan Green
<b>Format:</b> PDF
<b>Pages:</b> 94

Amongst the Adeptus Astartes there are few warriors so utterly committed to the Imperium and the service of mankind than the Iron Hands. With their conviction comes an implacable contempt for any show of weakness and a determination to expunge it whatever the cost. Their zeal even extends to practising bionic augmentation on their own bodies in the hope of achieving a physical form that will never betray their iron will.
