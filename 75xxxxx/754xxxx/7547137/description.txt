The Football League Show 18.08.2012

Highlights and analysis as the Football League returns to action, complete with new arrivals Blackburn, Bolton, Wolves, Fleetwood and York. Bolton manager Owen Coyle returns to the scene of former glories as Burnley meet the Trotters in an early Lancashire derby. Wolves, under new management, face a difficult opening match at Leeds United, and Championship play-off semi- finalists Cardiff entertain newly-promoted Huddersfield Town. Manish Bhasin presents, with expert analysis from Leroy Rosenior
