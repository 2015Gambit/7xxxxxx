APNOIA
Mars Bafaloukas
with Sotiris Pastra, Yulia Skafida, George Karamichos, Andrianna Mpampali, Lydia Fotopoulou, Akyllas Karazissis
Year of Production: 2010
Duration: 90
Genre: Drama, Thriller

case:
O Dimitri, a 23 year old swimmer, dives into the water of a dark pool after his success in the European Championship. Has fulfilled the dream of his life but feels more empty than ever. As his body floats on the water surface, images and memories plimyrizoun his mind, recalling his relationship with Elsa, an environmental activist who disappeared mysteriously.
