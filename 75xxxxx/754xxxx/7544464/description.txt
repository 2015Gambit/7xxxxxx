Young Felicity lives in a monastic school. The only way to live out her sexual fantasies is together with her girlfriend Jenny. But then she receives an invitation to her sister in Hong-Kong and can't wait to finally do the real thing. This an Australian answer to Emmanuelle with Glory Annen speaking perfect British English and being more romantic and less "femme fatale" than Sylvia Kristel's protagonist. Which doesn't really hinder her quest for new experiences...
