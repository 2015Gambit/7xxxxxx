INSYDERZ DISCOGRAPHY
-------------------------------------------------------------------

This discography may or may not be complete.  It contains the following 7 Insyderz titles...

 * 1997 - Motor City Ska
 * 1998 - Fight Of My Life
 * 1998 - Paradise - Maxi Single
 * 1998 - Skalleluia
 * 1999 - Skalleluia Too
 * 2001 - The Greatest And Rarest
 * 2003 - Soundtrack To A Revolution

The Insyderz are a Christian ska-punk band from Detroit, Michigan. They formed in 1996 and disbanded in 2005, but reformed in 2009. The Insyderz are one of the "big three" bands which represented the Christian ska scene, alongside the Supertones and Five Iron Frenzy.
