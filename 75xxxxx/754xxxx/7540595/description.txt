Xilisoft Apple TV Video Converter
 
Want to put movies, TV shows, videos into your Apple TV? Install Xilisoft Apple TV Video Converter and start converting your favorite video files so you can start watching them on your TV! Supported files are AVI, MPEG, WMV, RM, RMVB, MOV, MKV, and 3GP, then turn them into Apple TV MP4 (MPEG-4, H.264), FLV and MOV videos. Audio files (WMA, MP2 and OGG), including the ones extracted from videos are also supported. 

Additional Options such as customizing file size, splitting file, and setting various parameters such as frame rate, bit rate, and channel are available.

 Convert common audio formats to Apple TV MP3, AAC, WAV
 Extract audios from AVI, MPEG, WMV, RM video
