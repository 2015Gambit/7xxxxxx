The eagerly awaited follow-up to the New York Times bestselling A Lion's Tale documents Chris Jericho's rise to wrestling glory in the WWE.

A Lion's Tale gave readers a portrait of Jericho as a young man, the book ended with the author's WWE debut. Jericho now tells the story of life in the big leagues. But "making it" in the premier wrestling league in the world comes with its own set of obstacles and lessons.
