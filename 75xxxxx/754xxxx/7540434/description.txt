Puberty Blues is an Australian drama series on Network Ten that began on 15 August 2012.[1] It is based on the 1979 book by Kathy Lette and Gabrielle Carey, which was also the inspiration for the 1981 film Puberty Blues.[2] The opening theme song is "Are You Old Enough?" by Dragon.
