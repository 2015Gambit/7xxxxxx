London Olympics Closing Ceremony

Sociopolitical Documentary hosted by Paul Dickenson and Colin Jackson, published by BBC in 2012 - English narration


Information
------------------------------

Coverage of the 2012 Olympic Games closing ceremony, highlighting the achievements of the men and women who have taken part over the past 16 days, as the Olympic Flame is extinguished and London hands over to Rio de Janeiro, host city for the 2016 event.
