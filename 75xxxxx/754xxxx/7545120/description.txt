Consisting of the 3 Reaves brothers, Ike, Ira, and Loyd,from McRae, as well as Fred Rumble from Judsonia AR. Reaves White County Ramblers were one of the earliest recorded Ozark groups, and one of the most popular selling in the ''Hillbilly'' music market.

Recorded on Friday, April 27, 1928 in Chicago IL for Vocalion Records.
