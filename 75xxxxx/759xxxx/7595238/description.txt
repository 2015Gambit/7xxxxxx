The Next List is a 30-minute weekend television program on CNN. It airs every Sunday afternoon at 2pm ET/PT and is hosted by Dr. Sanjay Gupta. 

Each week, the show profiles innovators, visionaries, and agents of change from around the world who are steadily mapping the course to the future with their new ideas. Yves Behar is a designer, entrepreneur, and sustainability advocate. He is the founder of Fuseproject, the San Francisco and New York based design and branding firm he established in 1999. 

Behar is the Chief Creative Officer of the wearable technology company, Jawbone. He designs the Jawbone brand, packaging, communications and products. He designed the JAMBOX, a completely wireless, bluetooth compact audio speaker and also worked on UP, Jawbone's app-powered wristband. 

He is the chief industrial designer of One Laptop per Child (OLPC's) XO laptop, signing on with the project in 2005 and has been with the team since March 2006. This collaboration has led to two additional laptop prototypes, the OLPC XOXO and OLPC XO-3.

In 2008, Behar redesigned the NYC Condom logo and packaging, as well as NYC Condom vending machines for the New York City Department of Health as a part of an initiative to reduce HIV/AIDS and teen pregnancy.
