Dedication 4 is a mixtape by DJ Drama and Lil Wayne. The mixtape was originally supposed to be released on August 15, 2012[1], but got pushed several times[2][3] and was released on September 3, 2012. It is the fourth installment of Lil Wayne's "Dedication" series, following it's predecessors Dedication, Dedication 2 and Dedication 3. It's also the fourth installment of Lil Wayne's "Gangsta Grillz" tetralogy.
