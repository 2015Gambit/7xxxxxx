Issues 1-15 of 'Little Scrowlie'

Slave Labor Graphics, 2003-2006

Written by Todd Meister

Drawn by Jennifer Feinberg


Peep through Little Scrowlie's comic window, and watch as the over-privileged alterna-chick Elisabeth and her grumpy cat deal with the everyday mundanity of Cthulhu, Cthulhu's minions, and attempts to take over the world through armies of evil fashion zombies.
