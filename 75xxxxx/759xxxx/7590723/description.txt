Change your boot animation with a simple click!

With this wonderful little tool, you are able to change the boot animation of any 2.0+ Android phone. The only requirements are an active internet connection and a rooted phone.

Rooted phone does not mean only root. You need to also have access to /system partition!

Unsupported phones:
-Some tablets work but resolution is not yet supported.
-All Samsung phones/tablets come with a weirdly formatted boot animation. The only way to change this is to flash a rom that supports changing of boot animations.

Pro features include:
-Import ability
-Access to great animations.
