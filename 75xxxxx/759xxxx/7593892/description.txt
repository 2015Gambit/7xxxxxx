B3 system requirements

Minimum System Requirements 
Processor: Dual Core 
Memory: 2 GB 
Hard Drive: 15 GB for Digital Version, 10 GB for Disc Version 
Video Memory: 256 MB 
Sound Card: DirectX Compatible 
DirectX: 10 
Keyboard and Mouse 
DVD Rom Drive 

Recommended System Requirements 
Processor: Quadcore Processor 
Memory: 4 GB 
Hard Drive: 15 GB for Digital Version, 10 GB for Disc Version 
Video Memory: 512 MB 
Sound Card: DirectX Compatible 
DirectX: 11 
Keyboard and Mouse 
DVD Rom Drive
