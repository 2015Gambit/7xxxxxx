Bomb Squad Men: The Long Walk

War Documentary hosted by Ian McElhinney, published by BBC in 2012 - English narration


Information
------------------------------

Three former bomb disposal officers who served at the height of the Troubles, return to Northern Ireland for the first time in thirty years to review the defining moments of their careers and relive the times when they almost lost their lives.
