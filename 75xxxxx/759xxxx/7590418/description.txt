Story Line :

RIK (Gaurav Chakrabarty) : An honest, intelligent and docile software engineer. A class topper in school.

TITO (Indrasish Roy) : An uberpassionate film buff with high flying dreams of winning a Palm D'Or & an Oscar in the near future.

LADEN (Tanaji Dasgupta) : Economically challenged, living in the slums of Park Circus, a skillful craftsman and an unputdownable human being.

DJ (Gourab Chatterjee) : An intensely youthful disc jockey enjoying his role under the spotlight in Kolkata's charismatically lazy nightlife.

Four inseparable friends in school, their lives inextricably intertwined now. The core of their hearts share a common soft spot for KAMALIKA (Ridhima Ghosh), a spirited young woman of stunning beauty who shares similar feelings for all four of them.

Rang Milanti is a game to find the perfect match. A search for the exact shade to compliment the colour of your soul. Kamalika's wild adventure to find the ace in the pack of four.

In the thick of it all are the four eligible bachelors. Add to the mix a popular actress who's seen better days in her personal life, her husband - an aspiring actor, whose female RJ alter ego is more in demand than himself, a dubious young Russian woman with a newborn baby and a missing husband. The adventure gets progressively wilder as the characters slowly reveal their various shades of living colours.
