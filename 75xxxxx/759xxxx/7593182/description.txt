This edition collects the complete run of Ultra: Seven Days. In Spring City, where superheroes blur the line between law enforcement and celebrity, Pearl Penalosa, AKA Ultra, is a workaholic. At the prestigious agency, Heroine Inc., she has earned a legion of fans, a nomination for 'Best Heroine of the Year,' and a perfect life. Well...almost perfect. A chance encounter with a mysterious fortune teller prophesizes true love for Pearl within seven days.
