Snapshot! This is the very reason for Retro Affect's existence. Snapshot is a game about robots, photos, and adventure. Join PIC on his awesome journey into the unknown armed with only his camera that can capture the world around him! Snapshot has been nominated for an IGF award, has been in the Boston Indie Showcase, and even the PAX10! Check it out!
