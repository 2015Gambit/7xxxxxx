This movie tells the story of the latter years of Earl Long, a flamboyant governor of Louisiana (Paul Newman.) The aging Earl, an unapologetic habitue of strip joints, falls in love with young stripper Blaze Starr (Lolita Davidovitch.) Forced to choose between the office he holds and the stripper he loves, Earl chooses both.
