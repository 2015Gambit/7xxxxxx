A Bullet for Pretty Boy is a post-hardcore band formed in 2006 in Longview, Texas was formed by several close friends in the band decided to name themselves after the notorious bank robber Charles "Pretty Boy" Floyd. A Bullet for Pretty Boy is currently signed to Artery Recordings. 

A Bullet for Pretty Boy is at the moment:
Danon Saylor - vocals
Chris Johnston - Guitar
Joshua Modisette - Keys
