Episode 3 : Terror in Tokyo 

On Monday 20th March 1995 during morning rush hour, a terrorist organisation attacked five crowded commuter trains in the heart of Tokyo with nerve gas. Using a real-time split-screen format, this programme reveals, minute by-minute, the horrific effects that nerve gas has on people who are completely unaware of what is happening to them. It explains how their central nervous systems were paralysed by this terrifying man-made agent and how they suffocated where they stood. This anonymous, indiscriminate and seemingly motiveless attempt at mass murder shocked the world.
