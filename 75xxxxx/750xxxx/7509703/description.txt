Some folks seem to have be having problems with the hi-def version of this file, so here it is in a 680 MB AVI file. The Monkees "hip" swan song - a deconstruction of their trip as a rock band. Directed by Bob Rafelson and co-written with Jack Nicholson, it's a mashup of in-jokes, nonsense, a few interesting songs and lots of psychedelia.
