Description

NewPark Blockers is the 21st century bricks game. Three different worlds, 48 levels, constant challenges and lots, lots of adrenaline.

Break your score! You can become the best player in the world!

- 3 different worlds
- 48 Levels
- Special Powerups
- World records
- Fun levels

What's New in Version 1.2

* Improved mouse control: you do not need to keep pressing the mouse to move the paddle.

Requirements: OS X 10.6.6 or later
