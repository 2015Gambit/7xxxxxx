The Word Alive is an American metalcore band from Phoenix, Arizona, formed in 2008. They are currently signed to Fearless records. 

The Word Alive is:
Tyler "Telle" Smith - Vocals
Zack Hansen - Guitar
Tony Pizzuti - Guitar
Daniel Shapiro - Bass
Luke Holland - Drums
