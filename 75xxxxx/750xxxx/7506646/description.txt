Description

Three fireplaces, six waterfalls, four rain scenes, clouds, tranquil ocean and lake scenes - each in HD, and each with a custom designed ambient soundscape. Choose a single scene to play, or select a gallery set, or let Gaze shuffle through all the scenes in the app. A top ten entertainment app on the iPad in the US and Europe, now on the Mac!

Perfect for meditation, contemplation, for the second monitor while you work, or just for a quiet, natural break in the day.

Requirements: OS X 10.6 or later
