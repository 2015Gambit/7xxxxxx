Fascination Africa
Africa - the cradle of humanity, the heart of our Earth, a continent where the sun hits the Earth almost vertically. Fascinating landscapes, hosting a unique fauna which has perfectly adapted to the natural conditions. Come along with us to the realm of the sun and roam endless savannahs, watch elephants at the watering hole, be stunned by lions, zebras, giraffes, hippos and much more. Let the natives of Namibia show you their Africa!
