After the death of Pope Innocent VIII in 1492, Cardinal Rodrigo Borgia, with the help of his sons(!), bribes and cajoles other cardinals to vote for him in the conclave to elect the new pontiff. He succeeds, and assumes the papal throne as Alexander VI.
