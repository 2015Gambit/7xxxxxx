In this collection of posts ripped from her Diary of an Angry Stripper blog Sarah Tressler writes about her experiences as a Houston-based stripper.  Sarah's become infamous lately for 1) her blog post on getting boring head from Jeremy Piven (Ari Gold on Entourage) and 2) getting fired from her job as a society reporter on the Houston Chronicle when they found out she was a stripper. (Sarah has a Master's degree in journalism.)  If you haven't already been following her blog, then this is well worth a read.

This book is in HTML format, suitable for reading in a browser.
