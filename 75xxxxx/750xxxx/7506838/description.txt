Description

A large infestation of flesh eating aliens have invaded the Intergalactic Space Station "Dynamus". You are one of the last remaining survivors of the ship, holding up in the cargo bay. With the aliens surrounding your location, you decide to pilot your Mech and take a last stand against the intruders.

Can you survive against the coming hordes and live to see another day?

Contains:
-Upgradeable Weapons and Stats
-Hordes of enemies
-Hours of addictive fun

Requirements: OS X 10.6.6 or later
