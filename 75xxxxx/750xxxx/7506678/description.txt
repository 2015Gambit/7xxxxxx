Description

Icy Bouncy is a vivacious game released for Mac. The concept of the game is to make Snowman reach his destination.
You have to make the snow man overcome the hurdles like snow blocks to make way towards his home.

Move or bounce Snowman to make him reach his home and make the journey as exciting as possible.
The faster you make him reach his home, better the score.

The levels are well designed to give you a challenging gameplay. The sounds are soothing and let you enjoy the game.
