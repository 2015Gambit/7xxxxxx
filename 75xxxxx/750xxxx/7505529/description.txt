On August 14th, Nick Cave and the Bad Seeds will release the final set of remastered and expanded reissues of their past studio albums. Nocturama (2003), Abattoir Blues / The Lyre of Orpheus (2004), and DIG!!! LAZARUS, DIG!!! (2008) will be available as new CD/DVD Collectors Editions. Each set includes the remastered original stereo album, a specially commissioned short film made by UK artists Iain Forsyth and Jane Pollard, non-album b-sides from the singles, videos, and exclusive sleeve notes.
