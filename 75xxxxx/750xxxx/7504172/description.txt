Wolves at the Gate is an American Christian hardcore band originating from the village of Cedarville, Ohio. They are currently signed to Solid State Records. 

Wolves at the Gate is:
Steve Cobucci - Guitar
Jeremy Steckel - Guitar
Ben Summers - Bass
Nick Detty - Vocals
Ben Millhouse - Drums

03) Morning Star

08) Amnesty
09) Safeguards
10) Through the Night
