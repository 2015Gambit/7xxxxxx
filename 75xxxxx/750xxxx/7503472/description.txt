ΓÇóMovie Synopsis   


A whodunit murder mystery of a married woman, the suspects are college boy friend, the uncle of the girl, a playboy, aunty of her husband and the family advocate. The husband of the woman who had lost his virility in an accident, accidentally kills a friend of his wife who had come to visit them. The woman and her boy-friend, through the ruse of the spirit of the dead woman, trap the murderer.
