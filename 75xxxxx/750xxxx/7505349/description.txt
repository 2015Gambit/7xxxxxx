Once Upon a Time is an American fantasy drama television.

 Created by Lost and Tron: Legacy writers Edward Kitsis and Adam Horowitz,that premiered on Sunday October 23, 2011, on ABC. 
New episodes air Sunday nights at 8:00 pm ET/7:00 pm CT. On November 3, 2011, ABC ordered the back nine episodes for Once Upon a Time, bringing the first season to a total of 22 episodes. On May 10, 2012, ABC renewed the show for a second season, which began filming July 16 2012 and is scheduled to premiere September 30, 2012.
