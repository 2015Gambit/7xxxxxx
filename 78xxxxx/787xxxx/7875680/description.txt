reViSiT is an award-winning tracker that plugs into your VST
host (e.g. Cubase), enabling a fluid tracker user experience
that couples a powerful music notation with the advanced
features of digital audio workstations, such as sequencing,
audio recording, video synchronisation, mixing, effects,
mastering, scoring and more.

512mb
Sound Card with ASIO drivers*
Microsoft Windows 7
