Small collection of converter and export programs for Excel, Outlook, Outlook Express, and for creating vCards.
For activation of the programs, read the .txt file.

The included prgrams are:
- SysTools DBX Converter
- SysTools Excel to Outlook
- SysTools Excel to vCard
- SysTools Outlook Conversion
- SysTools vCard Export
- SysTools WAB Converter

Programs are tested with WinXP SP3 and Win7 Ultimate.
