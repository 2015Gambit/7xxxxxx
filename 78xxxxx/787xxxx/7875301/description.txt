                           Interactive Multimedia - 2010kaiser
                           ~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~


Interactive multimedia is clearly a field of fundamental research, social, educational and economical importance, as it combines multiple disciplines for the development of multimedia systems that are capable to sense the environment and dynamically process, edit, adjust or generate new content. For this purpose, ideas, theories, methodologies and inventions are combined in order to form novel applications and systems. This book presents novel scientific research, proven methodologies and interdisciplinary case studies that exhibit advances under Interfaces and Interaction, Interactive Multimedia Learning, Teaching and Competence Diagnosis Systems, Interactive TV, Film and Multimedia Production and Video Processing.

Title: Interactive Multimedia
Author: Ioannis Deliyannis
Publisher: InTech
Year: 2012
Format: PDF
