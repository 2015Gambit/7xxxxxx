Warrior is the upcoming second studio album by American recording artist Kesha, scheduled to be released on November 30, 2012, by RCA Records.[2] The album was written during 2011 and 2012, inspired by Kesha's spiritual journey, and was recorded between January and August 2012. Its music incorporates dance-pop, electropop, and electronic rock styles. Thematically, Warrior is intended to be more personal than her previous albums, Animal (2010) and Cannibal (2010), with an overall theme of "magic". Contributions to the album's production came from a wide range of producers including executive producer Dr. Luke. The album features songwriting from fun. and The Flaming Lips and features guest vocals from Iggy Pop.

The album's lead single, "Die Young", was released in the United States on September 25, 2012 and is due to be released in the United Kingdom on November 25, 2012. So far, "Die Young" has peaked at 4 on the Canadian Hot 100 and 3 on the US Billboard Hot 100, where it has been certified Platinum for selling more than 1,000,000 units. "C'Mon" serves as the lead promotional single for the album.
