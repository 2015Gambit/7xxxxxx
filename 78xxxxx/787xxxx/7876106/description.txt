Survival: The Ultimate Challenge is a PC strategy game developed and published by Activision Value released in 2001.

The concept of the game is to save a random group of people stuck on an island, a desert or another remote location.
By using what the nature has to offer, the player can, for example, use a leaf to make clothes or wood beams to make a shack.

It's a survival simulation with older graphics.

To play: MOVE ACTIVISION FOLDER TO C:/PROGRAM FILES and then RIGHT CLICK SURVIVAL.EXE and SET COMPATIBILITY TO WINDOWS 98 then OPEN SURVIVAL.EXE or link it to the desktop to play =)
