Wolverine v4 #1-20, 300-316, 1000 (2010-2013)
Publisher:  Marvel
Publication Date:  September 1, 2010 - November 21, 2012

The 2010 Wolverine ongoing series. This follows the events of Wolverine Origins as well as Wolverine Weapon X. Written by Jason Aaron, this volume starts with the storyline "Wolverine Goes To Hell".  After Issue 20, Marvel went to a Legacy numbering, replacing Issue 21 with 300.
