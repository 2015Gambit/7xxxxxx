"Far From The Edge" is the first cd of Pride . The Cd is an excellent debut with catchy melodic hard rock songs in the vein of Dare, White Lion and Def Leppard.

Genre: Melodic Hard Rock / Aor
Country: UK

Line-Up
Matt Mitchell: Vocals
Chris Green: Guitar
Adrian Johnson: Guitar
Simon Farmery: Bass
Rik Eade: Drums
Ivan Gunn: Keys
