The story of a mysterious wanderer who arrives in a small town in North Carolina in search of work but is ostracized by the locals because of rumors that refused to serve their country in World War II. He lives with a woman named Ruby, who is afraid that her husband will not never come back from the front, and her daughter, Pug. When aliens begin to assume the duties of husband and father, the townspeople try to sabotage his relationship with Ruby and Pug, revealing the secrets of his past.
