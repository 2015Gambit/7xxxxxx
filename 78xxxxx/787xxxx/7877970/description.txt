                     The Southern Italian Farmers Table - 2010kaiser
                     ~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~



The Southern Italian Farmers Table is a sumptuously illustrated cookbook featuring authentic recipes from over thirty agriturismi (working family farms that provide room & board to travelers) in central and southern Italy, where the cuisine served epitomizes the farm-fresh movement underway in the United States, the UK, and beyond.

Author(s): Matthew Scialabba, Melissa Pellegrino
Title: The Southern Italian Farmers Table: Authentic Recipes and Local Lore from Tuscany to Sicily
Publisher: Lyons Press; First edition
Year: 2012
Format: PDF
