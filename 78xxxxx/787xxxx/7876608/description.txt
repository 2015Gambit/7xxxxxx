This is my collection of 1500 EPUB and 660 PDF lesbian related EBooks

I have been collecting them for several months and organizing them and converting them from word, html, mobi, txt, ... to EPUB format with full metadata information (you can use Callibe to very easily convert them to any other eBook format), except for PDF EBooks, that I have left unconverted because I don't know of any PDF converter with good results.

I have corrected a lot of format errors for automatic converted PDF books, but I will never have time to read them all, so you will still found a lot of books with of half automatic PDF conversions.


Enjoy it.
