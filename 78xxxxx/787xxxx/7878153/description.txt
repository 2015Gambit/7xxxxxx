What is new in build 2321 (November 30, 2012)

Updates
- Adds support for Holiday Pack vol. 4.
- Improves program stability when using Magic Style or Magic Cut on 50 or 60 FPS videos.
- Adds support for PowerDirector Mobile projects.
- Adds support for NVIDIA GTX 600 series hardware encoding.
- Adds the MPEG-4 Full HD production profile for iPhone 5/4S.
- Adds support for NicoNico Douga in Traditional Chinese and English.
- Adds the "Add New Keyframe" and "Remove All Keyframes"options in the context menu of the PiP Designer.
- Adds the "Duplicate Previous Keyframe" and "Duplicate Next Key frame" options in the context menu of the Particle Designer.

Improvements
- Corrects the video duration of the resulting Magic Movie created movie.
- Improves MPEG-2 video encoder quality.
- Improves compatibility with NVIDIA display driver version 30X.XX.
- Improves H.264 video bitrate upper bound to 200 Mbps for 4K resolution
