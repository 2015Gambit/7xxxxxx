Race Music is the debut album by Caffeine Machine, of which Quinn Arlington Waters explains: "I wanted to make a record like in times of old, meant to be heard from start to finish.The writing, recording, performing and producingof this record took place in a South Philly bedroom, captured with four mics and a DIGI002, with no mods, onto ProTools 8."
