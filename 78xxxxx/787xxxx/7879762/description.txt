Dk:

Umbrellas dødelige T-virus fortsætter med at hærge på jorden, hvor det forvandler sine ofre til kødflænsende udøde. Menneskehedens sidste og eneste håb, Alice, vågner i hjertet af Umbrellas mest hemmelige anlæg, og afslører mere af sin mystiske fortid. Uden noget sikkert tilflugtssted fortsætter hun med at jage de ansvarlige for viruset, en jagt som der fører hende verden rundt. 

Men det hun finder, vil tvinge hende til at ændre opfattelse om alt hvad hun engang troede var sandt.




Eng:

The Umbrella Corporation's deadly T-virus continues to ravage the Earth, transforming the global population into legions of the flesh eating Undead. The human race's last and only hope, Alice (Milla Jovovich), awakens in the heart of Umbrella's most clandestine operations facility and unveils more of her mysterious past as she delves further into the complex. Without a safe haven, Alice continues to hunt those responsible for the outbreak; a chase that takes her from Tokyo to New York, Washington, D.C. and Moscow, culminating in a mind-blowing revelation that will force her to rethink everything that she once thought to be true. Aided by newfound allies and familiar friends, Alice must fight to survive long enough to escape a hostile world on the brink of oblivion. The countdown has begun.
