Welcome to Satulinna, Hirvensalmi. Here 7 Finnish stars will get to know each other.
Each artist has their own day during which they'll tell about their life and career. The artist of the day has come up with mutual activities with which they open their personality.
The atmosphere becomes tenser during mutual meals where the rest of the stars perform their own versions of the hits of the artist of the day.
This is....
           Vain elämää!
