 Judy Allen, "Plan a Great Wedding in Three Months or Less"
2007 | ISBN-10: 1402209053 | PDF | 96 pages | 4 MB

There may be many reasons why a couple opts for a short engagement or decides to move up their wedding date. These couples need a planner that helps them prioritize and move quickly to create a great wedding. Expert event planner Judy Allen shows couples how to:

-- Find immediately available sites and choose the right officiant quickly.
-- Prioritize wedding planning so nothing essential is missed or neglected.
-- Explore such quick turnaround alternatives as destination wedding/honeymoon options and elopement.
-- Save money as well as time on every aspect of the process.

Includes all the essential checklists, timelines, worksheets and resources couples need.
