Episode 18 - RECORDS THAT WILL NEVER BE BROKEN (september 22, 2007)

Plot: The hardest records to break in the NFL. Examples: Otto Graham's ten consecutive championship runs, Johnny Unitas's 47 consecutive games with a touchdown throw (which has since been broken by Drew Brees), and Tom Landry's 29 seasons as Dallas Cowboys coach
