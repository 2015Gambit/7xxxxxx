Alone In The Dark: The New Nightmare (GOG)

a.k.a.: Alone In The Dark 4

Genre: Adventure / Action / Horror
Release date: June 25, 2001
Compatible with: Windows XP, Windows Vista, Windows 7 (32 & 64 bit)
Developer / Publisher: DarkWorks S.A. / Atari
Game modes: single-player
ESRB Rating: Mature with Violence, Blood and Gore.

===

Edward Carnby and Aline Cedrac are about to face their worst nightmare! Out to avenge the murder of their friend and colleague, they arrive on Shadow Island only to discover that it is held in the grasp of an evil force.

Now they must uncover clues to help them reclaim three ancient tablets, before the ultimate terror is unleashed! Confronted by menacing creatures and unspeakable horrors, will they survive the night?

Features:
- The spiritual continuation of the famous Alone in the Dark series - the precursor to the survival horror genre!;
- A chilling and gripping story where there's always something hiding beneath the surface;
- Very immersive horror atmosphere that will make you jump out of your skin.

===

Minimum system requirements:
Windows XP or Windows Vista,
1 GHz Processor (1.4 GHz recommended),
256MB RAM (512 recommended),
3D graphics card compatible with DirectX 7 (compatible with DirectX 9 recommended)
