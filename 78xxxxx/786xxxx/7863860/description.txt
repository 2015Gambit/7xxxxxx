Evo Dash v1.0

Requirements: Android 2.0.1 + 

In Evo Dash, every playthrough is a completely different experience from the next one. As the game progresses, new opponents and bosses will cross your path in random order and with random properties.

Your goal is to survive as long as possible. Can you adapt to the new challenges, or will you perish while trying?

Features: 
Randomized gameplay: every time you play, the game changes completely. Never play the same game twice! 
Fiendish difficulty: try your luck with three difficulty settings, and unlock Extreme Mode for even more craziness! 
Colorful graphics: you are Spherie, the only sphere in Primitopia, where all creatures are made of primitive shapes. 
Original soundtrack by chiptune artist Chipzel.
