I'm a big João Gilberto fan and I have all cds he recorded. The problem with this cd is the quality of the recording. It isn't very good and on some songs the orquestra on the background makes it almost impossible to listen to Gilberto's voice and his guitar. There are some enjoyable songs when there is no orquestra, only Gilberto and his guitar. Only for this part it worth to buy this cd. If you are starting you should get this only after you get the others.
