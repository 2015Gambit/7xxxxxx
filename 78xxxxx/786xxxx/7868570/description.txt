The "Reloading Guide #7" is Nosler's latest and most extensive reloading manual to date. 864 pages are packed with information on all your favorite classic cartridges plus popular, new calibers such as the 300 AAC Blackout, 17 Fireball, 308 Marlin, 6.5 Creedmoor, 6.5 Grendel, 30 T/C and many more. This manual also includes data for Nosler's latest bullet lines Varmageddon, E-Tip, and Ballistic Tip Lead-Free along with the new 2013 released bullets. Detailed step-by-step explanations of the reloading process make this manual ideal for even the most novice reloader.
