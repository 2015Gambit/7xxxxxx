While Kandi settles into her new home with her man Todd, Kim must move out of her dream home in a rushed tizzy after only a year. Kim is forced to relocate her growing family right back into her old townhouse, giving the other ladies plenty to talk about. Porsha is still fuming over her fight with Kenya, but Kenya keeps her eye on the ultimate prize: a ring on her finger. Phaedra throws an over-the-top birthday party for her son Ayden, leading NeNe to deliver some unpleasant news to Cynthia, who wonders why she never received an invite.
