Reporting the World in an Age of Conflict

Sociopolitical Documentary hosted by Allan Little, published by BBC broadcasted as part of BBC The RSE Christmas Lecture series in 2010 - English narration


Information
------------------------------

BBC World Affairs Correspondent Allan Little describes some of the key moments from his career and answers questions about what it is like to report the world in an age of conflict.
