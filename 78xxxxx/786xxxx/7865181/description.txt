
This is the GOG release of the game Gangland by Sirius Games, in it you play Mario Mangano who has come from Palermo, Italy to the fictional Paradise City in the United States. In this game the merges elements of RTS, RPG's, and simulations you'll control yourself and others in the mafia as you work for your uncle and eventually take on your three incredibly dangerous brothers who fled Palermo. Your grandfather sent you for this task, can you take them on?
