Angela Devi Complete Photo And Video Clubs Sets.

Including The Collections of AngelaDevi.com, AngelaDevi.tv, Foxes.com, SurrealErotica.com, and including Angela's Promotional Modeling Shots (pre-SE/Foxes/AD). Over 220 Complete Sets (that's over 14000 Photos) & Over 20 Videos containing long unavailable Photos and Videos spanning her entire short lived career from 1998 to 2006.


This set is released as a Tribute to Angela Devi.

Angela Devi (Real Name: Shunali Dhingra)
July 30, 1975 - March 31, 2006 R.I.P.


Please note, the club set numbering for AngelaDevi.com and .tv do not stay in sequence (i.e. 1,2,3), instead numbering is based on release # spanning across both sites at first but is evident they stopped doing this after a while as eventually photo and video club numbers began to overlap. So do not think there are missing club sets as this collection has been verified against the official lists retrieved from AngelaDevi.com, AngelaDevi.tv, Foxes.com, and SurrealErotica.com using Archive.org's archives from 1998 to 2006.

All sets were purchased or downloaded, collected, and organized by me personally over the past 8 years. Recently, I spent countless days preparing this collection for upload, including Correctly Numbering & Renaming all sets to their original official club numbers and names, in addition, sorting out all duplicates.

I am finally ready to share it with everyone. I hope you love this collection as much as I do and help keep the spirit and legacy of Angela Devi alive.
