John Lennon - Imagine (2 disc edition) [1988]

This "biography" evolves around the nearly 240 hours of film and videotape
fortuitously taken by Lennon of his life. The archive footage is transformed
into a fascinating life story of one of the most complex and fascinating men of
the modern music era. This effort includes a 36 song soundtrack. Includes some
very personal and insightful footage, never before made available to the
public.   

Starring: John Lennon, Yoko Ono, Paul McCartney, George Harrison, Ringo Starr
Director: Andrew Solt
