A Lower Power


Robert O'Geen
USA
English
2009
Drama
98min
Matthew Lotto, J. William McMichael, Terri J. Freedman, Rafe Morgan Kossak, Adrian Anchondo, Donald Joseph, Gigi Guizado

Um jovem gay anseia rejeitar o hedonismo de seus pais, mas reprimir seus desejos sexuais podem custar-lhe seus amigos e o homem dos seus sonhos.

Thibodeaux, a young gay San Franciscan, yearns to reject his parents' hedonistic ways. But when his emerging sexual desires repeatedly get the better of him, he conjures up twisted, self-repressive rationalizations that threaten to drive away his friends and the hunky, mythic man of his dreams.
