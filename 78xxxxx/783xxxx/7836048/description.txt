Khokababu synopsis:

Khokababu is the story of Firdaus ( Shankar Das) who essays the role of an underworld Don who livs in Kolkotta along with his sister Pooja (Subhashree). Dev alias Khokhababu is a diectionless youngster. He joins Shankar Das's organization as an accountant where he gets to know his sister Pooja and ofcourse the inevitable happens. They fall deeply in love and the rest of the story evolves around how Khokababu plays some mind games to win Shankar Das's confidence and marry Pooja.
