Codename: Knockout is a comic book series published by the Vertigo imprint of DC Comics for 24 issues (numbered #0-23) between June 2001 and June 2003. It was created by writer Robert Rodi and artist Louis Small, Jr. The title was a comedy series featuring absurdist espionage plots and artwork heavily inspired by the good girl art of the 1950s and '60s.
