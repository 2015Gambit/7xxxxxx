Alan Carr: Chatty Man S09E09
===============================

Julia Davis chats about the DVD release of her Sky Atlantic sitcom Hunderby, which she co-wrote and starred in, and comedian John Bishop discusses his new stand-up DVD Rollercoaster. Spice Girls Melanie C, Emma Bunton and Geri Halliwell reveal details of Viva Forever, the new musical based on the hit songs of the girl group. Plus, Rita Ora performs her latest single Shine Ya Light.
