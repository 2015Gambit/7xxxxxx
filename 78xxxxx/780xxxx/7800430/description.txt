This is alternative traffic pack (ATP 2.1, October 2012) for the Flight1 Ultimate Traffic.

You no need to install any previous versions or updates for my ATP.


Warning: this traffic is for directX 9 mode!
Warning: you can't use this traffic without F1 license file.

This package includes:
a) modules and utilities of Flight1 Ultimate Traffic version 2.09 (UT2),
b) aircrafts models for FSX and for FS9 (FAIB, AIG, AIA, OSP, TFS, DJC, FMAI, FSP, ABAI, Makc-sim etc.),
c) day and night textures (avsim.su, avsim.net and others sources),
d) schedules of about 1,350 air companies (UTT, AIG, RATS etc),
e) lighting effects (A2Ashockwave 3D Lights Redux),
f) smoke effects for some aircrafts,
g) sounds from "Flight1 TSS Audio Environment - Airliner Edition (v.1.2)"
h) changed dynamics files (option "update dynamics" sets different values of reverse and braking AI aircrafts).
