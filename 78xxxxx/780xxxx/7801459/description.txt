CherryPlayer 1.2.3
CherryPlayer is a next generation media player. It brings together the most popular media functionality into one single product. CherryPlayer gives you endless of streaming music. It has an archive of millions of MP3 songs all available through a nice toplist or a simple search engine. CherryPlayer gives you a YouTube viewer and downloader. It is fully integrated with YouTube allowing you to search for, view and download all YouTube movies in the quality and format that you prefer. CherryPlayer can play all common media files with no need for additional codecs. It supports all major music and video formats out of the box. Best of all. CherryPlayer is completely free to use.
