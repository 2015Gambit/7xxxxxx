-----------------------------------------------------
LINDELL COOLEY DISCOGRAPHY
-----------------------------------------------------

This Lindell Cooley discography may or may not be completed.  Included are the following 4 releases...

 * 1996 - Revival At Brownsville
 * 2000 - Desperate For You
 * 2001 - Open Up The Sky
 * 2007 - Encounter Worship

Lindell Cooley is a Pastor and well-known Christian singer and worship leader, most notably known worldwide as the worship leader of the historic Brownsville Revival in Pensacola, Florida. He is known most for his soulful gospel, rock infused contemporary worship music. Cooley currently serves as president of Music Missions International (MMI) and Pastor at Grace Church in Nashville, Tennessee.
