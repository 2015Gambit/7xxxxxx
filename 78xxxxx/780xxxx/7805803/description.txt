ThumbsPlus version 9

ThumbsPlus is a highly customizable image database with thumbnails and batch editing. It makes it easy to catalog, organize, locate and maintain all of your graphics, multimedia and font files. It runs on Windows 2000, XP, 2003, 2008, Vista and Windows 7 and 8. Both 32 and 64-bit versions operating systems are supported..Version 9 includes access to Facebook photos (your albums and friends' albums), enhancements to Python scripting support, improved image metadata (XMP. EXIF and IPTC) handling, performance improvements, and more.

ThumbsPlus Pro now supports 16 bit per channel images: loading, viewing, editing and saving. The following functions work with 16-bit grayscale and 48-bit color images:

Color Profile
Swap Red & Blue
Invert Colors
Histogram
Remove Red-eye
Image Stamp
Combine
Compare
Filters
Processes
Resize
Rotate
Rotate to Line
Turn & Mirror
Trim
Autocrop
Crop/Add Borders
Cut, Copy and Paste
