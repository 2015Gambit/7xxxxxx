Celebration Day is a concert film by the English rock band Led Zeppelin, recorded on 10 December 2007, at London's O2 Arena. Prior to being released on several home video formats on 19 November 2012, the film was shown in theatres on 17 October. Both the performance, captured at the Ahmet Ertegun Tribute Concert, and the film have been widely praised.
