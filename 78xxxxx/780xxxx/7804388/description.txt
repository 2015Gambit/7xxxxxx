*Mature Readers Please*

Issues 1-2 of 'Skin Tight Orbit'

NBM, 1995

Written by Elain Lee

Illustrated by Michael Kaluta, Will Simpson, Mary Wilshire, Phil Winsdale, Max Dougles, Ken Hooper, Ray Lago, Tony Luke, Garbriel Morrissette, Jim Sherman


Adult themed science fiction short stories with a dark edge.


Issues one contains a story that 'exists somewhere in the Starstruck universe', and another couple stories that may or may not be related to 'Starstruck'
