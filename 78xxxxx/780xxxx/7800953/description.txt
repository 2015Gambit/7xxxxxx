Issues 1-3 of 'Tales From The Behavioral Sink - Insect Fear'

The Print Mint, 1970-1973


Contributors: Spain Rodriguez, Gilbert Shelton, Rory Hayes, Justin Green, Baby Jerry, Alan Shenker, Kim Deitch. S. Clay Wilson, Art Spiegelman, John Peck, Robert Crumb, Roger Brand, Jim Osborne, Willie Mendes, Charles Dallas, Larry S. Todd, Jack Jackson, Joe Schenkman


Underground horror anthology that goes pretty dark and weird where each story often ends with nudge, wink, punchline (one-liner) driven humor. Many of the 'big names' in the underground world contribute to this one.
