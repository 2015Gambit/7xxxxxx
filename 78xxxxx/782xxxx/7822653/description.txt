A terrifying revenge will come to this unsuspecting world, from Notre Dame de Paris.

You take a visit to Paris as a tourist,interrupted by an accident accidentally. The Crown of Thorns, the most precious and revered relic in the Notre Dame Cathedral, has disappeared. You want to have a look but didn't expect to be involved this swashbuckling revenge. For better or worse? It's up to you to track down the priceless artifact. Use your talents to crack the case and before it's too late.

Required Android O/S : 2.0.1+
