A Grammar Book of Finnish is a practical basic grammar for foreign learners of Finnish. It presents clearly the central features of the Finnish grammar, containing the grammar needed for speaking, understanding and reading ordinary standard Finnish.

The book stands as a good support for beginners as well as for more experienced learners. The different word forms and sentence structures are accompanied by charts, tables and plenty of examples to make it easier to understand and memorize things.
