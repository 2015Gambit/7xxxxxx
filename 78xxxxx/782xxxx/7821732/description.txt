From child prodigy to global phenomenon, Alan Yentob reveals the extraordinary 
life of Lang Lang, China's classical music superstar. 

With sell-out concerts around the world and a growing popularity that reaches 
far beyond traditional classical audiences, Lang Lang has redefined the idea of 
the celebrity concert pianist. His ability to connect with a younger generation 
has played a significant role in inspiring over 40 million Chinese children to 
take up the piano. 

In this feature-length documentary, imagine... explores the compelling personal 
story behind the Lang Lang phenomenon.
