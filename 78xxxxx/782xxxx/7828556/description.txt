'Meatsuit' is the debut album of the Italian alternative rockers FALLINGICE.

It was released worldwide in late 2010 via Ukdivision Records and Roadrunner Records Belgium.

The album was recorded with producer Alessandro Paolucci (RAW POWER) at West Link Recordings in Pisa, Italy, mixed by David Lenci (LINEA 77, CHARLOTTE HATHERLEY, ONE DIMENSIONAL MAN) at Red House Recordings and mastered at Precision Mastering in Hollywood, California, by Tom Baker (HELMET, NINE INCH NAILS, MARILYN MANSON, DEFTONES, ALTER BRIDGE).
