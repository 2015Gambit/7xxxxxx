Grotesk the Subhuman is an Arkansas hip hop artist from the city of Fayetteville. Since 2004, he has been recording and performing throughout the Ozark region. Grotesk has an extensive catalog and he has been remixed by producers from all over the world. He was nominated in 2012 for Best Hip Hop Artist by the Northwest Arkansas Music Awards.
