- + Info(wikipedia.org/wiki/Oktubre):

Oktubre is the second album by Argentine rock band Patricio Rey y sus Redonditos de Ricota, released in 1986.[1][2]
Hailed by critics and fans as the bands masterwork, the album has some of the groups signature songs such as "Jijiji" and "Motor Psico". The album is ranked at number 4 in the Argentine version of Rolling Stone's "Los 100 Mejores Discos del Rock Nacional" (The 100 Greatest Records in Argentine Rock).
