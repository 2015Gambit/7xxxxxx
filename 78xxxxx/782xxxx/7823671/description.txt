Read Me Before Downloading 

iOS 6.1 beta 2 can be installed using over the air software update from devices that have iOS 6.1 beta installed. Over the air software update can be initiated by choosing General to Software Update in Settings.

Be sure to backup your devices using iTunes 10.7 or through iCloud backup prior to installing iOS 6.1 beta. After installing iOS 6.1 beta, you can restore your device using either iTunes 10.7 or through your iCloud backup.

Determine the proper download for your device.
