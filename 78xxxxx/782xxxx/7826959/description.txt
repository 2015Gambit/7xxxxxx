The Bushcraft & Survival Training Unit was introduced to provide members of the Western Australia Police Service with the necessary knowledge and skills to carry out their duties in Outback Western Australia and to enable them to co-ordinate or participate in emergency operations and advise on Outback safety. In keeping with the WA Police Service mission to provide a safer and more secure Western Australia the Unit was concerned with the education of interested community groups and individuals.
