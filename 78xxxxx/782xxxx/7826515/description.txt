DC Universe: Legacies are a 2010-2011 ten-issue comic book limited series written by Len Wein and published by DC Comics. It details the perspective of an admirer of superheroes in the DC Universe from the Golden, Silver, Bronze, and Modern eras of comic books.

In a retirement home, an aged man, named Paul Lincoln, is an admirer of superheroes.  Paul has collected newspapers and magazines featuring superheroes.  In this series, Paul recounts his years of following the exploits of superheroes throughout the ages.
