Description:

The Sanford Guide has helped health care professionals make informed treatment decisions since 1969. The Sanford Guide to Antimicrobial Therapy 2010 is the 40th edition of the classic infectious diseases reference. You will find a collection of text and images reflecting our 40 year history; revamped news & notices sections containing content update notices and other relevant information; links to useful websites (taken from the Resources tables in the Sanford Guides), and an entirely new store which provides a much-improved shopping experience.
