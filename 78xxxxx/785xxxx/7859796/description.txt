*** Macro Chants Pack for PC version of PES 2013 ***

- Compatible if you don't use any patch
- Compatible with Pesedit Patch
- Compatible with PESJP Patch
- Compatible with Jenkey's gameplay tool 2.1
- everything edited and adapted for PC version of PES 2013
- audios downloaded in MP3-format and converted into ADX (stereo/24,000 Hz)
- High quality audio for increased realism
- All the new chants for the 2012/13 season
- More than 1,000 songs for 123 clubs included
