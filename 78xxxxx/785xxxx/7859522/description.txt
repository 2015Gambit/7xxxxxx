Angela Devi Complete Photo And Video Clubs (Sets). Including Collections from AngelaDevi.com, AngelaDevi.tv, Foxes.com, and a few other sets. Over 14000 Photos & Over 20 Videos!

This set is released as a Tribute to Angela Devi. It contains long unavailable Photos and Videos spanning her short lived career from 1999 to 2006.

Angela Devi (Real Name: Shunali Dhingra)
July 30, 1975 - March 31, 2006 R.I.P.

Please note, the club sets numbering do not stay in sequence (i.e. 1,2,3), instead numbering is based on release # spanning across all sites or some other system. So do not think there are missing club sets as this collection has been verified against retrieved lists from AngelaDevi.com, AngelaDevi.tv, and Foxes.com using Archive.org's archives from 1999 to 2006.

All sets were purchased, downloaded, collected, and organized by me personally over the past 8 years and I am finally deciding to share it with everyone.
