Episode 5 - Black Butterfly

The charred body of a medium is found in the gutted remains of her office after a fire.
And it soon turns out that she was murdered —suffocated with a plastic bag and doused in
kerosene, the woman was left to die in the flames. When another two murders take place in the same manner, it becomes clear to the Gotland police that they have a serial killer on their hands - but without having the slightest idea what the victims had in common, or who the next one might be. 

Maria Wern is forced to lead an investigation where neither the motive nor the victims seem to be connected — at the same time that her colleague Arvidsson’s loyalty is forcibly put to the test...
