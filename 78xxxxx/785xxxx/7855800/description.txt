Status Quo: Hello Quo

Arts Documentary hosted by Alan G Parker, published by Geezer & Blonde production in 2012 - English narration


Information
------------------------------

Status Quo: Hello Quo Extra 2  Access All Areas 
 This Access All Areas Collector s Edition includes 90mins of astonishing never seen before Quo footage!Climaxing with a reunion of the original four band members, even fans may be a little under whelmed by the final performance but it is great to see that even old rock stars can swallow their pride and work out their differences for a rocking reunion.  Quo fans will be mesmerized; everyone else will be amply entertained by this exhaustive rock-doc.
