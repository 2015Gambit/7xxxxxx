Inside the Worlds of Star Wars Trilogy
Dk Pub | 2004 | ISBN 0756603072 | PDF | 47 pages | 28 Mb
Written by James Luceno


Inside the Worlds of Star Wars Trilogy is a 47-page reference book that covers the locations of the original trilogy. It was written by James Luceno and illustrated by Hans Jenssen and Richard Chasemore with Curtis Saxton as consultant. The book is illustrated with 20 full-color, fantastically intricate cross-sections, and features an array of spot artworks, 3-D plans, more than 60 crystal-clear photographs, and detailed orthographic keys to create an extraordinary visual experience.
