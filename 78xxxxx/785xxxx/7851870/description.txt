After leaving prison, a gangster (Colin Farrell) decides to turn his life around and leave the crime.
With the new objective set, he is hired as security for a young actress (Keira Knightley) who lives secluded from the world, afraid of everything.
In their search for freedom, each in their own way, they end up finding love.
