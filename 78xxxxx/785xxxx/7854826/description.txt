Live at Hammersmith '82! is a Duran Duran limited edition DVD/CD 2-disc live album, released by EMI on 7 September 2009.

The album was recorded during The Rio Tour at the Hammersmith Odeon in London, UK on 16 November 1982. This was the third of three shows performed at the Odeon on the tour.
