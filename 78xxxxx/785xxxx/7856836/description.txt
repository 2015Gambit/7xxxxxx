'All good things come to an end'

After 6 years of bringing you the latest Hands Up, 
Trance, House and Hardstyle hits, Clubfanatix comes to
an end. 300 volumes, it's been one hell of a ride. But 
nowadays the EDM (and in particular Hands Up) scene is
nothing like it used to be. We hope you've enjoyed every
single volume we've released. I would like to thank
VANiLLA for starting this bootleg compilation, it's what
got me into EDM in the first place. Enjoy this last
volume and remember: we'll always have the memories.
