Match of the Day 24.11.2012

Duration: 1 Hour, 20 Min

Language : English 

Gary Lineker presents highlights of Saturday's six Premier League games, with analysis from Mark Lawrenson and Dion Dublin. Manchester United welcome QPR to Old Trafford, defending a 10-match all-time Premier League record against the side. QPR's only previous point at Old Trafford came in a goalless draw 20 years ago.

Aston Villa entertain Arsenal, hoping to finally end the Gunners' 13-match unbeaten run against Villa at Villa Park dating back to December 1998, while Sunderland welcome West Brom to the Stadium of Light, a fixture that has seen at least four goals scored on each of the last three meetings.
