Morphing Galaxy full version v1.7

Requirements: 2.1+

Experience how a cluster of shining particles appear like a morphing galaxy!


Experience how a cluster of shining and colorful particles appear like a morphing galaxy! This 3D visualisation is inspired by formulas and patterns from galaxy formations.
You can select between 28 different color modes. You can also change the size and brightness of the stars. It is also possible to adjust the speed, the backgrounds and much more. Battery saving functionality is also available.
HOW TO OPEN:
Menu>Wallpaper>LIVE wallpapers>Morphing Galaxy
This live wallpaper is very suitable for meditation or just relaxing, while watching the ever changing and morphing 3D galaxy.
Turn off "Halo effect" on the settings if it does not look like it should! "Halo effect" is a technique, which creates shining star objects. This is not supported on all devices. This app will look totally different with "Halo effect". Try the different settings for the "Halo effect" to experience this.
