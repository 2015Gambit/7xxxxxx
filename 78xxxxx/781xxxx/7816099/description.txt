A trip round the world is the ultimate travel experience. To do it at twice the speed of sound in a privately chartered Concorde, however, is a mere dream for most of us. 

Follow the cameras aboard, as this extraordinary journey takes in Manhattan, the South Seas, Australia, India, Africa, Petra and Venice. Join the cameras on this rare and ultimately exclusive journey that even most Concorde pilots do not get the chance to experience.

Feauturing excellent footage of take offs and landings from the cockpit and runway, these films showcase one of the most beautiful pieces of engineering ever created.
