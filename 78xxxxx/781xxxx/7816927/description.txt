Match of the Day Two 11.11.2012

Duration: 1 Hour 10 Min

Colin Murray presents the best of the day's Premier League action.

Champions Manchester City host Tottenham in a re-run of last season's game at the Etihad, which saw a late Mario Balotelli penalty give Roberto Mancini's side victory.

Liverpool traveled to Stamford Bridge to face Chelsea, who they did the Premier League double over last season, either side of defeat to Roberto di Matteo's men in the FA Cup final.

Elsewhere, Sam Allardyce takes West Ham to the north-east for a meeting with his former club, Newcastle United.

There is also a chance to catch up with the goals from Saturday's games.
