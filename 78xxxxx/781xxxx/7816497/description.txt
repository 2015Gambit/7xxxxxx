Bill Gates Co-founder of Microsoft (2012)
Publisher:  Bluewater Comics
Publication Date:  May 23, 2012

Written by Martin T. Pierro
Pencils by Zach Bassett
Inks by Maikel L. Garcia and Gabriela Lazbal

He has been called the sultan of software and the master of the modern PC, and now his story comes to comics. Follow the life of William Gates from his early days writing basic code through the rise of Microsoft to his current humanitarian works. See what has driven this amazing man to become an architect of the future.
