The "Edo Lilipoupoli (Here Lilliput)" was a children's radio series which began in 1976 and lasted until 1980. 
The series sounded in the Third Programme of the Greek Radio, which was at that time under the direction of Manos Hadjidakis. 
The show is considered nowadays 'legendary', both for originality and quality, and the effect on the overall period. (wikipedia)




Rosa Rosalia 


At the pink sugar beach
everybody talked about Rosa-Rosalia
who used to put in her cheeks
a little bit of strawberry cream
and in the pink sunrise
take her rosy piglet for a walk.

And clouds of pink
flamingo birds sang to her
Rosa-Rosalia.

Ah, Rosa, Rosa-Rosalia,
let's go to the concert together,
and, together with all the violins,
let a big pink cherry tree blossom
on our first kiss.

At the pink sugar beach
they still talk of Rosa-Rosalia
and they say that, in spring,
like a pink memory
passes by, far away in the pink sunrise,
her pink piglet.

And a pink music box made of cloth
still plays "Rosa-Rosalia".

Ah, Rosa, Rosa-Rosalia,
let's go to the concert together,
and, together with all the violins,
let a big pink cherry tree blossom
on our first kiss.
