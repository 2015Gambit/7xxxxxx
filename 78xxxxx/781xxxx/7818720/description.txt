This is the freshman release of Indianapolis-based Rock/Alternative band These City Limits.  If you're a fan of Muse, My Chemical Romance, or Foo Fighters, this is for you.  

They are comprised of members from Scarlet (Ferret Records), Ghost of Maine, Into the Silence, and Dent.   Produced by Andreas Magnusson (Black Dahlia Murder, Haste the Day) in 2011.

Full length due out summer of 2013.
