Imaginaerum by Nightwish, The Score

A CD with the soundtrack of the film was released for pre-order on Nightwish's official website. The songs are reinterpretations by Petri Alanko of songs previously released on the band's album Imaginaerum. The soundtrack will be officially released on November 10, 2012.
