Remembrance Sunday: The Cenotaph (BBC)

War Documentary hosted by David Dimbleby and Sophie Raworth, published by BBC in 2012 - English narration


Information
------------------------------

David Dimbleby and Sophie Raworth present as Her Majesty the Queen leads the nation's Remembrance Sunday commemorations from the Cenotaph in Whitehall. Dignitaries from around the Commonwealth, the prime minister, leading politicians, representatives of many of the world's religions and military leaders join thousands of veterans from countless conflicts for the two minute silence, service and march past. All gather to remember those men and women who have died in action serving their country.
