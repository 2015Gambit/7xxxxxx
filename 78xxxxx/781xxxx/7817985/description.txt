Ep 2: Excess Breeds Success
NeNe continues her reign of fame and fabulousness in Los Angeles as she promotes her new sitcom.
PREMIERE: Sunday November 11, 9 pm

NeNe continues her reign of fame and fabulousness in Los Angeles as she promotes her new sitcom, The New Normal. Meanwhile in Atlanta, Kim celebrates a birthday with the thought of homelessness looming on the horizon. Kandi settles into her new home and life with Todd, as she and Phaedra bond with the new Housewife on the block, Kenya Moore. Meanwhile, Kenya struggles with family pressures, the ticking of her biological clock, and a shocking revelation from her boyfriend. All comes to a head when Cynthia throws a party, which leads her to another faceoff with Kenya in round two of battle of the beauties.
