The Ed Show; November 19, 2012.

 Democrats are refusing to budge on letting the Bush tax cuts expire, even if that means letting all the tax rates expire. And now big labor is on board!

Rep. Bill Pascrell (D-NJ) and Robert Reich(former U.S. Labor Secretary, UC Berkeley Professor), talked about the latest movement in the fight for the middle class.

Then, a judge ordered mediation instead of allowing more than 18,000 jobs to vanish if the makers of Twinkies go out of business. Matt Taibbi (journalist for Rolling Stone), joined Ed for that.

Sen. John McCain asked for an apology from U.N. Ambassador Susan Rice. Katrina Vanden Heuvel (Co-owner/Editor of The Nation magazine), sounded off on that one.

Tea Party Rep. Allen West has been defeated but he just won't quit.  Ed found out what's next from Mitch Ceasar (Democratic Chairman, Broward County, Florida).
