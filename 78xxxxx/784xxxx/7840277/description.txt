Kung Fu - Cheng_Style_BaGua_Swimming_Dragon

Cheng style Ba Gua Zhang has an emphasis on fast wrestling and throwing, so contained within it are both chin-na and anti-chin-na techniques. You should learn and practice the eight palms before attempting to learn this complex and squirming form.

Presented here is the Cheng Style BaGua Swimming Dragon with Liu Jing-Ru.
