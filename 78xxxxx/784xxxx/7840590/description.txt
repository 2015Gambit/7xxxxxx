Hellgate



Genre: Thriller

Year: 2011

Country: USA, Thailand

Runtime: 93 min

Directed by John Pennell

Cast: Liz Burnett, Carrie Elves, John Henbest and others.



.: Plot:.

Western businessman, along with his Thai wife and son suffered a terrible accident during a visit to Bangkok. In short, they believe that hides a world between life and death where endless darkness.
