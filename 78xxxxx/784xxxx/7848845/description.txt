Dominican Republic: Baseball Dreams

Sociopolitical Documentary hosted by Seyi Rhodes, published by Channel 4 broadcasted as part of CH4 Unreported World series in 2012 - English narration


Information
------------------------------

Reporter Seyi Rhodes and producer Daniel Bogado travel to the Dominican Republic to meet the boys desperate to make it as US Major League baseball players. In a country where many are still very poor, baseball is one of the few routes out for young men. But the chance of making it is slim. And the cost of failure at 18 years old can be devastating. For more information visit channel4.com/unreportedworld or follow the programme on Facebook.
