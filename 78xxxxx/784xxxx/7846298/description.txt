          ***** CrackMyDay brings you Cabinet Creator Euro v2.0 *****

Cabinet Creator Euro version 2.0 is designed for the shop or individual who wants to start building their European styled cabinets, vanities, bookcases, etc. immediately. Cabinet Creator Euro will produce a cutlist for Euro cabinets with overlay or flush style doors and drawer fronts. With very little dimensional input, this program will produce a complete cutlist for each cabinet, (including cabinet boxes, face frames, doors, drawer fronts, shelves and drawer boxes). It will also produce a front elevation drawing to scale of each cabinet.  It also includes  a panel door program, a master cutlist program, and a calculator/converter program.  The program allows input and output in metrics, decimal inches and fractional inches, so whatever your measurement system it will work for you.
