Fiction, by Charles Narasi MD
September Mourning

The Year was 1964.

Two young and bright medical graduates from India embark on a much dreamed long journey to USA to pursue their goals of higher learning and advanced stidies.They both succeed in their endeavors only to get caught in turmoils during personal struggles in their own lives.The great desire to assimilate in the cultural divide brings unexpected results.They will become the victims of selfishness and greed and one of them loses the fight to survive.
