Painting for Photographers: Steps and Art Lessons for Painting Photos in Corel Painter and Adobe Photoshop
Pdf | Artistry Books | Sperling K | Eng | 128 Pages | 2009 | Isbn 0981816304 | 26 Mb

Photographers nowadays are interested in turning their digital photos into paintings in Corel Painter and Adobe Photoshop.
And now Painting for Photographers, published by Artistry Books, shows both the art lessons and software steps for transforming photos into works of art.

Written and illustrated by Painter Master Karen Sperling, one of the world's foremost Painter authorities and author of the first several Painter manuals and several previous Painter books, Painting for Photographers is the first art book for photographers.
