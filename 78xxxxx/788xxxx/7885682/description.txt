Bach: 48 Preludes and Fugues - The Well Tempered Clavier movie was released Sep 04, 2001 by the Image Entertainment studio. To commemorate the 250th anniversary of Bach's death four of the world's best classical pianists have gathered to record the 48 preludes and fugues of THE WELL TEMPERED CLAVIER. Bach: 48 Preludes and Fugues - The Well Tempered Clavier movie Andrej Gavrilov, Angela Hewitt, Joanna MacGregor, and Nikolai Demidenko each perform 12 preludes and fugues from selected locations in Barcelona, Venice, Oxford and Bach's birthplace, Eisenach to honor their instrument's spiritual grandfather.
