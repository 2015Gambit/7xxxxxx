Caprica is a science fiction drama television series.
It is a spin-off prequel of the re-imagined Battlestar Galactica, taking place about 58 years prior to the events of Battlestar Galactica.
Caprica shows how humanity first created the robotic Cylons who would later plot to destroy humans in retaliation for their enslavement.
Among Caprica's main characters are the father and uncle of Commander William Adama from the re-imagined Battlestar Galactica.
