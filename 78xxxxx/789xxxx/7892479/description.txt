What would you do if you discovered you were turning to stone? What would that extraordinary circumstance do to your life? Writer Joe Casey and Eisner Award-nominated artist Charlie Adlard answer these questions and more in this long-awaited, gut-wrenching original graphic novel drama. Thomas Dare was an ordinary man with ordinary problems, until fate steeped in to turn his life upside-down.
