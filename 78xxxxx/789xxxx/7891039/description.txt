Title: The Chosen: Well of Souls
Genre: RPG / fantasy / 3rd-person
Released: 15th December 2006
Developer: Rebelmind
Publisher: Meridian4
Description:
The Chosen: Well Of Souls is an action RPG set in a dark period of the 19th century. The story revolves around 3 characters: Frater Simon, Elena, and Tong Wong (respectively mage, thief, and warrior), who are members of the Mystery Guards Fraternities, organizations devoted to exterminating demons and dark forces. They are now after an immortal sorcerer, Marcus Dominus Ingens, who commands armies of demons, summoned with the souls of alchemists and is bent on destroying God's Envoy, a powerful being who appears every 200 years. There are more than a dozen quests filled with role-playing action, and each character's attributes can be modified and enhanced with more than 200 weapons, armor, and magic upgrades.

Bonus content: characters, manual, wallpapers.

How to play:
1) Run setup_the_chosen.exe and install the game;
2) Launch the game using main executable or shortcuts (DRM free).
