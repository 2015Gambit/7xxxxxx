nKPro Racing is one of the most realistic racing simulators of the year! Developed by Italian racing-simulation specialists Massarutto, nKPro Racing is a thoroughbred racer featuring a cutting edge physics engine.

This hyper-realistic driving experience also includes realistic weather which affects racing conditions, such as an aquaplaning simulation for those tricky wet weather races, interactive cockpits and telemetry screens providing accurate data output and analysis. With nine racing car classes encompassing Prototypes, Single Seaters and GT, multiplayer support for up to 15 players, along with a track editor that allows the online community to create and upload their own tracks, nKPro Racing is the ultimate racing challenge!
