Ep 4: Creative Clash
Jeffrey struggles on an install without Ross, while Martyn redecorates against his partner's wishes.
PREMIERE: Wednesday December 5, 10 pm


Ross jets off to New York for a modeling job, leaving Jeffrey in the lurch during the design of a multi-million dollar beach house in La Jolla, California. Martyn decides to redecorate his living room against the wishes of his partner Michael, and Mary clashes with fashion designer Monika Chiang over the design of her new Manhattan boutique.
