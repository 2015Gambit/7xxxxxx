           The Gale Encyclopedia of Mental Disorders(2 Vol)-2010kaiser
           ~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~



This comprehensive 2-vol. set provides understandable yet detailed information on mental disorders and conditions. Featuring an easy-to-use format and written in understandable language, the Encyclopedia includes entries for all 150 disorders classified in the Diagnostic and Statistical Manual of Mental Disorders, text revision (DSM-IV-TR). It also features entries for prescription, alternative and over-the-counter drugs, as well as the various therapies used to treat mental disorders.

Title: The Gale Encyclopedia of Mental Disorders (2 Vol. Set)
Author: Ellen Thackery, Madeline Harris
Pulisher: GALE
Year: 2011
Format: PDF
