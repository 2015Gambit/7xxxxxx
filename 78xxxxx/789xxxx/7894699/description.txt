Title: Railroad Pioneer
Genre: managerial / train / isometric
Released: 29th October 2003
Developer: Kritzelkratz 3000
Publisher: JoWooD Productions
Description:
Embark into a new world full of adventure, business, power and money. Brave pioneers set out to conquer the new world with their iron horses. As the owner of a small railroad company in the year 1830, your job is to build America's first transcontinental railroad line. Thanks to perfect planning, keen business know-how and a healthy portion of pioneering spirit, this is your chance to rewrite the history of the United States.

Bonus content: artworks, manual, soundtrack.

How to play:
1) Run setup_railroad_pioneer.exe and install the game;
2) Launch the game using main executable or shortcuts (DRM free).
