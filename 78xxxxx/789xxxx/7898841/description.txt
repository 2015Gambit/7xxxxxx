A complete and updated (as of 11/28/2012) Superman Chronology for the Post-Crisis, Pre-Flashpoint timeline.  Includes all appearances of Superman, all Supergirls, Superboy, Steel (John Henry Irons) and Lex Luthor between 1986 (following Crisis on Infinite Earths) and 2011 (up through Flashpoint).

This Part covers chapters 151-200, from the Matrix entity's return in 1999 through the aftermath of Our World's at War in 2002.

DC Comics, writers: Dan Jurgens, J.M. DeMatteis, Jeph Loeb, Jerry Ordway, Joe Kelly, Karl Kesel, Louise Simonson, Mark Shultz, Peter David, Ron Marz, Stuart Immonnen, Tom Peyer, et al.

Main Series: Superman (1987-2006) 143-176, Action Comics (1989-2011) 753-785, Superman: The Man of Steel (1991-2003) 88-120, The Adventures of Superman (1987-2006) 566-598, Superboy (1994-2002) 60-94, Supergirl (1996-2003) 30-64, Superman: The Man of Tomorrow 10-15 plus various mini-series and guest appearances in other book.
