"One Day at a Time (Em's Version)" is the second posthumous single released by American rapper Tupac Shakur from the soundtrack album Tupac: Resurrection: The Original Soundtrack. The track is American rapper Eminem's take on the 1996 original, which features both Shakur and Spice 1. Eminem's version features vocals from both himself and Outlawz.
