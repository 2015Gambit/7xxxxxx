The Rolling Stones... Queen... Blur...Coldplay...Depeche Mode...The Jam...David Bowie...Elton John...Paul McCartney...New Order...George Michael...Amy Winehouse...Stone Roses...T.Rex...Madness...Pulp...The Police...Rod Stewart...LilyAllen...Soul II Soul...Primal Scream...
Almost certainly the most comprehensive collection of British musical talent ever, the Best Of British gathers together 60 of our best-loved, unofficial national anthems from across 5 decades of world-beating rock 'n' roll.
Featuring a host of premier league artists who rarely clear for compilations, The Best Of British makes for an all-killer, no-filler ride from the 1960s to the 2000s, taking in Beat Groups, Glam Rockers, Singer-Songwriters, New Wave Icons, Electropoppers, Indie Gods and Stadium Superstars along the way.
