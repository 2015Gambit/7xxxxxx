The 2002 NPC USA Championships brought together an incredible array of rock hard and shredded competitors from throughout the nation for one incredible showdown in Las Vegas. The backstage warm-up area is packed with muscle as USA competitors ready to take the stage. You'll see competitors from the bantamweight and lightweight classes pump up with weights, oil up and practice their poses. Shot during both the Evening Show and Prejudging portions of the contest using multiple cameras, this pump room video features an incredibly up-close look at this contest's finest. Featuring Eryk Bui, Jessue Duque, Derik Farnsworth, Jim Propst, Heath Warren, and many more. 1 hour 22 minutes
