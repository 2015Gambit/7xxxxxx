Depths of Peril is a single-player action role-playing game with strong strategy elements. You play a faction leader protecting the barbarian city, Jorvik, from an onslaught of marauding monsters. At the same time, you compete with rival factions over who will rule the city. Barbarians choose their leaders by fighting to the death!
