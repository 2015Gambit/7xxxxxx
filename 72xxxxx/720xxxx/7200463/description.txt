Angry Birds is a puzzle based game with numerous levels. The premise of the game is that naughty pigs have captured eggs belonging to the angry birds and built structures made of various materials around them.

The object is to use a slingshot to fire your angry birds at the structures in order to free the eggs. The angry birds all have different abilities, such as being able to be split into three after being fired.
