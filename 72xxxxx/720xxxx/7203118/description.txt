The 14-year-old boy Walter (Haley Joel Osment) is to spend the summer in Texas with 
his two rather eccentric and embittered uncles, Garth (Michael Caine) and Hub (Robert 
Duvall). However, Walter is one surprise after another when his old uncles tell him 
stories about their experiences in North Africa. The two men have since lived in a 
country with beautiful princesses, golden mountains and rich sheiks. Whether these 
stories are true is not Walter. He feels very at home with his "new" family. And the 
two old men are now giving ground and very happy with the company of Walter. Then 
Walter's mother returned to pick him up ...

"Secondhand Lions" is written and directed by Tim McCanlies, who previously wrote the 
script of 'The Iron Giant "(" The Iron Giant "). It is a family comedy film set in the 
early sixties and is about a shy boy, played by Haley Joel Osment (The Sixth Sense ") 
that fully blossomed when he joined his oudooms resides in Texas. The peculiar and 
eccentric uncles are brilliantly played by the two old pros Robert Duvall and Michael 
Caine.
