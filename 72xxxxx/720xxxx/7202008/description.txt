Debut EP "reMixes 01" of remixes from the electronic weed band ZonK.  The first single is the title track "ZonK" based on a popular bong smoking dice game.  ZonK's debut album is set to drop June 1st 2012, so here's a good chance to catch some banging tracks.  Hip hop, dubstep, industrial, chillwave, it's all in this remix EP from various producers around the US.
