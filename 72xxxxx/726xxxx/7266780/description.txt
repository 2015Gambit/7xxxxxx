The One is a medieval fantasy set in a fantastic but at the same time very realistic world. After an era of peace the races that inhabit this land fall back into a dark middle ages. The lust for power creeps again and a bloody war appears imminent. The last glimmer of hope lies in the One, a mythical and legendary sword that Faras, Haggarth, Omir and Masdin are looking for, waiting for the great battle.

Published by GG Studios.
