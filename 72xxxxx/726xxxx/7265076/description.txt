Author   S.C.Stephens

Title   Thoughtless

Genre   Romance

Published   June 11th 2010

Plot

For almost two years now, Kieras boyfriend, Denny, has been everything shes ever wanted: loving, tender and endlessly devoted to her. When they head off to a new city to start their lives together, Denny at his dream job and Kiera at a top-notch university, everything seems perfect. Then an unforeseen obligation forces the happy couple apart.
