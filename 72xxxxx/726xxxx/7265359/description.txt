VA-FabricLive 63 Mixed By Digital Soundboy Soundsystem-CD-2012-WAV

Digital Soundboy Soundsystem have mixed Fabriclive 63, due for release in May.

The Digital Soundboy label was started by Shy FX in 2005 and has been an 
outlet for drum & bass and dubstep from artists like Skream, DJ Fresh, 
Redlight and Donae'o ever since. Fabriclive 63 marks a first for the label 
with "Digital Soundboy Soundsystem," a trio comprised of Shy FX and label 
regulars Breakage and B. Traits. It was mixed in a collaborative process, 
with each DJ offering up track selections and ideas, then coming together 
at the Digital Soundboy studio to find ways to mix them in sequence.
