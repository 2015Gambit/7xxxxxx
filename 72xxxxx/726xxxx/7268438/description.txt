Quarters Aquariums Limestone and Highlighters

Technology Documentary hosted by Zac Fine, published by Discovery Channel broadcasted as part of DC Factory Made series in 2009 - English narration


Information
------------------------------

Find out what it really takes to make lots and lots of quarters, how does a blast of water make a fish tank watertight, how do they blast limestone without blasting it into tiny bits, and why are highlighters yellow.
