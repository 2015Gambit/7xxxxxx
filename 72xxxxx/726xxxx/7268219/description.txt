BBC MOTD 13/05/2012

Duration: 1 Hour, 39 Min

Gary Lineker presents highlights of what promises to be the most dramatic ever finishes to a Premier League season.

One of the two Manchester clubs will win the title. They start the day level on points: City host relegation-threatened QPR, United travel to Sunderland.

Elsewhere, Bolton travel to Stoke knowing they have to win to have a chance of avoiding relegation. And Arsenal, Tottenham, Newcastle and Chelsea are all hoping to clinch European qualification.

There are ten games in total, plus Goal of the Season.
