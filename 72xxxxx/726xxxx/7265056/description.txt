Necrotik Fissure is a hungarian Harsh Noise/HNW formation. It's a side project of Inhos, from Halalnihil, which is a combination of Power Electronics, Harsh Noise, and some Black Metal. 

This is the third album by Necrotik Fissure, and in FLAC format. Very loud, extremely distorted static noise. Recommended to listening on any volume, and to sleep.
