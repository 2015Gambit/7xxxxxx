Safety Related Issues of Spent Nuclear Fuel Storage[Team Nanban][TPB]

J. D. B. Lambert, K. K. Kadyrzhanov | Published: 2007-05-09 | ISBN: 1402059019, 1402059027 | PDF | 370 pages | 7.44 MB

DESCRIPTION:

This book contains papers from a NATO-sponsored workshop in Almaty in September 2005, which discussed safety-related issues of 
storing spent nuclear fuel. Fifteen papers cover aluminum-clad fuel discharged from research reactors worldwide, 
while five papers examine stainless steel-clad fuel from fast reactors, and two Zircaloy-clad fuel from commercial light-water reactors.
