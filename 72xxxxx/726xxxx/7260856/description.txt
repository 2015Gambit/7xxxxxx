Trace (Tab Hunter) discovers the body of his brother Jerry and confronts Cattle Baron, Mr. Sutton, the crook responsible for his brother's death. During an argument, Trace shoots and injurs Mr. Sutton. Sutton sends his henchmen to hunt and kill a wounded and fleeing Trace. Maria,(Natalie Wood) a half-breed Mexican girl whose father was murdered by Sutton, becomes Trace's companion in flight.
