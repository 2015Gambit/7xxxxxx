Sepultura (Portuguese for "grave") is a Brazilian metal band from Belo Horizonte, formed in 1984. 

The band was a major force in the death metal, thrash metal and ultimately groove metal realms 

during the late 1980s and early 1990s, with their later experiments melding nu metal, hardcore punk
