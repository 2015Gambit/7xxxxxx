Elephunk is the third studio album by American hip hop group The Black Eyed Peas, released on June 24, 2003. The album charted at number 14 on the American Billboard 200 albums chart and was certified double platinum by the Recording Industry Association of America, and has gone on to sell 8.5 million copies worldwide, with 3.2 million in the United States alone.[2] The album was produced by Ron Fair and band member will.i.am, and was the first to feature current member Fergie and their first as "The Black Eyed Peas". Four singles were released from Elephunk, including "Where Is the Love?", which topped the singles charts of over ten countries.

UK edition of 2003 album features 16 tracks including 2 UK bonus tracks, 'Rock My Shit' & 'What's Goin Down'. Universal.
