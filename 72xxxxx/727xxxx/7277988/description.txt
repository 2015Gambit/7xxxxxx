DESCRIPTION:
Events Wargame: European Escalation develop in the period from 1975 to 1985. The two opposing sides are the NATO (the U.S. and Western Europe) and the Warsaw Pact (the Soviet Union and Eastern Europe). Fictional battles showing the possible course of events. Since both sides know that the beginning of the nuclear bombing would lead to the inevitable retaliation, and environmental catastrophe, being fierce fighting in Europe. It is these battles, and are the basis of single-player campaign.
Wargame: European Escalation is a real-time strategy with the use of IRISZOOM, which allows you at any time to display on the screen almost all of the battlefield, or to bring the camera to the ground so close that it will be possible to consider the shadows of the foliage on the tanks. Besides startling detail graphics, Wargame: European Escalation used in virtually all services EugenNet, which adds a wide range of networking opportunities.
