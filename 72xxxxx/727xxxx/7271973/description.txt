Tron: Uprising

Tron: Uprising is an upcoming American animated science fiction television series, part of the Tron franchise, which will air on Disney's television channel Disney XD, in the United States. The show is set to premiere on June 7, 2012.[2][1] The series will be directed by Charlie Bean, Joseph Kosinski and Sean Bailey, who will also act as executive producer. Edward Kitsis, Adam Horowitz and Sean Bailey will produce the series.[3] The series is set between Tron and Tron: Legacy. Tron: Uprising has been confirmed to be a 10-parted mini-series.[4]
