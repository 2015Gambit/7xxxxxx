Learn eight Acoustic Rock guitar songs with Jamie Humphries. 

Coldplay: Yellow, U2: One, Radiohead: High And Dry, Karma Police, David Gray: Babylon, REM: Losing My Religion, Paul Weller: Wildwood, Travis: Sing

This excellent DVD, suitable for beginners and intermediate players, is hosted by Jamie Humphries, guitar tutor at ACM, author of the acclaimed Giants Of Rock and Giants Of Metal guitar courses, and regular contributor to Guitar Techniques magazine. Learn eight complete songs with this easy to follow tutorial.
