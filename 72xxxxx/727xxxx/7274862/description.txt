Jose Perez Jr, a young music producer/graphic & web designer was born in Milwaukee, Wisconsin. Raised by his mother in a difficult environment, he grew up around music & quickly adopted the music making persona Young Zay in the 7th grade. Trying to find an escape he studied the art and began crafting his style. Now 20 years old and previously attended Full Sail University he is intending to make his mark in the music industry. From design to production it is all from the same mind. For inquiries contact beatzbyzay@gmail.com & visit the website www.YoungZay.com.
