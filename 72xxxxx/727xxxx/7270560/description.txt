Magic Gangs and Wrestlers

Sociopolitical Documentary hosted by Seyi Rhodes, published by Channel 4 broadcasted as part of CH4 Unreported World series in 2012 - English narration


Information
------------------------------

Reporter Seyi Rhodes and director Wael Dabbous travel to the Democratic Republic of Congo and enter the extraordinary world of professional wrestling. In the capital, Kinshasa, they find some of the superstars of the sport practicing 'black magic', and uncover allegations that many fighters are involved in gang violence and political intimidation.
