USAGE NOTATIONS
---------------
* Select "Use Default Catalog" during 1st launch: afterwhich, you can edit
in settings.

Note: to keep things simple, "default catalog" is located @ My Pics when
running. When you close LightroomPortable, the catalog is copied back into
PAF and is located in: LightroomPortable > Data > Lightroom.
