Composer, musical technologist and legendary electronic dance music pioneer, BT releases his anxiously anticipated new double disc compilation, Laptop Symphony. 
The result is an emotionally stunning and dynamic sonic sojourn which dances on the bleeding edge of technology. 

Laptop Symphony is guaranteed to thrill long time BT fans as well as those new to the EDM scene.

Release Date: 14th May 2012.
