CBR / ZIP | English | 36 Issues  (05.1988 - 12.1991)

Details the adventures of a group of adventurers based on the rules and settings of the Advanced Dungeons and Dragons Role Playing Game. The action generally was based around the city of Waterdeep. In tying in with the origin of the characters, game information for a majority of them was given in the pages at the back of issues.
