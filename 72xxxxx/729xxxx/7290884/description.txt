"The concert was recorded on Elton's 60th birthday, 25 March 2007, and coincides with his world 

record-setting 60th concert at Madison Square Garden in New York City.
In addition to his regular band, a choir also performs on several songs."

This recording is of extremely high quality as it is a direct conversion from the raw audio ripped directly from the Bluray release of this concert. It is encoded at 320kbps.
