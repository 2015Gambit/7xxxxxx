Rowing the Pacific

Travel Documentary hosted by James Cracknell, published by Discovery Channel in 2012 - English narration


Information
------------------------------

James Cracknell follows two ocean rowers and their deadly, world record breaking attempt to cross the Pacific Ocean. Can Mick and Chris accomplish their epic 7000 mile mission?
