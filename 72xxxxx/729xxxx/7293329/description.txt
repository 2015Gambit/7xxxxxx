Abora Recordings is proud to present Abora World Volume 1, a fine compilation of 22 tracks celebrating the labels 40th release. Sail around the world in an epic adventure of exotic and uplifting sounds.

Exclusively re-edited for this release, be sure to check out the 2012 Mix of Afternova - Sunset in Dubai, the JPL Dub Mix of Kerris - Falling and SoundLift's Extended Mix of Acapulco. Several brand-new tracks by Icone, Sequence 11, Dmitry Golban, Moein and Altima have been also been included.

Part 1 is an epic showcase mix of melodic uplifting trance, while Part 2 is more diverse with subwoofer-shaking basslines, groovy melodies and sizzling electro-trance.

Many talented uplifting and progressive producers are featured on this release, including: Afternova, Andy Blueman, SoundLift, Mike Foyle, JPL, Nery, Spark7, DoubleV and many more!

This 2 disc compilation includes the original mixes and DJ continuous mixes of each disc mixed by Steve Wensley.


Release date: 23rd May 2012.
