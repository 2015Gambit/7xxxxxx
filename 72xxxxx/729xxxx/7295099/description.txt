Instructions for installing Angry Birds Seasons - The Year of Dragon :

1. Single click on setup and Install the Game using Setup provided.


2. After installation do not run the game as we have to patch the game.

3. Double click the Patch provided and than click on patch you will be greeted by a 

message successfully patched.

4. Now just open the Game and click on activate full version and fill the serial 

 and then click on register.

5. your game is ready for play
