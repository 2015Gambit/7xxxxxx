The film is about a DJ Rahul Kher, who is in love with Tia, daughter of a retired I.G. of police. Soon after the retirement, Tias father converted himself to a complete Hindustanti, which according to him means wearing only kurta pyjamas and using no English words in conversations. Now Rahul needs to prove himself as the pakka Hindustanti to Tias dad. Meanwhile, an international terrorist Carlos is planning to blast the peace conference that will be held in Mumbai. On his journey, Carlos gets his head severed and his head ends up being in Rahuls custody. Rahul has no idea about whose head he is taking care of. He just wants to get rid of this head.
