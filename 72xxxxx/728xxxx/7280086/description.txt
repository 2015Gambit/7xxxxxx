The Fray is the second studio album by The Fray. Released on February 3, 2009, through Epic Records,[1] the album debuted at number-one on the Billboard 200 chart, and became a top 5 hit in Australia and Canada. It was nominated for a Grammy Award for Best Pop Vocal Album in 2010.[2] However, it lost to The E.N.D. by The Black Eyed Peas.
The album was preceded by the single "You Found Me," which charted in the top ten on the Billboard Hot 100 and reached the number-one spot in Australia.
