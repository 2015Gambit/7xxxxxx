TACGYM MANUAL Your expertly crafted, 79 page Field Manual to Tactical Gymnastics includes precise descriptions of the protocols and workouts within the TACGYM system. Elaborate reports for all 12 workout missions (Fubar, Snafu and Tarfu and all four levels of each: Delta to Alpha) are laid out in both written and photographic format. Included are the innovative scoresheets for tracking your results in repetition, time heart rate, technique, discomfort and exertional level. The master program charts allow you to schedule the calendar of your exercise through all four aspects of recovery. TACGYM INSTRUCTIONAL VIDEOS Each of these 12 mission briefings thoroughly describe the safety points, key mechanics, structural alignment and breathing techniques for each of the skills to be learned in the program of instruction.
