Title: Torin's Passage
Genre: adventure / fantasy / point&click
Released: 31st October 1995
Developer: Sierra
Publisher: Activision
Description:
Created by Al Lowe of Leisure Suit Larry fame, Torin's Passage is a fairy tale-like humorous adventure with cartoon-style graphics and animations. The protagonist of the game is Torin, the son of a farming family on the planet of Strata. An evil sorceress named Lycentia captures his family with a magic spell and he embarks on a quest to find her and free his parents. He travels to the "lands below" to worlds beneath the surface of the nested planet, through colossus crystal columns called phenocrysts. He is aided by a purple cat-like creature called Boogle, who is able to change itself into a variety of shapes.

Bonus content: design document, manual.
