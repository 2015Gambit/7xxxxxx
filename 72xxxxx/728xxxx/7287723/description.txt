Many people consider Danish as the most beautiful spoken language in the world.
Spoken Danish is soft and musical, and the script is graceful . If you learn to read it, you will be alternately praised and doubted by your fellow westerners, but appreciated by Danes, many of whom speak passable English, especially in Ebeltoft.
Speaking in Danish about Royal Dansk Danish Butter Cookies, Carlsberg Beer, love or god makes you feel you finally understood the topic.
