The Public Beta Environment is a test-centric realm used to find bugs before patches are released live. On PBE you can test things that are going to come out in next patches like new champions and items. You get 30lvl and IPs/RPs every week for testing.

You can find the sign up form here: https://pbe.leagueoflegends.com/en/signup/index
