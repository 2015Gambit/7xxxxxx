Dutch
In het oude Israel krijgt Koning David een visoen van God waarin wordt verteld dat zoon Solomon hem moet opvolgen als koning.
Zijn andere zoon is hoe dan ook van plan om op de troon te komen. 
Ondertussen maakt Egypte de weg vrij voor de Koningin van Sheba om Solomon te verslaan.

English
Shortly before his death in ancient Israel King David has a vision from God telling him that his younger son Solomon should succeed him as king.
His other son Adonijah is unhappy and vows to attain the throne.
Meanwhile the Egyptian Pharoah agrees to cede a Red Sea port to the Queen of Sheba is she can find a way to destroy Solomon,
whose wisdom and benevolent rule is seen as a threat to more tyrannical monarchs in the region. Sheba,
Pharoah, Adonijah, the leaders of the Twelve Tribes and his own God make life difficult for Solomon who is tempted by Sheba to stray.
