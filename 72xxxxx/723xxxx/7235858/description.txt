Bombardier Dash 8 Q400

The Dash 8 is a high performance twin-engine turboprop airliner produced by Bombardier Aerospace.The Q400 series  is the most recent variant and can carry up to 78 passengers. This version produced by FlyJSim is suitable for both novice and experienced X-Plane users. It includes many advanced systems that will make your flight experience enjoyable for months to come whether you are looking for a short flight or an extended route simulation.
