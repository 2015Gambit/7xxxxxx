
Chaos Is Me is the debut studio album by American screamo band Orchid. It was released in 1999 through Ebullition Records. Chaos Is Me heralded the change of the guard for extreme music, and brought both Amherst, Massachusetts and Hampshire College to the forefront of the scene's attention.
