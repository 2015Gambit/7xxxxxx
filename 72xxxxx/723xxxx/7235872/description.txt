Grandmaster Dance 3: 2000-2009 - 30 classic dance anthems in one stunning megamix from Mastermix producer Danny Holland. The mix features a selection of the biggest dance hits spread over a ten year period. Tracks include 'Make Luv', 'Starlight', 'Lola's Theme', 'I Like The Way', 'Make A Move On Me', 'The Creeps', 'Destination Calabria' & 'When Love Takes Over'. Each track is a proven floorfiller and have been mixed together perfectly to create the essential DJ set.
