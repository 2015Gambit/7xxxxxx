Gadkie Lebedi (2006)  Konstantin Lopushansky
(AKA The Ugly Swans)

Synopsis: The writer, Viktor Banev, is seconded to the UN mission in Tashlinsk, 
a Zone town shrouded in perpetual rain and mist. In reality Banev has come in search 
of his daughter Ira who is living in a boarding school for gifted children run by mysterious mutants known as [i]mokrytsy[/i]. (the wet ones). When the authorities plan to destroy the Zone together with its inhabitants, Viktor decides to rescue Ira.
