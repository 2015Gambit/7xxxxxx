

Recorded at the Hammersmith Apollo on the 17th July 2011. Filmed in front of a sold out audience and with a 13 strong camera crew we have captured the show at its best. Lasers, explosions, inflatable pigs and of course Skippy the Kangaroo. Over 2 hours of live performance and extras including a 30 minute documentary. Includes; Disc 1 Shine On You Crazy Diamond 1-5, Welcome To The Machine, Coming Back to Life, Arnold Layne, Sorrow, Learning to fly, Dogs, Speak To Me, Breathe, On The Run, Time, Breathe (Reprise), The Great Gig In The Sky, What Do You Want From Me, Careful with that Axe Eugene, Money, The Happiest Days Of Our Lives, Another Brick In The Wall part 2, Wish You Were Here, One Of These Days, Comfortably Numb, Run Like Hell. The Australian Pink Floyd Show is a live touring sensation which has now sold over three million tickets worldwide. Emerging back in 1988, the original and credible Floyd act TAPFS has been getting bigger and better ever since. The band were described by Floyd drummer Nick Mason on BBC 5 Live as "Very good, probably better than we are," and even engaged by David Gilmour to perform at his 50th birthday celebration! The Mirror hailed the act quite simply as, "The kings of the genre". The Australian Pink Floyd Show have also been described by Sunday Times as "The best tribute band in the world".
