Product Description
Learn five Muse tracks note for note, guitar lessons by Michael Casswell

This superb DVD will teach you five tracks from this multi million selling alternative rock band... learn each track note for note! Lessons include;

Plug In Baby
Hysteria 
Stockholm Syndrome 
Knights Of Cydonia 
Supermassive Black Hole

Michael Casswell is a highly sought after freelance guitarist and producer. He has played and toured with many well known bands and artists as diverse as Brian May, Cozy Powell and Ronan Keating to name just a few. His playing can be heard on numerous film and TV soundtracks.
