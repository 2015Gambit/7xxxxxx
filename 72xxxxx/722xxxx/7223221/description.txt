DPIT EFFEX
----------


DPIT Effex is a professional solution to create particle and fluid effects inside of Cinema 4D.

DPIT Plants
-----------
DPIT Plants is a procedural modeling and animation plugin to generate organic structures especially suited for trees and plants.

Installation Instruction
------------------------
1. Open it with latest version of WinRAR or 7-zip.
2. Extract the folder to plugins folder.
Usually C:/Program Files/MAXON/Cinema 4D RXX/plugins
3. Open Cinema 4D and enjoy.

Extra Notes
-----------
Works in mac too not tested though.
