Ghost Stories of Walter de la Mare

From BBC Radio 4 Extra.

All Hallows (Read by Richard E. Grant)

A weary traveller is oppressed by the supernatural atmosphere inside a gigantic cathedral by the sea.

Seaton's Aunt (Read by Toby Jones)

Two boys spend a holiday with an eccentric and sinister relative - an experience that will mark them forever.

Crewe (Read by Kenneth Cranham)

In a chilly railway station waiting-room, an old man shares the haunting tale of a post he once held.

A Recluse (Read by Anthony Head)

One hot night in the English countryside, a traveller must stay at a sinister gentleman's remote house.

The Almond Tree (Read by Julian Wadham)

A man recalls the love affair he witnessed as a child that is set to influence the rest of his life.
