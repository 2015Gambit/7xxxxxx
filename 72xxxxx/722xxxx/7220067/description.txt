Show Info:

It's Attack of the Summer Movies! Tonight on 'Attack of the Show,' Kevin Pereira and Sara Underwood talk to Jackie Earle Haley about his new role in Tim Burton's remake of 'Dark Shadows,' and Jessica Chobot checks out Stereo D to learn more about their 3D conversion technology for films like 'Titanic.' Chris Gore also brings a DVDuesday behemoth with reviews on the latest 3D releases of 'Underworld Awakening,' 'Captain America' and more.
