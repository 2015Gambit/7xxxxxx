Pandora's tower is a Nitendo Wii game.
It is a famous Action-RPG.

It is the PAL Multi5 (english, french, italian, spanish and german) version (SX3P01).
The voices are not in english, they are in jap (extracted from NTSC version).

The game is compressed in gcz.
It can easily be converted in iso with Dolphin-emu (It is explained how in a txt file).
