The Kidnap Diaries
Date: 26/04/2012
BBC Four

Dramatised account of film-maker Sean Langan's kidnapping
in 2008 while on a quest to become the first western
journalist to film the Taliban training camps.

In a bizarre meeting of East and West, the self-confessed
adrenaline junkie strikes up an unlikely friendship with
the deeply Islamic family holding him captive.

As his captors become his hosts, he begins to question his
own motives and discovers a common humanity across the vast
cultural divide.
