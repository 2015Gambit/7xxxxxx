Art historian Alixe Bovey follows the Gough Map, the oldest surviving road map of Britain, in search of the country as it was in the medieval era.


1/6 Alixe Bovey follows the trail north from York to the hotly-contested Scottish border.

First broadcast: 10 Apr 2008


2/6 Alixe Bovey heads west from Gloucester to Wales in the footsteps of Edward I's armies.

First broadcast: 17 Apr 2008


3/6 Alixe Bovey follows the ancient pilgrim trail from the east coast to London.

First broadcast: 24 Apr 2008


4/6 Medieval art historian Dr Alixe Bovey discovers the origins of precedent in common law.

First broadcast: 01 May 2008


5/6 Alixe Bovey explores medieval Scotland, a nation so young it still had no capital.

First broadcast: 08 May 2008


6/6 Alixe Bovey explores the West Country as it appeared in medieval times.

First broadcast: 15 May 2008


A short sample is included for evaluation.
