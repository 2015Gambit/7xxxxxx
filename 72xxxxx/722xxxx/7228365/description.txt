The Exes is an American comedy television series starring Donald Faison, Wayne Knight, Kristen Johnston, David Alan Basche and Kelly Stables.

It debuted on TV Land on November 30, 2011, and airs on Wednesday nights at 10:30 pm, following Hot in Cleveland. On February 2, 2012, TV Land renewed the series for a second season.
