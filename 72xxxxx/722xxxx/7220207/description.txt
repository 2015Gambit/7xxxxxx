Product Description
Learn five Oasis tracks note for note with Jamie Humphries

This excellent DVD will help you master the classic Britpop anthems that rejuvenated guitar playing in the nineties;

Live Forever
Dont Look Back In Anger 
Wonderwall
Roll With It
Champagne Supernova

Jamie Humphries is the author of the acclaimed Giants Of Rock and Giants Of Metal guitar courses. He is a tutor at one of the UKs leading music colleges, and a regular contributor to Guitar Techniques magazine.
