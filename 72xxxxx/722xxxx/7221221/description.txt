Cherry Lane - Ozzy Osbourne: The Randy Rhoads Years

The finest moments from the creative peak of one of rock's genuine all time greats, Ozzy Osbourne, featuring classic riffs and solos from the legendary Randy Rhoads.Learn the guitar styles and techniques Randy Rhoads made famous with Ozzy Osbourne. Each part is played up to speed, then broken down note-by-note in this 2-DVD set to provide and in-depth analysis of 8 songs.

 


Songs include:

Mr. Crowley
Crazy Train
Revelation (Mother Earth)
I Don't Know
Diary of a Madman
You Can't Kill Rock and Roll
Flying High Again
Little Dolls
