Lick Library Jam With Ozzy Osbourne


Product Description
Includes track tutorials and guitar jam tracks, taught by Danny Gill

This excellent DVD will show you how to nail seven Ozzy tracks featuring the guitar wizardry of Zakk Wylde, Jake E Lee and the inspirational and unique Randy Rhoads! Learn each song and play along with guitar jam tracks;

Over The Mountain, Miracle Man, Flying High Again, Crazy Train, Mr Crowley, Bark At The Moon, No More Tears .
