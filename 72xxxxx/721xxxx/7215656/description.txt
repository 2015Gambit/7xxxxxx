Title: Scooby-Doo! Phantom of the Knight
Genre: point&click / puzzle-solving / educational
Released: 30th March 2001
Developer: The Learning Company
Publisher: The Learning Company
Description:
Lost with Scooby, Shaggy, Fred, Velma and Daphne in the forests of Nova Scotia, you stumble across a medieval castle. Watch out, the place is haunted! A ghost clad in armour on a fire-breathing dragon - "Jinkies, it's the Phantom Knight!". Has he kidnapped the Princess? Get set for a challenging maze, puzzles and a magical wizard. Test your skills against the unchivalrous Knights.

How to play:
1) Download the game;
2) Mount or burn disc image;
3) Either:
a) run Setup.exe to install the game, then play with the disc inserted;
or
b) copy scooby folder from disc to hard drive, then play without the disc.

If you like the game, SUPPORT the developers!
