   With their best-selling astronomy textbook, HORIZONS, authors Mike Seeds 
   and Dana Backman help you understand your place in the universe--not just 
   your location in space but your role in the unfolding history of the phy-
   sical universe. To achieve this goal, they focus on two central questions: 
   "What Are We?" which highlights your place as a planet dweller in an evol-
   ving universe, guiding you to better understand where we came from and how
   we formed; and "How Do We Know?" which provides insights into how science 
   works and how the process of science can teach us more about what we are.
