The panel discussed North Carolina's Amendment 1, a strict anti-gay law which would amend their state constitution by banning same-sex marriage, same-sex civil unions, and threaten other legal protections. The panel also talked about "The Republican Brain" with author Chris Mooney, the politics of Osama bin Laden's death one year later, and much more. Joining Chris L. Hayes was:

PJ Crowley, former U.S. Assistant Secretary of State for Public Affairs.

Michelle Goldberg, senior contributing writer for The Daily Beast/Newsweek and author of The Means of Reproduction.

Esther Armah, host of WBAI-FM's "Wake Up Call" in New York.

John McWhorter, contributing editor at the New Republic and columnist for New York Daily News.

Jonathan Haidt, author of The Righteous Mind: Why Good People Are Divided by Politics and Religion.

Rev. Jasmine Brach-Ferrara, executive director of the Campaign for Southern Equality.
