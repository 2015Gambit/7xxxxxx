Madagascar is a 2005 soundtrack for the 2005 film of the same name. It features a film score by Hans Zimmer, as well as borrowed songs from various sources.

Track listing


01 Best Friends - Hans Zimmer, Heitor Pereira, Ryeland Allison & James S. Levine
02 I Like to Move It - Reel To Real & Sacha Baron Cohen
03 Hawaii Five-O - The Ventures
04 Boogie Wonderland - Earth, Wind & Fire
05 Whacked Out Conspiracy - James Dooley
06 Chariots of Fire - Vangelis
07 Stayin' Alive - The Bee Gees
08 Zoosters Breakout - Hans Zimmer
09 Born Free - John Barry
10 The Fossa Attack - Heitor Pereira
11 Beacon of Liberty - Hans Zimmer & James S. Levine
12 What a Wonderful World - Louis Armstrong
