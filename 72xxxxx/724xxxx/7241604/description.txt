Future Weapons Season 3
Mack goes the extra mile to track down the new weapons and defense systems taking their place in the world's arsenal to deal with the changing missions of 21st-century warfare. There's still plenty of firepower, but you'll get an inside look at many of the breakthrough technologies designed to take on the challenges of terrorism, bomb threats, and close combat urban battlefields.




10)  Kill Zone 
Future Weapons witnesses the birth of a revolution in air warfare - the unmanned bomber that can operate from aircraft carriers; the amazing optical systems for rifles that makes them super accurate; the assault rifle that fires a massive .50 calibre round and the manned attack helicopters that are becoming unmanned.
