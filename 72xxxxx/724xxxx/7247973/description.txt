Grateful Dead Download Series - A Night At The Family Dog 2/4/70
Initial release : 2005

A one-off addition to the series of shows made available by downloading from the official Grateful Dead site. The full set of the 2/4/70 show from the Family Dog at The Great Highway that was partly broadcast on PBS. There are three bonus tracks also from 1970 shows. 

Tracks


Hard To Handle 
Black Peter
Me and My Uncle
China Cat Sunflower
I Know You Rider
St. Stephen 
Not Fade Away
St. Stephen 
In The Midnight Hour

Bonus Tracks from 10/5/70, Winterland

Dancing In The Streets

Bonus Tracks from 12/31/70, Winterland

Monkey and the Engineer 
Good Lovin'

Musicians

Jerry Garcia - lead guitar, vocals 
Mickey Hart - drums 
Bill Kreutzmann - drums 
Phil Lesh - electric bass, vocals 
Ron "Pigpen" McKernan - organ, harmonica, vocals 
Bob Weir - rhythm guitar, vocals
