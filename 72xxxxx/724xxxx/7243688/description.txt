Adguard 5.3 is a new release of the powerful ad and popup blocking utility that is designed to protect users from unwanted ads on websites. It silently works in the background and automatically removes banners from the pages you visit, thereby saving your traffic and reducing the page loading time. Apart from blocking ads, the program also keeps you away from phishing and malicious websites, protecting both the users and the computers.
 The program supports both 32x and 64x Windows versions, and it works on all popular browsers. In the new version, Adguard has received a new language auto-detection system for picking a primary regional filter. Three new filters were added for the different segments of Internet: Japanese, Dutch and Portuguese/Spanish.
