Storyline

Rebecca travels to Paris after determining that the van with Michael was headed in that direction. She locates the van and has definitive proof that Michael was kept in a warehouse there. She also finds photographs suggesting that someone has had her and Michael under surveillance for the last 10 years. She has been banned from travel to France and contacts a one-time nemesis, the corrupt deputy chief of French Intelligence, Antoine Lussier. In return for his assistance in finding Michael, she agrees to turn over the hard drive with the evidence she has against him. ...
