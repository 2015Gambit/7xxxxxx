Title: Scooby-Doo! Jinx at the Sphinx
Genre: adventure / puzzle-solving / educational
Released: 16th November 2001
Developer: The Learning Company
Publisher: The Learning Company
Description:
Scooby and the gang travel to Egypt to visit Velma's cousin, Thelma, an Egyptologist. They arrive only to find that Thelma is missing and a mysterious mummy is terrorising the tourists! Uncover clues and eliminate suspects as you help the gang explore ancient tombs and ruins. Find Thelma and wrap the mummy up for good!

How to play:
1) Download the game;
2) Mount or burn disc image;
3) Run Setup.exe to install (it will start automatically if you have autorun enabled);
4) Play the game with the disc inserted.
