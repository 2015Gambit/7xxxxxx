Original title: The Unearthly

Language: Italian, English
Subtitles: Italian

Stars: John Carradine, Myron Healey, Marilyn Buferd, Allison Hayes

Release Date: 1957 
Genres: Sci-Fi Horror 
Director: Boris Petroff

IMDB:
----- 
http://www.imdb.com/title/tt0051134/

Plot:
-----
Professor Charles Conway is a mad scientist attempting to develop the 
proverbial fountain of youth by creating the 17th gland. Ignoring all 
aspects of scientific ethics, his research subjects are people who have 
no family and are under the impression that the doctor can cure their 
depression. However, his research hasn't been successful and his subjects
are turned into grotesque zombies. Some of Conway's patients begin to catch
on to his scheme and intend to stop him.

Trama:
------
Il dottor Charles Conway (John Carradine) e la sua assistente, la dottoressa
Sharon Gilchrist (Marilyn Buferd), lavorano alla creazione di essere umani 
immortali, usando una speciale ghiandola artificiale creata dallo stesso Conway.
I risultati non sono dei migliori e le creature mostruose, risultato degli 
esperimenti sbagliati, sono imprigionate nei sotterranei del laboratorio. 
Ma le cavie per gli esperimenti non mancano, dato che la temibile coppia 
gestisce una specie di sanatorio per neuropatici.
