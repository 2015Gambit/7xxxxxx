Discription
  Speedy is a very special racing game. You don't have to defeat
  opponents or get the best lap time. The aim of the game is to reach
  the goal safely. That's not as easy as it may sound. There are lots
  of obstacles like water, fences, other cars and holes which make it
  much more difficult to reach the goal. You'll find several bonus
  item which help you to get over the obstacles. You can collect
  coins and you will get a bouns-car for 25 collcted. At the
  beginnen of the game you have five cars. With each solver level you
  get a bonus car.
===*===*===*===*===*===*===*===*===*===*===*===*===*===*===*===*===*===

===*===*===*===*===*===*===*===*===*===*===*===*===*===*===*===*===*===
System requirements
  386/33, 4MB RAM, VGA
  Supports Soundblaster and compatible cars and Joystick
===*===*===*===*===*===*===*===*===*===*===*===*===*===*===*===*===*===

===*===*===*===*===*===*===*===*===*===*===*===*===*===*===*===*===*===
Controls
  Speedy is controlled by the arrow keys or Joystick. While Speedy
  flies you can force him to land by pressing "Space". "P" pauses the
  game. "ESC" quits the game.
