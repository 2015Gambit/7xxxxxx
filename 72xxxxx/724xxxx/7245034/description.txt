Kitty Wilkinson

From BBC Radio 4 Extra. Written by David Pownall; Full-cast dramatisation starring Maureen O'Brien, Kate Binchy, Tom Wilkinson, Keith Drinkel, John Rowe, Helena Breck, Peter Gunn, Melanie Hudson and Robert Glenister.

As cholera strikes 19th-century Liverpool, one woman combats squalor and institutional indifference.
