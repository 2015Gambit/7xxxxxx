Rob Roy

From BBC Radio 4 Extra. Written by Walter Scott; Full-cast dramatisation starring Liam Brennan, Sean Chapman, Tom George, Alec Heggie and Vicki Liddelle; Dramatised by Judith Adams.

Episode 1: Moving Northwards

Francis Obaldistone is banished to his family seat in the North of England where he encounters the beautiful and high-spirited Diana Vernon.

Episode 2: Over the Border

There's trouble afoot when Francis quarrels with Rashleigh and heads to Glasgow.

Episode 3: Helen MacGregor

Francis and Jarvie reach the Highland Line, heading for MacGregor country.
