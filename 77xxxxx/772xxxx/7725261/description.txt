Tacata' (alternative writings Tacatà and Tacatá) is an international hit by Tacabro from Catania (Italy) with Cuban artist Martínez Rodríguez as main vocalist for the release on 541 / N.E.W.S. labels in Italy, France and Sweden and with Sony Music in Denmark. Tacata'" was originally a hit in Italy and was credited to Romano & Sapienza featuring Rodríguez reaching #4 on the Italian Singles Charts.
