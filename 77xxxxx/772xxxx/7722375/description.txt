Announcing the premier release of the blocSonic netBloc compilation series! This series compiles some of the best netlabel music available into one tasty 10 track treat. Every release comes packed with album art in three formats - EPS, TIF, PDF as well as a PDF booklet containing full liner-notes. This release's booklet is 36 pages in length and features an exclusive introduction to blocSonic and it's netBloc series.

As will always be the case, the premier issue of the blocSonic netBloc features a diverse roster of artists. Blues, Electronic, Rock, Pop and Hip-hop are all represented.
