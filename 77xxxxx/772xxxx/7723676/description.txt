Numbers surround us everywhere. What secrets do they conceal? Find it out in Mysteriez: Hidden Numbers, a fun and addicting hidden object game! Put your hide-and-seek talents to test, inspect dozens of colorful canvases and try to find all 40 disguised numbers before the time is up. Each theme level pack includes ten tricky hidden object scenes. You will have only 5 minutes to scour a picture and prove your detective skills.
