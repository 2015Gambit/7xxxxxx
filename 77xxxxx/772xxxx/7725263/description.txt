The first half of the points racing season from the 1989 TNT Monster Truck Challenge. From Chicago to Bowling Green and on to the New York mini-series. Most of the shows are good quality and only a couple are missing. I apologize for some of the lower quality shows, these were the only copies I could find.

Races Included:
Chicago 3,4,5
El Paso 1,2
Louisville 1,2
Lima 1,2
Richmond 1,2
Bowling Green 1,2
Buffalo 1,2
Weedsport 1,2
West Lebanon 1,2

Missing races:
Chicago day 1 (Tuff Trax)
Raleigh (Tuff Trax)
