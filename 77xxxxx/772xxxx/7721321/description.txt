Sample answers to the two exams to receive a Fire Alarm Technician License to install, service/repair, inspect and sell fire alarm systems. All states require this license and have the same questions and answers on their exam. Q&As are taken from NFPA 72 and the Insurance Code. All that is needed to perform work as a Fire Alarm Technician is to take these two exams and get your license. No set on the job training required for this field. Enjoy
