Devin Townsend Project - By A Thread - KI DVD


November 2011 the DEVIN TOWNSEND PROJECT
played four epic sold out shows in London, England. By
A Thread - Live In London 2011 contains all four sold-out
UK shows where Ki, Addicted, Ghost, and
Deconstruction were performed in their entirety live. By A
Thread... contains all four shows captured on four DVDs,
plus a 5-CD set containing all the full live sets plus a
bonus CD compiling all of the encores from all four
evenings onto one disc.
