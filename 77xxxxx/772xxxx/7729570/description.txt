The Highly Anticipated release from 151 Feva Gang features 30 original joints that are guaranteed to give your ears an experience like no other! A Double CD Set which includes: Disk 1: Sho'Nuff & Disk 2: The Glow "The Last Swaggin (T.L.S.)" is a play off of the urban classic film "The Last Dragon" which takes you on a journey from the streets (encompassed by Disk 1 "Sho'Nuff") into the "Final Level" of enlightenment/mastery aka (Disc 2 "The Glow") which introduces listeners to both sides of 151 Feva Gang. While Disk 1 "Sho'Nuff" illustrates 151 Feva Gang's hardcore, edgy, and energetic brand of street culture, Disk 2 features the celebration of reaching the next level with a plethora of Electro Club Bangers that are sure to keep you moving from start to finish!
