Return of the Buffalo

Nature Documentary hosted by Melissa Leebaert, published by Discovery Channel broadcasted as part of DC An Inside Look series in 2004 - English narration


Information
------------------------------

The North American bison, an icon of the prairies and a powerful symbol of the freedom and tragedy of the America West. Once hunted to the brink of extinction, bison are beginning to flourish again. The movement to bring back the buffalo is growing and is bringing back the hope of the return of the Buffalo.
