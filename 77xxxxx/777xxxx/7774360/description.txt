This is a collection of books on neoconservatives and their influence in America, with particular reference to foreign policy and war.  It contains the following texts:

"The Neoconservative Revolution" by Murray Friedman
"The Israel Lobby and US Foreign Policy" by John Mearsheimer and Stephen Walt
"The Power of Israel in the United States" by James Petras
"The Transparent Cabal" by Stephen Sniegoski
"Neoconservatism as a Jewish Movement" by Kevin MacDonald
Several book reviews which develop the themes in the above books, often in franker terms.
