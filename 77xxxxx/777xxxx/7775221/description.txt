Englisch
Originally a 30 minute portion for an anthology film, Impostor was retooled into a full length feature film.
Based on the Philip K. Dick short story of the same name,
it follows the lead character Spencer Olham's quest to regain his identity after being suspected as an alien android,
in an future Earth at war with aliens that use the androids as bombs to destroy their enemies homeworlds.
