'Baba Yaga and The Wolf'

Koyama Press, 2010

Writer - Marek Colek

Pencillers - Marek Colek, Pat Shewchuk

Colorist - Marek Colek

Visually influenced by the dense forested landscape of British Columbia, Baba Yaga and the Wolf tells the story of Katerina, the journey she takes to the edge of the Underworld, and its gatekeeper, Baba Yaga, in order to save her husband Ivan from a terrible fate.
