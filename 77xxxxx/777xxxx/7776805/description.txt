                           Tomb Raider-Coyote Creek 2
                                                                     
                                    STORY 

Join Lara in her quest to unravel the mysteries of Coyote Creek. Help her free Chief Dark Cloud from the local jail and set off through an Apache underground burial cave in search of the fabled Golden City, adventuring through canyons, blowing up bridges, paddling through underground Apache temples until she finally confronts Kellar, the Aquatlantian who corrupted Natla, and destroys his alien gold making machines.  

Credits for this fan made game go to: Cowboy, EssGee, Gmac, & Titak.

Xpadder, with Xbox360 & PS3 profiles included.
