'Rift Raiders'

Kickstart Comics, 2011, 96 pages

Written by Mark Sable

Pencils and Inks by Julian Totino Tedesco

Colors by Juanmanuel Tumburus

Four orphaned teens have their lives turned upside down after discovering a strange truth. Their parents are time travelers trapped in time, and having been hidden from them all their lives, the orphans will stop at nothing to find them. Their only hope lies in a mysterious man who promises to help find their parents - but at a price.
