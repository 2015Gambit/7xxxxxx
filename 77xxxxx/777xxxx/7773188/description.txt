As the ladies gear up for the fifth season of "The Real Housewives of Atlanta," well-known Atlanta hairstylists and friends of the Housewives, Lawrence and Derek J take a trip back in time to share some of their favorite moments from the Atlanta women. As they count down and show their favorite clips, they offer their colorful insight and spill the "tea" (beans) on the ladies. After all, women tell their hairstylists everything and Lawrence and Derek J are ready to share.
