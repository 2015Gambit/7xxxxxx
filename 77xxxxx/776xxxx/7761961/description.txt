Concetta's destiny is forever marked by Fate when her parents die in tragic circumstances, forcing her to grow up alone with only the support that her boyfriend Santino. But when everything seems to improve, a the threats of imprisonment and death loom to further change Concetta's life forever. Great production in which love, death and sex are intertwined in exciting ways to create a climate that will delight the viewer.
