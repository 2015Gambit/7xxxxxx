Street Law: A Course in Practical Law's interactive methods focus on legal issues relevant to students' lives that help them develop the knowledge and skills essential to living in our law-related society.

Features:
- Invites students to consider issues related to ownership of ideas with intellectual property coverage
- Illustrates issues in the context of real-life and hypothetical situations with Case Studies. 'You Be the Judge' involves students in considering and deciding the outcome of a legal situation
- Offers an opportunity to study human rights issues in the United States with 'Human Rights U.S.A'
- Highlights variations in local law and procedures with 'Where You Live'
- Helps students learn how to advocate for change in the community and how to solve problems without going to court with 'Youth Act'
