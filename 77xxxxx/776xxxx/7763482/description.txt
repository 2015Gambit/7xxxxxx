The Ed Show; October 19, 2012.

 170 American workers in Freeport, Illinois, helped Sensata company become hugely profitable. But now they will have to find work, healthcare and retirement security someplace else.

Ed reported live from "Bainport" for a special edition of The Ed Show.

Ed showed how Republican economic policy has devastated the middle class all across the country and what is at stake in this election, now just around the corner.

And Ed had the very latest on Sensata's decision to close it's plant for the weekend in anticipation of Friday's The Ed Show.

Guests included Sensata workers Joanne Penniston and Bonnie Borman. Ed also talked with:

    Rep. Jan Schakowsky (D-IL)
    Cheri Bustos, U.S. House of Representatives Democratic candidate, Illinois 17thcongressional district
    Fred Redmond, International Vice President, United Steelworkers
    Mayor George Gaulrapp, Freeport, IL (D)
    John Nichols, Washington Correspondent of The Nation
