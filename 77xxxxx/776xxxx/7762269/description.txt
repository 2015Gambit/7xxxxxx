This full 18 track set was recorded as they laid waste to the world renowned Wacken festival in Northern Germany, in front of tens of thousands of devoted fans and nearing the end of the European leg of the band's hugely successful reunion tour. 

With a set list culled from each of At The Gates studio albums all of your favourites are here whether it is the old school rage of 'Kingdom Gone', the tragic melancholy of 'The Swarm' or the gun shot clinicality of any of the tracks taken from 'Slaughter Of The Soul'!
