In Harley-Davidson: Road Trip you'll experience the roar of the engine, the shine of the chrome and the feeling of power and freedom on the open road.

Conquer the open road in an adventure that puts you in control of your own Harley-Davidson motorcycle. From the seats of six authentic motorcycles, you are on a special assignment from HOG Magazine to chronicle a road trip across America.
