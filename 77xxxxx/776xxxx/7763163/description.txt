13 of Project Runway's most creative and contentious designers return for a second chance at the biggest prize ever. In the season s first team challenge, the designers flaunt their fashion attitudes in more ways than one. New host, supermodel Carolyn Murphy, joins mentor Joanna Cole and judges Isaac Mizrahi and Georgina Chapman for this season, guest judged by previous All Stars winner Mondo.
