This torrent encompasses the entirety of Jason Aaron's Incredible Hulk Run starting in October 2011 and ending October 2012. At the start of this new series, Hulk and Bruce Banner have somehow become separate entities. The Hulk has been living as a hunter for an underground tribe of Moloids, while Banner has become a deranged scientist living on an island and conducting experiments to try to re-trigger his transformations into the Hulk. 

How did Hulk and Banner become separated? How do the two act when they finally are not sharing the same body and mind? Find out in this incredibly awesome, crazy chapter in the saga that is The Incredible Hulk.
