Issues 1-3 of 'Hotspur'

Eclipse Comics, 1987

Written by John Ostrander

Penciled by Ken Waller

Inked by Romeo Tanghal

Color by Olyoptics


Medievaly, renaissancey, d&d-like, swashbuckling, superhero kinda thing. Ya know, with a suave, sarcastic hero, evil lizard man, a wizard, barbarians, ghosts, attractive women and a flying eyeball. That kind of thing. 

"Ostriches? We're riding ostriches?"
