The Story. 2070. On behalf of the European Navy, Dakota sets out to destroy a giant iceberg that's drifting toward important shipping lanes. But within the ice Dakota discovers a mysterious locked chest with contents so extraordinary and shocking that the high command immediately declare it a state secret.
