Information
------------------------------

The B-58 Hustler was the first supersonic bomber to enter production for the USAF, and the winner of no less than 19 speed and altitude records.

This video includes the B-58's first flights, its bombing accuracy, speed records and MITO (minimum interval takeoffs). Also featured are training versions, and the Hustler's radical new escape ejector system.
