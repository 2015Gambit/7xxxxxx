Medal of Honor is a realistic first-person shooter that redefines the genre. For shooter fans and modern military enthusiasts, Medal of Honor is a way to experience today's authentic warfare, because unlike anywhere else in the world of video games, Medal of Honor can deliver intense modern warfare gameplay inspired by the actions of the Top Tier 1 Operators currently active in the military. Features include access to Tier 1 Ops and Army Ranger storylines, authentic weapons and large-scale online multiplayer.
