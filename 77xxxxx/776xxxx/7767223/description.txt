A Wolf Called Storm

Nature Documentary hosted by Jeff Turner, published by BBC broadcasted as part of BBC Natural World series in 2012 - English narration


Information
------------------------------

Cameraman Jeff Turner spends a year in Canada's frozen north following Storm, an extraordinary wolf whose pack was first featured in Frozen Planet. Jeff follows Storm's pack as they hunt the giant buffalo herds that roam the area and learns how they survive in this harsh wilderness - and whether Storm can pass his hunting skills on to the new generation of wolf cubs.
