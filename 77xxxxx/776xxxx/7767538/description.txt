The Football League Show 27.10.2012

Manish Bhasin presents all the action from the Football League, as two of the Championship's early promotion pacesetters go head to head when Blackpool meet Brighton in a clash of the seasiders. Meanwhile, Derek McInnes's Bristol City have so far defied expectations of another season of struggle at the bottom of the table, and they entertain Steve Bruce's Hull City at Ashton Gate
