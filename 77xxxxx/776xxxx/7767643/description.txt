Greenhouse Gardener,
by David Shelton
(1980)

"The greenhouse dramatically extends the range of plants you can grow in your garden. In this book David Shelton, a senior member of the horticultural staff of the Agricultural Training Board, gives detailed advice on how to choose, erect, and equip different types of greenhouse, how to grow variety of flowering plants and food crops, and how to combat common pests and diseases of plants grown under glass."
