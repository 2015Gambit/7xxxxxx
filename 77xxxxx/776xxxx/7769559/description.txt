Survivalcraft v1.17 (update 24.10.12) 
Requirements: 2.2+
Overview: You are marooned on the shores of an infinite blocky world. Explore, mine resources, craft tools and weapons, hunt and make traps. Build a shelter to survive nights and share your worlds online. Possibilities are infinite in this sandbox survival and construction game.
 

If you played Minecraft Pocket Edition, BlockWorld, Block Story, Petravis or another block world sandbox, you will like this game.
