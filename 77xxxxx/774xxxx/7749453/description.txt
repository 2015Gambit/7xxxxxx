Childish Gambino - Royalty 
256 (VBR) AAC

Royalty (stylized as R O Y A L T Y) is the third mixtape by American rapper Childish Gambino, better known as Donald Glover, released July 4, 2012. The album has many featured artists and is produced by Glover and his co-producer Ludwig Goransson, as well as Beck, Boi-1da, Skywlkr, and Myke Murda. This is notably different from how Glover operates on other projects, as he has been known to not work with other artists much in his music
