Photo Supreme - software tool for owners of digital cameras. The most powerful and easy to use image cataloger. With Photo Supreme, you can import images from digital cameras, view, create catalogs, archive, edit, print, send by e-mail, create slide shows, publish images on the Internet, etc.

Features & Highlights
* Advanced search capabilities
* Light Table Compare
* Import Images
* Cataloging Images
* Share Images
* Take your images with you
* Editing Images
* Scriptable
