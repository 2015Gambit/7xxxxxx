The "Meet The Beatles" Compact 33 EP was released by Capitol Records in January 1964 for use in juke boxes only. 

The cover was the same as the "Meet The Beatles" LP, but the back was plain white. Each side contained 3 songs. 

1. "It Won't Be Long"
2. "This Boy"
3. "All My Loving"
4. "Don't Bother Me"
5. "All I've Gotta Do"
6. "I Want To Be Your Man."
