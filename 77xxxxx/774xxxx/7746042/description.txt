Seven (7) kids/childrens books. All good books just to read. These books are used in teaching philosophy to children. See teachingchildrenphilosophy.org

Knuffle Bunny - A little girl says her first words.
Emily's Art - Emily the artist enters a drawing contest.
Frederick - Frederick the mouse is a poet in a mouse community.
The Important Book - What is the essence of things? Of you?
The Giving Tree - Tree gives her all.
Dragons and Giants - Frog and Toad explore bravery.
Morris the Moose - What is a moose?
