Continuing with our Biblical theme...

The Bandy Man...
A souless being which has walked the Earth since man's existence. Only a whisper lingers from those who have come across him.

The Bandy Man...
A tale of God's first and unfinished creation.


Issues 1-3 of 'The Bandy Man'

Caliber Comics, 1996-1997

Written by Stefan Petrucha

Illustrated by Charlie Adlard, Nate Pride, Jill Thompson, Miran Kim



This one goes deep, dark and philosophical, mostly about creation. Each issue also contains a three-page text piece at the end, also about creation. I remember really enjoying both parts of the book when I read it.
