Stiff Upper Lip

History Documentary hosted by Ian Hislop, published by BBC in 2012 - English narration


Information
------------------------------

Ian Hislop asks when and why we British have bottled up or let out our feelings and how this has affected our history.

Full title: Ian Hislop's Stiff Upper Lip - An Emotional History of Britain.


1)  Emergence 
Ian Hislop charts how the stiff upper lip emerged in the late 18th and early 19th century.

2)  Heyday 
How the Victorians made the stiff upper lip a genuinely national characteristic.

3)  Last Hurrah? 
Ian Hislop takes a look at the ups and downs of the stiff upper lip since World War I.
