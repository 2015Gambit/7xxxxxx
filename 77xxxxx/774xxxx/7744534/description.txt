'The Worm: The Longest Comic Strip in the World'

Slab O' Concrete, 2000, 68 pages

Scripted by Alan Moore

In a single working day, over 125 British cartoonists gathered together in one place to create "the longest comic strip in the world." With a script by Moore, the list of contributors reads like a who's who of UK talent: Garth Ennis, Glenn Fabry, John Bolton, Liam Sharp, Dave Gibbons, Chris Riddell, Don Lawrence, and many others.
