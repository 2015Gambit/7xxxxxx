The evil Constructo Corp has done the unthinkable! They've started constructing brand new buildings over the once peaceful homes of the 'dillos! Now the 'dillos have to fight back and show Constructo Corp they're not just going to roll into a ball and play dead!
The 'dillos must strategically plant dynamite below the new establishments to obliterate them at just the right time!
Now it's up to you to direct the 'dillos to the right spots and help save their home before Construct Corp ruins it completely!
