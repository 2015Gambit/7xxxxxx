All 10 Seasons of Project Runway (2004-2012) + Project Runway All Stars season 1 (2012) 

Project Runway is an American reality television series which focuses on fashion design and is hosted by models Heidi Klum and Rachael Weingart. The contestants compete with each other to create the best clothes and are restricted in time, materials and theme. Their designs are judged, and one or more designers are eliminated each week.
