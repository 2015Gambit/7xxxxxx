Match of the Day 20.10.2012

Duration: 1 Hour, 30 Min

Language : English 

Gary Lineker introduces highlights from eight Premier League games, including the clash between Tottenham and Chelsea. The game at White Hart Lane sees Spurs boss Andre-Villas Boas face the club who sacked him in March after just nine months in charge of the Blues.

It is a similar story at Anfield, where Brendan Rodgers leads his Liverpool side out against Reading. The Northern Irishman had a brief spell with the Royals in 2009, before proving his managerial mettle with Swansea.

Reigning Premier League champions Manchester City face an awkward trip to West Bromwich Albion, where they were held to a goalless draw last season. Manchester United host Stoke at Old Trafford, while Arsenal head to Carrow Road to face Norwich.

Elsewhere, newly-promoted duo West Ham and Southampton meet at Upton Park, Fulham entertain Aston Villa and Swansea take on Wigan.
