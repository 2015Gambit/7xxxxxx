Adorn is a song by American singer Miguel, written and recorded as the first single from his second studio album Kaleidoscope Dream (2012). It peaked at #26 on the Billboard Hot 100 and #1 on the Hot R&B/Hip-Hop Songs chart, becoming Miguel's most successful single to date, as well as his third #1 on the R&B/Hip-Hop chart.
