Z.I.D 2 is twin stick shooter game.
Kill all Zombies in the dark. and You should defend yourself.

Shoot the car !! You can kill zombies at a time using the explosive vehicle.


****Features****

- Z.I.D 2 is 3D shooter game.

- High quality graphics and support a rigid body.

- Supply Normal 5 map mode and 2 Survival Map(unlimited levels).

- weapons upgrade system.

- Upgrade Items : Fire rate, damage, clip size, max capacity, hp.

- Weapons : M4A1, Submachine Gun, Shotgun, Grenade.
