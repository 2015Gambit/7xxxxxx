A historical fiction series based in a large part on Pharaoh Memnon's time along with his story and that of his mother Lostris through the eyes of his mother's slave Taita mixing in elements of the Hyksos' domination and eventual overthrow.
The 'Egyptian' novels are The Quest, and River God, The Seventh Scroll and Warlock. Set in the land of the ancient Pharaohs, this quartet vividly describes ancient Egypt and has a cast of unforgettable characters.
The Seventh Scroll is set in modern times, but reflects the other three through archeological discoveries.
