This is a collection of books on eugenics.  Many come from the golden age of eugenics (c. 1900-1930). More recent books include "Eugenics: A Reassessment" and "Dysgenics" by Richard Lynn, "Future Human Evolution" by John Glad, "Liberal Eugenics" by Nicholas Agar, and "The Future of Man" by Robert Klark Graham.  A number of book reviews pertaining to the subject are also included.
