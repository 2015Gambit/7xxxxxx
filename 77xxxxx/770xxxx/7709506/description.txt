Fully updated for the revised NSW Legal Studies syllabus, Cambridge Legal Studies Second Edition continues to combine the latest information on all aspects of the law with superior exam preparation in an accessible and student-friendly resource package.

Cambridge Legal Studies 2ed HSC covers all three parts of the HSC course, including Crime, Human Rights, and five options in Core Part III Consumers, Environmental Protection, Family, Workplace and World Order with an emphasis on the effectiveness of the law and law reform.

The series:
Cambridge Legal Studies Second edition provides up-to-date material on all aspects of the law, including legislation, statistical information and case studies. Available as a pack with the text or for purchase separately, the Study Toolkit includes a sample exam, suggested responses, study tips and practice questions to further develop study skills and provide even more support for students as they prepare for their exams.
