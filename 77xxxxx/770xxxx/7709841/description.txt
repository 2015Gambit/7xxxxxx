Bailey Jay (born on November 5, 1988 in Richmond, Virginia, United States) is an American transsexual pornographic actress, adult model, and podcaster. Bailey Jay began dressing as a girl at an early age and around the age of 15 started to transition to female. She originally gained fame on 4chan in 2007 under the moniker "Line Trap" after a video emerged of her attending Otakon that same year.

In 2011 and 2012 she won the AVN Award for Transsexual Performer of the Year. She also co-hosts the podcast Bailey Jay Show (formerly Bailey Jay Radio) with her husband, photographer Matthew Terhune, and hosts the transgender and transsexuality podcast Third Gender Radio. On March 21, 2012, Bailey Jay's single, "You're Getting Lucky Tonight", was released to iTunes.
