 Mad scientist has kidnapped your only son Tim and now you must defeat him! Stop his mechanical monsters and collect enough blue crystals to save your son in Whispered Secrets: The Story of Tideville game! Game will allow you to switch between hidden object and match 3 modes to make gameplay more variate. In the other room you will meet Tom's projection once again, he will told you that he has no much time and will provide you with a tip, how he can be saved. Download Whispered Secrets: The Story of Tideville Game and find a way to return your son back home!
