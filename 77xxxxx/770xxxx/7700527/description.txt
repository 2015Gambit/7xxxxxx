Get ready for a Skitload of your favourite funny bits from Australia's best loved sketch comedy show. Jam packed with over two hours of skit hot laughs, Classic Skithouse features all your faves including Tripod, The Australian Fast Bowler, Glen Bush, the 'I'll snap ya' guy, the Nuthin Suss Institute, a number of sketches involving urine and stacks more. With a cast of Australia's funniest on board, Classic Skithouse will spin both quickly and worthily in your DVD player. Stars Peter Helliar, Corinne Grant, Scott Brennan, Roz Hammond, Damian Callinan, Fiona Harris, Tom Gleeson, Cal Wilson, Michael Chamberlin, Jason Geary, Ben Anderson and TRIPOD.
