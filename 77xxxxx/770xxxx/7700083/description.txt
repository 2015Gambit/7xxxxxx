This is a nostalgic CD. It contains 25 great success of italian popular songs from old times, from years 30, 40 and 50 in Carlo Buti voice.

All songs were extracted from old discs, in 78 rpm or LP and submitted to a sound treatment through SOUND-FORGE software which allows us to get a perfect restoration from old song for "false" new songs. In fact, several restored songs are reproduced now as they were recorded in current date. 

All songs were recorded in mp3 in 160 bps.
