Your dutch language pack for office2010, they are official, tested, and working for both x64 and x86.

Simply select the package for your Office version and run it.

Mind: Office automatically instals x86, so unless you know you installed x64 you probably installed the 32 bit version. 

How to know:
- Open word
- File 
- Help
- In the right half of your screen, somewhere in the center, below whether your version is activited or not, you should see something like: 14.xxxxxx (32-bit)
