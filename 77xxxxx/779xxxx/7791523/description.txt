The Football League Show 03.11.2012

With clubs from Leagues One and Two involved in cup action, the Championship takes centre stage this week. Nigel Clough's Derby take on a Blackpool side struggling for form after a strong start to the season. Bolton, now under the management of Dougie Freedman, meet the league's big spenders, Cardiff City, while Gus Poyet's Brighton entertain Neil Warnock's Leeds United.
