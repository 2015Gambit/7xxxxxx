Wake Integrale #1 - Books 1-3
Publisher:  Delcourt

Battle cruisers, flagships, war frigates, maintenance shuttles and pirate vessels. Thousands of ships forming a great convoy launched into space in search of new habitable planets. The convoy is called WAKE and it contains alien races of many shapes, sizes, backgrounds and beliefs. But one remains entirely unknown: human beings. Wake (titled Sillage in the original French) is a science fiction graphic novel series created by Jean-David Morvan and Philippe Buchet.
This volume collects Wake 1 to 3: Fire and Cinders, Private Collection, and Gearing Up.
