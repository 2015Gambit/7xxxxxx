Issues 1-3 of 'Phoebe And The Pigeon People'

Kitchen Sink Enterprises, 1979-1981

ritten and Illustrated by Jay Lynch and Gary Whitney

Collections of strips originally from the weekly Chicago Reader.

Lynch's best known comic book stories involve the human-cat duo, Nard n' Pat, the featured characters in Bijou Funnies. 

For 17 years, through the 1970s and 80s, the comic strip Phoebe and the Pigeon People by Lynch and Gary Whitney, ran in The Chicago Reader.
