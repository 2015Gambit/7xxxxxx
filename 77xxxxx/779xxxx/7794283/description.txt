Issues 1-5 of 'Final Girl'

Antarctic Press, 2005


Written by Lee Duhig, David Hutchison, Joseph Wight

Penciled and Inked by David Hutchison

Colors by David Hutchison


A group of college students find themselves in a strange town surrounded by bloodthirsty monsters. The lost students encounter a fellow survivor and learn more of the horrors of Castle Hills. The journey into Uncle Hamm's amusement park reveals more secrets. Split up, the girls encounter horrors individually. Only one will survive to be the... Final Girl.
