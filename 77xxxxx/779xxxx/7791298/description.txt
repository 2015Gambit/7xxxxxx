The Century of Shadows T01 - The Stone (2012)
Publisher:  Uitgeverij Daedalus

1751 Under the thumb of Pope Benedict XIV, d'Abeau and Cylinia start their project: eliminate Weltman... This person is hidden under the name of baron d'holbach, a resolutely atheist brilliant mind, has brought together under its roof the greatest thinkers of Paris to celebrate their commitment to the encyclopedia of d'Alembert. That same evening, something valuable was stolen from him: a piece of meteorite.
