Dark Horse Horror Sampler
Publisher:  Dark Horse
Publication Date:  October 31, 2012

It's never to late to explore the expansive line of horror stories from Dark Horse Comics! Experience all the terror in this one of a kind sampler, featuring fourteen of Dark Horse's most thrilling series! This collection gives readers a sneak peek at new titles, including Colder from Paul Tobin and Juan Ferreyra, Tim Seeley's Ex Sanguine, and To Hell you Ride from Lance Henriksen. The Sampler also includes sneak peeks from ongoing favorites like The Goon, Hellboy, Buffy the Vampire Slayer: Willow, and Criminal Macabre. Your nightmares will never be the same!

Featuring stories by masters of horror Richard Corben, Tim Seeley, John Arcudi, Eric Powell, Mike Mignola, Guillermo del Toro, and more!

A great launching point for the discerning horror fan!

Dark Horse horror gets under your skin!
