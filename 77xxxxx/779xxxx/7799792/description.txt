The Desert Star / L'Étoile du désert #1-2
Publisher:  Dargaud
Publication Date:  1996

Writer, story:  Stephen Desberg
Arts, pencils, colours, cover:  Enrico Marini

It all started in 1870 in Washington. Matt Montgomery holds an important position in the Ministry of Interior: A little life routine administrative officer, dull. But this balance reassuring burst one evening when he returns home: his wife lying on the steps of their home, killed so terrible. Horror of horrors, their daughter suffered the same fate. Who committed such a crime and most importantly, why? Matt Montgomery then launched in the Far West in search of the killer ...
