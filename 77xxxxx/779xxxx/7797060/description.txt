Take Me Home is the upcoming second studio album by British-Irish boy band One Direction. It is scheduled for release on 12 November 2012 through Syco Records (Sony Music Entertainment). The album is a follow-up to One Direction's internationally successful debut album Up All Night (2011). Recording began in May 2012, Carl Falk, Rami Yacoub, Savan Kotecha, Ed Sheeran and Tom Fletcher have confirmed to be involved in the development. The album was preceded by lead single, "Live While We're Young", which was released on 28 September 2012.
