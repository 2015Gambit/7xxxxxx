Memphis Charlie album by Charlie Musselwhite was released Nov 30, 1993 on the Arhoolie label. The 14 performances on Memphis Charlie include some loose live sides and even a taste of slide guitar from Musselwhite. Memphis Charlie music CDs They're the work of a more mature artist than the brash kid on Stand Back. Memphis Charlie songs ~ All Music Guide
