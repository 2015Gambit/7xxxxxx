The Raven's Flight or The Flight of the Raven (Le vol du Corbeau)
Publisher:  Dupuis
Publication Date:  2002 and 2005

The Raven's Flight or Flight of the Raven covers the theater of life in Paris, France during the German occupation in World War II.  Denounced by an anonymous letter, Jeanne, a young member of the resistance, has been arrested by the French police.  The same day, Francis, a burglar without scruples and somewhat cynical, suffers the same fate and finds himself in the same cell of the same police station.
