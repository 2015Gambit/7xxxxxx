Early Rotton Kidz in exile period original experimental rock jazz from Tonga islands, recorded in 80s and 90s in Nuku 'Alofa and Vava'u featuring The Klingon (a lucky shot Tevita)on bass and alien sounds, Ofa (The White Snake) on rap type vocal, Sri Oool Fjolkunnigr (Ulu Kava) on guitar, keyboard, synthesizer and percussion, 'Iki (byalvan) on rhythm guitar, Umi (the kid in hat) Perkins on scratchy and astounding high rock vocals
