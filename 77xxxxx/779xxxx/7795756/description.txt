The Soujourn
Publisher:  Dupuis
Publication Date:  November - December 1995

In the small town of Cambeyrac in Aveyron, the Second World War disrupts people's lives with mobilization and the emergence of German patrols. Julien Sarlat, called for compulsory labor service, deserts and hids in the old dovecote village. The train in which he was to be on has been attacked and is considered dead. The pigeon, he attends his own funeral and becomes a privileged witness of the life of villagers, shared strength and sympathy for the occupant. He also enjoys watching the beautiful Cecilia.
