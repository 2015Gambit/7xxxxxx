Issues 1-4 of 'Millennium Fever'

Vertigo/DC Comics, 1995-1996


Written by Nick Abadzis

Penciled and Inked by Duncan Fegredo

Colors by Nathan Eyring


Jerome is sick of his life and weary of his virginity. In desperation he answers a personals ad and soon finds his entire life turned upside down. Maiya is the girl of his dreams: She's beautiful, and like Jerome, she's biracial. But Maiya is reluctant to reveal anything about her past. Soon strange people and events encroach upon Jerome's already-confused world in this lighthearted,
