Cute FLV Player is an easy to use media player to play Adobe Flash FLV video files. FLV videos are usually downloaded from video sites like YouTube. You can also play YouTube videos without having to download them just by opening its link. Other feature includes full screen mode, set the player on top and files association with FLV files.
