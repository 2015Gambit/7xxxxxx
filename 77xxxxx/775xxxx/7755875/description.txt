This is a collection of the works of Savitri Devi.  It contains the following texts:

The Lightning and the Sun
Defiance: The Prison Memoirs
Gold in the Furnace
Pilgrimage
Impeachment of Man
A Warning to the Hindus
Non-Hindu Indians and Indian Unity
Long-Whiskers and the Two-Legged Goddess
A Son of God
Joy of the Sun

Nicholas Goodrick-Clarke's biography of Savitri Devi is also included, as is a lecture by Jonathan Bowden.
