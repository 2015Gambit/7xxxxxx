Jonathan Hickman during his 3-year tenure on Fantastic Four, has had one of the best, and most critically acclaimed runs in Fantastic Four history. Introducing elements such as the death of Johnny Storm, the council of Reeds and launching of the Future Foundation, this is one era in Marvel's first family that should not be missed.

This torrent encompasses the entirety of Hickman's run, in organized chronological reading order, with each issue's title given. Enjoy!
