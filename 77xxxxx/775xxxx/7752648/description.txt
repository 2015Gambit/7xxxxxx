With Starfleet dedicating all of its dwindling resources to the destructive war against the Dominion, the U.S.S. Enterprise is sent out on a crucial mission to recruit other alien races for the devastating battle. But when the legendary Starfleet crew lands on the planet of the Gorn, they encounter a reptilian society on the verge of a revolution. Now in order to avoid the collapse of a civilization, Captain Picard, Commander Riker, Chief Engineer La Forge, Lt. Commander Data, and Commander Troi must find a way to gain the trust of the governing Gorn and defeat the fanatical forces of the revolt.
