Stous "Peirates" epivivazomaste se ena etoimorropo karavi me epikefalhs enan oxi akrivws petyxhmeno Peirath Kapetanio me ploysia geneiada. Me ena koureliariko plhrwma sthn yphresia tou kai fainomenika anhkseros gia ta anypervlhta empodia pou exei na antimetwpisei, o Peiraths Kapetanios exei ena oneiro: na nikhsei ton Mayro Belami kai thn Katlas Liz sthn koyrsa gia to vraveio tou Peirath ths Xronias. Einai mia anazhthsh pou parasyrei tous hrwes apo tis aktes ths ekswtikhs Matwmenhs Nhsou mexri tous omixlwdeis dromous tou Viktwrianoy Londinou. Kapou sth diadromh polemane me mia diavolikh vasilissa, enw synergazontai me enan erwtoxtyphmeno nearo episthmona, xwris pote na xasoun to stoxo tous: thn peripeteia!
