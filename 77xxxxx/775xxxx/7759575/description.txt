Take a trip back over the rainbow, to an Oz you never knew existed. Dorothy Gale and her new found friends have found the Emerald City, but it isn't exactly what they expected.

This volume collects the first six issues of the sold out mini series and includes a cover gallery and character designs as bonus material. It also includes a forward by L. Frank Baum descendant, Marc Baum.

Written by Tom Hutchison and art by Alisson Borges and Kate Finnegan
