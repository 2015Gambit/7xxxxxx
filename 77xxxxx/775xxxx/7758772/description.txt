Dreams and Nightmares is the debut studio album by American rapper Meek Mill. The album was scheduled for a summer 2012 release, but was pushed back to October 30, 2012. It will be released under Maybach Music Group and Warner Bros. Records. Nas, Rick Ross, Wale, Trey Songz, Drake, John Legend, Louie V, Kirko Bangz, and Mary J. Blige, all appear as guest features on the album.
