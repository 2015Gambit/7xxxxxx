This is a collection of the works of T.S Eliot.  It includes the following texts:

T.S. Eliot: The Poems
Poems: 1909-1925
Collected Poems 1909-1962
The Waste Land - Norton Critical Edition
The Annotated Waste Land with Eliot's Contemporary Prose
The Waste Land and Other Poems
Old Possums Book of Practical Cats
The Sacred Wood
The Possibility of a Poetic Drama
Christianity and Culture
Knowledge and Experience in the Philosophy of F.H. Bradley
Poetry and Drama
Selected Essays
The Second-Order Mind
The Letters of T.S. Eliot volume 2


Eliot's recordings of his poetry are also included, as are a lecture by Jonathan Bowden and an essay by Kerry Bolton.
