This is a collection of the works of William Butler Yeats.  It includes the following works:

The Collected Poems of W.B. Yeats
The Collected Works in Verse and Prose of William Butler Yeats [8 volumes]
A Vision [critical edition]
Mythologies
A Book of Irish Verse
Irish Fairy and Folk Tales

It also includes essays by Kerry Bolton, George Orwell, and Vic Olvir on Yeats, the Cambridge introduction to W.B. Yeats, and a lecture on Yeats by Jonathan Bowden.
