Julie and Salome find themselves transported to Wonderland where they come face to face with the rage of the Red Knight. With the fate of the world hanging in the balance two girls must defeat the Red Knight before he lets his madness loose onto the world. But will the price they pay be worth the insanity that will become their destiny. Witness the creation of the all new Queen of Hearts in this not to be missed conclusion to the mini-series that ties directly into the new Wonderland ongoing.
