Jonny Greenwood Is the Controller is a compilation album created by Jonny Greenwood, lead guitarist of Radiohead. The album culls Greenwood's favorite reggae and dub songs from the vaults of famed record label Trojan Records, in honor of the label's 40th anniversary. The album's booklet features artwork by Stanley Donwood, as well as liner notes written by Greenwood.
