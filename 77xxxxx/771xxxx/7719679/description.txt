One hundred years after a terrible war, the world lives in a fragile peace.
In the desert trading post of Dikea, a young boy named Marduk grows up in a peaceful culture of farmers. When a mysterious object falls into his father’s cabbage patch, Marduck discovers an ancient secret that could change the world.


Discover a grand conspiracy! Help a young farmboy avenge his father’s death! Uncover the secrets of a mysterious amulet! Enter the world of Götterdämmerung!
