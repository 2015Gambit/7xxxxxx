Eagle Shadow Fist, a.k.a. Fist of Anger, a.k.a. Ding tian li di
.
 During WWII the Japanese army occupies Northern China and kills the country hero along with many innocents. A hero actor named Bruce Li performs "Man Tin Cheung" the story of a country hero and lover, for the purpose of educating the peopledefending the country against the Japanese. During the performance the Japanese troops come to arrest Bruce. Bruce fights back heroically and with the help of an actress, in the show, he escapes.
 When Bruce and the actress enter the check station at the border they witness the Japanese assaulting Chinese citizens. Bruce kills the soldiers and escapes. Hay Tze and Chau Fung, two fellow actors, are captured by the Japanese troops, but Hay Tze manages to escape and get the message back to Bruce.
 Attempting to save Chau Fung, Bruce finds himself in the middle of the Japanese Wai Che Association where Chau Fung has been assaulted by the Japanese official Tin Chi. Bruce manages to save Chau Fung, killing the Japanese officers in the process.
