                              BLOODSPORT 1988

In tribute to his Ninja teacher, defence agent Frank Dux takes unauthorised leave and competes in the Kumite, a freestyle martial arts Olympiad held secretly every five years in Hong Kong.

As the bloody competition commences his colleagues try to stop him from taking part, and Dux finds himself facing formidable opponents both in and out of the brutal bloodsport arena....
