This is a collection of works by H.L. Mencken.  It includes the following texts:

The American Language
A Book of Calumny
A Book of Prefaces
Mencken on Religion
Notes on Democracy
Friedrich Nietzsche
Mencken on Mencken
In Defence of Women
Prejudices [the first three series]
Men Versus the Man
The American Credo

Also included are two biographies of Mencken - "The American Iconoclast" and "Mencken Revisited" - and an early appreciation of Mencken, titled simply "H.L. Mencken."
