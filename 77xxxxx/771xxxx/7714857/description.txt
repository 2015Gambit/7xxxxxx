Riots and Revolutions: My Arab Journey

War Documentary hosted by Nel Hedayat, published by BBC in 2012 - English narration


Information
------------------------------

Nel Hedayat meets the young rebels of the Arab spring and witnesses a new wave of violence breaking out around them.


1)  Egypt and Bahrain 
Nel finds out what is happening a year after the first revolutions.

2)  Libya and Syria 
Nel meets the young rebels of Libya and Syria to hear their stories.
