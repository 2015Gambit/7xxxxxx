The manual is aimed at the development of professional communication skills. Actual political and social themes of the modern Russian reality are pointed out.  At the same time the students will learn to use the main grammar constructions of the Russian language.
The book contains 14 lessons. Each lesson presents texts on a certain problem of the modern social lives in Russia and lexical, grammatical and speech exercises (writing ╨░nd speaking). Lessons also include general theoretical material on some difficult themes of the modern Russian grammar.
The manual is designed for the foreign post graduate students and other foreign specialists in humanities or social studies.
