KEPPEL ROAD, THE LIFE AND MUSIC OF THE BEE GEES traces the lives of the Gibb brothers from their childhood in Keppel Road, Manchester through their amazing career as one of the wrold's most successful singing and sonwriting groups.

Featuring indepth interviews with the brothers, archive footage and specially recorded new versions of classic hits including Jive Talking, Tragedy, To Love Somebody, NY Mining Disaster 1941, I've Gotta Get A Message To You and from their new '97 album, I Surrender, Still Waters and I Could Not Love You More.

An unforgettable video for all fans of one of the world's best loved and renowned groups.


Running time: 93 mins.

Audio: English 2.0 Stereo
