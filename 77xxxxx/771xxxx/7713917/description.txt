 
Loose Change Final Cut is the third installment of the documentary that asks the 

tough questions about the 9/11 attacks and related events.

This movie hopes to be the catalyst for a new independent investigation, in which 

the family members receive answers to their questions, and the TRUE PERPETRATORS of 

this horrendous crime are PROSECUTED and PUNISHED.
