'The Book Of Nod'

White Wolf Inc, 1997, 134 pages

Written by Sam Chupp and Andrew Greenberg

Illustrated by: Joshua Gabriel Timbrook, Lawrence Schnelli, E Allen Smith, Doug Alexander Gregory, Larry MacDougal, Robert MacNeil, Graig Gilmore, John Cobb, Erik Hotz, Timothy Bradstreet, Andrew Robinson, Ken Meyer Jr, John Bridges, Time Berg, Steve Casper


'Vampire Lore' relating to White Wolf's 'Vampire: The Masquerade' RPG. While the 'histories' being told here are definitely associated with the game-world, this book can be read (and enjoyed) by those with absolutely ZERO knowledge of the game.
