The Revenge (1978) by Andrew Sachs

The Revenge is an experimental radio play without words, written by Andrew Sachs, who is also the principal performer. It was recorded in binaural stereo, which means that it is best heard through headphones rather than speakers.

The_Revenge_long.mp3 contains an introduction by the author and Ronald Mason, head of drama for BBC Radio at the time at the start of the play and full credits at the end of the play.

The_Revenge_play.mp3 contains only the play, with no introduction or credits.
