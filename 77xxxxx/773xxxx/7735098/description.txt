This is a collection of the works of Harold Covington.  If includes the following texts:

The Northwest Quartet, comprising:
The Hill of the Ravens
A Distant Thunder
A Mighty Fortress
The Brigade

Covington's essay collection "Dreaming the Iron Dream"
"The March Up Country," an analysis of the status of white advocacy


Two historical novels: "Rose of Honor" and "Vindictus"

Covington's recommended reading list is also included.


For more information on Covington, see his website: http://www.northwestfront.org/
