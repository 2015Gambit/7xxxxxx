Issues 1-14 of 'The Dreamery'

Eclipse Comics, 1986-1989

Adapters/Writers: Donna Barr, Chris Weiman, Phil Foglio, Lydia Marano, Cathy Hill

Illustrators: Lela Dowling, Donna Barr, Phil Foglio, Monika Livingston, Sherry Watson, Cathy Hill, Edward Lear


Each issue contains 2 or 3 short stories. Early issues adapt Lewis Carroll's '
'jabberwocky' and 'Alice's Adventures In Wonderland', the latter of which runs from issues 2-7.
