Scattered across The Dark, the boy's loyal toys, once so eager to save him, have lost their way. Not only are they each battling the Boogeyman's minions, but their own doubts as well. As the toys fall farther into despair, the Boogeyman continues to tip the scales in his favor. The Dark One does not play fair, and he has recovered an item that may very well make him unbeatable. Could all hope be lost?
