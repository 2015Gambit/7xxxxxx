History Cold Case USA

History Documentary hosted by Jason Hildebrandt, published by Discovery Channel in 2011 - English narration


Information
------------------------------

Forensic experts from Texas State University Anthropology Laboratory investigate human skeletons found in the United States. Each set of bones are examined in staggering detail in order to shed new light on the history of the American people. Join Dr Michelle Hamilton and her team as they use state-of-art forensic techniques, DNA testing and cranio-facial reconstruction to identify the bodies, discover how they died and recreate their likenesses. Can modern day forensics science solve the mystery of these past lives and reveal the forgotten stories of ordinary people?


1)  Cincinnati 
In Cincinnati, forensic archaeologists are called in when work on a car park exposes 90 burial shafts. Who was buried here; and why are two skeletons entwined in the same casket?

2)  Upstate New York 
In upstate New York, forensic experts explore Fort William Henry, the site of a historic massacre. But they are baffled by one soldier's strangely marked remains; was he a spy?

3)  Sacramento 
Forensic archaeologists are asked to examine the human skeleton of a young man with no face. Was he murdered; and what do his bones reveal about the California Gold Rush?

4)  Denver 
In a Denver park recreation area, a strikingly large skeleton is found in an unmarked grave. Who was the victim and how did he die? Forensic archaeologists investigate.
