Zumba He Zumba Ha is an 2011 international hit song by DJ Mam's in Spanish, French and creole, a French dialect spoken in Martinique and Guadeloupe.[1] The song is written and composed by Mounir Belkhir, Luis Guisao and Soldat Jahman. The original hit was by Mam's featuring Soldat Jahman & Luis Guisao in 2011 followed by a 2012 remix by DJ Mam's featuring Jessy Matador & Luis Guisao that also became a hit.
