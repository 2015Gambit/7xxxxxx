Plot
=====
The show concerns the adventures of twin brother and sister Dipper and Mabel Pines, whose summer plans are ruined when their 
parents ship them off to their Great Uncle Stan in Gravity Falls, Oregon. Uncle Stan lives in and runs The Mystery Shack, 
a tourist trap which overcharges unlucky visitors for a glimpse at the world's most bizarre museum, but little do Dipper and 
Mabel know that there really is something very strange going on and they must rely on one another to find their way in this 
unfamiliar town
