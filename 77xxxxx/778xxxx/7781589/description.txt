This Stacie Orrico discography may or may not be complete.  Included within are the following 11 releases...

 * 2000 - Genuine
 * 2001 - Christmas Wish
 * 2002 - Bounce Back - Single

 * 2003 - Stacie Orrico
 * 2003 - Stuck

Stacie Orrico is an American Contemporary Christian and R&B singer, songwriter, and occasional actress. In 1998, she signed to ForeFront Records when she was 12 years old, and recorded her first album Genuine (2000), which sold 13,000 in the first week of release.
