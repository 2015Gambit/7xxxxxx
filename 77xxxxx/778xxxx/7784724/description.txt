Google Maps Terrain Downloader

Google Maps Terrain Downloader is a tool that help you to get small tile terrain map images from Google Maps. All downloaded small terrain images are saved on the disk. You can view downloaded maps by Terrain Map Viewer Or you can combine them into a big BMP map. 
Note: The downloaded images are only for your personal purpose, non-commercial use.
