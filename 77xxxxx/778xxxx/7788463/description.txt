USA: Talk Radio Nation

Sociopolitical Documentary hosted by Krishnan Guru-Murthy, published by Channel 4 broadcasted as part of CH4 Unreported World series in 2012 - English narration


Information
------------------------------

With decision time just days away in the US election, reporter Krishnan Guru-Murthy and director Sue-May Oram meet the talk-radio hosts broadcasting to a country more polarised than ever before.
