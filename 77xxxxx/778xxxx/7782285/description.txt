Formerly known as Human Rejection, Murder Made God are consisting of Dennis ,Stelios ,Chris and George (Margin Of Existence).
Musically the band will continue from where HR stopped as there is a lot of Brutal, and Aggressive Death Metal songs ready.
Especially we wanna say a huge Thanks to everyone who supported HR through the years and will still support Murder Made God!!!
Finally a 3 promo songs
release has taken place in December 2011, Recorded, mixed and mastered by Stelios "Stelth" Koslidis (Human Rejection, Margin of Existence, Head Cleaner, Mass Infection e.t.c.)
This 3 song promo caught the attention in one of the best and brutal US records label BRUTAL BANDS ,who signed the band a multi-album contract in April of 2012!!!
Murder Made God will have the chance to add their name next to such a killer roster Devourment,Emeth,Gorgasm,Abysmal Torment,Abominable Putridity, Insidious Decrepancy etc.
And will have the opportunity to release some sick and brutal music!
Recordings for the debut full length album will be completed until the fall of 2012.
