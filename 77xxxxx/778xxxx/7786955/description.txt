Eway 2011 - Australia Street Maps-Melbourne-Sydney-Brisbane-Perth.iso

2011 Street Maps of Australian Cities Melbourne, Sydney, Brisbane and Perth.These maps are the same as those printed in the books. Eway is Windows software which manages the maps in proper digital format. You can enlarge and shrink the view and pan around etc. You can even mark areas and Eway will tell you the distance you may have walked or drove!

These maps are much better than those found in most GPS units. They are much more detailed to say the least.

Just mount or burn the iso and install the Windows application.
