Microsoft VirtualEarth Birdseye Downloader

Microsoft VirtualEarth Birdseye Downloader is a cute tool that help you to get small tile bird's eye view images from Microsoft VirtualEarth (Bing maps). It supports North, East, South, West four directions. All downloaded small images are saved on the disk. You can view downloaded maps by Map Viewer Or you can combine them into a big BMP map.

Note: The downloaded images are only for your personal purpose, non-commercial use.

Supported OS:

Windows 95/98/2000/XP/2003/Vista/Win7.

Homepage: http://www.allallsoft.com/mvbd/
