Ice Runner, a cute Squirrel, Charming ice&snow world!
Ice Runner is a cute Squirrel in the risky journey to overcome many obstacles, including Monster, Snowball, Fire & Flame, ice picks and Bee. Simple game rule but it's surprisingly challenging.
Ice Runner is a fast paced jumping game, fairy tale ice background, funny sound and music, with HD graphics and special game play. Tap screen to jump to avoid obstacles in your adventure! You need to keep good tempo to avoid all obstacles on the running way.
This will also be a very attractive game, If you select "Easy" difficulty, Ice runner is especially fun for children of all ages.
Enjoy to be a Ice Runner!
FEATURES:
- Very fast paced gameplay
- Really challenging
- Increasing difficulty from easy to bone breaking
- Different difficulties
