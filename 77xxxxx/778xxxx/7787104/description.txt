Contributors: Clive Barker, P. Craig Russell, Steve Niles, Hector Gomez, Scott Hampton, Chuck Wagner, Fred Burke, Klaus Janson, John Bolton, Denys Cowan, Michael Davis, Bo Hampton, Steven E. Johnson, Alan Okamoto, Jim Pearson, Stan Woch, Mark Farmer, Fred Von Tobel, Tim Conrad
