Release Date:04 Oct 2012

Language:Malay

Genre:Comedy / Romance

Director: Prof. A. Razak Mohaideen

Cast: Iedil Putra, Aaron Aziz, Erra Fazira

On the verge of another failed attempt at finding a girlfriend, a dejected and hopeless Ijoe (Iedil Putra) stumbles across a love guru (Aaron Aziz), who guarantees his tips can help Ijoe find his dream girl in 30 days. But when the love guru's tips does not seem to work and he is about to lose his best friend Kira, he must learn to find the strength and wisdom to decide whether he should continue listening to the love guru or lose his true love forever.
