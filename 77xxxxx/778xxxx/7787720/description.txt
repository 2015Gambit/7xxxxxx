Your help is very necessary to a small hedgehog Frodo.
This version without ads.
Frodo is a small hedgehog to whom your help is very necessary. At Frodo very short legs and it can't go, but Frodo can slide. Help Frodo to reach home. With this good deed you will be helped by your sharpness and force of gravitation.
This version of game contains 60 fascinating levels. If you like our game, we will surely make free updating with new, even more interesting levels.
Principle of game very simple. Break some things to clear a way. The quicker you will make it the more points will earn at each level. The gun will help to break things or to lift Frodo up. Hot air will force a hedgehog to soar. Try! It is simple, but it is very interesting.
Having bought this game you receive 60 fascinating levels and a good way to spend time.
