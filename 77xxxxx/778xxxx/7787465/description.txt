Ramparts - Unseeing Eyes

Heavy Metal, Tundra Publishing, 1993, 50 pages

Written by Turf

Illustrated by Joel Mouclier

Translated by Mary Irwin

Scripted and Edited by Gregory Scott Baisden

The world of Lamantine. A world of huge wooden machines, of great ramps stretched over the sea, of cable cars gliding above he city. Also a suffering world. Plagued by a virulent disease without a cure, a virus eating away the bodies of some, and the souls of others...
